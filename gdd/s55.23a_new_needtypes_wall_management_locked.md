## 55.23a New NeedTypes — Wall Management — LOCKED

Four new NeedTypes are required to fully support the Wall management objective decomposition. All four follow existing NeedType patterns and plug into GDD mechanics designed in Section 2.4.

| **NeedType** | **Used By** | **Priority** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| MAINTAIN_FORTIFICATION | Strengthen Kaiu Wall, Crab Wall Tower Commander | HIGH — first Koku spend, non-negotiable SI floor |
| ORDER_SHADOWLANDS_SORTIE | Strengthen Kaiu Wall, Crab Wall Tower Commander | MED — intelligence-gated, never overrides SI or garrison minimum |
| DEPLOY_SCOUTS | Strengthen Kaiu Wall, Crab Wall Tower Commander, Hiruma school characters | HIGH — standing patrol is never optional; prerequisite for ORDER_SHADOWLANDS_SORTIE |
| MANAGE_TAINT | Strengthen Kaiu Wall, Kuni Witch Hunter characters, any Wall garrison commander | MED-HIGH — catastrophic if neglected over multiple seasons |

**MAINTAIN_FORTIFICATION — Full Definition:**

- Triggers when: Tower SI drops below 6, OR horde detected and SI is below 10 (emergency repair mode), OR seasonal decay is projected to push SI below 6 before next repair window.

- Action: spend Koku from tower allocation on repairs. Breach-level recovery (SI 0) requires Kaiu engineers physically present. Routine maintenance requires no special character.

- Success: SI at or above 6 (routine), or as high as budget allows (emergency). Acts on known information only — Taisa estimates SI from observable Wall condition, not a clean readout.

**ORDER_SHADOWLANDS_SORTIE — Full Definition:**

- Triggers when: scout reports indicate elevated Shadowlands activity in adjacent province (observable symptom of Medium or High SS), jade and garrison conditions are met, and scout intelligence is current (last report within one season). Without scout coverage ORDER_SHADOWLANDS_SORTIE cannot fire — DEPLOY_SCOUTS fires first.

- Action: commit portion of garrison into adjacent Shadowlands province. Force size per SS tier: Small (Medium SS, −1 SS), Medium (High SS, −2 SS, Taisa autonomous), Large (−3 SS, requires Shireikan approval). Jade allocated per Section 2.4.15 sortie rules. Combat resolved via mass combat system against generated Jigoku Horde scaled to SS.

- Blocked when: jade stockpile below minimum allocation, garrison below minimum threshold after detachment, SI below 6 and horde detected simultaneously. Intelligence quality affects force sizing — stale intel may cause over or underestimation of required force.

**DEPLOY_SCOUTS — Full Definition:**

- Triggers when: no scout deployed in adjacent province (standing patrol gap), scout overdue (last report more than one season old), SS symptoms escalating and current coverage insufficient, or horde detected and all available scouts needed for direction tracking.

- Action: assign qualified named character to reconnaissance. Hiruma Scouts primary — Rank 1 always knows direction of Empire in Shadowlands. Other schools can scout with reduced effectiveness and higher Taint risk. Zone assigned by mission: Wall-Sight (standing patrol), Three Days’ March (SS assessment), Deep Shadowlands (Shireikan authorisation required).

- Blocked when: no qualified character available. Taisa flags coverage gap to Shireikan immediately — scout shortage is a serious operational deficiency.

**MANAGE_TAINT — Full Definition:**

- Triggers when: any named garrison character has Taint above Seeds of Darkness, Tea stockpile drops below one season of coverage, a character reaches Taint Rank 3 (elevated monitoring), or a character reaches Taint Rank 4 (immediate removal protocol).

- Three sub-actions: (1) Routine monitoring — Kuni tracks Tainted characters, administers Tea, records growth checks. Passive. (2) Tea rationing — when supply is partial, allocate to highest-Rank characters first, flag shortage to Shireikan. (3) Rank 4 removal — character pulled from active duty immediately, Kuni determines disposition (monastery, execution, or Lost threat response), garrison roster updated, replacement requested via bushi pipeline.

- Blocked when: no Kuni or Taint-detection capable character present. Taisa escalates to Shireikan — a Tower without Taint monitoring is a serious vulnerability. Acts on known information only — Taint Rank of garrison members is only known if a qualified character has assessed them.

**DISPATCH_COURTIER — ActionID — Full Definition — LOCKED:**

Available to Champion and Shireikan tier only. Sends a courtier physically to a specific Family Daimyo to formally request Wall troop commitment. Harder to refuse than a letter. Demands an answer in the same season.

- Triggers when: garrison shortage is flagged for a specific Tower AND the letter system has been tried AND the target Daimyo’s position on the “Send bushi to Tower X” action topic is still below +50 after one season of letters.

- Action: courtier travels to target Daimyo’s location. Consumes 1 courtier AP — the Champion cannot dispatch the same courtier twice simultaneously. Plants Wall Garrison Shortage topic in Daimyo’s awareness if not already present. Applies strong position modifier on arrival. Triggers the receiving Daimyo’s decision path immediately (see below).

- Cost: political capital drain. Reserved for genuine emergencies after the letter system has been exhausted. Not for routine shortages.

- Targets: target_npc_id (the receiving Daimyo), target_province_id (the specific Tower in shortage). Both required.

**Receiving Daimyo Decision Path — LOCKED:**

When a courtier arrives the Daimyo cannot defer. Unlike letters which allow position to accumulate over multiple seasons, the courtier visit resolves in the same season. The Daimyo evaluates their current position on the “Send bushi to Tower X” action topic and responds immediately.

- On arrival: Wall Garrison Shortage topic planted in Daimyo’s awareness with a strong position boost from the courtier’s persuasion modifier. Enters active topic list at elevated priority. Personality modifiers apply immediately: Chugi +15, Yu +8, Meiyo +8, Jin +6 pushing toward compliance; Kyōryōku −5, Seigyo −5 pushing toward refusal (per Section 2.4.12 table).

- Position at or above +50 — comply: Daimyo fires ASSIGN_GARRISON targeting the Wall Tower. Troop commitment executes. Honor gain applied scaled to contribution size (per Section 2.4.12 table). Permanent positive disposition modifier with the Champion.

- Position below +50 — refuse: Daimyo declines. Honor loss applied immediately scaled to Wall urgency — minor loss during quiet periods, serious loss during active horde assault. Permanent negative disposition modifier with the Champion. The Champion remembers who helped and who did not.

- No deferral: the Daimyo cannot say “I will consider it.” The courtier standing in their hall requires a decision. This season, yes or no.

**Champion Decomposition Update — LOCKED:**

The “Strengthen the Kaiu Wall” decomposition tree (Branch A, Step 1) already handles garrison checks via DEFEND_PROVINCE. The Champion’s additional pipeline for rallying support from other Daimyo follows this priority order after the existing garrison check fires:

- Step 1 — Letter system: if garrison shortage flagged and no letter sent this season to a Daimyo with available troops, fire SEND_LETTER with Wall Garrison Shortage topic. Attempt at least one season of letters before escalating.

- Step 2 — Courtier dispatch: if garrison shortage persists AND target Daimyo’s position on “Send bushi” topic is still below +50 after one season of letters AND a courtier is available, fire DISPATCH_COURTIER targeting that Daimyo.

- Step 3 — Wall-wide emergency: if garrison shortage is critical (horde incoming, SI below 6, garrison at minimum) AND courtier has been dispatched AND Daimyo has refused, trigger Wall-wide emergency declaration (Section 2.4.14 Decision 6). Compels all Crab lords to respond.

### 55.23.2 "Keep my clan’s military the dominant force in the region"

**Nature:** Competitive, outward-watching. Constantly compares own military strength to neighbors. Requires intelligence about rival forces.

**Who gets this:** Kyōryōku and Ketsui characters. Lion Clan heavily.

#### 2.1 The Scouting Question: Who Does the Rolling?

A lord doesn’t personally scout enemy borders. They assign a vassal — a scout-type character (Shinjo rider, Shosuro agent, bushi with Stealth/Reconnaissance). The lord’s decomposition produces ASSIGN_VASSAL_OBJECTIVE. The vassal’s engine fires, decomposes the assignment, and the vassal travels to the border and rolls their own skills.

This means SCOUT_ENEMY is not an ActionID the lord selects directly. It’s an action the vassal selects after being assigned an intelligence-gathering objective. The vassal’s decomposition for “Gather intelligence on Clan X’s military” produces:

TRAVEL_TO (border province)

Then: INVESTIGATE_PROVINCE (using Stealth + Perception, adapted for military observation rather than insurgency detection)

The vassal’s report feeds back to the lord via letter or return visit, updating known_clan_strengths

**New ActionID: SCOUT_ENEMY (1 AP, vassal-level).** Available at AT_OWN_HOLDINGS or ON_CAMPAIGN in a border province. Roll: Perception + Battle vs TN 20 (observing military posture) or Stealth + Agility vs TN 20 (covert scouting). Success: learn approximate army size (±20%), locations, training level, mobilization status. Failure: nothing learned. Critical failure: scouts detected — topic generated. The roller is the vassal, not the lord.

func _decompose_military_dominance(obj: Objective, ctx: ContextSnapshot) -> ImmediateNeed:

var need = ImmediateNeed.new()

# ═══ STEP 1: Do I know how my military compares? ═══

var neighbor_strengths = ctx.known_clan_strengths

if neighbor_strengths.is_empty():

# No intelligence. Delegate scouting to a vassal.

if ctx.is_lord:

var scout_vassal = _find_best_scout_vassal(ctx)

if scout_vassal:

need.need_type = NeedType.ASSIGN_OBJECTIVE

need.target_npc_id = scout_vassal

need.priority = 2

return need # Vassal receives 'gather military intel on Clan X'

# No suitable scout vassal. Write letters asking contacts for info.

need.need_type = NeedType.SEND_LETTER

need.priority = 1

return need

else:

# Non-lord: gather intel personally at court

need.need_type = NeedType.GATHER_INTELLIGENCE

need.priority = 2

return need

# ═══ STEP 2: Am I dominant? ═══

var my_clan = CharacterDB.get(ctx.npc_id).clan_id

var my_strength = neighbor_strengths.get(my_clan, 0)

var strongest_rival = 0

var strongest_rival_clan = ""

for clan_id in neighbor_strengths:

if clan_id != my_clan and neighbor_strengths[clan_id] > strongest_rival:

strongest_rival = neighbor_strengths[clan_id]

strongest_rival_clan = clan_id

var dominance_ratio = my_strength / max(strongest_rival, 1.0)

# ═══ STEP 3: Act based on dominance ═══

if dominance_ratio >= 1.5:

# Clearly dominant. Maintain through training.

var raw_units = MilitarySystem.get_all_units(ctx.npc_id).filter(

func(u): return u.training_level < 3)

if raw_units.size() > 0:

need.need_type = NeedType.TRAIN_TROOPS

need.target_army_id = raw_units[0].id

need.priority = 1

return need

need.need_type = NeedType.ACQUIRE_RESOURCE

need.target_resource = "arms"

need.priority = 1

return need

elif dominance_ratio >= 1.0:

# Ahead but not safely. Build up.

if ctx.is_lord:

var can_levy = MilitarySystem.get_available_levy_pu(ctx.npc_id) > 0

if can_levy:

need.need_type = NeedType.LEVY_TROOPS

need.priority = 2

return need

need.need_type = NeedType.TRAIN_TROOPS

need.priority = 2

return need

need.need_type = NeedType.TRAIN_SKILL

need.priority = 1

return need

else:

# BEHIND a rival. Urgent buildup.

if ctx.is_lord:

need.need_type = NeedType.LEVY_TROOPS

need.priority = 3

return need

# Non-lord: alert the lord

need.need_type = NeedType.SEND_LETTER

need.target_npc_id = CharacterDB.get(ctx.npc_id).lord_id

need.priority = 2

return need

#### 3.2 Shugenja vs Bushi Paths

The Competence Modifier creates completely different behavior for shugenja and bushi pursuing this objective:

**Bushi path:** INVESTIGATE_PROVINCE (physical), PATROL_PROVINCE, ORDER_DEPLOY. Roll Battle, Kenjutsu. They fight the Taint with steel. When a Province Taint Manifestation insurgency is detected, they enter the ASCII map to suppress it physically.

**Shugenja path:** COMMUNE_WITH_SPIRITS (detect Taint remotely), PERFORM_RITUAL (spiritual purification at temples), INVESTIGATE_PROVINCE (using Lore: Shadowlands as primary skill). They fight the Taint with prayer. When they enter the ASCII map, they purify rather than slaughter.

Both paths are valid. The engine produces them through competence, not special-case code. A Rank 4 Kuni Shugenja has Theology 4, Lore: Shadowlands 4, Battle 1 — the shugenja actions score +5 to +10 higher from competence alone.

#### 3.3 Edge Cases

**EDGE CASE:** Non-Crab character far from the Shadowlands: Contributes at court (advocate for military support, fund Wall defense, spread awareness through Topic System). A Phoenix courtier advances this objective by ensuring the Empire takes the threat seriously.

**EDGE CASE:** Shadowlands quiet, no Taint detected: Shifts to prevention: patrol, train, stockpile, advocate. The threat is dormant, not gone. This keeps the NPC vigilant.

**EDGE CASE:** Maho user discovered (intelligence from Probe or covert action): The NPC’s objective demands action against any Jigoku agent. Decomposition checks known topics for maho-related intelligence and routes to EXPOSE_SECRET_PUBLIC or CHALLENGE_TO_DUEL targeting the maho user. The Shadowlands threat intersects with the political system.

**EDGE CASE:** Multiple simultaneous Taint events: Province triage fires. Highest momentum crisis first. Delegate to vassals for secondary sites.

#### 3.4 Worked Example

**NPC:** Kuni Yori (Gi/Chishiki, Crab Shugenja, Rank 4). Standing obj: “Eliminate Shadowlands threat.” At temple near Kaiu Wall. No active crisis. Holds a Jigoku bleed event topic from a report 2 seasons ago — unexplained animal deaths in a nearby province.

**Phase 2:** Step 1: no active crisis. Step 2: no detected Taint insurgency. Step 3: Jigoku bleed event topic exists! Returns INVESTIGATE_THREAT targeting that province. Priority 2.

**Phase 3 (AT_TEMPLE):** Options: COMMUNE_WITH_SPIRITS, PERFORM_RITUAL, TRAVEL_TO (target province), MEDITATE, WRITE_LETTER.

**Phase 5:** COMMUNE_WITH_SPIRITS: ObjAlign 80 (for investigating a spiritual threat remotely) + Chishiki +10 + Competence (Theology 4 = +5) = 95. TRAVEL_TO: ObjAlign 70 + 0 + 0 = 70. COMMUNE wins. Yori reaches out through the spirit world to detect whether the bleed event is connected to an actual Taint source.

**Multi-day arc:** Day 1: COMMUNE_WITH_SPIRITS. Success: Yori senses a corruption source in the province’s eastern hills. Day 2: TRAVEL_TO the province. Day 3: arrives. INVESTIGATE_PROVINCE (now with spiritual foreknowledge — heading straight for the eastern hills). Finds a corrupted shrine. Day 4+: ASCII map quest — enter the shrine, purify it, fight what’s causing the corruption. The engine created a spiritual detective arc using only existing systems.

### 55.23.4 "Maintain peace — avoid war at all costs"

**Nature:** Pacifist, diplomatic. Actively prevents, de-escalates, and ends armed conflicts. The anti-war objective.

**Who gets this:** Jin, Rei, Gi characters. Courtiers. Crane clan (historically peacemakers).

#### 4.1 Decomposition Tree

#### 4.2 New NeedType: PREVENT_WAR

PREVENT_WAR fires when the Topic System shows a conflict topic gaining momentum between two clans but war hasn’t started yet. It’s earlier in the timeline than SEEK_PEACE (which fires during active war). The scoring:

| **ActionID** | **ObjAlign for PREVENT_WAR** | **Rationale** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| MOVE_TOPIC_POSITION | 95 | Shift court opinion against escalation. |
| RAISE_DISPOSITION | 80 | Build rapport with both sides. |
| ATTEND_COURT | 70 | Get to where the decision is being made. |
| WRITE_LETTER | 60 | Urge restraint from afar. |
| NEGOTIATE | 85 | Direct mediation between parties. |
| OFFER_FAVOR | 50 | Create obligation that discourages war. |

#### 4.3 Edge Cases

**EDGE CASE:** NPC’s own clan is at war: Deepest Bushido dilemma. Chugi says fight (duty), Jin says stop (compassion). Primary objective overrides standing, but standing colors HOW they fight: Jin leans +10 toward OFFER_TERMS in sieges, +12 toward SEEK_PEACE. They fight defensively and advocate ceasefire at every opportunity.

**EDGE CASE:** Two OTHER clans are about to fight (NPC uninvolved): Third-party mediation. ATTEND_COURT where both are represented. RAISE_DISPOSITION with both. MOVE_TOPIC_POSITION toward de-escalation. Emergent mediation through existing mechanics.

**EDGE CASE:** Justified war (Shadowlands incursion): Even peace-minded NPCs accept defensive wars. Standing objective doesn’t override crisis response. But they push for the war to end as soon as the threat is contained.

**EDGE CASE:** Bushi with this objective: They channel violence into the least destructive form. ISSUE_DUEL_CHALLENGE to resolve a border dispute through single combat rather than mass battle — one person dies instead of thousands. The peace-seeking warrior.

#### 4.4 Worked Example

**NPC:** Doji Hotaru (Jin/Dōsatsu, Crane Courtier, Rank 4). Standing obj: “Maintain peace.” At Winter Court. Active tension: Lion-Scorpion border conflict (Tier 3 crisis topic, momentum 45, no formal war yet). Both clans have representatives present.

**Phase 2:** Step 1: no active war. Step 2: rising tensions exist. At court → MOVE_TOPIC_POSITION on the conflict topic. Priority 3.

**Phase 5:** PERSUADE (arguing against escalation): ObjAlign 95 + Jin +5 + Dōsatsu +5 + Competence (Sincerity 4 = +5) = 110. CHARM: 80 + +5 + +3 + Etiquette 5 = +10 = 98. PERSUADE wins.

**Multi-day arc:** Morning: PERSUADE the Lion delegation (argue against war). Afternoon: RAISE_DISPOSITION with the Scorpion contact (build enough rapport to be heard). Day 2: PERSUADE the Scorpion. Day 3: DELIVER_GIFT to the Lion contact (Crane artisan peace offering). Day 4: TRIGGER_COMMITMENT — push both toward a Public Declaration of ceasefire. Day 5: both sides at “Leaning Yes.” Hotaru prevented a war through sustained court diplomacy. The Crane at their finest.

### 55.23.5 "Build the strongest possible fighting force under my command"

**Nature:** Internal, self-improving. Focused inward: make MY troops the best. Quality over quantity. Distinguished from “keep dominant” by not caring about neighbors.

**Who gets this:** Kyōryōku, Ketsui, Kanpeki characters. Military commanders.

#### 5.1 Decomposition Tree

func _decompose_strongest_force(obj: Objective, ctx: ContextSnapshot) -> ImmediateNeed:

var need = ImmediateNeed.new()

if not ctx.is_lord:

need.need_type = NeedType.TRAIN_SKILL

need.priority = 1

return need

# ═══ PRIORITY LADDER: systematic force improvement ═══

var all_units = MilitarySystem.get_all_units(ctx.npc_id)

var raw = all_units.filter(func(u): return u.training_level == 0)

var drilled = all_units.filter(func(u): return u.training_level == 1)

var trained = all_units.filter(func(u): return u.training_level == 2)

# Priority 1: Train Raw → Drilled (urgent — Raw troops are a liability)

if raw.size() > 0:

need.need_type = NeedType.TRAIN_TROOPS

need.target_army_id = raw[0].id

need.priority = 3

return need

# Priority 2: Train Drilled → Trained

if drilled.size() > 0:

need.need_type = NeedType.TRAIN_TROOPS

need.target_army_id = drilled[0].id

need.priority = 2

return need

# Priority 3: Equipment — ensure iron upkeep

if not MilitarySystem.can_sustain_iron_upkeep(ctx.npc_id):

need.need_type = NeedType.ACQUIRE_RESOURCE

need.target_resource = "iron"

need.priority = 2

return need

# Priority 4: Levy more if sustainable

if MilitarySystem.get_available_levy_pu(ctx.npc_id) > 0:

var rice_ok = ctx.rice_stockpile > MilitarySystem.upkeep_cost(ctx.npc_id) * 3

if rice_ok:

need.need_type = NeedType.LEVY_TROOPS

need.priority = 1

return need

# Priority 5: Push Trained → Veteran (excellence)

if trained.size() > 0:

need.need_type = NeedType.TRAIN_TROOPS

need.target_army_id = trained[0].id

need.priority = 1

return need

# Everything Veteran. Maintain resources.

need.need_type = NeedType.ACQUIRE_RESOURCE

need.target_resource = "arms"

need.priority = 1

return need

#### 5.2 Kanpeki Interaction

**Critical personality-driven behavior:** A Kanpeki lord with this objective conditionally blocks ORDER_DEPLOY when any unit in the deploying army is below Training Level 2 (Trained). The perfectionist refuses to send inadequate forces into battle. This makes them dangerously slow to respond to crises — a genuine weakness of the archetype.

#### 5.3 Edge Cases

**EDGE CASE:** No Forge (can’t produce Arms from Iron): PURCHASE_MARKET scores highest for resource acquisition. More expensive but the only option.

**EDGE CASE:** Provinces at Shortage (can’t sustain more troops): Feasibility check catches this. NPC focuses on training existing troops rather than levying. Quality within sustainable means.

**EDGE CASE:** Primary objective requires deploying untrained troops: Primary overrides standing. Lord deploys Raw troops because the crisis demands it. Standing objective reasserts after: replace and retrain the losses.

#### 5.4 Worked Example

**NPC:** Akodo Arasou (Yu/Kanpeki, Lion Bushi, Rank 4). Standing obj: “Build strongest force.” At holdings. Army: 2 Raw Ashigaru, 1 Trained Bushi Retainer, 1 Drilled Ashigaru. Arms: 15. Iron: 6.

**Phase 2:** Priority 1: Raw units exist. Returns TRAIN_TROOPS targeting first Raw Ashigaru. Priority 3.

**Phase 5:** DRILL_TROOPS: 100 + Yu +8 + Kanpeki +12 = +15 cap + Battle 4 = +5 = 120. Wins overwhelmingly.

**Multi-day arc:** Days 1–10: Drill Raw Company A → Drilled. Days 11–20: Drill Raw Company B → Drilled. Days 21–30: Drill Drilled companies → Trained. Days 31+: Push Bushi Retainer to Veteran. Then push Ashigaru to Veteran. Only after ALL units are Veteran does Arasou levy more. The perfectionist general, methodical and relentless.
