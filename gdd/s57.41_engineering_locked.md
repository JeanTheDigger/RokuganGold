# Section 57.41 — Engineering — LOCKED

## 57.41.1 Engineering Rank 5 Mastery

Characters with Engineering skill rank 5 or higher gain a +5 flat bonus
added to the kept total of any Engineering roll that is either:

- **Cooperative**: two or more characters contributing to the same roll
- **Cumulative**: a multi-session progress track where each AP investment
  adds incrementally to a running total

### Affected Actions

| ActionID | Type | Mastery Applies |
|----------|------|-----------------|
| FORTIFY_WALL_SECTION | Cumulative SI progress track | **Yes** |
| Field Fortification (battle opportunity, s11.7) | Cumulative battle construction | Yes (blocked on s11.7a) |
| Kaiu Path of Stone technique | Cooperative ritual | Yes (blocked on s40) |

SEAL_WALL_BREACH is a **single-roll single-character emergency check** (GDD
s2.4.16) and does **not** receive the Rank 5 bonus.

### Stacking

The +5 flat bonus is additive with the Kaiu Method technique (+1k0 on
Engineering/Agility rolls, school rank 1). A Kaiu Engineer at Rank 5 with
the Kaiu Method technique benefits from both.

**Constant locked in this section:**

| Constant | Value | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| `ENGINEERING_MASTERY_FLAT_BONUS` | `5` | Flat added to kept total for cumulative/cooperative Engineering rolls |
| `ENGINEERING_MASTERY_RANK` | `5` | Minimum Engineering rank to receive the bonus |

## 57.41.2 Kaiu Engineer Standing Need

NPCs whose `school` is `"Kaiu Engineer"` automatically receive a standing
objective to maintain Wall Tower structural integrity, without requiring a
lord directive.

### SI Thresholds and Standing Assignments

| Condition | Standing Need | Priority |
|-----------|--------------|----------|
| Any Wall Tower `wall_si < 7` | `MAINTAIN_FORTIFICATION` | 2 |
| Any Wall Tower `wall_si == 0` (breach) | `MAINTAIN_FORTIFICATION` | 1 |
| No Wall Tower `wall_si < 7` | No auto-assignment (system is healthy) | — |

The NeedType is `MAINTAIN_FORTIFICATION` in both cases. When SI=0 the priority is
elevated to 1 to compete with crisis overrides. The NPC engine naturally selects
SEAL_WALL_BREACH over FORTIFY_WALL_SECTION at breach because FORTIFY is blocked
when SI=0 (executor returns "si_is_zero_use_seal"). SEAL_WALL_BREACH scores 100
under MAINTAIN_FORTIFICATION vs FORTIFY_WALL_SECTION at 95.

**Wall Tower only.** Castle walls and provincial fortifications do not have
SI tracking in the current implementation; those thresholds are deferred
until SI tracking is implemented for those settlement types.

### Assignment Rules

- Assignment fires once per tick if no standing objective already exists for
  the character.
- If a standing objective already exists (any type), it is not overridden by
  this assignment (magistrate UPHOLD_LAW, FIND_NEW_LORD, etc. take precedence
  for mixed-role characters — in practice Kaiu Engineers hold no other
  standing roles).
- The function checks the lowest `wall_si` across all Wall Tower settlements.
  Priority 1 (breach) fires if **any** tower is at SI=0; priority 2 fires if
  **any** tower is below 7 (and none is at 0).

### Non-Kaiu Engineers

NPCs with Engineering skill but without `school == "Kaiu Engineer"` do not
receive auto-assigned wall maintenance objectives. They require an explicit
lord directive (ASSIGN_VASSAL_OBJECTIVE).
