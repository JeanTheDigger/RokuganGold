## s4.3a Famine Recovery Threshold — LOCKED

### Context

GDD s4.3.6 specifies the starvation cascade recovery path:

> "Total recovery from Famine to Clear takes four seasons of uninterrupted adequate food."

The four-season recovery sequence: Famine → Hunger → Shortage → (Shortage maintained) → Clear.
Each step requires one season of positive rice balance.

### Mechanic

The simulation tracks famine recovery in `_process_famine_crises()`. A province enters the
recovery counter (`_famine_tracking`) when its starvation stage drops below HUNGER
(i.e., it reaches Shortage or Clear for the first time). The counter increments each
seasonal tick the province remains below HUNGER.

When the counter reaches `_FAMINE_RECOVERY_THRESHOLD`, the famine crisis topic resolves
and the province's `active_crisis_id` is cleared.

### Locked Value

**`_FAMINE_RECOVERY_THRESHOLD = 4`**

**Derivation:** Directly from GDD s4.3.6 "four seasons of uninterrupted adequate food."
The threshold counts seasons at non-starving status. Even if a province enters tracking
at Shortage (having already spent some seasons recovering from Famine), requiring 4
additional seasons of positive rice balance ensures the GDD's full four-season window
is observed before the crisis is considered resolved. This is conservative but faithful
to the GDD's intent that "recovery from war and famine takes years, not seasons,
preserving the weight of catastrophic events."

|----------|-------|----------|
