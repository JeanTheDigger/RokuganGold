## 12.8c Seduction Roll Formula — CONFIRMED ZERO VALUES

This section confirms two constants in `SeductionSystem` that were zeroed during the
invented-content audit and remained marked "pending GDD specification." Both are confirmed
correct at 0. No design gap exists.

---

### BASE_TN = 0 — CONFIRMED

**GDD formula (s12.8):**
> "Core roll: Temptation (Seduction) / Awareness vs target's Etiquette (Courtesy) /
> Willpower + Honor Rank (per The Strength of Honor)"

The defense formula is complete: `etiquette_rank + willpower + honor_rank`. No base
addend is implied. The formula IS the defense score — `BASE_TN = 0` is the correct
interpretation.

**Representative defense values:**

| Target profile | Etiquette | Willpower | Honor Rank | Defense TN |
|----------------|-----------|-----------|------------|------------|
| Low-status / low-honor | 1 | 2 | 1 | 4 |
| Average samurai | 3 | 2 | 4 | 9 |
| High-honor courtier | 5 | 3 | 7 | 15 |
| Maximum (theoretical) | 7 | 4 | 10 | 21 |

Low TNs for average characters are intentional. Seduction is effective in Rokugan —
that is the fiction. The Honor Rank addend is the primary counterbalance: a character
who maintains honor 7+ is genuinely hard to seduce even for a trained Scorpion agent.

**Attacker pools for reference:**

| Seducer | Temptation | Awareness | Roll pool | Avg total |
|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| Novice | 1 | 2 | 3k2 | ~9 |
| Trained | 3 | 3 | 6k3 | ~16 |
| Expert | 5 | 3 | 8k3 | ~19 |

A trained Scorpion agent (Temptation 3+) succeeds reliably against average samurai
(TN 9) and competes against high-honor characters (TN 15). This matches the fiction.

---

### INFAMY_GAIN = 0.0 — CONFIRMED

**GDD text (s12.8):**
> "Honor cost: 'Using a Low Skill' per the Honor table (Section 4.6). School exemptions apply."

The GDD specifies an honor cost only. Infamy is not mentioned. Seduction is a covert
act — if undiscovered, no public reputation damage accrues. Infamy consequences from
the affair *do* reach the actor when the secret is exposed through the existing
scandal topic pipeline, but that is handled by the topic/secret system, not here.

**`INFAMY_GAIN = 0.0` is correct. No design gap.**
