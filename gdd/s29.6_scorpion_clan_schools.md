## 29.6 Scorpion Clan Schools

**BASIC SCHOOLS:**

**Bayushi Bushi [CR]:**

Perfect weapons of discord. Every student is trained in social manipulation, stealth, and poisons alongside weapons — capable of turning clans against one another with minimal effort. Techniques focus on dirty tricks: seizing initiative advantage, exploiting existing edges, and crippling opponents with Conditions to turn the tide against superior foes.

- Benefit: +1 Intelligence

- Skills: Courtier (Manipulation), Defense, Etiquette, Iaijutsu, Kenjutsu, Sincerity, any one Skill

- Honor: 2.5 | Outfit: Light Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, any 1 weapon, Traveling Pack, 5 koku

- Rank 1 — The Way of the Scorpion: +1k1 to Initiative Rolls. +5 to Armor TN against any opponent with lower Initiative.

- Rank 2 — Pincers and Tail: The Feint Maneuver costs only 1 Raise instead of 2.

- Rank 3 — Strike at the Tail: When assuming a Stance, choose a target within 30’. If you hit them this Turn, the target is Fatigued as if without rest for 24 hours. Each Reactions Stage, the affected opponent may roll Earth Ring (TN 25) to negate; if they fail, the effect persists until the next Reactions Stage.

- Rank 4 — Strike From Above, Strike From Below: Make melee attacks as a Simple Action instead of a Complex Action.

- Rank 5 — The Pincers Hold, The Tail Strikes: Once per encounter, spend a Void Point as a Complex Action to attack with a melee weapon. On a hit, the target is Stunned. Each Reactions Stage, the affected opponent may roll Earth Ring (TN = damage dealt) to end the Stun; if they fail or do not attempt it, the Stun ends the following Reactions Stage.

**Bayushi Courtier [CR]:**

Masters of manipulation and the exploitation of secrets. The Scorpion information network is largely a product of Bayushi courtiers ferreting out minute details to bring ruin to individuals, families, or entire clans. Any foe, no matter how implacable, can be opened up by a Bayushi courtier’s abilities.

- Benefit: +1 Awareness

- Skills: Calligraphy, Courtier (Gossip), Etiquette, Investigation, Sincerity (Deceit), Temptation, any one High Skill

- Honor: 2.5 | Outfit: Extravagant Clothing, Wakizashi, any 1 weapon, Calligraphy Set, Traveling Pack, 5 koku

- Rank 1 — Weakness is My Strength: When making a Contested Social Roll, gain one Free Raise per 3 points of Mental or Social Disadvantages your opponent possesses (max 5 Free Raises). Also gain a Free Raise when using Courtier to spread gossip, and need not Raise to conceal that you are the source.

- Rank 2 — Shallow Waters: After conversing with someone for a few minutes, make a Contested Investigation/Awareness vs Etiquette/Awareness roll. On success, learn the identity and rank of their lowest Mental Trait and lowest Social Skill (at least 1 Rank). Spend a Void Point to also learn their next-lowest Mental Trait and Social Skill.

- Rank 3 — Secrets are Birthmarks: Contested Courtier (Manipulation)/Awareness vs Etiquette (Courtesy)/Awareness to lure a target into revealing one Mental or Social Disadvantage (or Advantage if they have none). With GM permission, a revealed Disadvantage may allow you to take a 2-point Blackmail Advantage against that individual at no XP cost.

- Rank 4 — Scrutiny’s Sweet Sting: In Contested Social Skill Rolls against someone you hold Blackmail on, their dice cannot explode. In any Contested Social Skill Roll, spend a Void Point to force the opponent to use a Mental Trait of your choice instead of the normal Trait.

- Rank 5 — No More Masks: School Rank times per session, make a Contested Courtier (Gossip)/Awareness vs Etiquette (Courtesy)/Awareness roll (+5k0 bonus if you hold Blackmail on the target). On success, inflict one of the following Social Disadvantages for one month: Social Disadvantage, Sworn Enemy, or Obligation (to someone of your choice). Cannot target the same person until the effect expires.

**Shosuro Actor [Ninja]:**

The Shosuro Butei (Acting) Academy is a famous acting school — but its most promising students receive instruction in poison, stealth, and assassination, and are given contracts within the Scorpion spy network to build the false personas they use throughout their espionage careers. Silent killers who take their clan’s sacred duty extremely seriously.

- Benefit: +1 Awareness

- Skills: Acting, Etiquette, Knives, Meditation, Sincerity (Deceit), Stealth, any 1 High or Low Skill

- Honor: 1.5 | Outfit: Fine Clothing, Assorted Costumes and Disguises, Knife, Traveling Pack, 10 koku

- Rank 1 — The First Face: Learn one persona. When spending a Void Point on Acting or Sincerity (Deceit), gain +3k1 instead of the normal +1k1.

- Rank 2 — The Subtle Sting: +2k0 to attack rolls when wielding a Small weapon.

- Rank 3 — The Second Face: Learn a second persona. May now spend 2 Void Points (instead of 1) when enhancing a School Skill roll. Stacks with Rank 1.

- Rank 4 — The Viper’s Kiss: Attack as a Simple Action instead of a Complex Action when using a Small-sized weapon.

- Rank 5 — The Third Face: Learn a third persona. When attacking a foe who is unaware or does not expect danger from you, Raises are not limited by your Void.

**Shosuro Infiltrator [Ninja] [CR]:**

Silent, merciless killers who take no pleasure in their role. Their existence is unknown to most of the Empire. They specialize in attacking unknowing targets and destroying them utterly — virtually undetectable in stealth, and devastating once hidden from their opponent.

- Benefit: +1 Reflexes

- Skills: Acting, Athletics, Ninjutsu, Sincerity, Stealth (Sneaking) 2, any one Skill

- Honor: 1.5 | Outfit: Ashigaru or Light Armor, Sturdy Black Clothing, Daisho, Bow or Knife, Traveling Pack, 5 koku

- Rank 1 — The Path of Shadows: No Honor loss for use of Low Skills or Ninjutsu Weapons in service of the Scorpion Clan (GM approval). +2k0 to all Stealth Skill rolls.

- Rank 2 — Strike From Darkness: When declaring an attack against an opponent unaware of your presence, Raises are not limited by Void and you roll additional dice equal to your School Rank on the attack roll. Against an opponent suffering a Condition effect, roll half that number of additional unkept dice instead. The two effects are not cumulative — choose one.

- Rank 3 — Steel Within Silk: When attacking from ambush as above, or using Ninjutsu weapons, make melee (or shuriken ranged) attacks as a Simple Action. Alternatively, take a Complex Action to make a number of shuriken ranged attacks equal to your School Rank.

- Rank 4 — Whisper of Steel: Even after attacking, opponents must win a Contested Investigation (Notice)/Perception vs Stealth/Agility roll to detect you. Only usable when making a single attack per round. GM may rule this does not work where concealment is impossible.

- Rank 5 — The Final Silence: After a successful damage roll, spend a Void Point to increase any two dice to show 10s (these do not explode). May spend multiple Void Points this way, each increasing up to two damage dice.

**Soshi Magistrate [Bushi] [EE]:**

Descended from techniques taught by Soshi Saibankan himself. The principle method the Scorpion use to educate magistrates, yoriki, city guards, and border patrols. Teaches a mixture of armed and unarmed techniques to suppress brawls and defeat bandits with equal skill.

- Benefit: +1 Agility

- Skills: Athletics, Defense, Intimidation (Control), Jiujutsu, Kenjutsu, Lore: Underworld, any Bugei Skill

- Honor: 2.5 | Outfit: Light Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, any 1 weapon, Traveling Pack, 5 koku

- Rank 1 — The Face of Justice: +1k0 to Intimidation; +2k0 instead when used against someone of lower Status Rank. +1k0 damage bonus with melee attacks.

- Rank 2 — Rise to Meet the Challenge: When Prone, Free Action to roll Athletics/Agility (TN 20) to regain your feet. +1k1 bonus to any roll made to control a Grapple.

- Rank 3 — The Magistrate’s Cut: Once per opponent per skirmish, by successfully making three Raises for a Called Shot on a melee attack, temporarily Blind a foe until the Reactions stage of the following Round.

- Rank 4 — Certainty of Purpose: Make melee attacks (including unarmed) as Simple Actions rather than Complex Actions.

- Rank 5 — Relentless Resolve: Once per skirmish, gain extra unkept dice equal to your Intimidation Skill Rank on all attack rolls for one Round. Cannot be used in Center Stance.

**Soshi Shugenja [CR]:**

Masters of subtle magic. The Soshi understand that the kami are completely alien creatures with no firm concept of honor — they can and will do virtually anything asked of them if properly entreated. The Soshi excel at channeling the kami into feats of stealth, trickery, and deception. Their ability to cast non-damaging spells without any outward indication makes them perfect for infiltration and court manipulation.

- Benefit: +1 Awareness

- Skills: Calligraphy (Cipher), Courtier, Etiquette, Lore: Theology, Spellcraft, Stealth, any one Skill

- Honor: 2.5 | Outfit: Robes, Wakizashi, Knife, Scroll Satchel, Traveling Pack, 5 koku

- Affinity/Deficiency: Air / Earth | Starting Spells: Sense, Commune, Summon, 3 Air, 2 Fire, 1 Water

- Technique — The Kami’s Whisper: When casting any spell with no potential to inflict damage, sacrifice an additional spell slot of the same Element to eliminate all visible signs of the spell having been cast. All spell effects take place normally — there is simply no outward sign of what has happened. Free Raise on any spell with the Illusion keyword.

**Yogo Wardmaster [Shugenja]:**

Masters of ward magic — protective charms similar to Toritaka exorcism wards but significantly more flexible. The Yogo guard their methods carefully; even their Soshi cousins know little of these secrets. Their unique affinity covers all non-Void Ward-keyword spells rather than a single element.

- Benefit: +1 Intelligence

- Skills: Calligraphy (Cipher), Etiquette, Investigation, Lore: Theology, Spellcraft, Stealth, any 1 High or Low Skill

- Honor: 1.5 | Outfit: Robes, Wakizashi, Knife, Scroll Satchel, Traveling Pack, pack of twenty paper wards, 3 koku

- Affinity/Deficiency: Affinity for all non-Void spells with the Wards keyword; Deficiency for all spells with the Travel or Craft keywords. No standard elemental Affinity or Deficiency.

- Starting Spells: Commune, Summon, 3 Ward Spells, 3 other spells (may not be Void Spells).

- Technique — Way of the Wardmaster: When casting any spell with the Wards keyword, Raise a number of times equal to the spell’s Mastery Level to instead place the spell into a paper ward. The ward holds the spell latent until you activate it (Simple Action) by placing it on a target, discharging the spell immediately. Only you may activate your wards. Placing a ward on an unwilling target requires an unarmed melee attack (Spellcraft/Agility). An active ward may be physically destroyed (Simple Action) ending all ongoing effects; if destroyed before discharge, the spell slot is recovered normally. You cannot recover a slot cast into a ward until after the ward discharges. The following spells are treated as having the Wards keyword: Air — To Seek the Truth, By the Light of the Moon, Benten’s Touch, Summon Fog; Earth — Armor of the Earth, Courage of the 7 Thunders, Force of Will, Wholeness of the World; Fire — Extinguish, Fires of Purity, Disrupt the Aura, Haze of Battle, Globe of Everlasting Sun; Water — Reversal of Fortunes, Rejuvenating Vapors, Wisdom and Clarity, Near to Ice.

**ADVANCED SCHOOLS:**

**Scorpion Instigator [Courtier] [CR]:**

The Clan of Secrets’ elite courtiers. Given access to rigorous training designed to exploit their own weaknesses until they become strengths. Masters at controlling the flow of conversation so that falsehoods pass undetected even against trained investigators. All members must possess at least one Dark Secret and divulge it to their sensei upon joining.

- Requirements: Awareness 5, Intelligence 5, Perception 3 | Skills: Courtier 6, Etiquette 5, Sincerity 5, Stealth 4 | Other: Blackmail on at least 4 different individuals.

- Rank 1 — The Depths of Dishonor: When making a Contested Social Skill Roll, gain +Xk0, where X is the difference between your Honor Rank and your opponent’s.

- Rank 2 — Sheath Your Lies in Truth: If anyone is trying to determine whether you are lying, spend a Void Point to gain +3k3 on the Contested Social Skill Roll to conceal your falsehood (instead of normal +1k1).

- Rank 3 — Pull the String: If you possess Blackmail on someone, spend 10 minutes conversing with them and spend a Void Point as a Complex Action to suggest an action. Win a Contested Courtier (Manipulation)/Awareness vs Etiquette (Courtesy)/Willpower roll — on success, the target must take the action or lose 4 Honor (provided the action is not life-threatening and its consequences are not worse than revealing the Blackmail).

**Scorpion Saboteur [Ninja] [IH]:**

The military elite of the Scorpion shinobi forces. While other shinobi focus on spying and assassination, the Saboteurs undertake military defense of Scorpion lands using every tactic available — including forbidden gaijin pepper — to weaken, distract, and cripple enemy armies.

- Requirements: Fire 4, Reflexes 4 | Skills: Craft: Explosives 3, Stealth 5, Knives 4, Ninjutsu 4 | Other: Must be trained in a Scorpion school with the [Ninja] tag.

- Rank 1 — Follow the Pincers: +1k1 to all Stealth rolls. No Initiative penalties for being surprised, Dazed, or Fatigued.

- Rank 2 — No Mistakes: When spending a Void Point on a Craft: Explosives roll, gain +2k2 instead of +1k1. Suffer no ill effects from low rolls with Craft: Explosives and are immune to the optional “fizzle” rule.

- Rank 3 — Seeking Weakness: When fighting with a Small weapon (melee or ranged), make one attack as a Free Action each Round in addition to any other actions. Also ignore the effects of your opponent’s armor on both Armor TN and Reduction when fighting with a Small weapon.

**ALTERNATE PATHS:**

**Bayushi Distracter [Courtier]:**

Specialists in spreading false information to confuse and disrupt opponents. The more rumors suffuse a court, the easier it is for the Masters of Secrets to prevail.

- Replaces: Bayushi Courtier 3. Requires: Sincerity (Deceit) 3.

- Technique — Smoke Screen: Roll Sincerity (Deceit)/Awareness vs target’s Investigation/Awareness (targets with a directly relevant Lore Skill may substitute Lore/Intelligence). On success, the target must call one Raise for no effect any time they make a Social Roll involving the misinformed topic. Call additional Raises to increase the number of wasted Raises required. Effects last one week; cannot target the same person within the same week.

**Bitter Lies Swordsman [Bushi] [CR]:**

Founded by Bayushi Tangen — a man who capitalized on chance and refused to accept anything as out of reach. The Bitter Lies style embodies no-holds-barred adventurous fighting, relying on sheer force of will and natural fortune to survive situations where logic says survival is impossible.

- Replaces: Bayushi Bushi 3. Requires: Kenjutsu 3.

- Technique — The Dark Sword of Bitter Lies: Spend a Void Point to immediately reroll any one roll. The reroll suffers no penalty to the TN even if the original roll failed. You may keep whichever result you prefer. You may not reroll the same roll again by any means.

**Fading Shadows [Shugenja]:**

A tiny sect operating out of the Hidden Moon Dojo, devoted to studying the Nothing and its manifestations in Rokugan. They recognize a disturbing link between the Nothing and the Void — and exploit it to defeat the Nothing’s machinations. Only Scorpion of irreproachable loyalty and discretion are even considered for induction.

- Replaces: Any Scorpion Shugenja 5. Requires: Void 4, Spellcraft 5, Forbidden Knowledge: Lying Darkness. Must be inducted into the Hidden Moon Dojo.

- Technique — Unravel the Shadow: Simple Action — spend a Void Point to attempt a Contested Void vs Willpower roll against any Shadowspawn or minions of the Nothing within sight. On success, creatures are revealed as what they truly are and cannot reassume a false identity while in your sight. Free Action — spend a Void Point when casting a spell or making a physical attack for it to have full effect on a minion of the Nothing regardless of normal resistances or immunities.

**Kuroiban [Shugenja]:**

The Black Watch. Originally created by the Yogo, who still lead the organization. Draws recruits from both Scorpion shugenja schools. Its very existence is known to only a handful outside the clan — the Kuni family being the most notable. Trained to sense the Taint through their connection to the kami.

- Replaces: Any Scorpion Shugenja 4. Requires: Willpower 4, Lore: Maho 3, Lore: Shadowlands 3. Must have been chosen to serve in the Kuroiban.

- Technique — The Black Watch: Any time you target someone with a spell (including benign spells), expend an additional spell slot (any Element) as a Free Action and make a Contested Willpower roll. On success, you sense whether the target has at least one full Rank of Taint. If trained in the Yogo Shugenja School, this technique may also be triggered when placing a ward, activating at the moment the ward is placed.

**Saigo’s Blades [Bushi]:**

A small but effective iaijutsu branch of the Bayushi Bushi school, named for Bayushi Saigo whose victories over other clans’ duelists were so notable that graduates of the dojo became known informally — then formally — by his name.

- Replaces: Bayushi Bushi 3. Requires: Iaijutsu 3.

- Technique — Saigo’s Technique: When dueling any opponent you have previously used the Intimidation Skill upon successfully, gain +5 to your roll during the Assessment Phase. By spending a Void Point, report one statistic of your choice as being one Rank higher during the Assessment Phase.

**The Scorpion Elite Guard [Bushi]:**

Each member trains to adapt his fighting style to a specific enemy clan — becoming an intimidating opponent to any member of that clan, and gaining the ability to pose as one of that clan’s samurai. Known within the Scorpion as “Violators.” Most opponents believe them to be a merely highly proficient bushi unit like any other.

- Replaces: Bayushi Bushi 3, Shosuro Actor 3. Requires: Lore (selected clan) 3, Kenjutsu 5. Heart of Vengeance [Selected Clan] may substitute for the Lore requirement at GM’s option.

- Technique — The Eyes of My Enemy: Select one Great Clan when learning this Technique (cannot be changed). Attack as a Simple Action when targeting a samurai from this clan. Once per skirmish against a samurai from this clan, spend a Void Point as a Simple Action to prevent that samurai from using his School Techniques for his next two Turns. Cannot be used in iaijutsu duels.

**Scorpion Loyalist [IH]:**

Those Scorpion willing to do anything at all to fulfill their lord’s commands. Can probably be traced back to the internal split within the clan during the Gozoku era, but rose to prominence during the Clan Coup. Warriors, priests, and courtiers who formed the bulk of the forces in the Imperial City during the coup.

- Replaces: Any Scorpion School at Rank 2. Requires: Courtier 4 (courtiers), Spellcraft 4 (shugenja), or one Weapon Skill at 4 (bushi/ninja).

- Technique — No Matter the Cost: Sacrifice up to School Rank points of Honor to gain a bonus on one roll made against non-Scorpion opponents. Bushi/ninja gain +1k0 per Honor point on attack rolls; courtiers gain the same on Contested Social Skill Rolls; shugenja gain the same on spell-casting rolls for Air spells targeting those opponents.

**Scorpion Maskmaker [Courtier/Artisan]:**

The Scorpion take mask artistry to a level far beyond any other clan — hardly surprising given the importance of masks to their culture. These artisans know how to direct attention away from the wearer’s face to the distracting mask itself.

- Replaces: Bayushi Courtier 2, Soshi Shugenja 2, Yogo Wardmaster 2. Requires: Craft: Maskmaking 5, Sincerity (Deceit).

- Technique — Conceal the Eyes: Once every three months, craft a mask with two Raises on a Craft: Maskmaking roll — the mask matches the abilities of the Inheritance Advantage with the Sincerity Skill. +2k0 on any Social Skill roll to detect falsehood or concealed intentions in others.

**The Scorpion’s Tail [Bushi/Ninja]:**

Chain weapon specialists. Their style centers on the kusarigama — using it as both an infiltration tool and a vicious weapon capable of disabling opponents at range.

- Replaces: Bayushi Bushi 3, Shosuro Infiltrator 4. Requires: Athletics 4, Chain Weapons 4.

- Technique — The Tail’s Reach: +1k1 to Athletics rolls when using any chain weapon as a climbing tool. Attack with a kusarigama at up to 15’ distance. When attacking with a kusarigama, call three Raises to inflict the Lame Disadvantage on the opponent — lasting until the Wounds from the attack are healed.

**Scorpion Weaponmaster [Bushi]:**

The rare handful of Scorpion who possess the perfect combination of natural talent and extensive training to elevate combat beyond simple war. Often found within the ranks of the Bitter Lies legion. In the hands of a Scorpion master, a weapon is but a brush and combat is his art.

- Replaces: Bayushi Bushi 4. Requires: Any three Weapon Skills at 2.

- Technique — I Am A Weapon: School Rank times per skirmish, as a Free Action use any other melee Weapon Skill you possess for attack rolls with your current weapon. No Mastery Abilities or Emphases apply when using this Technique. Effect lasts School Rank Rounds; while active, make melee attacks as a Simple Action.

**Shadow Blades [Ninja]:**

Among the most proficient killers in the Empire. They appear in no formal documentation anywhere in the clan’s annals. When they take the field, there are no survivors. The location of their secret dojo is unknown to all save those who dwell within it and family daimyo or higher within the Scorpion.

- Replaces: Bayushi Bushi 3 or Shosuro Infiltrator 3. Requires: Ninjutsu 3.

- Technique — Never Beyond My Reach: Make melee attacks as a Simple Action when wielding a katana or any weapon with the Ninja keyword. When wielding a Ninja keyword weapon, +1k1 to attack and damage rolls with that weapon.

**Shadow Hunter [Shugenja] [CR]:**

Elite Soshi shugenja-assassins who meld with darkness and reveal themselves only to devastate their foes. Sent against targets that Shosuro ninja are unable to eliminate due to protection or difficulty.

- Replaces: Any Scorpion Shugenja School 3. Requires: Stealth 3.

- Technique — Scourge in Shadow: When casting a damaging spell targeting an opponent unaware of your presence, gain +2k0 on the Spell Casting Roll. Spend a single spell slot as a Free Action to gain +1k1 on a Knives or Stealth Skill Roll.

**Shosuro Assassins [Ninja]:**

Rank 6 — the venomous sting of the Scorpion. Trained to dispatch all manner of targets in every circumstance, relying on tanto as small, easily concealed weapons ideal for infiltration. Their very existence is one of the Empire’s greatest sources of paranoia.

- Replaces: N/A (Rank 6). Requires: Stealth 5, Knives 5, must have attained Rank 5 in either Shosuro Infiltrator or Shosuro Actor.

- Technique — The Hidden Blade: When attacking a surprised or unaware opponent with a knife, spend a Void Point as a Free Action. Your attack targets a vital spot — Raises for damage on that attack are kept dice (+1k1 per Raise) instead of unkept (+1k0).

**Shosuro Defilers [Courtier]:**

Elite specialists in the destruction of reputations — spreading lies and calumnies backed with just enough truth to make them effective. A true Defiler is ready to give his life in return for shattering the name, honor, and repute of an enemy of the Scorpion.

- Replaces: Bayushi Courtier 3 or Shosuro Actor 3. Requires: Sincerity (Deceit) 3, Glory Rank 3.0+.

- Technique — Shameless Slander: Slander someone in court (directly or indirectly) and roll Sincerity (Deceit)/Awareness vs Etiquette (Courtesy)/Willpower (+1k1 bonus if the slander is based on a real secret or disgrace). On success, the target loses Honor and gains Infamy equal to your current Glory Rank. If they do not promptly and effectively answer your slander within the first week, they also lose 1 Rank of Devotion from all Allies. If you lose the Contested Roll, you lose 3 Glory. Cannot target the same person more than once per month.

**Sons of Shadow [Bushi/Shugenja]:**

Both branches of the Soshi family — priesthood and law enforcement — dedicated to ensuring the danger presented by the Colonies is never realized. Whatever horrors they must perform, they accept as necessary burdens. If all goes as planned, the other clans will never know the suffering the Scorpion have spared them.

- Replaces: Soshi Magistrate 3, Soshi Shugenja 3. Requires: Lore: Shugenja 3 or Lore: Law 3 (whichever applies to the opposite school).

- Technique — None Shall Know My Path: Soshi Shugenja — Simple Action, spend a spell slot to gain temporary Skill Ranks equal to School Rank in Athletics, Defense, Intimidation, or Lore: Underworld. Soshi Magistrate — Simple Action, spend a Void Point to gain temporary Skill Ranks equal to School Rank in Calligraphy, Courtier, Etiquette, Lore: Theology, Spellcraft, or Stealth. Temporary Ranks last one hour. No Mastery Abilities are gained.

**Soshi Deceivers [Shugenja]:**

For centuries the darkest secret of the Soshi family — combining Air magic and the power of Nothing for sinister effects. After the exposure of the Lying Darkness the original Deceivers were purged, but the organization was later restarted in the belief that its powers can be replicated without relying on the Shadow’s influence. Whether this is truly successful remains to be seen.

- Replaces: Soshi Shugenja 3.

- Technique — Way of the Shadow: Sacrifice a spell slot of any Element to gain perfect vision in absolute darkness. Alternatively, spend an Air or Void spell slot to gain +1k1 to Stealth, Sleight of Hand, or Sincerity (Deceit) Skill Rolls. May not spend more spell slots than your Insight Rank on any one Skill Roll.
