## s52.7 Clan Induction — LOCKED

This section defines the formal ceremony by which a ronin is adopted into a
Great Clan. Clan induction is rare, requires sustained demonstrated worth,
and is the only mechanism by which a character's `clan` field permanently changes
post-generation (other than Clan Destruction from s53).

---

### Part A — Overview

Clan induction is a two-stage process:

1. **Worthiness threshold** — the ronin must accumulate deed credits proving
   service to a specific Family (tracked per-Family in `supply_ledger`).
2. **Induction ceremony** — the Family Daimyo performs the ActionID
   `PERFORM_CLAN_INDUCTION`, which is a costly, public, irreversible act.

Induction is never an NPC self-selected action. It only fires when a lord
explicitly chooses it. The NPC engine scores it under the `FILL_VACANCY`
NeedType at a low baseline score (lords must genuinely want to absorb this
particular person into their family).

---

### Part B — Deed Credits

Deed credits represent demonstrated service to a specific Family. They are
stored as `supply_ledger["contract_deeds_for_family"]`, a Dictionary keyed
by `family_name` (String).

**Ways to earn deed credits:**

1. **Contract completion** (automatic, s52.6 Part F): each clean contract
   completion increments the counter for the lord's family by 1. This is
   the primary path.

2. **Lord's Recognition** (civilian order action `GRANT_DEED_CREDIT`, 0 AP,
   1 civilian order): a lord may manually grant 1 deed credit to a co-located
   ronin for exceptional service that a contract did not capture. No skill roll.
   The ronin must be co-located. Once per ronin per season per lord.

   The `grant_deed_credit_cooldown` dict on the lord's `supply_ledger` tracks
   which ronin IDs received a manual grant this season: `{ronin_id: season_count}`.
   A new grant to the same ronin in the same season is silently skipped.

**Minimum deed threshold for induction eligibility:**
`INDUCTION_MIN_DEEDS = 3`

The ronin must have at least 3 deed credits for the specific Family of the
inducting Family Daimyo before `PERFORM_CLAN_INDUCTION` is available.

---

### Part C — The PERFORM_CLAN_INDUCTION ActionID

**Context:** `AT_OWN_HOLDINGS`
**AP cost:** 2 (the ceremony is a significant commitment of personal time)
**Who can use it:** Family Daimyo only (status 6.0 – 6.99 via `lord_rank`)
**Skill roll:** Courtier / Awareness vs TN 20 (the ceremony must be performed
with the proper rites; failure means the ceremony was conducted poorly)

**Prerequisites (checked before roll, action unavailable if any fail):**
- Inductee is co-located with the inducting Daimyo
- Inductee has deed credits ≥ `INDUCTION_MIN_DEEDS` for this Daimyo's family
- Daimyo's disposition toward the inductee ≥ `INDUCTION_MIN_DISPOSITION = 51`
  (Friend tier; the GDD describes induction as requiring a Daimyo who "really
  likes" the ronin — the bottom of Friend tier is the minimum for genuine
  personal approval)
- Inductee is not `permanent_ronin`
- Inductee does not already belong to this Clan (no-op guard)
- Inductee holds no conviction for TREASON, MAHO_USE, or
  UNSANCTIONED_COVERT_KILLING

**Koku cost:** `INDUCTION_KOKU_COST = 10`
The ceremony requires significant gifts, banqueting, and official documentation.
Koku is deducted at roll time (Pattern B — always paid).

**On roll failure:** The ceremony is bungled. The inductee remains ronin; koku
is spent; no deed credits are lost. A TIER_4 PERSONAL topic is generated
(embarrassment, not scandal).

---

### Part D — Induction Outcome

When `_process_clan_induction_writebacks()` fires on a successful
`PERFORM_CLAN_INDUCTION`:

1. `inductee.clan = daimyo.clan` — primary clan change.
2. `inductee.family_name = daimyo.family_name` — the inductee joins the
   inducting family, not a generic clan pool.
3. `inductee.lord_id = daimyo.character_id` — they serve the inducting Daimyo.
4. `inductee.role_position = "samurai"` — baseline retainer position.
5. `inductee.status = max(inductee.status, 1.0)` — minimum samurai status.
6. `inductee.permanent_ronin = false` — induction clears even the disgrace flag.
   (Five disgraces are social failures. A Daimyo who chooses to overlook them
   and induct anyway supersedes the social consensus.)
7. **Glory change:** Inductee gains `INDUCTION_INDUCTEE_GLORY_GAIN = +1.0`.
   The Daimyo gains `INDUCTION_DAIMYO_GLORY_GAIN = +0.3` (public act of
   mercy/discernment).
8. **Collective disposition:** `CollectiveDispositionSystem.apply_family_event()`
   fires: the inducting family's baseline toward the inductee shifts by
   `INDUCTION_FAMILY_BASELINE_SHIFT = +15` (they are now kin, but newly-joined
   kin — full family baseline develops over time).
9. **Deed credits cleared:** `supply_ledger["contract_deeds_for_family"]` entry
   for this family is removed (the deeds have been redeemed).
10. **Standing objective cleared:** `FIND_NEW_LORD` standing objective removed.
11. **Clan deed history preserved:** `supply_ledger["former_ronin_inducted_by"] = daimyo_id`
    for audit trail.

**Topic generation:** A TIER_3 POLITICAL topic is created: the induction of a
ronin is clan-level news. `subject_character_id = inductee.character_id`.

---

### Part E — NPC Scoring

`PERFORM_CLAN_INDUCTION` is scored under `FILL_VACANCY` NeedType at
**score 20** (lowest slot; lords choose this only when disposition is high AND
deeds are met AND no clan samurai can fill the vacancy).

The NPC engine's Phase 4c precondition filter enforces all prerequisites from
Part C. Without prerequisites met, the action is filtered out regardless of score.

`GRANT_DEED_CREDIT` is a civilian order action (0 AP, 1 civilian order) scored
under `FILL_VACANCY` NeedType at **score 30** (more accessible than induction
itself; lords with high disposition toward a ronin may proactively recognize
their service even without a vacancy pressing them).

---

### Part F — Induction and Permanent Ronin

Part D item 6 specifies that induction clears `permanent_ronin`. This is a
deliberate design choice: a Family Daimyo who performs a formal ceremony,
spends 10 koku, and publicly adopts a ronin into their family has made a social
statement that supersedes all prior social censure. The five-disgrace threshold
reflects other lords' opinions; the inducting Daimyo's act replaces those
opinions with their own. This path should be rare in practice — the
`INDUCTION_MIN_DISPOSITION` gate of 51 is extremely high for someone with
five prior disgraces.
