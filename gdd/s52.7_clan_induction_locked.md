## s52.7 Clan Induction — LOCKED

This section defines the formal ceremony by which a ronin is adopted into a
Great Clan. Clan induction is extremely rare — a matter of years of service,
demonstrated extraordinary worth, and high-level clan approval. It is the only
mechanism by which a character's `clan` field permanently changes post-generation
(other than Clan Destruction from s53).

---

### Part A — Overview

Clan induction is a three-stage process:

1. **Sustained service** — the ronin must accumulate deed credits proving
   multi-season service to a specific Family (tracked per-Family in `supply_ledger`).
2. **Family Daimyo approval** — the Family Daimyo must formally grant approval
   via the `APPROVE_CLAN_INDUCTION` civilian order action before any ceremony
   can proceed.
3. **Induction ceremony** — a Provincial Daimyo (or higher-ranked lord) of the
   same Family performs `PERFORM_CLAN_INDUCTION`, which is costly, public, and
   irreversible.

**Who performs the ceremony:** Provincial Daimyo (`lord_rank >= PROVINCIAL_DAIMYO`).
Family Daimyo and Clan Champion may also perform it (they meet the rank threshold).

**Who grants approval:** Family Daimyo only (`lord_rank >= FAMILY_DAIMYO`).
Approval can be granted remotely — the Family Daimyo does not need to be
co-located with the ronin.

---

### Part B — Deed Credits

Deed credits represent demonstrated service to a specific Family. They are
stored as `supply_ledger["contract_deeds_for_family"]`, a Dictionary keyed
by `family_name` (String).

**Ordinary deed credits:** Each clean contract completion (s52.6 Part F)
increments the deed counter for the lord's Family by 1.

**Minimum deed threshold for induction eligibility:**
`INDUCTION_DEED_THRESHOLD = 8`

Eight deeds represents roughly two years of sustained seasonal service — the
GDD's intent that induction is genuinely earned over time, not a quick process.

---

### Part C — Extraordinary Deeds

A single **extraordinary deed** is required in addition to the 8-deed threshold.
An extraordinary deed is a clean contract completion (`is_clean = true` at expiry)
that met all three of the following conditions simultaneously at expiry time:

- **A63 — Active war:** the contracting lord's clan is currently at war
  (at least one active WarData with `clan_a` or `clan_b` matching the lord's clan)
- **A64 — Active crisis:** the contracting lord's province has `active_crisis_id >= 0`
- **A65 — Continuous service:** the contract lasted `INDUCTION_MIN_CONTINUOUS_SEASONS = 3`
  seasons or more (stored in `supply_ledger["contract_duration_seasons"]` at acceptance)

Extraordinary deed count is stored separately in
`supply_ledger["extraordinary_deeds_for_family"]`, a Dictionary keyed by family name.

---

### Part D — Family Daimyo Approval (APPROVE_CLAN_INDUCTION)

Before any ceremony can proceed, the Family Daimyo must formally issue approval.

**ActionID:** `APPROVE_CLAN_INDUCTION`
**Context:** `AT_OWN_HOLDINGS`, `AT_COURT`
**AP cost:** 0
**Civilian order cost:** 1
**Who can use it:** Family Daimyo only (`lord_rank >= FAMILY_DAIMYO`)
**Skill roll:** None — this is an executive decision.

**Prerequisites:**
- The target ronin has `INDUCTION_DEED_THRESHOLD = 8` deed credits for this Family
- The target ronin has `INDUCTION_EXTRAORDINARY_DEED_REQUIRED = 1` extraordinary deed
- FD's disposition toward the ronin ≥ `INDUCTION_MIN_DISPOSITION = 51`
- The ronin does not already have a pending approval

**Approval is stored** as `supply_ledger["family_daimyo_approval"] = family_daimyo_id`.

The approval can be granted remotely — the Family Daimyo need not be co-located.
Any Provincial Daimyo of the same Family may then perform the ceremony once
approval is on file.

---

### Part E — The PERFORM_CLAN_INDUCTION ActionID

**Context:** `AT_OWN_HOLDINGS` (ceremony is held at the sponsoring lord's holdings)
**AP cost:** 2 (the ceremony is a significant commitment of personal time)
**Who can use it:** Provincial Daimyo or higher (`lord_rank >= PROVINCIAL_DAIMYO`)
**Skill roll:** Courtier / Awareness vs TN 20 (the ceremony must be performed
with the proper rites; failure means it was conducted poorly)

**Prerequisites (all required, action unavailable if any fail):**
- Sponsoring lord's rank ≥ `PROVINCIAL_DAIMYO`
- Inductee is co-located with the sponsoring lord
- Inductee has deed credits ≥ `INDUCTION_DEED_THRESHOLD = 8` for this lord's Family
- Inductee has extraordinary deed count ≥ `INDUCTION_EXTRAORDINARY_DEED_REQUIRED = 1`
- `supply_ledger["family_daimyo_approval"]` is set (Family Daimyo approved)
- Sponsoring lord's disposition toward the inductee ≥ `INDUCTION_MIN_DISPOSITION = 51`
- Inductee is not `permanent_ronin`
- Inductee does not already belong to this Clan
- Inductee holds no conviction for TREASON, MAHO_USE, or UNSANCTIONED_COVERT_KILLING

**Koku cost:** `INDUCTION_KOKU_COST = 10`
The ceremony requires significant gifts, banqueting, and official documentation.
Koku is deducted at roll time (Pattern B — always paid).

**On roll failure:** The ceremony is bungled. The inductee remains ronin; koku
is spent; no deed credits are lost. A TIER_4 PERSONAL topic is generated
(embarrassment, not scandal). The Family Daimyo's approval is NOT revoked.

---

### Part F — Induction Outcome

When `_process_clan_induction_writebacks()` fires on a successful
`PERFORM_CLAN_INDUCTION`:

1. `inductee.clan = sponsor.clan` — primary clan change.
2. `inductee.family = sponsor.family` — the inductee joins the sponsoring lord's family.
3. `inductee.lord_id = sponsor.character_id` — they serve the sponsoring Provincial Daimyo.
4. `inductee.role_position = "Samurai"` — baseline retainer position.
5. `inductee.status = max(inductee.status, 1.0)` — minimum samurai status.
6. `inductee.permanent_ronin = false` — induction clears even the disgrace flag.
7. **Glory change:** Inductee gains `INDUCTION_INDUCTEE_GLORY_GAIN = +1.0`.
   The sponsoring lord gains `INDUCTION_DAIMYO_GLORY_GAIN = +0.3`.
8. **Collective disposition:** All living members of the sponsoring family
   receive `INDUCTION_FAMILY_BASELINE_SHIFT = +15` toward the inductee.
9. **Supply ledger cleared:** Deed credits, extraordinary deeds, and FD approval
   for this family are all erased. `contract_duration_seasons` is also cleared.
10. **Standing objective cleared:** `FIND_NEW_LORD` standing objective removed.
11. **Audit trail:** `supply_ledger["former_ronin_inducted_by"] = sponsor.character_id`.

**Topic generation:** A TIER_3 POLITICAL topic is created: the induction of a
ronin is clan-level news. `subject_character_id = inductee.character_id`.

---

### Part G — NPC Scoring

`PERFORM_CLAN_INDUCTION` is scored under `FILL_VACANCY` NeedType at
**score 20** (lowest; lords choose this only when all prerequisites are met AND
no clan samurai can fill the vacancy).

`APPROVE_CLAN_INDUCTION` is scored under `FILL_VACANCY` NeedType at
**score 50** (Family Daimyo approves worthy ronins proactively when they reach
the threshold — a meaningful but selective act).

`HIRE_RONIN` remains at score 60 (more accessible — simple contract hire is the
most common path to getting a ronin's service).

---

### Part H — Induction and Permanent Ronin

Part F item 6 specifies that induction clears `permanent_ronin`. This is a
deliberate design choice: a Family Daimyo who approves and a Provincial Daimyo
who performs a formal ceremony, spending 10 koku and publicly adopting a ronin
into their family, have made a joint social statement that supersedes all prior
social censure. This path should be extremely rare in practice — the 8-deed
threshold, extraordinary deed requirement, and two-lord approval chain make it
accessible only to ronin who have genuinely served a family for years under
difficult conditions.
