# 33. Spells — Air

**MASTERY LEVEL 1:**

**Arrow's Flight**

- Air 1 (Battle) | Range: Touch | Area: 1 arrow | Duration: 3 Rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 Round), Special (+1 arrow per Raise)

Entreats Air kami to guide an arrow, causing it to unerringly strike its target if fired within the duration. Requires at least 1 Rank of Kyujutsu to fire. Because the kami ensure the hit, the shot cannot benefit from Raises or Techniques.

**Blessed Wind [CR]**

- Air 1 (Defense) | Range: Personal | Area: 10' radius | Duration: Concentration | Raises: Special (target another with 3 Raises)

Summons a swirling aura of wind that deflects arrows and projectiles. While concentration is maintained, adds +15 to Armor TN versus all non-magical ranged attacks.

**By the Light of the Moon [CR]**

- Air 1 | Range: Personal | Area: 20' radius | Duration: 1 minute | Raises: Area (+5' radius), Duration (+1 minute)

Reveals all non-magically concealed objects within the area as luminous outlines visible only to the caster. Reveals secret compartments, trap doors, concealed weapons, etc.

**Cloak of Night [CR]**

- Air 1 (Illusion) | Range: Touch | Area: One object | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+1 hour)

Wraps one non-living object smaller than the caster in an invisible embrace. The object becomes invisible to the naked eye. Magical detection automatically succeeds if the spell's Mastery Level is higher; equal Mastery requires a Contested Air Roll. The object remains physically present and detectable by all senses except vision.

**Gathering Swirl**

- Air 1 | Range: Centered on caster | Area: 20' radius | Duration: 1 Round | Raises: Area (+5' radius per 2 Raises)

Commands Air kami to gather specified unattended items and deposit them in a neat stack at a location of the caster's choosing within the area (including someone's hands). The caster must name the items when casting. Cannot snatch items from another person's grasp or person. Also useful for retrieving concealed or hidden items.

**Legacy of Kaze-no-Kami [CR]**

- Air 1 (Craft) | Range: School x10 miles | Area: One known individual | Duration: Special | Raises: Area (+1 individual), Range (+10 miles)

Creates a spirit bird that delivers a spoken message (up to 1 minute) to a named recipient within range. The bird appears normal, but dissipates if it takes any damage. Delivers the message as a whisper and then disappears. If the recipient is within range but inaccessible (e.g. inside a sealed building) the bird waits up to one week. If the recipient is out of range, the bird flies randomly and disappears at the edge of line of sight.

**Nature's Touch [CR]**

- Air 1 | Range: 10' | Area: One creature | Duration: Special | Raises: Range (+10')

Allows the caster to speak to a natural animal and ensure it understands. Works only on natural animals. Does not guarantee the animal will be friendly or comply with requests. Lasts as long as the caster maintains full and undivided attention on the animal while continuing to speak with it.

**Tempest of Air [CR]**

- Air 1 (Thunder) | Range: Personal | Area: Cone 75' long, 15' wide at end | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Area (+5' width), Damage (+1k0), Range (+5' length), Special (+5 to Air TN vs Knockdown per Raise)

Summons a powerful gust from the caster's position. All targets in the area suffer 1k1 Wounds and must make a Contested Earth vs caster's Air roll. Every target who fails is Knocked Down.

**Token of Memory [CR]**

- Air 1 (Craft, Illusion) | Range: 10' | Area: One illusory object up to 1 cubic foot | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Area (+1 cubic foot), Duration (+1 hour)

Creates a flawless visual illusion of one object. Appears real until the spell expires. To replicate a specific familiar object, declare one Raise; the owner may make a Contested Perception vs Spell Casting Roll to detect the forgery. Illusions are stationary and disappear if placed in a situation requiring movement. Objects have no physical substance and cannot bear weight or inflict damage.

**To Seek the Truth [CR]**

- Air 1 | Range: Personal / Touch | Area: One individual | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Duration (+1 minute)

Purges the target's mind, negating temporary mental or social penalties from Techniques, Wound Ranks, or other spells. The Spell Casting TN is increased by an amount equal to the Technique Rank, Wound Rank, or spell Mastery Level that caused the penalty. Cannot negate permanent Disadvantages.

**Voice of the Wind**

- Air 1 | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target person | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Range (20' for 2 Raises), Duration (+2 minutes per Raise)

Air kami lend timbre, depth, and resonance to the target's voice. For the duration, the target gains the Voice Advantage (if already possessed, effects stack) and +1k0 to Social Skill Rolls that involve speech.

**Way of Deception [CR]**

- Air 1 (Illusion) | Range: 20' | Area: One illusory duplicate of the caster | Duration: Concentration + 5 minutes | Raises: Area (+1 duplicate per 2 Raises), Range (+5'), Special (see below)

Creates a perfect duplicate of the caster that mirrors all of the caster's actions. Disappears if the caster moves outside normal range. With 2 Raises on the Spell Casting Roll, the caster may leave the area and the illusion holds its last position for as long as concentration is maintained.

**Yari of Air [CR]**

- Air 1 (Craft, Thunder) | Range: Personal or 20' | Area: One created weapon | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Damage (+1k0), Duration (+5 minutes), Range (+5')

Summons a weapon of pure air (default: yari; 1 Raise to change to any other spear). DR 1k1. If the caster lacks the Spears Skill, uses School Rank instead. If the caster has Spears, grants one Free Raise on Feint or Increased Damage Maneuvers. Disappears if lost from the hand. May instead appear in an ally's hands within 20' — the ally is treated as the caster but does not gain the Free Raise.

**MASTERY LEVEL 2:**

**Benten's Touch [CR]**

- Air 2 | Range: Personal/Touch | Area: Target individual | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Range (5' for 1 Raise)

Air kami whisper suggestions to others, causing them to perceive the target more positively. Target gains +1k1 + Air Ring to all Social Skill Roll totals for the duration.

**Blessed Wind of Lady Sun**

- Air 2 (Ward) | Range: Self | Area: 10 square feet | Duration: Concentration | Raises: Area (+5 sq ft)

All Void and Awareness-based rolls gain +1k0 within the area of effect. All hostile actions suffer -1k0. Hostile actions based on Awareness (such as Intimidation) suffer -2k0 instead. The caster may use the Meditation Skill without breaking concentration.

**Call Upon the Wind [CR]**

- Air 2 (Travel) | Range: Personal or 20' | Area: Target individual | Duration: 1 minute | Raises: Duration (+1 minute), Range (+5')

Grants the target limited flight — Free Move Actions only, never more than 10' per Round. Heavy winds can interfere or prevent movement. At duration's end the target drifts harmlessly to the ground regardless of height.

**Elemental Cipher**

- Air 2 (Illusion) | Range: Touch | Area: 1 written page | Duration: 1 month | Raises: Duration (+1 week per Raise), Area (+1 page per Raise)

Makes a piece of writing completely unintelligible to everyone except the original author and the intended recipient (named as a whisper when cast). Cannot be broken by traditional decryption. Other shugenja may read the text by rolling Spellcraft/Intelligence against the original Spell Casting Roll total. Known only to the Imperial families.

**Flight of Doves**

- Air 2 (Illusion) | Range: 25' | Area: Story told by 1 target person | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Range (+5' per Raise), Duration (+5 minutes per Raise)

Air kami illustrate a story being recounted by a designated individual, supplying a visual and auditory illusion drawn from the storyteller's mind. The blend of story, sound, and imagery is compelling — devised by the Shiba Illusionists primarily for entertainment but with practical applications.

**Freedom of the Air**

- Air 2 (Wards) | Range: 50' | Area: 1 residential building | Duration: Hours equal to caster's Air | Raises: Area (3 Raises for an inn-sized building, 6 Raises for a castle)

A cleansing ritual that entreats Air kami to sweep dirt, insects, and filth from a building. Also has a spiritually purifying effect: kansen, ghosts, and other hostile disembodied spirits within are compelled to leave for the spell's duration. Extremely powerful spirits or those with strong attachment to the building may resist (GM's discretion). Seppun Shugenja School Technique may be applied.

**Garbled Tongue**

- Air 3 (Illusion) | Range: 30' | Area: 2 conversing persons (one may be caster) | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Area (+1 person per 2 Raises), Duration (+1 minute per Raise)

Air kami create a second false layer of speech heard by everyone except those participating in the actual conversation. The real participants hear the true conversation clearly. Speakers' lips will not match the false speech. A shugenja may pierce the deception with a Contested School Rank/Air roll against the caster.

**Heart Betrays Eyes**

- Air 2 | Range: 50' | Area: One individual | Duration: Three rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round), Range (+10')

The next unusual thing the victim sees instead appears completely normal to them. The victim’s behavior is not directly changed; they simply perceive something ordinary where there is something unusual. A guard might walk through a ransacked room and see it as undisturbed. The victim may make an Investigation roll (TN = caster's Air × 5) if subtle clues exist. Any major clues end the effect immediately.

**Hidden Visage [CR]**

- Air 2 (Illusion) | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Area (target another in line of sight for 3 Raises), Duration (+5 minutes)

Alters the caster's facial features just enough to appear to be a different person of the same age, build, race, and gender. Cannot impersonate specific individuals or people radically different from the caster. The difference is subtle — enough to be mistaken for a close relative.

**The Kami's Whisper [CR]**

- Air 2 (Illusion) | Range: 50' | Area: 10' radius | Duration: 1 round | Raises: Area (+5' radius), Duration (+1 round), Range (+5')

Creates a false sound — either a voice or a natural sound (animal growl, running water, etc.) — no louder than a normal speaking voice. Cannot impersonate a specific person's voice. If a voice, limited to twenty words.

**Mists of Illusion [CR]**

- Air 2 (Craft, Illusion) | Range: 20' | Area: 10' radius | Duration: 1 minute | Raises: Area (+5'), Duration (+1 minute), Range (+5')

Creates visual-only illusions of any object, individual, or image the caster can imagine. Illusions are stationary and must fit within the area of effect. No auditory, olfactory, or tactile component.

**Quiescence of Air**

- Air 2 (Wards) | Range: Caster | Area: 30' diameter sphere | Duration: 10 Rounds | Raises: Range (center on object up to 50' away for 2 Raises), Duration (+3 Rounds per Raise)

Creates a sphere of absolute silence through which no sound passes in either direction. Once created, the bubble does not move even if the caster relocates. Characters on one side gain 2 Free Raises on Stealth rolls against characters on the other side. Ninube shugenja may cast this as a Nothing Spell.

**Request to Hato-no-Kami**

- Air 2 (Travel) | Range: 150' | Area: 1 bird | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Range (+50' per Raise), Duration (+1 hour per Raise)

A prayer carried by Air kami to a random bird within range (no effect if no bird is present). The bird lands on the caster's hand and may be given a simple task (carry a message, distract an enemy). With Animal Handling/Awareness (TN 20), may be given a more complex task (GM's judgment on what the bird can comprehend). The bird will not take suicidal actions and flees if injured.

**Secrets on the Wind [CR]**

- Air 2 | Range: 10 miles | Area: 20' radius | Duration: Concentration | Raises: Area (+5' radius), Range (+5 miles)

Requires a 10-minute uninterrupted preparation ritual in the designated area. Within 48 hours after the ritual, the caster may cast this spell and overhear everything said in the prepared area. Disruption ends the effect and requires a new ritual. Only one area may be prepared at a time.

**Whispering Wind [CR]**

- Air 2 (Divination) | Range: 20' | Area: Target individual | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+5')

Air kami assess whether the last thing said by the target was true or false. The kami have no concept of personal opinion; if the target sincerely believes their statement was true, the kami will judge it as true.

**Wind-Born Slumbers**

- Air 2 | Range: 50' | Area: 1 target person | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Range (+10' per Raise), Area (+1 person per 2 Raises), Duration (+1 minute per Raise)

Air kami insinuate themselves into the target, urging weariness and sleep. A physically and mentally inactive target automatically falls asleep (awakens from touch or loud noise). An active target instead suffers Fatigue for the duration (if already Fatigued, penalties worsen as if an additional day without rest).

**Wolf's Proposal [CR]**

- Air 2 (Illusion) | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Area (target another for 2 Raises), Duration (+5 minutes), Special (+1 Honor Rank per 2 Raises)

Creates a subtle aura of suggestion that does not disguise the caster but causes others to perceive him as slightly more benevolent than he is. For the duration, the caster's Honor Rank is considered 3 Ranks higher for purposes of any Lore: Bushido rolls made to determine it.

**MASTERY LEVEL 3:**

**Air Kami's Blessing**

- Air 3 | Range: Personal | Area: Caster | Duration: 8 hours | Raises: Special (double the bonus for 4 Raises)

Only taught in schools with Air Affinity. Aligns the Air kami within the caster's soul, granting an addition of Air Ring to all Awareness-based rolls and to Armor TN. Typically performed during daily morning meditations. Effects cannot be stacked.

**Essence of Air [CR]**

- Air 3 (Defense) | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 5 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round)

The caster becomes insubstantial, unable to interact with physical objects. Remains on the ground but may pass through solid objects at 1 foot per Round. Water Ring is halved (rounded down) while insubstantial. No other spells may be cast until returning to solidity.

**The Eye Shall Not See [CR]**

- Air 3 (Defense) | Range: Personal or Touch | Area: Self or target individual | Duration: Concentration | Raises: None

Air kami whisper in the ears of those within 20' of the target, causing convenient distraction. The target is not invisible but those within 20' will not see them as long as no loud noises or attention-drawing actions are made. Those beyond 20' are unaffected.

**Garbled Tongue**

- Air 3 (Illusion) | Range: 30' | Area: 2 conversing persons | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Area (+1 person per 2 Raises), Duration (+1 minute per Raise)

Air kami create a false layer of speech heard by all except those actually participating in the conversation. The real participants hear the true exchange. Lips will not match the false speech. A shugenja may pierce the deception with a Contested School Rank/Air roll against the caster. Devised by the Asahina; later acquired by the Soshi.

**Mask of Wind [CR]**

- Air 3 (Illusion) | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Area (target another for 2 Raises), Duration (+10 minutes)

Petitions Air kami to create an elaborate illusion adopting the appearance of any humanoid creature of approximately the same size (within 1 foot taller or shorter). Cannot replicate someone too dramatically different in size (e.g. not a goblin or ogre).

**Master Cloud's Eyes**

- Air 3 (Kolat) | Range: 50' | Area: One target creature | Duration: 2 minutes | Raises: Duration (+1 minute)

The caster views the world through the eyes of another living creature within line of sight. Sight only — no other senses and no control over where the target looks. Caster suffers -2k2 to Perception-based rolls for their own body for the duration.

**Soul of Kaze-no-Kami**

- Air 3 | Range: 20' | Area: 1 target person | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Duration (+2 minutes per Raise)

Infuses the target's soul with cooling serenity, rendering them resistant to emotional manipulation and psychological pressure. Gain +2k2 to resist any Social Skill Roll or Fear effects. However, suffer -2k0 to Awareness-based Social rolls (the same icy serenity makes it harder to perceive others' emotions).

**Striking the Storm [CR]**

- Air 3 (Defense) | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 3 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round)

Summons a cocoon of swirling air that increases the caster's Armor TN by +20 against both melee and ranged attacks. The force of the winds prevents the caster from hearing others speaking to them.

**Summoning the Gale [CR]**

- Air 3 (Defense) | Range: 50' | Area: 30' radius around target | Duration: Concentration | Raises: Range (+5')

Swirling winds surround a designated target, preventing ranged attacks in either direction. Everyone within the area gains +15 to Armor TN against ranged attacks, but also suffers -3k3 to all ranged attack rolls.

**Summon Fog [CR]**

- Air 3 | Range: 100' | Area: 50' radius | Duration: 1 minute | Raises: Area (+5' radius), Duration (+1 minute), Range (+10')

Coalesces Air kami into a thick obscuring fog. Visibility within the area is reduced to 5 feet. Absorbent materials become damp or wet. Small open flames may be extinguished (GM's discretion). Highly damaging to rice paper.

**Touch of Air's Grace**

- Air 3 (Illusion) | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target person | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+10 minutes per Raise)

Air kami subtly enhance the target's physical attractiveness. Negates the effects of the Disturbing Countenance and Benten's Curse Disadvantages for the duration. If the target lacks one or both, instead grants the corresponding Advantages (Dangerous Beauty and/or Benten's Blessing). No additional benefit if the target already possesses both Advantages.

**Your Heart's Enemy [CR]**

- Air 3 (Illusion) | Range: 25' | Area: One target individual | Duration: 5 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round), Range (+5')

Air kami manifest as an illusion of whatever the target fears most (a person, item, or scene). Generates Fear 4 for the target. Only the target sees the specific illusion; others see only a hazy small fog cloud.

**MASTERY LEVEL 4:**

**Call the Spirit [CR]**

- Air 4 | Range: Special | Area: Target spirit | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Duration (+1 minute)

An extremely powerful Summon variant that calls any particular spirit from any spirit realm. Realm denizens that are not spirits (such as Fortunes) are immune. If the caster knows something specific about the spirit, they can summon that particular spirit. The spell prevents the spirit from attacking first, but it will not necessarily be friendly. Disappears when duration expires. Use with caution — can potentially summon extremely dangerous creatures such as oni.

**Castle of Air**

- Air 4 (Defense, Illusion) | Range: Self | Area: 20' radius around caster | Duration: 10 rounds | Raises: Duration (+2 Rounds per Raise), Special

Air kami create a circular barrier of false sounds and images around the caster that confuses attackers. Once cast, the barrier remains in place regardless of caster movement. Those inside when it was cast can perceive the illusions as insubstantial. Those outside attacking those within must make a Contested Perception vs caster's Air roll each Round: success allows a normal attack that Round; failure imposes -5k0 to attack rolls that Round. Does not affect enemy spells.

**False Realm [CR]**

- Air 4 (Battle, Illusion) | Range: 250' | Area: 100' radius | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Area (+10' radius), Duration (+10 minutes)

Completely alters the appearance of terrain within the area of effect to any appearance the caster chooses. All senses are convincingly affected. The illusions have no substance and cannot be physically touched.

**Funeral Rites**

- Air 4 | Range: N/A | Area: N/A | Duration: 5 minutes

A one-hour ritual prayer performable only in the company of a recently deceased individual's relative within 24 hours of the funeral (both must have properly participated). Upon successful completion, the caster and the relative may briefly converse with the spirit of the departed. Other shugenja may assist (each uses a spell slot and adds +1 minute to the duration). Exact results are at the GM's discretion but may include important information, advice, or undertaking an incomplete task. Devised by the Kitsu sodan-senzo.

**Gift of Wind [CR]**

- Air 4 (Illusion) | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Duration (+1 minute)

Air kami render the caster completely invisible to all non-magical vision. The caster can still be touched, heard, and smelled. Attacking another person immediately ends the spell — the kami consider it ruining the joke.

**Howl of Isora**

- Air 4 (Thunder) | Range: 100' | Area: 40' diameter | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+20' per Raise), Area (+5' to diameter for 2 Raises), Damage (+1k0 per Raise)

Calls on the Fortune Isora to unleash a blast of storm-wracked air and lightning. Inflicts 3k2 damage on everyone within the area of effect. Targets must roll Earth (TN 30) or become Fatigued. Also damages weak physical objects — paper walls blown out, scrolls soaked, etc. Those with the Blood of Osano-Wo blessing are immune.

**Know the Mind [CR]**

- Air 4 | Range: 10' | Area: Target individual | Duration: 3 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round), Range (+5')

Air kami pluck the target's most immediate thoughts and whisper them to the caster. The caster hears whatever the target is actively thinking. Asking the target a question mentally surfaces the answer even if the target had no intention of speaking it. A Contested Perception vs Awareness roll also reveals the target's true emotional state regardless of physical appearance.

**Look into the Soul**

- Air 4 (Divination) | Range: 50' | Area: 1 target person or creature | Duration: 1 Round | Raises: Area (+1 target per 2 Raises), Special (+1 Ring learned per Raise)

A mystical lens formed by Air kami allows the caster to perceive the world as spirits do. Reveals the target's 2 lowest Rings and their constituent Trait values. Additional Rings can be learned with Raises. If Rings are equally balanced, reveals them randomly. Cannot and will not reveal the target's Void Ring.

**Netsuke of Wind [CR]**

- Air 4 (Craft, Illusion) | Range: Touch | Area: One hand-held object | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+10 minutes)

Air kami coalesce into a solid object that can be held in one or both hands, weighing no more than 20 pounds. The creation is an illusion but functions fully, including dealing damage as a weapon. Disappears completely at duration's end.

**Seeking the Way**

- Air 4 (Illusion) | Range: Touch | Area: Caster's trail for 1/2 mile | Duration: 3 hours | Raises: Duration (+1 hour per Raise), Area (+1 person's trail per Raise), Special (+1/4 mile of trail per Raise)

Convinces Air kami to hide the caster's tracks and evidence of passage, replacing them with a false trail leading in a completely different direction for the same distance. The false trail follows a reasonable route. Someone tracking by scent can still detect the original trail. Skilled trackers may see through the illusion by rolling Hunting/Perception against the caster's Spellcraft/Air.

**Symbol of Air [CR]**

- Air 4 (Ward) | Range: Touch | Area: Special | Duration: Permanent | Raises: None

Inscribes a ward on a solid object (most often a door or passageway). Anyone attempting to pass through must make a Contested Earth vs caster's Air roll. Those who fail must succeed at a Willpower Roll against the Spell Casting Roll total or fall into a deep sleep for 1 hour (unawakeable by normal means, but instantly awakened by any attack or harm). Only one Symbol of Air may exist at a time. Cannot overlap with other Symbol elements. May be dispelled by another casting of Symbol of Air or by destroying the inscribed surface.

**Tenjin's Ear**

- Air 4 (Travel) | Range: Caster | Area: 30' radius | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Range (change to Touch for 3 Raises), Area (+5' radius per Raise), Duration (+1 minute per Raise)

Special: Unicorn Clan shugenja only. Persuades Air kami to carry the true meaning of words to the caster's ear, making all audible human speech within the area intelligible regardless of language. Does not grant enhanced hearing (whispers remain inaudible) and does not bestow any capacity to speak in return.

**Whispers of the Forgotten**

- Air 4 | Range: 50' | Area: 1 target person | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Range (+25' per Raise), Duration (+1 minute per Raise)

Air kami evoke the target's memories of past crimes and shames, distressing and distracting them. For the duration, the target must call one Raise for no effect on any Skill or Spell Casting roll. If the target has 3+ points in Mental or Social Disadvantages, must call 2 Raises instead. Characters who truly have no haunting past sins are immune (GM's discretion).

**Wisdom of the Kami**

- Air 4 | Range: Touch | Area: Caster | Duration: 1 minute | Raises: Duration (+1 minute per Raise), Special (+1 additional Skill Rank once, for 3 Raises)

Air kami enhance the caster's mind, granting +1 Rank in all Skills currently possessed (including Mastery Abilities that would result). Cannot grant Ranks in Skills the caster does not already possess. When the duration expires, all benefits are lost and memories of any insight gained are at best fuzzy. Severe headaches are known to afflict those who try to recall specific details.

**MASTERY LEVEL 5:**

**Cloud the Mind [CR]**

- Air 5 | Range: Touch | Area: One target individual | Duration: Permanent | Raises: Special (+1 day of effect)

Considered blasphemous by most honorable shugenja — use is grounds for excommunication or execution in many orders. Forces Air kami to invade the target's mind and disrupt memories. On a successful Contested Air vs Earth roll, the target loses all memories of the past week (five days). The caster may then suggest false memories of what happened during that time. Resistance varies by individual. The Commune spell can detect the manipulation if the kami are specifically asked.

**Defender From Beyond**

- Air 5 (Battle) | Range: 30' | Area: 1 summoned spirit | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Duration (+1 minute per Raise)

Special: Kitsu shugenja only. Petitions Air kami to carry a message to Yomi, calling for the aid of an ancestral shiryo. If the Spell Casting Roll succeeds, a shiryo arrives in 5 Rounds. The spirit will offer advice, knowledge, or combat aid but will not act dishonorably. A typical shiryo has the Spirit trait, all Rings at 3, and Rank 4 in relevant Skills. Returns to Yomi when duration expires or if reduced to zero Wounds. The GM may allow summoning a specific named spirit with additional Raises, or send a more famous/powerful shiryo if circumstances warrant.

**Draw Back the Shadow [CR]**

- Air 5 | Range: 100' | Area: 30' radius | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Area (+5' radius), Range (+10')

Within the area, all illusions created by spells of Mastery Level 4 or lower are automatically dispelled. Mastery Level 5 or 6 illusions require a Contested Air Roll between the caster and their creator. Ongoing non-illusion magical effects may also be dispelled but require a Contested Ring Roll (caster's Air vs creator's appropriate Ring).

**Echoes on the Breeze [CR]**

- Air 5 | Range: Personal | Area: One target individual | Duration: Concentration | Raises: None

Sends the caster's voice across the Empire as a whisper to any known individual. Establishes a two-way link lasting as long as the caster concentrates. Both participants hear each other as whispers only. Both are instantly aware when the connection is forged, and either can end it at any point.

**Facing Your Devils**

- Air 5 | Range: 30' | Area: 1 target person or creature | Duration: 10 Rounds | Raises: Range (+10' per Raise), Duration (+2 Rounds per Raise)

Calls on Air kami to test the karmic and elemental balance of a soul by deliberately misaligning the target's Elements. Switches the target's highest and lowest Traits for the duration. May cause changes to Ring Ranks with potentially severe consequences (e.g. a dropping Earth Ring reducing Wound ranks). The caster may choose affected Traits in case of a tie. Often regarded as a wicked spell by clan shugenja.

**Legion of the Moon [CR]**

- Air 5 (Illusion) | Range: Personal | Area: 10' radius from caster | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Area (+5'), Duration (+1 minute)

Renders every individual the caster chooses within the area of effect completely invisible to all normal senses. Anyone affected who performs any action physically interacting with another individual is immediately excluded from the spell's effect.

**Slayer's Knives [CR]**

- Air 5 (Thunder) | Range: 30' | Area: Corridor 10' wide | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Area (+3' wide), Damage (+1k0 per 3 Raises)

Summons a corridor of cutting wind. DR equals caster's Air Ring +2k0 (e.g. Air 4 = 6k4 damage) against everything in the path. Paper and light cloth are destroyed instantly. Anyone damaged must succeed at an Earth Ring Roll (TN 20) or be Knocked Down.

**MASTERY LEVEL 6:**

**Rise, Air [CR]**

- Air 6 | Range: 30' | Area: One summoned spirit | Duration: Concentration | Raises: None

Summons a massive kami of pure air in a vaguely humanoid shape roughly 10 feet tall. Moves up to 10' × Air Ring per Round. Generates powerful winds in a 20' radius that prevent Simple Move Actions within. All Physical Traits equal to caster's Air Ring. Attacks with Jiujutsu Skill Rank equal to half the caster's Air Ring. Treated as a human with Earth equal to the caster's Air Ring for Wounds but suffers no Wound Penalties. Invulnerable. Dispelled if reduced to zero Wounds.

**The False Legion [CR]**

- Air 6 (Battle, Illusion) | Range: Personal | Area: Within 100' of caster | Duration: Concentration | Raises: Area (+10'), Special (+5 illusory figures per Raise)

Creates up to Air Ring × 10 illusory figures within the area. Figures may be as detailed or vague as desired (caster must be familiar with the appearance). Fully mobile and will take any action the caster desires as long as they remain within the area of effect. Can be seen, heard, and smelled but cannot physically interact with objects or individuals.

**Piercing the Heavens**

- Air 6 | Range: N/A | Area: N/A | Duration: 5 Rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 Round per 3 Raises)

Special: Phoenix Clan shugenja only. A sacred secret of the Phoenix, never intentionally shared with any other school. May only be performed within a temple or shrine devoted to a specific Fortune, and may never be cast more than once per month. If successfully cast, a mote of the named Fortune's essence manifests within the shrine's sacred icon, permitting brief communion with the divine.
