## G. Wood Guardian Outdoor Degradation

Wood guardian statues lose 1 quality tier per 5 IC years of outdoor exposure.
Minimum quality: Normal (tier 1).
Stone and bronze: no degradation.

Rate: −1 tier per `5 × 360 = 1800` IC days.

## H. Mantis Figurine Bonus

Recipient is Mantis Clan: +3 Free Raises on DELIVER_GIFT (above standard quality-based FRs).
Figurine collection topic: 3+ figurines sharing creator_id OR same theme field → TIER_4 topic.
Topic fires once per season per qualifying cluster (most prominent cluster = highest average quality).

## J. Zone Slot Eligibility (settlement-level proxy)

`statue_slot`: TEMPLE, SHINDEN, MONASTERY only.
`guardian_slot`: TEMPLE, SHINDEN, MONASTERY only (entrance zone proxy).

Neither slot available at castles, courts, private homes, or secular settlement types.

Stone: +20% to all rates.

Zone destruction: wood destroyed, stone 50%, bronze 70%.
