# 15. Court System — LOCKED

Court is a battlefield with different weapons. A courtier's failure can doom an entire clan just as surely as a lost war. Court sessions are the primary arena where political outcomes are decided — wars begin and end here, alliances form, resources are negotiated, and Imperial favor is won or lost. Court is not just talking. It is beauty, skill, cultural expression, gifts, insults, private deals, and the careful movement of opinion across days and weeks.
