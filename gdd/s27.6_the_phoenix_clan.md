## 27.6 The Phoenix Clan

The most spiritual clan in the Empire — masters of elemental magic, keepers of the Empire's religious traditions, and devoted to the preservation of life above all other values. The Phoenix are led not by their warrior family (the Shiba) but by the Isawa Elemental Council, an arrangement unique in the Empire. Their lands are among the most beautiful and peaceful in Rokugan. Their shugenja are the most powerful in the Empire.

- Clan identity: Spiritual, pacifistic, arrogant about magical power. The 'arrogant Phoenix' stereotype is known empire-wide. Intensely scholarly. Will go to war when truly threatened but deeply prefer alternatives.

- Key historical events: Isawa's oath to Shiba; the formation of the Elemental Council; the Agasha defection; tensions with Dragon over Tamori; various Shadowlands incursions the Phoenix helped contain.

- Inter-clan relations: Generally peaceful. Tension with Dragon over the Agasha defection and the Tamori. Good relations with Crane. Cooperative with Crab on Shadowlands matters when needed.

**The Asako Family:**

Motto: 'An untested virtue is not a virtue at all.' Calm, likeable scholars — historians, geographers, loremasters. Often serve as diplomats by default. Host the Imperial Winter Court in their beautiful lands. The true heart of the family is the secretive mystical order of henshin — monks who pursue apotheosis through the manipulation of elements via riddles and mysteries. Also maintain the Inquisitor order hunting maho-tsukai.

**The Isawa Family:**

Motto: 'Power comes from our hands, our minds, and our hearts.' The heart of the Phoenix Clan. Largest and most powerful shugenja family in the Empire — probably outnumber all other shugenja families combined. Study all four Elements and Void. Five Elemental Masters lead the clan politically. Test children on the day of birth for magical ability. Students develop personalities reflecting their Element after years of study. Believe all human life is sacred — descended from the divine blood of Moon and Sun.

**The Shiba Family:**

Motto: 'Choosing between two evils is still choosing evil.' The warrior family of the Phoenix — but one that swore eternal oath to protect and obey the Isawa. Often chafe under pacifistic Isawa directives. Pursue scholarly study of the Tao alongside martial training. Their fighting style — developed by the founding Kami — is elegant with polearms and naginata. Respected in court for calm self-control and selfless devotion. The Empire's smallest Great Clan (until Mantis elevation) means every shugenja life is precious, making Shiba yojimbo among the most dedicated in the Empire.
