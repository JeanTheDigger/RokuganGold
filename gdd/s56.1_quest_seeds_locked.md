## 56.1 Quest Seeds --- LOCKED

Quest seeds are the world-state conditions that produce ASCII map missions. Every seed originates from an existing game system. The following seeds are identified, organized by source system. All seeds listed below already have simulation-level mechanics defined elsewhere in the GDD. This section documents their role as ASCII map mission triggers.

### 56.1.1 Insurgency System Seeds (Section 11.11)

- **Ronin Bandit Uprising:** Clear the bandit hideout. Engage trained ronin fighters in wilderness or settlement terrain. Scales with insurgency Strength. Source: Stability drops below 75, ronin accumulation, war aftermath.

- **Peasant Revolt:** Suppress armed civilians. Morally complex. Honor cost for military suppression baked into Section 11.11. Map is a village, not a cave. Source: Stability drops below 50, governance failure, famine.

- **Maho Cult:** Infiltrate and destroy a cult lair. Requires shugenja support; bushi provide the muscle. Underground lair, cultists, potential Taint exposure. Investigators can be attacked by the cult. Source: Stability drops below 25 or PTL rises.

- **Province Taint Manifestation:** Purge corrupted land spawning Gaki, zombies, bog hags, tainted animals, and minor oni. Bestiary enemies (Section 54) scale with Strength. Source: Province Taint Level reaches 3 or above.

- **Nezumi Infestation:** Underground warren crawl. Tunnels, ambush points, warriors defending the colony. Pure physical combat, no spiritual angle. Source: food availability, proximity to Shinomen Forest or Shadowlands border.

### 56.1.2 Shadowlands / Kaiu Wall Seeds (Section 2.4)

- **Wall Sortie (Small/Medium/Large):** Venture beyond the Kaiu Wall to reduce Shadowlands Strength. Engage Shadowlands creatures, return. Jade budget determines protection. Failed sorties mean casualties for nothing. Source: Taisa orders per Section 2.4.11.

- **Horde Defense:** A discrete Shadowlands horde attacks a tower segment. Defend the wall. ASCII map siege combat. Source: Shadowlands Strength accumulation per Section 2.4.10.

- **Oni Manifestation:** A named Oni has manifested. Boss-tier encounter. Source: Maho Cult reaches Strength 10, or extreme PTL per Section 16.3.

### 56.1.3 Military Operation Seeds (Section 11.7, 55.21)

- **Siege Assault / Defense:** Storm a castle or defend one. Generated from war objectives. Source: Section 55.21.2.2 military primary objectives.

- **Siege Relief:** Break through a besieging army to relieve allies inside. Source: Section 55.21.10.

- **Field Battle (Personal Scale):** Player bushi fights within a mass battle on the ASCII map while armies clash on the World Map. Source: Army Combat System Section 11.7.

- **Scouting Mission:** Enter enemy border province, observe military strength, avoid detection. Stealth-oriented bushi content. Source: Section 55.21 SCOUT_ENEMY action.

### 56.1.4 Criminal Economy Seeds (Section 11.4)

- **Trade Route Protection:** Bandits raiding caravans. Escort or patrol a route segment. Source: Ronin Bandit Uprising trade route Koku drain.

- **Urban Gang Suppression:** City-based criminal networks. Different terrain from wilderness bandit camps. Source: Section 11.4.2.

- **Pirate (Wako) Raid:** Coastal or river encounter. Ship-to-shore or port combat. Source: Section 11.4.3.

### 56.1.5 Travel / Movement Seeds (Section 4.3.11)

- **Road Encounter:** 15% chance per under-garrisoned province traversed. Bandits accost the character. Emergent ASCII map combat triggered by world state. Not a formal quest but uses the same generation systems. Source: named character movement risk per Section 4.3.11.

### 56.1.6 Assassination System Seeds (Section 12.8, 55.21.15)

- **Defend Against Assassination:** Someone sent a shinobi. The player’s stronghold becomes a security mission. Detect and stop the assassin. Source: EXECUTE_ASSASSINATION action targeting a player-associated character.

- **Execute Assassination:** If the player is the covert operative. Infiltrate target settlement, three phases (access, execution, concealment). Source: COMMISSION_ASSASSINATION per Section 12.8.

### 56.1.7 Spirit Realm Seeds (Section 2.6)

- **Maigo no Musha Haunting:** Ghost with unfinished business. Could be combat (onryou) or investigation/delivery. Most narratively rich quest type. Source: Spirit realm activity per Section 2.6.4.

- **Gaki-do Manifestation:** Hungry dead in corrupted locations. Environmental corruption plus hostile spirits. Source: Section 2.6.2, locations with elevated PTL.

- **Toshigoku Bleed:** Realm of Slaughter bleeds through at old battlefields. Combat-heavy, spirits of fallen warriors. Source: Section 2.6.8.

### 56.1.8 Character Corruption Seeds (Section 11.11)

- **Hunt the Defector:** A named character has been corrupted by a Maho Cult or defected during a revolt. Track them down. The world generated the traitor; now someone has to find them. Source: Character Corruption and Defection mechanics per Section 11.11.
