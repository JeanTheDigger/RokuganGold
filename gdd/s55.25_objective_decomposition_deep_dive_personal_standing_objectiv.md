## 55.25 Objective Decomposition Deep-Dive — Personal Standing Objectives

**STATUS:** LOCKED. Uses only existing GDD mechanics.

Eight standing objectives covering honor, protection, knowledge, excellence, family, bushido, glory, and vengeance. These are the most individually focused objectives — they define who the character IS rather than what their clan needs.

### 55.25.1 "Honor my ancestors through my deeds"

**Nature:** Spiritual, context-dependent. What “honoring ancestors” looks like depends entirely on where the NPC is and what they’re doing. At court: perform brilliantly. At temple: pray. At holdings: govern well. In battle: fight honorably.

**Who gets this:** Meiyo and Makoto characters. Characters from families with strong ancestral traditions.

#### 1.1 Decomposition

func _decompose_honor_ancestors(obj: Objective, ctx: ContextSnapshot) -> ImmediateNeed:

var need = ImmediateNeed.new()

match ctx.context_flag:

ContextFlag.AT_COURT:

need.need_type = NeedType.SEEK_GLORY

need.priority = 2

return need

ContextFlag.AT_TEMPLE:

need.need_type = NeedType.PERFORM_RITUAL

need.priority = 2

return need

ContextFlag.AT_OWN_HOLDINGS:

if ctx.is_lord:

var ranked = _triage_provinces(ctx)

if ranked.size() > 0 and ranked[0]._triage_score > 30:

return _decompose_secure_territory(obj, ctx)

need.need_type = NeedType.TRAIN_SKILL

need.priority = 1

return need

ContextFlag.ON_CAMPAIGN:

need.need_type = NeedType.REST # combat is handled by battle system

need.priority = 1

return need

_:

need.need_type = NeedType.SEEK_GLORY

need.priority = 1

return need

This is the most context-sensitive objective. The NPC’s behavior shifts completely based on location. No single action dominates — the engine produces a character who prays at temples, performs at court, governs at home, and fights on campaign. A well-rounded samurai.

#### 1.2 Edge Cases

**EDGE CASE:** NPC has low Honor (below Rank 3): The ancestors would be ashamed. Decomposition should check Honor level first and route to RESTORE_HONOR (Gap 6) as a sub-objective before pursuing Glory or duty. A disgraced samurai cannot honor their ancestors while dishonored.

**EDGE CASE:** NPC’s family has a famous ancestor with specific virtues: The decomposition should weight actions aligned with that ancestor’s known virtues. A descendant of a famous duelist honors their ancestor by winning duels. A descendant of a great poet honors them through PUBLIC_PERFORMANCE. This would require a family_tradition tag on the character sheet — currently not in the GDD.

**FUTURE SUGGESTION:** A family_ancestor_tradition tag that weights specific actions could create deeply personalized “honor ancestors” behavior. For example: ancestor was a famous smith → CRAFT scores +15. Ancestor was a war hero → martial actions +15. Worth considering when the family history system is expanded.

#### 1.3 Worked Example

**NPC:** Mirumoto Hitomi (Meiyo/Ketsui, Dragon Bushi, Rank 3). At Dragon Champion court. Morning AP.

**Phase 2:** At court → SEEK_GLORY. Phase 5: PUBLIC_DEBATE scores 95 + Meiyo +5 + Ketsui +5 + Competence (Courtier 2 = −5) = 100. ISSUE_DUEL_CHALLENGE: 90 + Meiyo +8 + Ketsui +10 = +15 cap + Iaijutsu 4 = +5 = 110. Hitomi challenges a rival to a duel to honor her ancestors through martial prowess. A Dragon bushi’s definition of “honoring ancestors”: prove yourself with steel.

### 55.25.2 "Protect those under my care"

**Nature:** Defensive, caretaking. Test Case E (Shinjo Haruko) is the reference implementation. The NPC prioritizes the safety and wellbeing of their dependents, vassals, and province residents.

**Who gets this:** Jin characters heavily. Lord-tier characters. Parents. Characters with dependents.

#### 2.1 Decomposition

This is the province triage objective. Already implemented as Test Case E. The decomposition:

EVALUATE_PROVINCES → triage worst province → DEFEND_PROVINCE / INVESTIGATE_THREAT / PATROL_PROVINCE

If no province issues: RAISE_DISPOSITION with vassals and dependents (maintain the relationship of trust)

If at court: advocate for policies protecting civilians (anti-war, food sharing, garrison funding)

Full decomposition tree is in the NPC Engine Master Spec, Test Case E. No additional work needed.

#### 2.2 Edge Cases

**EDGE CASE:** NPC is not a lord (has no provinces): “Those under my care” means immediate companions, family, and junior samurai in their retinue. Decomposition shifts to: RAISE_DISPOSITION with dependents, TRAIN them (if they’re juniors), defend them physically if danger arises (travel together, accept duels on their behalf).

**EDGE CASE:** War threatens the province: This is the primary trigger for crisis override. The NPC drops everything to defend. INITIATE_WAR_CHECK or DEFEND_PROVINCE fires at priority 3. Jin personality makes them offer terms before fighting (OFFER_TERMS +10 lean) and accept surrender readily.

**EDGE CASE:** Starvation threatens: ACQUIRE_RESOURCE (rice) fires at maximum priority. A Jin lord with this objective will SHARE_SUPPLIES before anything else — they’d rather starve themselves than let their people go hungry.

### 55.25.3 "Accumulate knowledge and wisdom"

**Nature:** Scholarly, exploratory. The NPC values learning above all else. They seek to understand the world through study, conversation, travel, and spiritual inquiry.

**Who gets this:** Chishiki and Dōsatsu characters. Shugenja. Dragon Clan (Togashi monks, Kitsuki investigators). Phoenix Clan scholars.

#### 3.1 Decomposition

func _decompose_accumulate_knowledge(obj: Objective, ctx: ContextSnapshot) -> ImmediateNeed:

var need = ImmediateNeed.new()

match ctx.context_flag:

ContextFlag.AT_COURT:

# Court is a goldmine of information

# Priority: PROBE high-status characters (learn their secrets)

# Then: GATHER_INTELLIGENCE (learn court dynamics)

# Then: LISTEN_REFLECT (absorb through conversation)

var unprobed = ctx.characters_present.filter(

func(id): return not IntelDB.has_been_probed(ctx.npc_id, id))

if unprobed.size() > 0:

var best = _pick_highest_status(unprobed)

need.need_type = NeedType.GATHER_INTELLIGENCE

need.target_npc_id = best

need.priority = 2

return need

need.need_type = NeedType.GATHER_INTELLIGENCE

need.priority = 1

return need

ContextFlag.AT_TEMPLE:

if ctx.school_type == SchoolType.SHUGENJA:

need.need_type = NeedType.INVESTIGATE_THREAT

need.priority = 2

return need

need.need_type = NeedType.PERFORM_RITUAL # study and meditation

need.priority = 1

return need

ContextFlag.AT_DOJO:

need.need_type = NeedType.TRAIN_SKILL

need.priority = 2

return need

ContextFlag.AT_OWN_HOLDINGS:

# Study, train Lore skills, write letters to scholars

need.need_type = NeedType.TRAIN_SKILL

need.priority = 1

return need

_:

# Travel to places of learning

need.need_type = NeedType.ATTEND_COURT # courts are where knowledge flows

need.priority = 1

return need

#### 3.2 Edge Cases

**EDGE CASE:** Shugenja vs Bushi scholar: The Competence Modifier handles this. A shugenja’s COMMUNE_WITH_SPIRITS uses Theology (likely 4–5), scoring high. A bushi scholar’s TRAIN uses their martial skill (likely 4–5), scoring high for physical training. The shugenja accumulates spiritual knowledge; the bushi accumulates martial mastery. Same objective, different expressions.

**EDGE CASE:** NPC has already Probed everyone at court: GATHER_INTELLIGENCE becomes broad rather than targeted — learning court dynamics, topic positions, and alliance structures through LISTEN_REFLECT and READ_CHARACTER. The NPC shifts from interrogative to absorptive.

**EDGE CASE:** NPC discovers a forbidden text or dangerous knowledge (maho scroll, etc.): If Chishiki is their Shourido virtue, they may pursue the knowledge despite the danger. Gi characters refuse. This is a personality-filter decision at the action level, not the decomposition level.

**FUTURE SUGGESTION:** A STUDY_LORE action (1 AP, at holdings or temple with library) that directly advances a Lore skill faster than generic TRAIN could create a richer scholarly experience. Would need a library/text system. Worth considering when the temple/dojo detail pass is done.

#### 3.3 Worked Example

**NPC:** Kitsuki Kaagi (Dōsatsu/Chishiki, Dragon Courtier, Rank 3). At Crane Champion court. 8 attendees he hasn’t Probed. Morning AP.

**Phase 2:** At court. Unprobed targets exist. Returns GATHER_INTELLIGENCE targeting the highest-Status unprobed attendee.

**Phase 5:** PROBE: ObjAlign 95 + Dōsatsu +12 + Chishiki +10 = +15 cap + Competence (Investigation 5 = +10) = 120. READ_CHARACTER: 90 + +15 cap + (Investigation = +10) = 115. PROBE wins. Kaagi systematically reads the court like a book — learning each attendee’s objectives, positions, and secrets. Eight attendees = 4 days of intelligence gathering. By court’s end, Kaagi knows everything about everyone. The Dragon investigator archetype, emerging purely from the scoring formula.

### 55.25.4 "Achieve personal excellence in my role"

**Nature:** Self-improvement focused. The NPC wants to be the best at what their school trains them to do. A bushi wants to be the greatest swordsman. A courtier wants to be the most persuasive voice in the room. A shugenja wants the deepest communion with the kami.

**Who gets this:** Kanpeki heavily (the perfection drive). Ketsui (determination to improve). Available to all roles.

#### 4.1 Decomposition

func _decompose_personal_excellence(obj: Objective, ctx: ContextSnapshot) -> ImmediateNeed:

var need = ImmediateNeed.new()

var school_primary = SchoolDB.get_primary_skill(ctx.school_type)

match ctx.context_flag:

ContextFlag.AT_DOJO:

# Best training environment. Use it.

need.need_type = NeedType.TRAIN_SKILL

need.priority = 3

return need

ContextFlag.AT_COURT:

# Practice school skills through use

if ctx.school_type == SchoolType.COURTIER:

need.need_type = NeedType.SEEK_GLORY # court performance IS the practice

need.priority = 2

return need

if ctx.school_type == SchoolType.BUSHI:

# A bushi at court can still duel and train

need.need_type = NeedType.TRAIN_SKILL

need.priority = 1

return need

# Shugenja: meditate

need.need_type = NeedType.PERFORM_RITUAL

need.priority = 1

return need

ContextFlag.AT_OWN_HOLDINGS:

need.need_type = NeedType.TRAIN_SKILL

need.priority = 2

return need

ContextFlag.AT_TEMPLE:

if ctx.school_type == SchoolType.SHUGENJA or ctx.school_type == SchoolType.MONK:

need.need_type = NeedType.PERFORM_RITUAL

need.priority = 3 # This IS their excellence

return need

need.need_type = NeedType.PERFORM_RITUAL

need.priority = 1

return need

_:

need.need_type = NeedType.TRAIN_SKILL

need.priority = 1

return need

**The key:** TRAIN_SKILL uses the NPC’s XP advancement system (Section 22.5). NPCs gain 0.05 XP per OOC day as baseline. The TRAIN action likely doesn’t change this rate (it’s passive) but represents the NPC’s choice to spend AP on self-improvement rather than other pursuits. When the AP produces a skill roll (via practice), it contributes to school advancement.

#### 4.2 Edge Cases

**EDGE CASE:** NPC is already the highest-ranked practitioner of their skill at this court: Excellence is never achieved — it’s directional. They continue training. At court, they can demonstrate excellence through PUBLIC_PERFORMANCE or PUBLIC_DEBATE, which earns Glory (recognition of their skill) while also providing practice. The engine naturally cycles between training and demonstrating.

**EDGE CASE:** Courtier at their own holdings (no court to practice at): They train through WRITE_LETTER (practice calligraphy and rhetoric), study etiquette texts (TRAIN), and prepare for the next court. Between courts, a courtier sharpens their tools.

**EDGE CASE:** Kanpeki personality: Will not PUBLIC_DEBATE unless certain of winning (conditional block on PUBLIC_DEBATE when opponent’s Courtier exceeds their own). Will not ISSUE_DUEL_CHALLENGE unless Iaijutsu is superior. The perfectionist trains until they’re ready, then demonstrates flawlessly. They may appear to procrastinate but they’re actually preparing obsessively.

#### 4.3 Worked Example

**NPC:** Kakita Toshimoko (Kanpeki/Ketsui, Crane Bushi, Rank 5). At dojo. Iaijutsu 7.

**Phase 2:** AT_DOJO. Returns TRAIN_SKILL, priority 3.

**Phase 5:** TRAIN: ObjAlign 100 + Kanpeki +12 + Ketsui +8 = +15 cap + Competence (Iaijutsu 7 = +20) = 135. Nothing else comes close. Toshimoko practices iaijutsu from dawn to dusk. He’s already the greatest duelist in the Empire and he’s STILL training. That’s Kanpeki.

### 55.25.5 "Elevate my family name"

**Nature:** Identical to “Advance my family’s standing within the clan hierarchy” (Political Standing #3). Same decomposition. The distinction is cosmetic — “elevate” implies a broader scope (reputation beyond the clan, not just hierarchy within it), but the mechanical path is the same: SEEK_GLORY + RAISE_DISPOSITION with high-Status characters + attend prominent courts.

Full decomposition tree is in the Political Standing deep-dive, Section 3. No additional work needed. Cross-reference: Shiba Tsukune worked example.

#### 5.1 Only Distinction

"Elevate family name" has a slightly broader target pool than “advance within hierarchy.” The hierarchy objective focuses on decision-makers within the NPC’s own clan. The family name objective targets reputation across the Empire — so ATTEND_COURT at OTHER clans’ courts scores higher, and SEEK_GLORY at Winter Court is the ultimate venue. The decomposition can use a context check: if at own clan’s court, focus on decision-makers. If at another clan’s court, focus on Glory.

### 55.25.6 "Live by Bushido without compromise"

**Nature:** Internal, restrictive. This is not about DOING things — it’s about NOT doing things. The NPC tightens their own personality filter, refusing more temptations and accepting more costs for honorable behavior. When it IS active as a decomposition target, it routes to RESTORE_HONOR or spiritual discipline.

**Who gets this:** Meiyo heavily. Gi and Makoto. Monks. Characters who have experienced a spiritual awakening.

#### 6.1 How It Works Mechanically

This objective has TWO effects:

**Passive:** The NPC’s personality filter tightens. Actions that a normal NPC with the same virtue profile might take under pressure (compromising on Gi when stakes are high, breaking Makoto under duress) are blocked unconditionally. The objective overrides the “critical circumstances” exceptions in the filter system. Bushido without compromise means without compromise.

**Active (when decomposing):** If Honor is below the NPC’s expected level, routes to RESTORE_HONOR (Gap 6). If Honor is adequate, routes to PERFORM_RITUAL, MEDITATE, or TRAIN_SKILL — the spiritual discipline path.

#### 6.2 Decomposition

func _decompose_live_by_bushido(obj: Objective, ctx: ContextSnapshot) -> ImmediateNeed:

var need = ImmediateNeed.new()

# Is my Honor below my starting value? (personal standard)

var starting_honor = CharacterDB.get(ctx.npc_id).starting_honor

if ctx.honor_rank < starting_honor:

return _decompose_restore_honor(

_make_honor_obj(starting_honor), ctx)

# Honor is adequate. Pursue spiritual discipline by context.

match ctx.context_flag:

ContextFlag.AT_TEMPLE:

need.need_type = NeedType.PERFORM_RITUAL

need.priority = 2

return need

ContextFlag.AT_COURT:

# Demonstrate Bushido through action: refuse dishonorable offers,

# make Public Declarations of principle, keep promises.

need.need_type = NeedType.SEEK_GLORY

need.priority = 1

return need

_:

need.need_type = NeedType.TRAIN_SKILL

need.priority = 1

return need

#### 6.3 Edge Cases

**EDGE CASE:** NPC is offered a favor that would require dishonorable action later: The tightened filter blocks the acceptance. A normal Gi character might accept under extreme duress; this NPC refuses regardless.

**EDGE CASE:** The lord orders something questionable: Deepest conflict in L5R. Chugi says obey. Bushido-without-compromise says refuse. The objective’s passive effect makes the NPC refuse even lawful orders if they violate Bushido. This generates a Disobedience topic, Honor loss for disobedience but Honor gain for refusing a dishonorable act (Table 2.3: “following orders despite misgivings” is actually positive at low ranks but NEGATIVE at high ranks). A high-Honor samurai who refuses a dishonorable order is living their objective.

**EDGE CASE:** NPC has Shourido virtues alongside this: Rare combination but possible. The Bushido objective suppresses Shourido-driven behavior. The NPC’s Shourido lean values are effectively zeroed while this objective is active — they fight their own darker impulses. This creates a rich internal conflict character.

### 55.25.7 "Advance my personal Glory and reputation"

**Nature:** Self-promoting, public. This is Gap 2 (SEEK_GLORY) as a standing objective. Full implementation already exists.

Complete decomposition is in the Gap 2 implementation. Cross-reference: Kakita Yumi worked example (PUBLIC_PERFORMANCE at 125, Hida Kozan comparison showing bushi vs courtier Glory strategies). No additional work needed.

### 55.25.8 "Seek vengeance for [historical wrong]"

**Nature:** Targeted, consuming. The NPC has a specific person or family responsible for a wrong and will not rest until satisfaction is achieved. This is Gap 3 (CHALLENGE_TO_DUEL) as a standing objective.

**Who gets this:** Characters with historical modifier table entries indicating a severe event (Section 22.4). Yu and Kyōryōku characters. Characters whose family suffered a specific wrong.

Complete decomposition is in the Gap 3 implementation: _decompose_seek_vengeance(). Cross-reference: Matsu Ryota worked example (ISSUE_DUEL_CHALLENGE at 160). The decomposition covers: locate target → travel to target → establish pretext → assess capability → challenge or delegate.

**GDD GAP (RESOLVED):** Assassination path for covert-personality characters (Seigyo/Chishiki). All mechanics fully designed: COMMISSION_ASSASSINATION and EXECUTE_ASSASSINATION ActionIDs (Category 6, Section 55.21.15), ELIMINATE_CHARACTER NeedType with Annex A alignment entries, Section 12.8 assassination system (LOCKED, Phases 1–4), Kolat Lotus sect full decomposition including ARRANGE_PROXY_DUEL (Section 54.7c). The ELIMINATE_CHARACTER decomposition branches on personality filter: Seigyo/Chishiki/low-Honor characters route to COMMISSION_ASSASSINATION; Yu/Gi/high-Honor characters route to ISSUE_DUEL_CHALLENGE. All ActionIDs, skill mappings, and consequence chains are registered.

#### 8.1 The Unique Challenge: Standing Vengeance

Unlike the primary objective version (“Avenge Character X” which completes), the standing objective “Seek vengeance for historical wrong” is directional. The wrong may be against a family or clan, not a specific living person. The decomposition must handle:

**Target is a specific living person:** Identical to Gap 3 decomposition. Locate, pretext, challenge.

**Target is a family/lineage (original wrongdoer is dead):** The NPC targets the current head of the offending family, or the highest-status descendant available. The vengeance transfers across generations — canon L5R behavior.

**Target is a clan (collective wrong):** The NPC treats any member of the offending clan as a valid target. They seek out the most prominent member available and challenge them. This is the most extreme form — it’s a blood feud that could last generations.

#### 8.2 Decomposition Addition for Standing Vengeance

func _decompose_standing_vengeance(obj: Objective, ctx: ContextSnapshot) -> ImmediateNeed:

# Determine the current target

var target = obj.target_npc_id

if target and not CharacterDB.is_alive(target):

# Original target dead. Find their successor.

target = CharacterDB.get_successor_or_descendant(target)

if not target and obj.target_clan_id:

# Clan-level wrong. Target the highest-status member available.

var clan_members = ctx.known_contacts.get(obj.target_clan_id, [])

if clan_members.is_empty():

# Don't even know anyone from the offending clan.

var need = ImmediateNeed.new()

need.need_type = NeedType.IDENTIFY_CONTACT

need.target_clan_id = obj.target_clan_id

need.priority = 2

return need

target = _pick_highest_status(clan_members)

if not target:

# Cannot find anyone to target. Objective stalls.

var need = ImmediateNeed.new()

need.need_type = NeedType.GATHER_INTELLIGENCE

need.priority = 1

return need

# Have a target. Delegate to the Gap 3 vengeance decomposition.

obj.target_npc_id = target

return _decompose_seek_vengeance(obj, ctx)

#### 8.3 Edge Cases

**EDGE CASE:** Original target dies before the NPC reaches them: Vengeance transfers to the successor. If no successor exists (family line ended), the standing objective becomes obsolete — one of the only cases where a standing objective has a “done” state (Section 18.6). The engine triggers a standing objective replacement.

**EDGE CASE:** NPC and target are at the same court but NPC is outmatched: Gap 3 decomposition handles this: if outmatched and not Yu/Kyōryōku, the NPC trains or delegates to a champion. The vengeance burns but prudence wins — unless Yu overrides.

**EDGE CASE:** NPC’s lord orders them to make peace with the target’s clan: Standing objective conflicts with primary (lord’s order). Per priority cascade, primary overrides standing. The NPC obeys... publicly. But the standing vengeance persists, and when the primary objective completes, the vengeance resurfaces. A Seigyo NPC might pursue the peace objective while secretly gathering leverage against the target for later. Makoto NPC openly tells their lord they will obey but cannot forgive.

**EDGE CASE:** The vengeance is unjust (the NPC’s family was actually in the wrong): The engine doesn’t know this. The NPC pursues vengeance based on their belief, not truth. If they discover the truth through GATHER_INTELLIGENCE or PROBE, this could trigger a standing objective change (major life event: learned the truth about their family’s shame). This is emergent narrative from the information system.
