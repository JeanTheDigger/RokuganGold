**CRAFTING EXTRAORDINARY WEAPONS:**

Weapons of standard quality are considered “Average.” Weapons of higher quality are “Excellent” — possessing special properties that set them apart.

- To craft an Excellent weapon: use the regular Crafting rules but triple both the cost of materials and the koku value of the weapon. (An Excellent Wakizashi = 45 koku; an Excellent Naginata = 30 koku.)

- Minimum Skill Rank: Craft: Weaponsmithing 7. Kaiu or Tsi smiths may create Excellent weapons at Skill Rank 5.

- A smith who creates an Excellent weapon may call Raises to confer one Special Quality. Failure on the Crafting roll means the weapon is wholly ruined — must try again. Tsi or Kaiu school characters receive one Free Raise for conferring Special Qualities.

Weapon Special Qualities:

- Balanced: Wielder gains +1k0 on attack rolls. Raises: 4

- Radiant: Counts as Jade for attacking Invulnerable targets. Raises: 6

- Signature: Bears a unique signature stamp identifying the creator. Raises: 2

- Swift: Wielder gains +5 to Initiative Score while using this weapon. Raises: 4

- True: Decreases the target’s Reduction by the wielder’s Strength. Raises: 6

- Unbreakable: This weapon cannot be broken. Raises: 5

**CRAFTING SACRED WEAPONS:**

A smith of a clan that possesses a Sacred Weapon (per L5R 4th Edition Core Rulebook, p. 152–153) may attempt to craft one using Craft: Weaponsmithing. The smith must first create the appropriate Excellent Quality weapon with 7 Raises (6 Raises for a Kaiu smith). A samurai may only attempt to create a Sacred Weapon of their own clan — Crane may only make Kakita Blades, Crab may only make Kaiu Blades, and so forth.

**ROKUGANI FIREARMS:**

Rokugani firearms are an optional element. All Rokugani firearms require three Complex Actions to load or reload. When fired, they ignore the effects of armor on both Armor TN and Reduction. GMs may rule that the user’s Perception is added to the gun’s damage roll (comparable to Strength for melee weapons). For a non-Iron Rokugan setting, it is suggested to incorporate only the Kakiyari, Teppo, and Bajozutsu, with only the Bajozutsu having the Samurai keyword.

**Teppoudo (Intelligence) — Weapon Skill**

Emphases: Fire-Lance, Hand-Cannon, Bajozutsu, Teppo. The skill of maintaining and firing a Rokugani firearm. Places great emphasis on mindfulness, calm deliberate intention, and careful breathing — as much a practice of zen-like focus as rote and memory.

- Mastery 3: +1k0 to all damage rolls when firing a Rokugani firearm.

- Mastery 5: Loading a Rokugani firearm requires one fewer Complex Action.

- Mastery 7: +0k1 to all damage rolls when firing a Rokugani firearm.
