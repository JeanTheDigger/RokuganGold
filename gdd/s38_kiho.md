# 38. Kiho

Kiho fall between spells and bushi Techniques — elemental abilities of the Brotherhood of Shinsei. Four categories: Internal (align chi to increase own abilities), Kharmic (external, non-offensive effects), Martial (delivered via unarmed or atemi attacks), and Mystical (overtly supernatural, inexplicable). All Kiho have a Mastery Level; a monk meets it if School Rank + relevant Ring equals or exceeds it. Non-Brotherhood affiliated monks pay 1.5× normal cost; shugenja pay 2× and use Ring only (no School Rank). Non-Brotherhood characters may never know more Kiho than their total Monk or Shugenja School Rank.

Activation: Spend a Void Point (Free Action), or Meditation/Void roll (TN 15 = Complex Action, TN 30 = Simple Action). For atemi activations these are all Free Actions. Only one Internal, one Kharmic, and one Mystical Kiho may be active at a time. Multiple Martial Kiho may be active, but only one Kiho effect may be delivered per unarmed strike per Turn (unless the monk has multiple unarmed attacks).

Atemi Attacks: Precise nerve-cluster strikes that deal no normal damage — only the Kiho effect occurs. All forms of armor double their normal Armor TN bonus against atemi attacks. Atemi must touch bare skin. Kiho requiring atemi have (Atemi) after the Ring/Mastery entry.

**AIR KIHO:**

**Air Fist**

- Air 3

- Type: Internal

Channel the energy of wind into speed. While active (and making only unarmed attacks), Initiative Score is increased by +5. Making any other type of attack immediately cancels the effect. Unarmed strike damage is reduced by Air Ring. Lasts a maximum of one day.

**Calling the East Wind**

- Air 5

- Type: Martial

When activated, make a single unarmed kick attack, leaping through the air up to Air Ring ×10 feet. The leap adds +1k0 damage to a successful attack, and grants one Free Raise toward the Knockdown Maneuver on this attack.

**Censure of Thunder**

- Air 7 (Atemi)

- Type: Martial

On a successful atemi strike: target suffers 1k1 Wounds from a minor electrical shock and automatically drops their weapon as if struck by a Disarm Maneuver (the chi flow through their hand is interrupted). The target may spend 2 Void Points to negate the Disarm, but if they do, they suffer an additional 2k2 Wounds from a more severe electrical shock. Damage from this Kiho bypasses armor Reduction.

**Eye of the Eagle**

- Air 5

- Type: Internal

As a Complex Action, focus chi by remaining utterly still and unmoving. Once done, so long as concentration is maintained and position is not changed, can see a number of miles equal to School Rank. Level of detail is sufficient to notice physical characteristics of individuals, but not fine detail such as reading.

**Flee the Darkness**

- Air 6

- Type: Kharmic

Become immune to the influence of the Shadowlands Taint (and other supernatural ailments such as the Lying Darkness) for Rounds equal to Air Ring. Prevents new Taint acquisition and negates all penalties and benefits of existing Taint while active. If any deliberate action is taken that would normally cause Taint acquisition (such as casting maho), the effect ends immediately and the Taint is gained as normal.

**The Great Silence**

- Air 4 (Atemi)

- Type: Mystical

Delivered by atemi attack. On success: the target is unable to speak for Rounds equal to Air Ring.

**Harmony of the Mind**

- Air 5

- Type: Mystical

Make a Contested Air Roll against a target within line of sight to learn one of the following: their highest or lowest Ring, their highest Skill Rank, or their highest-cost Spiritual Advantage or Disadvantage. Each successful Raise grants one additional piece of information. Usable Air Ring times per day.

**Hurricane Palm**

- Air 7

- Type: Martial

After a successful unarmed strike, spend one Void Point. The strike inflicts only half normal damage (rounded down), but knocks the opponent back twice Air Ring in feet and leaves them Prone. Any opponent successfully struck is moved by this effect even if they take no Wounds from the blow.

**Inari's Wrath**

- Air 8

- Type: Mystical

Spend a Void Point (Free Action), then take a Complex Action to take a deep breath and hold it. On the following Round, exhale as a Complex Action to create a cone of freezing air (School Rank ×5 feet long, School Rank ×2 feet wide at the end). Any living creature caught within suffers cold damage with DR equal to Air Ring. Certain physical materials may also be damaged (GM's discretion).

**Riding the Clouds**

- Air 3

- Type: Mystical

While active, make a Simple Move Action to leap a maximum distance of Air Ring ×10 feet. Effect lasts until one such leap has been made, then is expended.

**Soul of the Four Winds**

- Air 4

- Type: Internal

Align chi with the wind to dramatically increase reaction time and evasiveness. While active, Armor TN is increased by Insight + Air Ring.

**Stain Upon the Soul**

- Air 3 (Atemi)

- Type: Martial

Successful atemi attack disrupts the target's chi, causing crippling pain throughout the body. Target suffers a penalty to all TNs as if suffering Wound Ranks equal to the caster's Air Ring. Lasts Rounds equal to Insight Rank + Air Ring. Cannot afflict a target multiple times; superseded by (not cumulative with) actual Wounds. Considered impure and wicked by several Brotherhood orders.

**Steal the Air Dragon**

- Air 7

- Type: Kharmic

Draw chi inward to become less noticeable and easily overlooked. While active, gain additional rolled and kept dice equal to Air Ring on all Stealth Skill Rolls. Lasts minutes equal to Insight Rank.

**Strike Through the Wind**

- Air 5

- Type: Mystical

Spend a Void Point (Free Action). While active, transmit the force of blows through the air as if it were an extension of the body. Unarmed melee attacks may target opponents as far as School Rank ×25 feet away, but must be made as a Complex Action.

**Touch of the Storm**

- Air 6 (Atemi)

- Type: Martial

Requires a successful atemi strike. On success: make a Contested Air Roll against the opponent. If successful, the opponent suffers damage with DR equal to Air Ring in the form of a violent electrical discharge. This damage ignores all forms of Reduction unless it specifically mentions electrical damage.

**Thunder's Word**

- Air 6

- Type: Mystical

As a Complex Action, draw a deep breath and shout a single word of power filled with the essence of the Thunder Dragon. All living beings capable of hearing the shout must make a Contested Air Roll against the caster (each rolls separately against a single caster roll). Those who fail are Dazed for Rounds equal to Air Ring.

**Way of the Willow**

- Air 5

- Type: Internal

While active, if the caster has not yet taken their Turn during the current Round, they may spend a Void Point to immediately interrupt any opponent declaring a melee attack against them. The interruption allows either a Move Action away from the opponent or an unarmed attack against them. Once used, the Kiho effect ends. Rendered inactive if not used within one day of activation.

**The Wind's Vision**

- Air 4

- Type: Internal

Fire an arrow at a target that is out of sight but within range. The arrow must still travel in a straight line — it cannot pierce stone or turn corners, but firing through a paper screen, in total darkness, or through heavy smoke are all possible. The caster must know the target exists and its approximate location within 30 feet of its actual position.

**EARTH KIHO:**

**Bishamon's Grasp**

- Earth 7

- Type: Kharmic

Can only be activated in Defense or Full Defense Stance. While active, on the caster's Turn may make Grappling attack rolls as Free Actions against opponents who have attacked since the last Turn (one Free Action attack per qualifying opponent — overrides normal stance restrictions on attacking). Opponents in a controlled Grapple may be Thrown as a Free Action rather than a Complex Action (damage roll suffers -1k1 if GM allows Throw damage). The caster may remain in Defense/Full Defense Stance while maintaining a Grapple.

**Cleansing Spirit**

- Earth 4

- Type: Internal

While active, roll additional dice equal to half Earth Ring (rounded down) on any roll made to resist the effects of poison or disease.

**Depths of the World**

- Earth 4

- Type: Internal

When activated (Complex Action only), immediately roll to recover from a non-permanent Condition or other effect that allows recovery rolls (such as Dazed). May be activated even when not normally allowed to perform actions (e.g. while Stunned).

**Earthen Fist**

- Earth 3

- Type: Internal

While active, may only adopt Defense or Full Defense Stance. If an opponent makes a melee attack against the caster and misses, on the caster's next Turn they may attempt the Disarm Maneuver against that opponent (if within range) for no Raises and regardless of normal stance restrictions, ending the Kiho's effect. Rendered inactive if not used within one day of activation.

**Earth Needs No Eyes**

- Earth 3

- Type: Internal

Attune to minute vibrations in the earth to detect movement. While active, detect the approach of any creature or individual moving across the ground within Insight Rank ×50 feet. Location of moving things is known; stationary things are not. If active when a skirmish begins, cannot be ambushed or surprised by any land-bound opponent. Lasts minutes equal to Earth Ring.

**Earth Palm**

- Earth 6 (Atemi)

- Type: Martial

Delivered by atemi strike. On success, declare whether attacking the target's Fire or Water: attacking Water imposes -4k0 to all damage rolls; attacking Fire forces the target to make two Raises with no benefit on every attack roll (these Raises do not count against the maximum Raises allowed). Effects may be combined through multiple applications. Each effect lasts Rounds equal to Earth Ring, ending on the Reactions Stage. Each reapplication resets the duration for the chosen effect. Cannot be used more than once per Turn.

**Embrace the Stone**

- Earth 5

- Type: Mystical

When activated, gain Reduction equal to twice Earth Ring. Stacks with all other Reduction sources (armor, spells, etc.). Lasts Rounds equal to Earth Ring.

**Grasp the Earth Dragon**

- Earth 5

- Type: Internal

While active, TN penalties at all Wound Ranks are reduced by Earth Ring. May also continue to take Simple Actions when at the Down Wound Rank. Lasts Rounds equal to Earth Ring.

**Harmony in Earth**

- Earth 6

- Type: Internal

Must remain perfectly still for Turns equal to (10 minus Earth Ring). Once accomplished, immediately detect any spiritual and physical impurities within 50 feet × Earth Ring for one Round. Learn the direction, general nature (Taint, poison, disease, things not native to Ningen-do), and relative strength of each impurity. May make an appropriate Lore Skill roll to learn one additional piece of information (GM's choice). Detects a total number of impurities equal to Void, strongest first. Intelligent beings detectable by this Kiho may resist with a Contested Earth Roll.

**Rest, My Brother**

- Earth 5 (Atemi)

- Type: Martial

Restricted to a handful of Brotherhood orders. Deals normal unarmed damage plus additional unkept damage dice equal to the opponent's Shadowlands Taint Rank. If the target is human or formerly uncorrupted (not an oni or inherently Tainted creature), they lose all benefits of the Shadowlands Taint for Rounds equal to Earth Ring, and are freed from the madness and mental instability it causes.

**Rising Mountain**

- Earth 6

- Type: Kharmic

Lasts Rounds equal to Insight Rank +1. While active, every time an attacker makes a Raise on any offensive combat action against the caster (not including spells), the caster gains Reduction equal to twice the number of Raises made. Free Raises do not trigger this. The Reduction is gained the moment Raises are declared, regardless of whether the attack succeeds. Cumulative but cannot exceed Earth ×5. Lasts until Kiho is deactivated or the skirmish ends.

**The Rolling Avalanche**

- Earth 5 (Atemi)

- Type: Martial

Delivered by atemi strike that deals normal unarmed damage. On a successful strike, damage roll is increased by +Xk0 where X is Earth Ring.

**Root the Mountain**

- Earth 5

- Type: Internal

While active, Knockdown Maneuvers against the caster require 2 extra Raises to succeed. Any attempt to force the caster to move against their will (including Knockdown Maneuvers) requires a Contested Earth Roll in addition to any other costs. The caster may not make Move Actions without dispelling this effect.

**Shadowed Mountain**

- Earth 6

- Type: Internal

While active, may choose to immediately enter Full Defense Stance immediately before being attacked (once per activation). After activating this Kiho, cannot change Stances during the next opportunity to do so (normal Turn chance or other effect). Lasts the remainder of the day or until used.

**Speed of the Mountains**

- Earth 4 (Atemi)

- Type: Kharmic

Delivered by atemi attack. On success: the target's Water Ring is considered 2 Ranks lower for determining Move Action distance. Lasts Rounds equal to twice Earth Ring.

**Way of the Earth**

- Earth 4

- Type: Martial

While active, opponents engaged in a Grapple with the caster suffer additional Wounds per Round equal to Earth Ring (regardless of who is controlling the Grapple). Damage inflicted during the Reactions Stage. Lasts minutes equal to Earth Ring.

**Wholeness in All**

- Earth 6

- Type: Kharmic

Requires two hours of uninterrupted meditation before activation (Complex Action only). Must call one Raise on the activation roll per mundane poison, disease, or similar non-supernatural effect currently afflicting the caster. On success, purge a number of such impurities equal to remaining Void Points (not counting the Point spent to activate). All those Void Points are then spent as part of the effect. Does not work on most supernatural afflictions (Taint, magical plagues) but GM has discretion on semi-magical conditions.

**FIRE KIHO:**

**The Body is an Anvil**

- Fire 4

- Type: Mystical

Skin becomes extremely hot to the touch. Anyone who touches the caster (including those making unarmed attacks and those struck by the caster's unarmed attacks) suffers Wounds equal to Fire Ring. Lasts Rounds equal to twice Fire Ring.

**Breaking Blow**

- Fire 3

- Type: Martial

While active, unarmed strikes inflict full damage to all non-metal substances (wood, stone, etc.) without ill effects on the caster from striking such objects. Lasts minutes equal to Fire Ring. GM is the ultimate judge of how much damage a given inanimate object can withstand.

**Channel the Fire Dragon**

- Fire 6

- Type: Internal

While active, all damage suffered from heat or cold is reduced by -2k2. Includes fire-generating spells and other Kiho. Lasts one day.

**Dance of the Flames**

- Fire 6

- Type: Martial

While active, may make unarmed attacks as a Simple Action rather than a Complex Action. Must make an unarmed attack against an opponent (not an ally or inanimate object) every Round while active — if this requirement is not met, the effect ends immediately.

**Destiny's Strike**

- Fire 4

- Type: Martial

While active, whenever struck by a melee attack, may immediately make a single unarmed counterattack. If the Turn has not yet been taken, this counts as the Turn. If the Turn has been taken, this is a Free Action instead. The counterattack ignores Wound Penalties from the attack being responded to. Lasts until the end of the skirmish.

**Falling Star Strike**

- Fire 7 (Atemi)

- Type: Martial

May only be activated by spending a Void Point (no Meditation/Void roll allowed). Also requires two Raises on the unarmed attack. On a successful hit: inflicts normal unarmed damage plus additional damage with DR equal to Fire Ring in rolled and kept dice. Additionally, make a Contested Fire Roll against the target — if successful, the target is Blinded for hours equal to Fire Ring.

**Fire's Fleeting Speed**

- Fire 4

- Type: Kharmic

While active, increase the distance moved by any Move Action by +5'. Lasts Rounds equal to Fire Ring.

**Flame Fist**

- Fire 3 (Atemi)

- Type: Martial

On a successful atemi strike: the target suffers a TN penalty to all actions equal to three times Fire Ring. Lasts Rounds equal to Fire Ring.

**The Mind's Fire**

- Fire 4

- Type: Internal

While active, gain +2k2 to all Intelligence-based Skill Rolls. When the Kiho ends, the caster is immediately Fatigued.

**Seven Storm's Fist**

- Fire 6 (Atemi)

- Type: Martial

On a successful atemi strike: make a Contested Fire Roll against the target. If successful, the target is Stunned for one Round.

**Sever the Dark Lord's Touch**

- Fire 5 (Atemi)

- Type: Mystical

On a successful atemi strike against an unintelligent undead creature: make a Contested Roll of Fire vs the undead's Shadowlands Taint. If successful, the undead is instantly destroyed — its connection to Jigoku severed. Special: Mastery requirement is considered 1 Rank lower for Kuni Shugenja and Kuni Witch-Hunters.

**Unbalance the Mind**

- Fire 5 (Atemi)

- Type: Mystical

On a successful atemi strike: the target is Dazed.

**WATER KIHO:**

**As the Breakers**

- Water 4 (Atemi)

- Type: Kharmic

On a successful atemi strike: the target loses one Simple Action this Round. May only target opponents who have not yet acted in the present Round. An opponent may not be affected by this Kiho more than once per skirmish.

**Buoyed by the Kami**

- Water 3

- Type: Mystical

While active, move across the surface of water as if it were Basic Terrain. Effect only continues as long as Simple Move Actions are taken each Round. Lasts a maximum of five minutes, after which it may be reactivated.

**Chi Protection**

- Water 4 (Atemi)

- Type: Martial

Must be activated by spending a Void Point (no Meditation/Void roll). Activated immediately after successfully striking a target (opponent or willing ally) with an atemi strike. The target regains Wounds equal to Water Ring immediately and at the start of every subsequent Turn for Rounds equal to Insight Rank. Cannot be used on oneself.

**Dharma Technique**

- Water 7 (Staff)

- Type: Martial

Requires a staff maintained in constant motion (expend a Simple Action on the staff's movement each Round). While active, when a spell specifically targets the caster (not area-effect), may make a Contested Staves/Water vs shugenja's Spell Casting Roll. If successful, the chi disperses the spell, negating its effects against the caster (other targets are not protected). May be used Water Ring times per skirmish. At GM's option may also defend against maho spells, but requires 3 Raises on the Contested Roll.

**Freezing the Lifeblood**

- Water 7 (Atemi)

- Type: Martial

On a successful atemi strike: the target is paralyzed — effectively Stunned and unable to take Move Actions — for Rounds equal to Insight Rank.

**Musubi**

- Water 5 (Staff)

- Type: Internal

While active and holding and moving a staff (considered a Free Action), add Water Ring + Staves Skill Rank to Armor TN. Additionally, for each Simple Action spent in the same Round to enhance the Kiho, Armor TN is increased by Water Ring again for that Round (bonus ends at the start of the caster's next Turn).

**Partaking the Waters**

- Water 6

- Type: Internal

While active, gain Reduction equal to Water Ring. Stacks with all other Reduction sources (spells, armor, etc.). Lasts Rounds equal to twice Water Ring.

**Ride the Water Dragon**

- Water 3

- Type: Kharmic

While active, recover Wounds equal to Water Ring during the Reactions Stage of each Round. Lasts Rounds equal to Insight Rank.

**Slap the Wave**

- Water 7

- Type: Mystical

Must be activated by spending a Void Point (no Meditation/Void roll). Strike hands together and release a powerful kiai shout. Everyone in the caster's forward-facing arc within Water Ring ×5 feet must succeed at a Contested Water Roll against the caster or become Dazed.

**Tasaii-Do**

- Water 6 (Staff, Atemi)

- Type: Martial

Activated with a staff attack (rather than an unarmed strike). After striking the opponent, make a Contested Water vs target's Earth roll. On success: the target is Stunned.

**Waves in All Things**

- Water 4

- Type: Mystical

Make an unarmed attack by striking a body of standing water or the earth. The effects of this strike are felt by one opponent in contact with that water or earth within Water Ring ×10 feet. The effect is obvious and cannot be performed discreetly. If the caster was concealed, their position is immediately revealed to the target and those in their immediate vicinity.

**VOID KIHO:**

**Banish All Shadows**

- Void 4 (Atemi)

- Type: Kharmic

On a successful atemi strike: the target may ignore the effects of their highest-point Disadvantage for Rounds equal to Void Ring. Does not affect Spiritual or Social Disadvantages.

**Death Touch**

- Void 7 (Atemi)

- Type: Mystical

Must successfully strike an opponent with three atemi strikes on three consecutive Rounds after activation. Spend an additional Void Point after the third strike. The target's Rings are reduced by 1 each hour; maximum penalty equal to Insight Rank. If any Ring reaches 0, the target falls into a catatonic state and must make three Contested Void Rolls against the caster. If all three are lost by 5 or more, the target dies. Use of this Kiho can be grounds for excommunication from the Brotherhood.

**Eight Directions Awareness**

- Void 5

- Type: Mystical

While active, become aware of all living things within Void Ring ×10 feet, and also aware of any deliberately concealed object or individual within that range (secret doors, hidden compartments, buried objects, etc.).

**Knowledge from Within**

- Void 4

- Type: Internal

Must be meditating or in Center Stance to activate. While active, become generally aware of the presence of Elemental spell effects and nemuranai within Void ×10 feet. Know the general direction and nature of each effect or object (e.g. 'a Fire spell in that direction') but not their specific identity or exact location.

**Mind/No-Mind**

- Void 6 (Atemi)

- Type: Mystical

Make an atemi strike and a Contested Void vs target's Fire roll. If successful: the target's mind is momentarily purged of all thoughts and they forget everything that happened during the last minute. At GM's option, the target may also be Dazed for one Round. Has helpful applications such as calming someone overwhelmed with fear, rage, grief, or uncontrolled emotion.

**Rebuke of the Heavens**

- Void 5

- Type: Kharmic

May target any one supernatural creature not native to Jigoku or Tengoku. Activated as a Complex Action by performing a gesture of spiritual rebuke (chanting a sutra, shaking a ringed staff, forming a mudra). Make a Contested Meditation/Void vs the creature's highest Ring roll — on success, the creature suffers -Xk1 to all rolls (X = Monk School Ranks) until slain or departed from the caster's presence. Available to monks only, not shugenja.

**Sense the Balance**

- Void 6 (Atemi)

- Type: Kharmic

On a successful atemi strike: may spend a Void Point to learn the total number of Spiritual Advantages or Disadvantages (one or the other, caster's choice) the target possesses. On a successful Contested Void Roll, also learn the name and specific nature of the highest-point Advantage or Disadvantage (e.g. not only that the target is Cursed by the Realms, but which Spirit Realm cursed them). Subsequent uses against the same target reveal new Advantages or Disadvantages until all are known.

**Silent Solace**

- Void 5 (Atemi)

- Type: Kharmic

On a successful atemi strike: the target must expend two spell slots to cast a single spell. Lasts Rounds equal to Void Ring.

**Song of the World**

- Void 3

- Type: Kharmic

Target an opponent within 50 feet and succeed at a Contested Void Roll against them. If successful: the target's Initiative Score is reduced by 5 and the caster's is increased by 5.

**Spin the Kharmic Wheel**

- Void 8 (Atemi)

- Type: Kharmic

Expend all remaining Void Points when activated. The target of the unarmed attack loses one randomly determined Social, Spiritual, or Mental Disadvantage and gains a new randomly determined Social, Spiritual, or Mental Disadvantage of equal value. The GM may choose rather than select randomly if a more interesting or believable result would result.

**Striking Through the Void**

- Void 7

- Type: Martial

Controversial within peaceful Brotherhood sects. While active, may spend a Void Point on damage rolls for unarmed strikes (one Void Point per attack).

**Touch the Void Dragon**

- Void 4

- Type: Internal

While active, one Ring and its associated Traits are considered one Rank higher. Which Ring is affected depends on the environment: mountains = Earth, seashore = Water, plains = Air, desert or volcanic areas = Fire.

**To the Last Breath**

- Void 3

- Type: Kharmic

Any one selected individual within 20 feet when the Kiho is activated gains one Void Point on their next Turn. No target may benefit from this Kiho more than twice per day.

**Void Fist**

- Void 4 (Atemi)

- Type: Martial

Unlike other atemi attacks, appears to be a normal damaging strike. Must make one successful Raise to use. A successful strike inflicts no damage, but the caster immediately regains 2 Void Points. Can only be used on enemies; has no effect on allies or targets without Void Points.

**The World Disappears**

- Void 6

- Type: Internal

Must be meditating or in Center Stance to activate. While active, float a few inches above the ground and move at normal speed across any surface (ground, water, molten lava) without touching it. Immune to Grappling and Entangling attacks while active.
