## 12.9a Intimidation Public TN Base — LOCKED

This section formalizes the base TN increase for public intimidation, left unspecified
by GDD s12.9. The main section specifies the per-Raise increment (+5) but omits the
base number on success.

---

### Source Derivation

**GDD-specified values (s12.9):**

| Context | Base TN increase | Per-Raise |
|---------|-----------------|-----------|
| Private (in-person) | +10 (explicit) | +5 |
| Letter | +5 (explicit) | none |
| Public (court) | ??? | +5 (explicit) |

**Private wording:** "Success raises the TN on their next action toward you or your
objective this session by +10. Each Raise adds +5 to that TN."

**Public wording:** "Instead it raises the effective TN they must overcome to act on
that position. [...] Each Raise increases the difficulty by +5."

The public section specifies the per-Raise increment but skips the base. Both private
and public use the same contested roll formula (Willpower + Intimidation vs Etiquette +
Willpower + Honor Rank). The GDD describes public as "the most powerful form of
intimidation" — its extra power lies in the social consequences (witness disposition
losses, Honor/Infamy cost, potential Disgrace topic), not a mechanically different base.

---

### Value — LOCKED

| Constant | Value | Rationale |
|----------|-------|-----------|
| `PUBLIC_TN_INCREASE_BASE` | 10 | Same as private intimidation. Both are in-person contested rolls. The GDD's silence on the public base indicates no difference from private, not a zero baseline. "Success raises the effective TN" requires a non-zero base. |

---

### Notes

`PUBLIC_TN_INCREASE_PER_RAISE = 5` is GDD-confirmed (s12.9: "Each Raise increases
the difficulty by +5"). `PRIVATE_TN_INCREASE_BASE = 10` and
`PRIVATE_TN_INCREASE_PER_RAISE = 5` are GDD-confirmed (s12.9: "raises the TN... by
+10... Each Raise adds +5"). `LETTER_TN_INCREASE = 5` is GDD-confirmed (s12.9:
"raises TN on their next relevant action by +5").
