## s52.6 Temporary Ronin Contracts — LOCKED

This section defines task-based, time-limited employment of ronin by lords.
A contract hire is distinct from permanent retainer service (s52.5 Part D):
the ronin gains a lord and a role, but their `clan` does not change, and
the arrangement terminates automatically at the contract's end.

---

### Part A — Contract Types

Three contract types are available. Each maps to a standing objective that
the lord assigns to the hired ronin on acceptance.

| Contract Type | Assigned NeedType | Typical Duration |
|---------------|------------------|-----------------|
| `PROVINCE_DEFENSE` | `DEFEND_PROVINCE` | 1–3 seasons |
| `MAGISTRATE_AIDE` | `UPHOLD_LAW` | 1–2 seasons |
| `MILITARY_SERVICE` | `LEVY_TROOPS` | 1–3 seasons |

Duration is specified by the lord at hire time as a number of seasons (1, 2, or 3).
`contract_end_ic_day` is stored in the ronin's `supply_ledger` dict.

---

### Part B — The HIRE_RONIN ActionID

**Context:** `AT_OWN_HOLDINGS`, `VISITING`
**AP cost:** 1
**Who can use it:** Lords only (`LORD_ONLY_ACTIONS`)
**Skill roll:** Courtier / Awareness vs TN 10 (confirming the lord can articulate terms)

The lord selects a co-located ronin (not `permanent_ronin`, not already employed)
and a contract type. The lord must be able to pay the upfront fee (see Part C).

**Auto-rejection conditions (checked before roll):**
- Lord's disposition toward the ronin ≤ −1
- Ronin's `permanent_ronin = true`
- Lord knows the ronin holds a conviction for TREASON, MAHO_USE, or
  UNSANCTIONED_COVERT_KILLING

**On success:** Fires a reactive event `CONTRACT_OFFERED` into the ronin's
`pending_events`. The ronin evaluates and responds next tick (see Part D).

**On roll failure:** No effect; the lord fumbled the offer. No cooldown.

---

### Part C — Payment

The lord pays an upfront sum in koku at offer time. Payment is deducted when
the `CONTRACT_OFFERED` reactive event is accepted by the ronin (not at offer
time, in case the ronin declines).

Payment scales by contract type and duration:

| Contract Type | Per-Season Rate |
|---------------|----------------|
| `PROVINCE_DEFENSE` | 3 koku/season |
| `MAGISTRATE_AIDE` | 2 koku/season |
| `MILITARY_SERVICE` | 2 koku/season |

Total payment = rate × duration_seasons.

**CONTRACT_PAYMENT_PROVINCE_DEFENSE_PER_SEASON = 3**
**CONTRACT_PAYMENT_MAGISTRATE_AIDE_PER_SEASON = 2**
**CONTRACT_PAYMENT_MILITARY_SERVICE_PER_SEASON = 2**

---

### Part D — Ronin Decision (Reactive)

When a `CONTRACT_OFFERED` event arrives in the ronin's `pending_events`,
the ronin evaluates acceptance.

**Auto-accept conditions:** Any of the following cause immediate acceptance
regardless of personality:
- Desperation state is active (`check_desperation()` → `"desperate"`)
- Disposition toward the offering lord ≥ 31 (Friend tier)

**Personality-gated acceptance (when not auto-accepting):**
- Chugi virtue: accepts if contract type matches DEFEND_PROVINCE or UPHOLD_LAW
- Meiyo virtue: accepts if lord's status ≥ 4.0
- Ketsui virtue: always accepts (employment over wandering)
- Seigyo virtue: accepts if no active FIND_NEW_LORD objective conflict exists
- Ishi virtue: refuses if desperation is not active (self-reliance)
- Default (no strong virtue): accepts if disposition ≥ 0

**On decline:** `−1` disposition between ronin and lord. No other consequence.

---

### Part E — Contract Terms on Acceptance

When `_process_contract_acceptance_writebacks()` fires:

1. `RoninSystem.accept_into_service()` is called (sets `lord_id`, `role_position`,
   restores status to 1.0 minimum, applies `HIRING_GLORY_RECOVERY = +0.3`).
2. `contract_end_ic_day = ic_day + (duration_seasons × SEASON_LENGTH_DAYS)` is
   stored in the ronin's `supply_ledger`.
3. The lord's koku is deducted by the total payment.
4. The lord assigns the contract-type NeedType as the ronin's primary objective
   (via `objectives_map`), with `source = "contract"` and `assigned_by = lord_id`.
5. `RoninSystem.record_income()` is called to reset the desperation timer.
6. `FIND_NEW_LORD` standing objective is erased from `objectives_map`.

---

### Part F — Contract Expiry

Each day, `_process_contract_expiry()` checks every employed character with
`supply_ledger.get("contract_end_ic_day", -1) >= 0` against `current_ic_day`.

When `contract_end_ic_day <= current_ic_day`:

**Clean completion** (ronin's primary objective has `source = "contract"` and
is in ACTIVE or COMPLETED state):
- `RoninSystem.make_ronin()` is called with cause `DISMISSAL` (formal release).
  Glory loss is `GLORY_LOSS_DISMISSAL = 0.3` per s52.5.
- `GLORY_CONTRACT_COMPLETION_BONUS = +0.5` applied before release. Net effect
  when starting from clean service: −0.3 dismissal + 0.5 bonus = +0.2 net.
- `supply_ledger["contract_deeds_for_family"][family_name] += 1` increments the
  deed counter for the lord's family (see s52.7 Part B).
- `contract_end_ic_day` key removed from `supply_ledger`.

**Abandoned** (ronin's primary objective was abandoned or replaced):
- `RoninSystem.make_ronin()` with cause `DISMISSAL` (no bonus).
- Lord disposition toward ronin: −5.
- No deed credit.

---

### Part G — Early Termination by Lord

The lord may terminate a contract before expiry using the civilian order action
`TERMINATE_CONTRACT` (0 AP, 1 civilian order). This requires no skill roll.

**On termination:**
- `RoninSystem.make_ronin()` with cause `DISMISSAL`.
- Half of remaining koku (rounded down) is returned to the lord.
- Lord disposition toward ronin: −3 (dismissing a contracted ally early is
  socially awkward but not shameful).
- No deed credit for the ronin.
