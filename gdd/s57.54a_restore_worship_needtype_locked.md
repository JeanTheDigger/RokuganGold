# s57.54a — RESTORE_WORSHIP NeedType Alignment Scores (LOCKED)

## Source

Section 57.54.10b specifies `SUPERNATURAL` topic category → `RESTORE_WORSHIP` NeedType
for `_build_local_tier3_candidates()`. The NeedType name is locked in §57.54.10b line 361,
but that section does not specify the objective_alignment.json action scores.

This addendum locks the action scores for `RESTORE_WORSHIP` as a NeedType.

## Anchor

GDD §57.54.10b (SUPERNATURAL | RESTORE_WORSHIP mapping) authorises this NeedType.
GDD §57.54.10a (RESTORE_WORSHIP conclusion → [PERFORM_RITUAL, BUILD_INFRASTRUCTURE,
GATHER_INTELLIGENCE]) informs which action categories are appropriate responses.
GDD §4.3.21 (Kami Worship Economy) defines worship acts as the primary resolution path
for fortune displeasure and worship failure.

## Action Scores

The RESTORE_WORSHIP NeedType represents a Family Daimyo responding to a local supernatural
crisis (realm overlap, fortune displeasure, elemental imbalance) by restoring worship
compliance. Calibrated against the PERFORM_RITUAL NeedType (100/90/60/30) and the
PATROL_PROVINCE NeedType (similar urgency scale).

| ActionID | Score | Rationale |
|----------|-------|-----------|
| PERFORM_WORSHIP | 90 | Primary act of worship; directly addresses fortune displeasure |
| PERFORM_RITUAL | 80 | Formal ritual appeasement; slightly below direct worship (ritual may serve other ends) |
| BUILD_SHRINE | 70 | Material commitment to the fortune; tangible long-term response |
| FOUND_TEMPLE | 60 | Larger worship infrastructure; stronger signal, higher cost |
| PURIFY_TAINTED_GROUND | 55 | Spiritual cleansing; most relevant for Shadowlands-origin supernatural events |
| ASSIGN_VASSAL_OBJECTIVE | 45 | Lord directing vassal shugenja; valid but indirect |
| MEDITATE | 35 | Baseline spiritual practice; less directly targeted than worship acts |
| FOUND_MONASTERY | 30 | Long-term investment; appropriate but indirect and slow |
| DO_NOTHING | 0 | Standard |
