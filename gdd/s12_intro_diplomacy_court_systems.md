# 12. Diplomacy & Court Systems

Court and diplomacy are as central to Rokugan as warfare. A courtier's failure can doom an entire clan just as surely as a lost battle. The political systems of the game operate across four distinct layers, each functioning at a different scale and intimacy level. These layers interact constantly — disposition shapes what happens in personal visits, personal visits build the relationships that determine who gets invited to formal courts, and what happens at formal courts ripples back into disposition across the entire political landscape.
