## 12.6a Deliberate PC-Initiated Conversation

Daily Conversation (Section 12.6) fires by probability — a player cannot count on it triggering with a specific target on a given IC day. Deliberate PC-Initiated Conversation is the player-only mechanic for seeking out a specific co-located named character and initiating conversation directly, bypassing the RNG. It models a samurai walking across the courtyard to speak with someone specifically rather than waiting to bump into them.

**Availability:** player characters only. NPCs interact with each other through the Daily Conversation RNG; they do not have access to this deliberate-initiation mechanic. The asymmetry is intentional — the NPC Decision Engine already models NPC social life through existing systems (Daily Conversation, visits, letters, court actions), and adding a deliberate-initiation option to NPCs would produce pathological behaviour (every NPC in a settlement walking up to every other NPC every day).

**Trigger conditions:** the PC and the target named character must be co-located in the same settlement (same castle, town, village, or waystation). Mutual disposition ≥ +11 (Acquaintance tier) is required — the same threshold as Daily Conversation. A PC cannot deliberately initiate conversation with a Stranger; they must first build acquaintance through formal social actions (CHARM, DELIVER_GIFT, PUBLIC_PERFORMANCE, etc.) to cross the +11 threshold, at which point organic conversation becomes available.

**Budget:** a PC may deliberately initiate up to 3 conversations per IC day. This budget is separate from the 5-per-IC-day cap on Daily Conversations (Section 12.6) — a PC could participate in up to 5 RNG-triggered Daily Conversations AND 3 deliberately-initiated conversations in the same IC day, for a combined maximum of 8 conversational exchanges per IC day. Across a full OOC day (4 IC days per Section 13), this permits up to 12 deliberate conversations alongside up to 20 RNG conversations. Value PROVISIONAL pending balancing.

**AP cost:** 0 AP. A deliberate-initiated conversation is a free interaction, consistent with Daily Conversation being a free passive mechanic. The budget of 3 per IC day is itself the throttle — no AP is additionally consumed.

**Resolution:** the conversation resolves using the standard Daily Conversation mechanics from Section 12.6. Each participant shares one topic from their active topic pool (weighted by personal relevance); if the topic is new to the recipient, it transfers; if already known, the topic’s momentum is refreshed. Both participants gain +1 mutual disposition per the standard drift. Strongly opinionated topic handling (Section 12.6 Opinion Shifting) applies identically. The mechanical outputs are identical to a Daily Conversation — the only difference is that the PC chose who to talk to rather than the RNG choosing.

**Target character’s cap interaction:** a deliberate-initiated conversation does NOT count against the target NPC’s Daily Conversation cap (N = 5). The target NPC’s RNG budget for that IC day is unchanged. This means a popular NPC who has already maxed their 5 RNG conversations for the day can still accept a deliberate PC initiation — they are available to be approached by a player even on a busy social day.

**Relationship to formal social actions:** deliberate-initiated conversation is distinct from formal social ActionIDs (CHARM, NEGOTIATE, PERSUADE, PROBE, READ_CHARACTER, etc., Section 14 Category 1 and Category 5). Those actions cost 1 AP each and produce mechanical effects beyond topic exchange — disposition shifts from contested rolls, intelligence extraction, emotion provocation. Deliberate conversation produces only the Daily Conversation effects: topic exchange and +1 disposition drift. A PC who wants to deliberately shift disposition toward a target still fires CHARM or PERSUADE at 1 AP; a PC who simply wants to exchange topics and pass time with an acquaintance fires deliberate-initiated conversation at 0 AP. The two mechanics are complementary, not substitutes.

**Player-to-player conversation:** when two player characters are co-located in the same settlement or the same ASCII map zone, they may converse freely through the chat interface without invoking any mechanic, AP cost, or budget. This is out-of-character roleplay flow, not a game-state event. Topic exchange between PCs still requires the Daily Conversation check or deliberate-initiation mechanic to register formally in the topic_ids system — the RP chat itself is narrative colour. A PC who RPs telling another PC about a secret through chat has not mechanically transferred the topic; they must either wait for Daily Conversation to fire with that topic selected, use a deliberate-initiation slot, or use REVEAL_SECRET_PRIVATE (1 AP) to formalise the transfer. This preserves the integrity of the topic propagation system while allowing unlimited narrative dialogue.
