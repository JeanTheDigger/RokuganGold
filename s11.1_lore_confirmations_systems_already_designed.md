## 11.1 Lore Confirmations — Systems Already Designed

- The hypocrisy theme is canon. Chapter Six opens with a sensei explicitly dismantling the fiction that samurai do not care about money, calling it 'a polite fiction.' Our resource system is lore-accurate.

- Tax in Rice is confirmed. The book states taxes were paid in rice at 40–50% of harvest historically. Our cascade structure is faithful.

- 1 Koku = 1 year's rice for 1 person is confirmed. The book states explicitly that 'the value of the Empire's coinage is in theory based on rice.' Our core equation is canon.

- The Crab's food dependency is confirmed. The Wall garrison is described as a 'perpetual drain' that means the Crab barely feed themselves even in good years. Our mountain terrain modifier is lore-faithful.
