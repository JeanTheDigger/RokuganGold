## 16.1 Crisis Momentum Scale — LOCKED

Every active crisis has a momentum value from 0 to 100. Momentum accumulates each daily tick the crisis remains unresolved. Once it reaches 100 it stays there until the crisis is addressed. Resolving a crisis resets momentum to 0.

**Momentum Thresholds — LOCKED:**

- 0–10: Rumor — barely noticed, not yet a formal topic.

- 11–25: Minor Topic — discussed at provincial courts, mentioned at higher ones.

- 26–50: Secondary Topic — present at Clan Champion courts, secondary at Winter Court.

- 51–75: Major Topic — dominates Clan Champion courts, prominent at Winter Court.

- 76–100: Unavoidable Crisis — the main topic of Imperial Winter Court, cannot be ignored by anyone.

**Momentum Gain Per Daily Tick — LOCKED:**

- Tier 1 crisis (supernatural/existential): +3 momentum per daily tick.

- Tier 2 crisis (major political/military): +2 momentum per daily tick.

- Tier 3 crisis (local/regional): +1 momentum per daily tick.

**Scope Multiplier — LOCKED:**

- 1 province affected: ×1.

- 2–3 provinces affected: ×1.5.

- 4–6 provinces affected: ×2.

- 7+ provinces affected: ×3.

- Example: A Tier 2 war affecting 5 provinces gains +2 × 2 = +4 momentum per daily tick. It reaches Major Topic status in roughly 13 real-world days.

- Example: A Tier 1 Maho cult just discovered in 1 province gains +3 × 1 = +3 momentum per daily tick. Its nature makes it alarming immediately regardless of small scope.
