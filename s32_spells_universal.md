# 32. Spells — Universal

Universal spells may be cast in any of the four standard elements (not Void) unless otherwise noted. They are available to all shugenja regardless of Affinity or Deficiency.

**UNIVERSAL SPELLS:**

**Sense [CR]**

- Ring/Mastery: All 1 | Range: Personal | Area of Effect: 50’ radius | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+10’)

Sense the presence, quantity, and rough location of elemental spirits (not kansen) of the chosen element within range. Most commonly used to locate spirits for Commune, but also functions as a basic location device — e.g. casting Sense (Water) while lost in the wilderness to locate a water source.

**Commune [CR]**

- Ring/Mastery: All 1 | Range: 20’ | Area of Effect: Self | Duration: Concentration | Raises: Additional questions (1 per Raise), Clarity of answers

Speak with one local elemental kami, asking questions it will answer honestly to the best of its ability. Typically invokes the most active spirit of the chosen element in the area; if all local spirits are quiescent, the GM may require 1–2 Raises to wake one. Base effect: 2 questions. Raise for additional questions (1 per Raise) or for clarity (kami notoriously struggle to comprehend human behavior and context).

- Air spirits: Playful and easily distracted. Convey information as emotions, riddles, or jokes. More interested in feelings than facts. Communing with Air can be frustrating.

- Earth spirits: Straightforward and blunt but often uninterested in mortal behavior. Poor understanding of human emotion. Tend to focus on obscure details (color of clothing, weight of a horse).

- Fire spirits: Irritable and temperamental. Often angry at being summoned unless propitiated with something to burn. When pleased, provide the clearest and most accurate information.

- Water spirits: Communicate through soundless visual images — helpful for investigating past incidents, but cannot convey scent, sound, or emotion, making their information potentially incomplete or misleading.

Note: Spirits remember everything but do not experience time as mortals do. Asking about events from long ago usually requires Raises to make the caster’s wishes clear.

**Summon [CR]**

- Ring/Mastery: All 1 | Range: 30’ | Area of Effect: 1 cubic foot | Duration: Permanent | Raises: Range (+10’), Quantity (+1 cubic foot), Composition (1–4 Raises for specific type)

Summon a modest quantity (1 cubic foot) of the chosen element. Appears in any open space within range — cannot be placed inside another physical object or living creature. Summoned material behaves normally: earth falls, water soaks, air blows away, fire winks out without fuel.

Generally cannot be used effectively in combat, though clever shugenja may find modest uses (e.g. Summon (Fire) to ignite a fuel-soaked foe). More commonly used for practical purposes: summoning water in a desert, lighting a campfire. Raises may summon specific subtypes (wood or iron for Earth, tea for Water — GM sets cost at 1–4 Raises). Cannot create rare or precious materials (gold) or spiritually powerful substances (jade, crystal).

**Command**

- Ring/Mastery: All 1 | Range: 30’ | Area of Effect: 1 cubic foot | Duration: Permanent | Raises: Area (+1 cubic foot), Range (+10’), Special

A controversial spell devised by Iuchi during the Ki-Rin Clan’s exile. Rather than entreating elemental spirits, it commands the Elements directly. Outside Rokugan, where kami were quiet or silent, this was highly effective. Inside the Empire it brings the wrath of the spirits.

Effect: Create a quantity of the element (as Summon) or reshape an existing quantity — e.g. command a stone table to break or become round. GM may require Raises for complex shapes.

- Penalty within Rokugan: Every use increases all Spell Casting Rolls in the commanded element by +5 TN for the next 24 hours.

- Importuning: May replace the Commune roll when Importuning. The Spellcraft roll succeeds automatically, but the TN penalty for casting spells of that element becomes +10 (instead of +5) for 24 hours, including the spell being Importuned.

**Transmute**

- Ring/Mastery: Any non-Void 2 | Range: 10’ | Area of Effect: One object up to 10 cubic feet | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+10’), Area (+5 cubic feet), Additional Element (+2 Raises per extra element)

A product of Agasha’s deep study of the Elements and their interconnected nature. Taught secretly among the highest ranks of the Agasha and Tamori schools — normally only available to shugenja of Rank 5 or higher, though Agasha herself has been known to bestow it on favored descendants.

Effect: Transform the Elements within a single inanimate physical object into other elements. Example: a wooden table (Earth) could become Air, Water, or Fire. Cannot affect Void. Cannot affect living creatures, complex structures, or objects whose mass exceeds the area of effect.

- Multiple elements: For an object made of multiple elements or to transmute into multiple elements, Raise twice per additional element involved. Example: a lit candle (Earth + Fire) costs 2 Raises to become a single element; a wooden table (Earth) becoming Fire + Air also costs 2 Raises.

- GM notes: The more complex and sophisticated the object, the more elements it contains. The daisho (katana and wakizashi) are believed to contain all five elements including Void and are immune to this spell.
