## 57.41 Engineering

The Engineering skill is already plumbed into existing systems: ORDER_FORTIFY (Category 7, Engineering secondary), FORTIFY_WALL_SECTION and SEAL_WALL_BREACH on the Kaiu Wall (Section 11.7), siege system events (Section 11.7), the Kaiu Engineer school techniques (Section 29), and the Akodo Siege Strategist and Kaiu Siegemaster alternate paths (Section 29). This section formalizes the two points not handled elsewhere: the Rank 5 Mastery Ability, and the standing Kaiu Engineer NPC behaviour.

### 57.41.1 Rank 5 Mastery

Per Section 24.3, a character with Engineering rank 5 or higher gains +5 to the total of any Engineering Skill Roll that is part of a Cooperative (multiple characters contributing to one task) or Cumulative (multi-session progress track) roll. This applies to FORTIFY_WALL_SECTION, SEAL_WALL_BREACH, Kaiu Path of Stone structural reinforcement, Field Fortification (Section 11.7 battle opportunity), and any joint engineering effort with two or more Engineering contributors. The bonus does NOT apply to single-roll single-character checks — a one-off Engineering roll receives no Rank 5 bonus. The bonus stacks with school-granted bonuses such as The Kaiu Method (+1k0 to School Skills). The document does not yet define a generalized Cooperative Skill Roll framework; until it does, the Rank 5 bonus applies case-by-case to the explicitly cooperative or cumulative Engineering activities listed above.

### 57.41.2 Kaiu Engineer NPC Standing Need

NPCs in the Kaiu Engineer school have a standing ImmediateNeed to maintain the structural integrity of fortifications in their assigned province or Wall Tower. When SI falls below threshold (default: SI 7 for Wall Towers, SI 8 for castle walls, SI 6 for provincial fortifications), FORTIFY_WALL_SECTION fires at priority 2. If SI reaches 0 (breach), priority escalates to 1 and SEAL_WALL_BREACH fires with urgency. The need clears when SI is restored to threshold. Non-Kaiu NPCs with Engineering skill do not fire the action spontaneously — they require a lord directive (ORDER_FORTIFY from their lord, or ASSIGN_VASSAL_OBJECTIVE routing them to the work). Engineering actions score under MAINTAIN_FORTIFICATION (Section 57.8.1): FORTIFY_WALL_SECTION 90, SEAL_WALL_BREACH 100, ORDER_FORTIFY 85.
