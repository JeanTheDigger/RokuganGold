## 55.8 Investigation & Patrol Actions

**STATUS:** LOCKED

Solves: “Bushi lord hears rumors of brigands but has no way to investigate.” Connects to the Insurgency System (GDD Section 11.11).

### 55.8.1 New NeedTypes

**INVESTIGATE_THREAT:** NPC holds a detection hint topic but no confirmed crisis. Must find out what’s happening.

**PATROL_PROVINCE:** Province stability below 75, no specific crisis. Preventive action.

### 55.8.2 New Actions

**INVESTIGATE_PROVINCE (1 AP):** Triggers Section 11.11 Phase 3 detection roll. Requires physical presence. Roll: Intelligence + Investigation vs Concealment × 5. Success: insurgency detected, crisis generated. Failure: concealment +1. If no insurgency exists: province confirmed clear, confidence reset to FRESH.

**INVESTIGATE_RUMOR (1 AP):** Evaluate a Tier 4 topic’s credibility. Roll: Perception + Investigation vs TN 20. Success: learn whether rumor is substantiated.

**ORDER_PATROL (1 Civilian or Military Order, lord-tier — Section 57.34):** Assign a unit to patrol for one season. Halves insurgency spawn chance, reduces insurgency Concealment by −1, reduces bandit encounter chance from 15% to 5%.

### 55.8.3 Decomposition: INVESTIGATE_THREAT

Decision tree: Am I in the target province? No → TRAVEL_TO. Yes + not yet investigated this season → INVESTIGATE_PROVINCE. Already investigated → PATROL_PROVINCE or wait.

### 55.8.4 The Delegation Pattern

A courtier daimyo (low Investigation skill) will score ASSIGN_VASSAL_OBJECTIVE higher than INVESTIGATE_PROVINCE (competence modifier steers them away). They delegate to a bushi vassal via Information Transfer (Section 6). The vassal’s engine decomposes to TRAVEL_TO → INVESTIGATE_PROVINCE. This emerges from the scoring formula, not special-case code.
