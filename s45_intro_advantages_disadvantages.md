# 45. Advantages & Disadvantages

Advantages are purchased with Experience Points during character creation or play. Disadvantages award Experience Points and impose ongoing penalties or complications. Point costs are listed after each name. Clan-specific purchase discounts are noted in each entry.

**ADVANTAGES:**

**Absolute Direction [Mental] (1 pt) [CR]**

Always know which direction is north, no matter the circumstances. Does not function if more than one day's travel inside the Shadowlands.

**Allies [Social] (Variable) [CR]**

Carefully nurtured social connections. Total cost = Influence + Devotion. Influence: 1 pt (minor, no land or appointments), 2 pts (moderate, some land or political influence), 4 pts (major, vast lands or prestigious appointments). Devotion: 1 pt (would help but not risk honor), 2 pts (would risk honor secretly), 4 pts (would do anything regardless of cost). Crane characters pay 1 pt less.

**Balance [Mental] (2 pts) [CR]**

Inherent calm and serenity. When adding Honor Rank to rolls to resist Intimidation or Temptation, gain an additional +1k0.

**Blackmail [Social] (Variable) [CR]**

Extremely unremarkable; others have difficulty recognizing you. May voluntarily increase by 10 the TN of any roll to determine your Honor or identify you by Glory.

**Dark Paragon [Mental] (5 pts) [CR]**

**Spy Network [Social] (8 pts) [EoE]**

Access to an intelligence network. Once per OOC week, the spymaster designates one focus target from three categories. The network produces intelligence automatically each tick during the week based on the chosen focus. Daidoji pay 7 pts, Scorpion pay 6 pts, Kolat pay 5 pts.

Character Focus: The network watches a specific named character. Each tick, one previously unknown fact about that character is revealed to the spymaster. Candidates include: current primary objective, a hidden disposition value toward a specific person, a concealed Disadvantage or Dark Secret, their actual Honor Rank (vs. perceived), or their current location. The engine selects the most relevant unrevealed fact based on the spymaster’s own active objective. Once all discoverable facts are exhausted, subsequent ticks yield no new information and the spymaster should reassign focus.

Place Focus: The network monitors a specific location (province, city, court, or castle). During the OOC week, any Tier 3 or Tier 4 topic generated at that location that the spymaster does not already know is revealed to them. One topic per tick, prioritized by recency. If no unknown topics were generated at that location during the week, no intelligence is produced.

Army Focus: The network tracks a specific enemy army. Once during the OOC week, the spymaster receives the army’s most recent known position on the world map, its approximate size (PU count), and the identity of its commander. If the army moved during the week, the position reported is the most recent one the network observed. Army focus does not reveal army composition, equipment tier, or morale — only position, size, and commander.

NPC Trigger Rule: An NPC with Spy Network selects a focus target aligned with their current primary objective. Political objectives default to Character Focus on the most relevant rival or target. Military objectives default to Army Focus on the nearest or most threatening enemy force. Economic and territorial objectives default to Place Focus on the relevant province or trade hub. The focus is reassigned automatically when the NPC’s primary objective changes.
