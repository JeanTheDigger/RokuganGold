## 57.12 ActionID Naming Reconciliation --- LOCKED

The gap sections (55.21.x) introduced ActionID names that differ from the Section 14 Consolidated Action Reference in some cases. The following reconciliations apply when transcribing alignment entries:

STORM_ASSAULT (gap section) = CONDUCT_STORM_ASSAULT (Section 14). Use CONDUCT_STORM_ASSAULT.

ORDER_SORTIE (gap section) = CONDUCT_SORTIE (Section 14). Use CONDUCT_SORTIE.

OFFER_TERMS (gap section) = NEGOTIATE_SURRENDER (Section 14). Use NEGOTIATE_SURRENDER.

DEPLOY_ARMY (gap section) = ORDER_DEPLOY (Section 14). Use ORDER_DEPLOY.

LEVY_TROOPS (gap section) = ORDER_LEVY (Section 14). Use ORDER_LEVY.

New ActionIDs introduced in gap sections that need addition to the Section 14 master list, action_skill_map.json (Annex D), and context generators: ISSUE_DUEL_CHALLENGE, SEEK_PRETEXT (ActionID), DRILL_TROOPS, FORCE_MARCH, EVALUATE_CLAN_STRENGTH, COMMISSION_ASSASSINATION, EXECUTE_ASSASSINATION, ABORT_OPERATION, BLOCKADE_TRADE_ROUTE (ActionID). ESTABLISH_TRADE_ROUTE removed (v559d). Skill mappings for most are defined in their respective gap sections.
