## 22.1 Two Types of Named NPCs — LOCKED

**Type 1 — Canonical Characters:**

Named characters who exist in L5R lore. Pre-built at game start with fixed attributes sourced from sourcebook material — Toturi, Doji Hoturi, Bayushi Kachiko, and others. Their Ring values, skills, personality, history, and relationships are set at game start. They do not need generation — they need faithful implementation.

- Canonical characters occupy the highest-profile positions at game start — Clan Champions, family daimyo, major Imperial figures.

- Their attributes, personality virtues, and starting disposition values are derived from lore rather than generated.

- When a canonical character dies, their position is filled by a procedurally generated replacement — the world does not generate another canonical character.

**Type 2 — Procedurally Generated Characters:**

Fill all roles that canonical characters do not occupy — the thousands of Gunso, Chui, provincial daimyo, temple keepers, courtiers, and magistrates that populate the world. Generated at game start using templates, and again when positions become vacant.

- Generated using templates based on Clan + Family + School + Insight Rank combinations.

- At game start the world is populated with generated characters at various Insight Ranks to fill all necessary positions.

- After game start, new characters only enter the world at Insight Rank 1. No new high-Rank characters appear from nowhere.
