## 27.1 The Crab Clan

Creed: 'Knowledge, Preparation, Action.' Founded by Hida, tasked by Emperor Hantei to defend the Empire against the Shadowlands after the First War. The Crab have effectively spent their entire existence at war — at least one Crab samurai has died defending the Empire every day since the clan's founding. They are perpetually exhausted, perpetually underappreciated, and perpetually necessary. Most of their military strength is tied down on the Kaiu Wall, leaving them vulnerable within the Empire to political and economic maneuvering by rivals.

- Clan identity: Pragmatic, crude by Imperial standards, deeply honorable in their own way. They believe sacrifices must be made for duty. They respect strength and perseverance above all.

- Key historical events: The First Yasuki War with the Crane (Yasuki defection to Crab); the Maw's attack and construction of the Kaiu Wall; Kisada's ill-fated alliance with the Shadowlands; recovery of Shiro Hiruma; Daigotsu's assault on the Wall; the Second and Third Yasuki Wars.

- Inter-clan relations: Chronic enemies of the Crane (Yasuki dispute, territorial rivalry). Grudging respect for Lion military strength. Cordial with Scorpion on maho-hunting matters (Kuni-Kuroiban cooperation). Contemptuous of clans that don't appreciate the Wall's necessity.

**The Hida Family:**

Motto: 'I will not fail.' The undisputed leaders of the Crab Clan — the largest family in the clan and one of the largest in the Empire. Strategists, officers, and rank-and-file warriors. The popular image of a hulking brute is partly cultivated by the Yasuki. In truth the Hida are far from stupid — some of the Empire's greatest military strategists have been Hida. Deeply respectful of mothers. Training is brutal, requiring students to enter the Shadowlands and return with a creature's head before graduating.

**The Hiruma Family:**

Motto: 'A single fire against the darkness always burns brighter.' Swift, agile scouts and yojimbo — smaller and more nimble than Hida warriors. Lost Hiruma Castle to the Maw, spent three centuries training with Shinjo scouts, developed new techniques. Famous for their powers of observation and silence. Notable for producing a disproportionate number of berserkers during the centuries without a home.

**The Kaiu Family:**

Motto: 'The strength of the mountain lies at its base.' The best engineers in the Empire without dispute. Built the framework of the Kaiu Wall in 73 days. Also skilled smiths crafting the finest weapons and armor. Known for meticulous testing, elaborate siege engines, cunning traps — and remarkably, for making the Empire's finest toys as stress relief.

**The Kuni Family:**

Motto: 'Sometimes you have to look into the darkness to bring forth the light.' The clan's shugenja and researchers — study Shadowlands creatures, maho, and the nature of oni so the clan is always prepared. Highest incidence of madness of any family in the Empire. Conduct strange experiments, dissect living Shadowlands creatures. Known for their distinctive face paint applied each morning. Maintain the Witch-Hunters who roam the Empire hunting maho. On good terms with the Scorpion Kuroiban.

**The Toritaka Family:**

Motto: 'There are not enough names.' Originally the Falcon Clan, absorbed by the Crab in 1125 after being devastated by the Shuten Doji. Experts in detecting and defeating evil spirits and ghosts. Also some of the finest falconers in the Empire — they use trained birds as extra sets of eyes that can detect unnatural things.

**The Yasuki Family:**

Motto: 'May your cup always be full and your hands never be empty.' Originally a Crane family that defected to the Crab during the First Yasuki War. The clan's merchants and diplomats — make sure supplies and political support flow to the Wall. Some of the finest merchants in the Empire. Chronic bad blood with the Crane. Sometimes spread rumors that Hida are stupider than they are, which is actually useful.
