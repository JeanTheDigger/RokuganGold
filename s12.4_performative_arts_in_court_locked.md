## 12.4 Performative Arts in Court — LOCKED

A formal court is not merely a series of conversations and negotiations. It is a full cultural experience. A courtier who performs a tea ceremony, recites a poem, stages a music performance, or presents an art piece is performing a political act. Successful performances enhance the performer's social standing, affect disposition across the room, and can shift the political atmosphere of an entire court session.

**Performative Arts (framework):**

- Performances are court actions — a player character or NPC can spend their court time performing rather than negotiating.

- Successful performances raise the performer's Honor and Glory and improve disposition with those who witnessed and appreciated them.

- The quality of the performance is determined by the relevant skill roll (Artisan, Performance, Tea Ceremony, Poetry, etc.) against a TN determined by the audience's taste and the cultural context.

- A truly masterful performance can shift the political atmosphere of a court session — making other characters more receptive, softening tensions, or embarrassing a rival who cannot match it.

- Each clan has distinct tastes — a Lion court values martial themes and historical epics, a Crane court values refinement and elegance, a Crab court tolerates kabuki but finds noh boring.

- Performances can be offered as gifts — commissioning or personally performing for a specific lord is a form of gift-giving with its own disposition effects.

✓ DONE — Court performance mechanics fully designed across two sections. Public Performance (broadcast, 1 AP): Roll relevant Artisan/Performance Skill + Awareness vs TN 15. Skill by art form: poetry = Awareness + Artisan (Poetry), dance = Awareness + Perform (Dance), theater = Awareness + Acting, music = Awareness + Perform (instrument), tea ceremony = Awareness + Tea Ceremony. Success: +2 disposition all witnesses, +0.3 Glory. Each Raise: +1 disposition per witness. Critical failure: −2 disposition all witnesses, −0.3 Glory. Performance fatigue: diminishing returns on repeat performances by the same performer at the same court within an OOC day (full → half → zero). Venue requirement: PUBLIC_PERFORMANCE validator requires zone flagged performance_permitted: true. Perform Personally For (targeted, 1 AP): same roll vs TN 15. Success: strong disposition gain with recipient, +0.2 Glory on masterful success. Failure: small disposition loss. PERFORM_FOR exempt from fatigue and venue restrictions. Both actions fully detailed in Section 15.4. Glory values in Section 4.6. Clan taste modifiers are narrative flavour only — no mechanical TN adjustment by audience clan.
