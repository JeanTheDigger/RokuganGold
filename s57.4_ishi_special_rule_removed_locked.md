## 57.4 Ishi _SPECIAL_RULE Removed --- LOCKED

SUPERSEDES: The _SPECIAL_RULE entry in Annex B (personality_lean.json) under ISHI. In-place edit applied --- rule replaced with explanatory note.

**Problem: **The rule ("Lean +15 toward continuation of last action type. Lean -10 toward any action representing a change in approach.") required action history memory, an action type taxonomy, and a definition of "change in approach" --- none of which exist in the stateless engine architecture.

**Resolution: **Ishi stubbornness is already captured by three existing mechanisms: (1) Strong negative personality leans on pivoting actions (SEEK_PEACE -25, NEGOTIATE -8, REQUEST_ALLIED_AID -15). (2) Hard personality filters blocking SURRENDER, _CHANGE_COURSE, SEEK_PEACE, NEGOTIATE when committed. (3) Explicit exemption from approach_ineffective and approach_capped penalties in the Approach Evaluation System (Section 55.30.3).

**New Ishi exemption in Section 55.30.3: **NPCs whose Shourido virtue is Ishi do not receive the approach_ineffective or approach_capped penalties. The measurement_needed bonus still fires --- Ishi NPCs can measure, they just ignore what they learn. This makes Ishi the only virtue that continues a failed approach indefinitely, which is the correct characterization of Will.
