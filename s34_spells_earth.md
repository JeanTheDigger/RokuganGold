# 34. Spells — Earth

**MASTERY LEVEL 1:**

**Armor of Earth [CR]**

- Earth 1 (Battle, Defense) | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 10 rounds | Raises: Duration (+2 rounds)

Infuses the caster's body with Earth, granting Reduction equal to Earth Ring + School Rank against all physical and magical attacks. Water is considered 1 Rank lower for movement while the spell is active.

**Courage of the Seven Thunders [CR]**

- Earth 1 (Battle) | Range: 30' | Area: Up to caster's School Rank targets | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Duration (+1 minute), Targets (+1)

Infuses targets (may include caster) with unyielding Earth-borne courage. Targets gain +5k0 to resist any Fear effect, magical or natural. Samurai not of the original Seven Great Clans receive +3k0 instead. Anyone with at least 1 full Rank of Taint cannot benefit (but this does not reveal their Tainted nature). May be cast as a combined ritual by multiple shugenja who know the spell, adding their highest single Earth Rank to combined School Ranks for determining targets.

**Earth's Stagnation [CR]**

- Earth 1 | Range: 50' | Area: One target | Duration: 6 rounds | Raises: Duration (+2 rounds), Range (+10'), Targets (+1, max 4 total)

Calls on the Earth in the target's body to weigh them down. Target suffers -2k0 to all Agility Trait rolls and Water Ring is considered 1 Rank lower for movement distance.

**Earth's Touch [CR]**

- Earth 1 (Defense) | Range: Touch | Area: One target | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+1/2 hour), Targets (+1 per 2 Raises, max 3 total)

Invokes Earth kami to strengthen the target's health and mental fortitude. One of the target's Earth Traits (caster's choice) is increased by 1 for the duration. Does not increase the Ring itself.

**Elemental Ward [CR]**

- Earth 1 (Wards) | Range: Touch | Area: One target | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+1/2 hour), Targets (+1 per 2 Raises)

Earth spirits armor the target's body and soul against one chosen element (not Void or Maho). Spells of that element suffer a penalty equal to caster's School Rank ×5 when cast on the protected target. This includes friendly spells of that element such as healing.

**Jade Strike [CR]**

- Earth 1 (Jade, Thunder) | Range: 100' | Area: One target | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Damage (+1k0), Range (+10'), Targets (+1, max 5 total)

Summons jade Earth kami as a blast of iridescent green energy that unerringly strikes the target (cannot be intercepted, though Magic Resistance and magical defenses can thwart it). Inflicts DR 3k3 only against targets with at least 1 Rank of Taint — non-Tainted targets suffer no damage. Casting on a non-Tainted target is generally considered a grave insult (excepting perhaps the more paranoid Kuni).

**Jurojin's Balm [CR]**

- Earth 1 | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+1/2 hour), Targets (+1 per 2 Raises, max 5 total)

Fills the target with the purity and vigor of Earth, driving out poisons. If the target suffers any poison or toxin during the duration (or is already affected), they may re-roll any failed Stamina roll to resist it with +2k0 on the second roll. If the second roll also fails, the poison has full effect. Side effect: cures drunkenness and prevents intoxication for the duration.

**Minor Binding [CR]**

- Earth 1 (Craft) | Range: 60' | Area: 1 target | Duration: 2 hours | Raises: Duration (+1 hour), Range (+20')

Calls forth manacles of iron formed from pure Earth spirits to safely imprison a minor Shadowlands creature (Earth 3 or less, must be Tainted). Cannot affect the Lost, Oni Lords or their spawn, or creatures with Earth 4+. Target is rendered physically helpless for the duration. Manacles crumble instantly to dust when the spell expires.

**Soul of Stone [CR]**

- Earth 1 (Defense) | Range: Touch | Area: One target | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+1/2 hour)

Fills the target's soul with the unyielding strength of stone. Gains +3k0 to resist emotional manipulation, distractions of desire, Courtier (Manipulation) rolls, Temptation, Compulsions, and similar effects. Also suffers -1k0 to all Awareness Trait rolls and Awareness-related Skill Rolls made to influence others.

**Stone's Endurance**

- Earth 1 (Travel) | Range: Self or Touch | Area: One target creature | Duration: 6 hours | Raises: Duration (+1 hour per Raise)

Calls on the strength of stone to enhance the target's physical endurance. Target is immune to Fatigue from lack of rest and is considered to have Stamina 1 Rank higher for rolls or effects specifically keying on Stamina (poison resistance, drowning duration, etc). Cannot sustain beyond 24 consecutive hours of use. When the spell expires, the target is automatically Fatigued until resting for at least half the time spent under the spell.

**Tetsubo of Earth [CR]**

- Earth 1 (Craft, Jade) | Range: Personal or 20' | Area: One created weapon | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Damage (+1k0), Duration (+5 minutes), Range (+5')

Summons a tetsubo of pure earth studded with stones. DR 2k2. Default form is a tetsubo; 1 Raise changes it to any other heavy weapon. Caster may use School Rank in place of Heavy Weapons Skill. If the caster has Heavy Weapons Skill, grants one Free Raise on Knockdown maneuver (does not apply if granted to an ally). Disappears if lost from hand. May appear in an ally's hands within 20' — ally treated as caster but without the Free Raise bonus.

**MASTERY LEVEL 2:**

**Be the Mountain [CR]**

- Earth 2 (Defense) | Range: 30' | Area: 1 target creature | Duration: 4 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round)

Earth kami cover the willing ally target with a stony barrier. Target gains Reduction equal to 5 × caster's School Rank (maximum 20) but cannot take Simple Move Actions for the duration. Free Move Actions are still permitted.

**Earth Becomes Sky [CR]**

- Earth 2 (Jade, Thunder) | Range: 100' | Area: 1 target creature | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Damage (+1k0), Targets (+1), Special (make boulders Jade for 2 Raises)

Summons huge boulders and hurls them to unerringly strike one or more targets. DR equals the caster's Earth Ring against each target. If striking multiple targets, DR is reduced by 1k1 per additional target (minimum 1k1). Boulders are normal mundane stone and cannot bypass Reduction or Invulnerability unless infused with Jade (2 Raises).

**Embrace of Kenro-Ji-Jin [CR]**

- Earth 2 (Travel) | Range: Personal or Touch | Area: 1 target creature | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+1/2 hour)

The target may literally dive into the ground, which becomes traversable as easily as water. Can see 100 yards through the earth, move in any direction at normal speed through earth. Cannot attack, cast spells, speak, hear, or interact with those outside while underground. Emerging immediately ends the spell. If the spell ends while still underground, the Earth spirits expel the target to the nearest open air.

**Force of Will [CR]**

- Earth 2 (Battle) | Range: 50' | Area: 1 target | Duration: 2 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round, max 4 rounds)

Earth kami bolster the target's will to live to supernatural levels. Target is immune to all Wound Rank penalties and effects for the duration — including the effect of being dead if the Out rank is filled. When the spell expires, full Wound effects apply immediately. Typically provides a brief window for healing or a final battle in the face of certain death.

**Grasp of Earth [CR]**

- Earth 2 | Range: 50' | Area: 1 target | Duration: 5 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round), Range (+5'), Targets (+1 per 2 Raises)

Earth kami seize the target in a stony grip. Target can only move 3' per round with a Simple Move Action and cannot move at all with a Free Move Action. May break free with a Complex Action and a Raw Strength roll (TN = 5 × caster's Earth).

**Hands of Clay [CR]**

- Earth 2 (Travel) | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Duration (+5 minutes)

Earth spirits infuse the caster's hands and feet, allowing them to merge with wood, earth, and stone. Caster can walk and climb along sheer surfaces at half normal movement speed. Can hang from ceilings and move along them (3' per Simple Action). When the spell ends the effect concludes without warning, potentially causing a fall.

**Jurojin's Curse**

- Earth 2 | Range: 30' | Area: One target creature | Duration: 1 day | Raises: Range (+10' per Raise), Area (+1 creature per 2 Raises), Duration (+1 day per 2 Raises)

Removes all protection of Jurojin (Fortune of Health and Longevity) from the target. For the duration, the target's Earth is considered 3 Ranks lower (minimum 1) for healing injuries and resisting disease or poison.

**Rites of Preservation**

- Earth 2 (Imperial) | Range: 10' | Area: One human corpse | Duration: 1 day | Raises: Duration (+1 day per 3 Raises)

A 1-hour ritual that entreats Earth kami to protect a corpse against putrefaction and unclean influences, keeping it pristine until cremation. The blessed corpse will not decay for the duration and is immune to being animated by maho or the Taint. Considered an honorable religious duty for shugenja despite its association with dead flesh.

**Taming the Beast**

- Earth 2 | Range: 50' | Area: One natural creature | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Area (+1 creature per 2 Raises), Duration (+1/2 hour per Raise)

A Kitsune family secret, thus far never taught outside their ranks. Earth kami soothe a wild animal's spirit, causing it to become temporarily tame. Caster makes a Contested Earth roll (Free Action); on success, the creature ceases all hostile activity and may be given simple commands via Animal Handling (GM sets TN by command complexity). Does not work on spirits, supernatural creatures, or Tainted creatures. Repeated castings may permanently tame the animal (GM's discretion).

**The Mountain's Feet [CR]**

- Earth 2 (Defense) | Range: Personal or 20' | Area: 1 target | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+1/2 hour), Targets (+1 per 2 Raises)

Roots the target's feet to the Earth, granting +3k0 to resist all Knockdown maneuvers. Any spell seeking to knock down, lift, or otherwise break the target's connection to the Earth must win a Contested roll of its Element against the caster's Earth.

**Wholeness of the World [CR]**

- Earth 2 (Defense) | Range: Personal or 20' | Area: 1 target creature | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Duration (+1 minute), Range (+5'), Targets (+1 per 2 Raises)

Infuses the target with Earth so thoroughly that their Rings and Traits become wholly resistant to any effort to change them. Any physical or magical effect that would raise or lower the target's Traits or Rings is completely ineffective for the duration.

**Whispers of the Land**

- Earth 2 | Range: Centered on caster | Area: 50' radius | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Area (+50' radius per Raise), Duration (+5 minutes per Raise), Special (+1 day of tracks per 2 Raises)

Earth spirits reveal the tracks of any person who has walked on them within the last 3 days. The surface (stone, earth, or wood) literally pushes up in the shape of the tracks, creating a clearly visible path. Unlike Air revelation spells, this spell also reveals normal unconcealed tracks too faint or old to normally be seen (e.g. servant footprints on a dojo floor). Devised by the Kitsune; now also used by the Yogo and Kuni.

**MASTERY LEVEL 3:**

**Bonds of Ningen-Do [CR]**

- Earth 3 (Wards) | Range: 500' | Area: 1 target spirit creature | Duration: 30 days | Raises: Duration (+1 day), Range (+10'), Targets (+1 per 2 Raises)

A powerful binding ritual (10 minutes to cast, reduced by 1 minute per assisting shugenja, minimum 1 minute). Affects creatures from Sakkaku, Chikushudo, Gaki-Do, Toshigoku, or Yume-Do. If bound: creature must obey the caster's commands for the duration (suicidal commands break the spell). If dispelled: creature immediately leaves Ningen-Do for its native realm and cannot return until duration ends. Either way, the creature regards the caster as an enemy afterward.

**Earth Kami's Blessing [CR]**

- Earth 3 (Battle) | Range: Personal or 20' | Area: Caster or 1 target creature | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Duration (+1 minute)

Infuses the target with the strength and resilience of Earth. Target gains +2 Wounds per Wound Rank and +1k1 on all rolls involving the Earth Ring and its associated Traits. When the spell expires, all Wounds suffered remain — this may drive the target into a lower Wound Rank or kill them.

**Earth's Protection [CR]**

- Earth 3 (Defense, Wards) | Range: Personal | Area: 10' radius from caster | Duration: Concentration | Raises: Area (+5' radius per 2 Raises, max 30' radius), Special (damage reduced by additional +1k1 per 2 Raises)

Earth spirits protect the area around the caster from Air, Fire, and Water spells. Any hostile Air, Fire, or Water spell cast within the area (or extending effects into it) suffers +10 TN to the Spell Casting Roll. Damage those spells inflict on creatures within Earth's Protection is reduced by 1k1 (minimum 1k1).

**Earthen Wave**

- Earth 3 | Range: Self | Area: 10' × 50' | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Area (+5' to either dimension per Raise)

Sends a rolling shockwave through the ground in a chosen direction. All creatures in the area must make a Contested Strength vs caster's Earth Ring roll or be knocked Prone as if struck by a Knockdown maneuver. Creatures with more than two legs gain 2 Free Raises on this roll.

**Groves of Stone [CR]**

- Earth 3 (Craft, Defense) | Range: Centered on caster | Area: 15' radius circular barrier | Duration: 10 Rounds | Raises: Area (+5' radius per 2 Raises), Duration (+2 Rounds per Raise), Special (+5 TN to climb per Raise)

Erupts huge stones from the ground to create a circular barrier around the caster. Cannot be broken through without a siege engine (500 Wounds, Reduction 15 vs siege). Can be clambered over with a Complex Action and an Athletics/Strength roll vs caster's Earth ×5. Crumbles to loose earth and dust when the spell expires.

**Murmur of Earth [CR]**

- Earth 3 | Range: Touch | Area: 100' radius around caster | Duration: 1 round | Raises: Area (+50' radius per Raise), Special (+5 TN per 2 Raises)

Excites and angers Earth spirits, briefly shaking the ground like a minor earthquake (not affecting the ground immediately beneath the caster). Anyone in the area must roll Agility (TN 20) or be knocked Prone, suffering 1k1 damage and being Dazed for 1 Round. Furnishings and items may be knocked over (GM adjudicates cascading effects). Causes 4k2 damage to all structures in the area (ignoring Reduction) under Siege rules.

**Purge the Taint [CR]**

- Earth 3 (Jade) | Range: Personal | Area: 50' radius around caster | Duration: Permanent | Raises: Area (+10' radius)

A 1-hour ritual that calls on Earth to purge Shadowlands Taint from land, plant life, and inanimate objects. Cannot remove Taint from living creatures or powerful Tainted artifacts. Cost: the elemental kami in the area are also weakened, imposing +15 TN to all Spell Casting rolls within the area afterward. If cast over a wide area, the kami may permanently depart, leaving a gray lifeless wasteland.

**Sharing the Strength of Many [CR]**

- Earth 3 | Range: 20' | Area: 1–6 target persons | Duration: 5 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round), Range (+5')

Infuses up to 6 targets (including the caster) with the power of Earth. For the duration, the lowest Earth Ring Rank among all targets is added to the total of all Ring, Trait, and Skill rolls made by any of them. Does not apply to Spell Casting rolls.

**Shelter of the Earth [CR]**

- Earth 3 (Craft, Illusion) | Range: Touch | Area: One target creature | Duration: 30 minutes | Raises: Duration (+10 minutes per Raise)

Earth flows up to conceal the target under a realistic-looking facsimile of a natural object (tree, boulder, etc). The target can hear and breathe normally but cannot interact with the outside world. Speaking, moving, or being hit immediately ends the spell. The false shell resists physical blows with the same strength the real object would have. Earth kami choose an appropriate object for the area but cannot create the form of manmade objects, limiting the spell's usefulness inside towns or castles.

**Strength of the Crow [CR]**

- Earth 3 (Jade) | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target | Duration: Hours equal to caster's School Rank | Raises: Duration (+1 hour), Targets (+1)

Infuses the target with powerful resistance to Shadowlands Taint. Target gains +5k5 to all rolls resisting new Taint from outside sources, and all maho spells suffer +10 TN to affect them. Does not protect against increases to Taint the target already possesses, and cannot protect against Taint gained from casting maho.

**Strike as Stone [CR]**

- Earth 3 (Battle) | Range: Self or Touch | Area: One target person | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Duration (+1 minute per Raise)

Earth spirits harden the target's hands and forearms to stone. Hands cannot be physically harmed for the duration (enabling feats such as prying open a lock with fingers). Unarmed Damage Rating is increased by +2k0.

**Time's Deadly Hand [CR]**

- Earth 3 | Range: Touch | Area: One physical object | Duration: Permanent | Raises: Range (Touch to 50' for 3 Raises)

Causes Earth kami within an object to abandon it, weakening its physical strength permanently. Wood warps and flakes; stone and metal become brittle; cloth becomes fragile. Weapons lose 2k1 from DR; armor loses 5 from Armor TN bonus and 3 from Reduction. Weakened structures can be exploited for sabotage. Does not generally affect awakened items (nemuranai), as their spirits resist the entreaty.

**The Wolf's Mercy [CR]**

- Earth 3 | Range: 50' | Area: 1 target creature | Duration: 10 rounds | Raises: Range (+10'), Targets (+1)

Calls on Earth to afflict the target, weighing down their body and impeding their muscles. Target's Earth Ring is lowered by 1 Rank (Wound Ranks reduced correspondingly; can potentially kill if Wounds already suffered). Strength Rank is also lowered by 1. If the target has at least 1 full Rank of Shadowlands Taint, Earth spirits punish more severely, lowering Earth Ring by 2 Ranks (minimum 1).

**Wooden Prison [CR]**

- Earth 3 (Defense) | Range: 50' | Area: One target creature | Duration: 5 Rounds | Raises: Range (+10' per Raise), Duration (+1 Round per Raise), Special (+1k0 to plants' Strength per Raise)

Causes living plants and trees to rapidly grow and twine around the target. Target is automatically Entangled. On subsequent Turns, target may escape with a Simple Action Contested Strength roll against the plants' Strength of 4. Allies can cut the target free in two Turns' worth of effort. Plants drop and shrivel when the target escapes or the spell expires. Cannot function inside buildings, in deserts, on barren terrain, or target flying creatures.

**MASTERY LEVEL 4:**

**Armor of the Emperor [CR]**

- Earth 4 (Defense) | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 5 Rounds | Raises: Duration (+2 Rounds)

Earth spirits envelop the caster, mitigating all damage from physical or magical attacks. Each individual damage die has its total reduced by the caster's Shugenja School Rank. Cannot reduce a die's total below 0.

**Earth Dragon's Ward [CR]**

- Earth 4 (Wards, Jade) | Range: Touch | Area: 20' radius | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Duration (+5 minutes per Raise), Special (+1k1 to caster's Willpower roll per 2 Raises)

Requires a warding symbol inscribed on a solid surface (5 minutes to inscribe) prior to casting. Once cast, the ward makes it extremely difficult for anyone within the area to be possessed (by a ghost, gaki, oni, spell, or any other means). Any possessing attempt requires a Contested Willpower roll against the caster first. The caster is aware of the attempt and whether it succeeded, but not the nature or identity of the possessing force. Cannot drive out spirits already possessing someone.

**Essence of Earth [CR]**

- Earth 4 (Battle) | Range: Personal or 20' | Area: 1 target creature | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Duration (+1 round), Range (+5'), Targets (+1 per 2 Raises), Special (+1 Earth Ring Rank per 2 Raises, max +3)

Infuses the target with true Earth strength. Target's Earth Ring is considered 1 Rank higher (Wounds increased correspondingly). When the spell ends, Earth spirits depart and Wounds return to normal — possibly resulting in death if injuries were severe.

**Maw of the Earth [CR]**

- Earth 4 (Battle) | Range: 40' | Area: ~10' radius | Duration: Permanent | Raises: Range (+20' per Raise), Special (+10' depth and +1k1 damage per 2 Raises)

Earth spirits wrench open a large pit roughly 20' deep. Anyone within 10' of the pit must roll Reflexes vs the caster's Earth or fall in, suffering 3k2 damage and being trapped until rescued or able to climb out. Climbing out is a Complex Action requiring Athletics/Strength (TN = pit depth in feet).

**Sapphire Strike [CR]**

- Earth 4 (Jade, Crystal, Thunder) | Range: 50' | Area: One target creature | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+20' per Raise), Targets (+1 per Raise), Damage (+1k1 per Raise)

A Kuni family innovation from researching crystal's power against the Lying Darkness. Appears like Jade Strike but shows a distinctive blue-green tinge. DR 4k4, but only against targets specifically vulnerable to Jade or Crystal. Considered both Jade and Crystal for all game effects including penetrating Invulnerability or Reduction.

**Symbol of Earth [CR]**

- Earth 4 (Wards) | Range: Touch | Area: Special | Duration: Permanent | Raises: Damage (+1k0)

Inscribes a ward on a passageway. Those passing through must make a Contested Air vs caster's Earth roll. Those who fail are struck with a shockwave and must make a Strength roll (TN = Spell Casting Roll total) or be knocked Prone and Stunned. Tainted targets also suffer 2k2 Wounds. Only one Symbol of Earth may exist at a time. Cannot overlap with other element Symbols. Dispelled by another Symbol of Earth casting or by destroying the inscribed surface.

**The Earth Flows [CR]**

- Earth 4 (Battle) | Range: Centered on caster | Area: 1 square mile | Duration: 1 hour (or 2 Mass Battle Rounds) | Raises: Duration (+1/2 hour or 1 Mass Battle Round per 2 Raises)

Calls on Earth kami to re-arrange the landscape of a battlefield favorably. Awards +3k2 to the commander's Mass Battle roll to determine who is winning, and +1k1 to individual Mass Battle Table rolls for samurai on the caster's side. Casting this spell on both sides of the same battle can trigger a massive earthquake equivalent to the Rank 5 Earthquake spell. Devised by the Kitsu family.

**Tomb of Jade [CR]**

- Earth 4 (Jade) | Range: 50' | Area: 1 target creature | Duration: Concentration | Raises: Range (+10'), Special (+1k1 to caster per Raise)

Calls pure jade Earth spirits to consume the Taint within the target. Can only target someone with at least 1 full Rank of Taint (failure does not automatically reveal a Taint-free target). Each Round: Contested Earth roll between caster and target. If the target wins, the spell ends. If the caster wins, the target takes 2k2 Wounds as their body begins to transform into jade. Continues each round until the target resists, caster stops concentrating, or target dies. Those killed are transformed into jade statues that crumble to mundane dust after 24 hours.

**Wall of Earth [CR]**

- Earth 4 (Defense) | Range: 100' | Area: Max 10' high and 100' wide | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Area (+1' height or +10' width per Raise), Duration (+1 minute), Range (+10')

Earth spirits rise up to form a thick, powerful barrier that can be shaped as desired (curved, on a hillside, or circular). Requires Strength roll vs (Earth + School Rank) ×5 to break through. Strong enough to hold back a raging flood, lava flow, or hurricane winds for its duration. Crumbles away in moments when the spell expires.

**MASTERY LEVEL 5:**

**Drawing on the Mountain [CR]**

- Earth 5 (Battle, Defense) | Range: 300' | Area: One target structure up to a small castle | Duration: 1 day | Raises: Range (+150' per Raise), Area (+1 structure per 3 Raises), Duration (+1 day per 3 Raises)

Invokes Earth to strengthen a physical structure against all harm. Wood and stone are infused with the strength of mountains, allowing the structure to withstand floods, earthquakes, and siege engines. Under Siege rules: Wounds and Reduction of the target structure are doubled for the duration. When the spell expires, extra Wounds are lost — if this drops the structure to zero Wounds, it immediately collapses.

**Earthquake [CR]**

- Earth 5 | Range: Personal | Area: 1 mile radius | Duration: 1 minute | Raises: Area (+500 yards), Duration (+1 minute per 2 Raises)

Unleashes a terrible earthquake centered on the caster (who alone is unaffected). Utterly destroys all wooden buildings within the radius. Severely damages stone structures. All persons within the area are thrown to the ground, remaining Prone and Stunned for the duration, suffering 2k1 Wounds. Those inside buildings (including the caster) suffer 6k6 damage from falling debris and collapsing roofs. Casting within range of a major inhabited area is generally considered an act of war.

**Grounding Energy [CR]**

- Earth 5 (Defense, Wards) | Range: Centered on caster | Area: Caster and all allies within 20' radius | Duration: 3 Rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 Round per 2 Raises)

Devised by the Yogo. Earth spirits fortify the caster and companions against maho. Any maho spell targeting the caster or any ally within the area has its Spell Casting TN increased by 10 × the target's Earth Ring.

**Major Binding [CR]**

- Earth 5 (Jade, Wards) | Range: 100' | Area: 1 target Shadowlands creature | Duration: 12 hours | Raises: Duration (+1 hour), Range (+50')

A 10-minute ritual (reduced by 1 minute per assisting shugenja, minimum 1 minute). Can target any Lost, any Shadowlands creature, or other Tainted spirit creatures. On a successful Contested combined Earth Rings vs target's Earth roll, manacles of pure elemental jade imprison the creature and render it helpless with the pain of searing bonds. When the spell expires, manacles crumble to dust and the creature is liable to seek immediate vengeance.

**Strike at the Roots [CR]**

- Earth 5 | Range: 50' | Area: 1 target creature | Duration: 3 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round), Range (+10'), Targets (+1 per 2 Raises), Special (+1k1 to caster per Raise)

A more devastating form of The Wolf's Mercy. Caster must win a Contested Earth roll against the target. If the target loses, their Earth Ring is immediately reduced to 1 for the duration. This also reduces Wound Ranks and may result in immediate death if injuries have already been suffered.

**The Kami's Strength [CR]**

- Earth 5 (Battle) | Range: 30' | Area: 1 target creature | Duration: 5 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round), Range (+10')

Fortifies one person with the power of Earth. Target gains Reduction 20 and increases Strength and one other physical Trait by the caster's Earth Ring. In return, the target cannot take Simple Move Actions (Free Move Actions are still permitted).

**The Kami's Will [CR]**

- Earth 5 (Defense) | Range: 30' | Area: 1 target creature | Duration: 10 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round), Range (+10')

Infuses one person with the stubbornness and determination of Earth. Target's Willpower is increased by the caster's Earth Ring. All spells (friendly or hostile, not including maho) targeting them suffer -XkX to Spell Casting Roll, where X = caster's Earth Ring. In return, the target suffers -Xk0 (X = caster's Earth Ring) to all Social Skill rolls due to their overwhelming stubbornness.

**MASTERY LEVEL 6:**

**Essence of Jade [CR]**

- Earth 6 (Defense, Jade) | Range: 30' | Area: 1 target creature | Duration: 10 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round), Range (+5'), Targets (+1 per 2 Raises, max 3 total)

Target radiates sacred green jade light. Cannot gain Shadowlands Taint and is completely immune to all maho spell effects for the duration. Cannot be cast on anyone with at least 1 full Rank of Taint — pure jade spirits recoil from such individuals, immediately alerting the caster to their Tainted nature.

**Power of the Earth Dragon [CR]**

- Earth 6 (Defense) | Range: 50' | Area: 1 target creature | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Duration (+1 minute), Range (+10'), Targets (+1), Special (+10 Wounds absorbed per Raise)

Calls on the Dragon of Earth to enshroud the target with protection against all forms of harm. Earth spirits absorb all damage the target suffers. Limits: nemuranai can bypass the protection. If a specific target's protecting spirits absorb a total of 100 Wounds, they are exhausted and the spell's effects on that target end.

**Prison of Earth [CR]**

- Earth 6 (Wards) | Range: 30' | Area: 1 target creature | Duration: Permanent | Raises: Range (+5'), Special (+1k1 to caster per Raise)

The most powerful binding spell available to Earth shugenja. Requires a gem or pearl (or other rare precious item at GM's discretion) in which to imprison the creature. Can target any creature native to Jigoku, Gaki-do, or Toshigoku, or any non-human Tainted creature (1+ full Rank). Caster wins a Contested Willpower roll vs the target — on success, the creature's physical body vanishes and its essence is imprisoned within the item indefinitely. Destroying the item releases the creature (usually prompting vengeance against the caster or descendants).

**Rise, Earth [CR]**

- Earth 6 | Range: 30' | Area: One summoned spirit | Duration: Concentration | Raises: None

Summons a massive Earth kami in a vaguely humanoid shape roughly 10' tall with broad body and thick stone limbs. Moves up to 5 × Earth Ring per Round. Footsteps shake the earth in a 20' radius, preventing Simple Move Actions within. All Physical Traits equal to caster's Earth Ring. Attacks with Jiujutsu Skill Rank equal to half the caster's Earth Ring. Can carry up to 1,000 lbs. Blows strong enough to destroy wooden structures and shatter 1-foot stone walls. Wounds as a human with Earth equal to the caster's Earth Ring but no Wound Penalties. Invulnerable. Dispelled at zero Wounds.

**Soldiers of Clay [CR]**

- Earth 6 (Craft, Battle, Jade) | Range: 100' | Area: N/A | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+1/2 hour per Raise), Special (+2 soldiers per Raise)

Found solely in Phoenix libraries — most famously used by Isawa Tadaka and Isawa Sezaru. Creates 10 human-sized stone warriors animated by Earth kami. They move, fight, and act as the caster commands, obediently and fearlessly. Traits and Rings equal to the caster's Earth Ring. Attack (with stone swords considered Jade) as though they have Kenjutsu 4. Reduction equal to twice the caster's Earth. Collapse into loose earth when destroyed or when the spell expires.
