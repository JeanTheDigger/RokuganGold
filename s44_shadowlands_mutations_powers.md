# 44. Shadowlands Mutations & Powers

**MUTATIONS:**

Mutations are the result of gaining Ranks in Shadowlands Taint and represent difformities that must be concealed. They manifest automatically upon gaining the relevant Taint Rank.

**Albinism**

Skin and hair become deathly pale or stark white. Can be concealed with cosmetics. -1k0 to Social rolls if true appearance is known.

**Beast of Fu Leng**

The most terrible mutation. Completely lose human form, distorting into a bestial or monstrous body (typically a quadruped or bloated ogre-ish humanoid). Cannot be hidden. Causes Fear 1 in anyone who sees it.

**Chitinous Armor**

Skin hardens into an insect-like shell, exposing inhuman nature. Grants Reduction 10 (stacks with Reduction from armor, spells, or other effects).

**Demonic Eyes**

In darkness or shadow, eyes glow with unnatural blue light. Allows sight through darkness and other visual impairments (smoke, fog, etc.).

**Discolored Skin**

Skin turns unnaturally pale or dark. Can be explained away as the result of an indoor or outdoor lifestyle, but inflicts +5 TN to all Social rolls.

**Distorted Limbs**

One arm or leg (random) becomes misshapen or twisted, rendering it useless. Obvious deformity, extremely difficult to conceal (though explainable as an injury). If a leg: gain the Lame Disadvantage. If an arm: -3k0 to all rolls made with that arm.

**Extra Digit**

An additional finger or toe sprouts. Minor deformity; does not automatically suggest Taint but unsettles those who notice it: -1k0 to all Social Skill rolls. If cut off, regrows within 48 hours.

**Extra Eye**

An additional eye sprouts somewhere on the body. +1k0 to Perception rolls and Perception-based Skill rolls, but the eye must be uncovered (and thus noticeable) to gain this benefit.

**Extra Limb**

An additional arm or leg sprouts. Obviously Tainted in appearance (discolored skin, claw-like nails). 50% chance of being non-functional, hanging limp — the obstruction and clumsiness causes -1k0 to all Agility-based and Reflexes-based rolls.

**Forked Tongue**

Tongue becomes long and forked like a snake’s. Concealing this while speaking or eating requires Acting/Awareness (TN 20).

**Foul Odor**

Exceptionally putrid and disgusting body odor, impossible to ignore. No amount of bathing or perfume can suppress it. -1k0 to all Social rolls.

**Jigoku's Blood**

Blood becomes black and foul-smelling. Anyone who sees the character injured will immediately recognize this as an obvious symptom of the Taint.

**Tentacles**

One or more tentacles sprout, partially controllable. Can be used to pick up small objects, make attacks with Small weapons, or initiate a Grapple. Anyone seeing them will immediately recognize the character as Tainted.

**Tough Hide**

Skin becomes rough and leathery, appearing unnatural or diseased. -2k0 to all Social rolls. Grants Reduction 5.

**Undead Visage**

Gains the outward appearance of being undead: deathly gray skin, visible skin decay, deeply sunken eyes. Cannot be concealed. Causes Fear 3 in anyone who sees it.

**Vile Teeth**

Teeth become distorted or discolored to the point of appearing inhuman (jagged, animal-like fangs, deep brown or sickly yellow, etc.). Concealing this while talking or eating requires Acting/Awareness (TN 20).

**Wings**

Large wings sprout, allowing flight for short distances. May be feathered, leathery, or reptilian. Can be folded and concealed beneath a cloak or haori with Acting/Awareness (TN 25). Folding or unfolding is a Simple Action. When unfolded, Move Actions are calculated at Water +2.

**MINOR SHADOWLANDS POWERS:**

Using any Minor Shadowlands Power forces an Earth Taint roll (TN 15) or gain 1 additional Point of Taint.

**Above the Elements**

All normal elemental spells (non-maho) that could affect you suffer +10 TN to their Spell Casting Rolls. However, maho spells targeting you gain a Free Raise. Any shugenja who casts Sense within range will notice a distinct absence of kami around you.

**Blackened Claws**

Obsidian claws concealed within the fingertips, extendable as a Free Action. DR 2k3 with unarmed attacks (replacing the normal 0k1). Considered obsidian for bypassing Reduction and Invulnerability. Compulsive urge to use them: must make a Willpower Roll (TN 10) any time a fight begins using a weapon other than the claws.

**Blessing of the Dark One**

Unnatural resistance to pain and physical harm. Wounds at each Wound Rank are increased by 3.

**Blood Knows Blood**

Continuously sense the location of any portion of your blood or flesh (not hair or fingernails), no matter where they are. If you also possess Blood Domination, you can track any creature that has consumed your blood.

**Calligraphy of Thought**

Read the surface thoughts of anyone within line of sight as a Free Action, requiring a Contested Air Roll. On success, sense whatever the target is thinking at that moment. Cannot uncover deeper emotions or subconscious knowledge.

**Child of Darkness**

Shadowlands creatures will not attack unless threatened. If communication is possible, they may obey commands, especially if you appear powerful or persuasive.

**Eyes of Hell**

See with unnatural clarity through any visual impairment (darkness, shadows, fog, smoke, etc.) at will. Whenever this power is used, eyes glow with an unnatural light (usually green or blue), betraying presence and nature.

**Fear**

Once per skirmish, project an aura of inhuman terror: all enemies within sight are afflicted with a Fear 5 effect.

**Jade Sense**

Innate sense for jade, crystal, and obsidian within Earth ×10 feet. Concealing magic can obstruct this sense. Cannot detect Jade Petal Tea.

**Master of Blood**

May cast maho for one fewer Wound than normally required. Taint gained from casting maho is reduced by Earth Ring (minimum 1). All non-maho spells suffer +10 TN.

**Master of Shadows**

Add Taint Rank in unkept dice to all Stealth Skill rolls. Gain the Discolored Skin mutation if not already possessed, as skin develops a dark and shadowy tone.

**Mind of Darkness**

May embrace the Taint to enhance rolls with mental Traits (Intelligence, Willpower, Awareness, Perception) in the same manner as physical Traits. If Lost, add Taint Rank to the total of all rolls with mental Traits as well as physical Traits.

**Monstrous Strength**

Swollen, unnatural build that unsettles others. -1k0 to all Social rolls. Add Taint Rank in unkept dice to all Strength rolls, Strength-based Skill rolls, and Damage rolls.

**Sense Purity**

As a Free Action, sense the presence of non-Tainted humans within 50 feet (Contested Awareness Roll). Extremely difficult to surprise as a result. Can sense at greater distances up to half a mile, but this requires a Complex Action and the target receives two Free Raises on their Awareness roll.

**Uncanny Speed**

Water Ring considered 2 Ranks higher for determining movement distance with Simple or Complex Move Actions. In return, fidgety and nervous — cannot sit still for more than a few minutes.

**Unholy Stamina**

May go without rest or sleep for up to three days with no penalties of any kind. Must be reactivated after three days, requiring another roll to resist gaining Taint.

**MAJOR SHADOWLANDS POWERS:**

Using any Major Shadowlands Power forces an Earth Taint roll (TN 20) or gain 1 additional Point of Taint.

**Armor of Death**

Each time you inflict at least 15 Wounds on an opponent, gain Reduction 10 (Reduction 5 against jade) for the remainder of the skirmish. Stacks with other Reduction sources but cannot stack with itself.

**Beside the Darkness**

Meditate uninterrupted for 2 hours and make a Meditation/Willpower roll (TN 20). On success, “metabolize” the Taint for 8 hours — not considered Tainted for mechanical purposes during that time; spells and Techniques targeting Tainted creatures do not affect you. Physical mutations remain visible. Cannot end early. While active: cannot use other Shadowlands Powers, cannot cast maho, cannot embrace the Taint.

**Beyond the Elements**

+15 TN on any elemental magic targeting you. In exchange, maho spells targeting you gain two Free Raises. Stacks with Above the Elements.

**Blood Domination**

Any creature that ingests even a spoonful of your blood (1 Wound worth) is subject to your demands. Contested Willpower Roll to force obedience for Taint Rank hours. Cannot force direct self-destruction, but all other commands (including potentially lethal orders) must be obeyed. If the roll fails, the target is aware of the attempt and cannot be commanded again for one full day.

**Blood Shouting**

Any creature that has ingested any of your blood must be within hearing. When you take Wounds, emit a ghastly terrifying scream — half the damage you would have taken is inflicted on the person who ingested your blood instead. If multiple such persons are within hearing, the damage is divided among them (rounded up).

**Chosen of Fu Leng**

Unnatural strength of will. Any attempt to influence you with Social Skills, Techniques, or mind-controlling spells automatically fails.

**Disrupt the Chi**

Simple Action. Target any opponent within sight. Contested Willpower Roll (you add Taint to your total; the target adds their Honor). On success: the target must use their lowest Trait for all Skill and Trait rolls for the next ten Rounds.

**Drawing Out the Darkness**

Maintain physical contact with another creature for at least 1 minute and make a Contested Willpower Roll. On success: the victim gains Taint Points equal to your current Taint Rank. On failure: nothing happens, though the victim feels unease and perhaps a chill.

**Evade the Unworthy**

Become completely invisible (Simple Action), concealing your body and everything you carry (including helpless/unconscious persons). Does not conceal noise or scent. Penetrable by magical effects such as By the Light of the Moon. Ends immediately after making an attack.

**Father of Lies**

Add Taint Rank in kept dice to all rolls with Temptation, Intimidation, and the Deceit emphasis of Sincerity.

**Feeding on Flesh**

Simple Action. Feed on the flesh of a dead person or creature, or one who is helpless (Down or Out, bound securely, etc.). Inflicts 1k1 Wounds on the target if still alive. Heals you a number of Wounds equal to 5 × Taint Rank.

**Flight of Darkness**

Call on Air kansen to carry you through the sky. Fly at will at the same speed as normal ground movement, without need for spells or wings.

**Protection of the Dark**

Considered Invulnerable in the same manner as some creatures. Whenever damage is ignored due to this Power, skin glows with throbbing black energy that is impossible to hide.

**Strength of Madness**

Earth Ring times per day, suffer Wounds equal to Earth Ring to replicate the effect of spending a Void Point. Cannot be used to reduce damage; all other normal Void Point effects (activating School Techniques, +1k1 bonus to rolls, etc.) can be duplicated.

**Strength of the Dark One**

+5 Wounds per Wound Rank. Stacks with Blessing of the Dark One.

**Thy Master's Will**

The Taint will not allow you to truly die. If killed, return from the dead at some later time as an intelligent undead creature. Retain all Rings, Traits, abilities, School Techniques, and Skills, but no longer have a Void Ring. The body is undead and will begin to rot and decay over time.

**Undead Strength**

The body does not feel pain and continues to function normally regardless of injuries. No Wound penalties — operate at full effect until actually killed.

**Unearthly Regeneration**

At the start of each Turn, heal Wounds equal to Taint Rank. Healed injuries leave no scars. Lost limbs are not regrown, but will reattach if held in place for a few minutes.

**Unholy Beauty**

No outward visible effects of the Taint. All physical mutations already possessed fade away. Shadowlands Powers can still be gained normally but exhibit no perceptible physical symptoms. Internally the body compensates with redundant or distorted organs, cancerous tumors, and other distortions.

**AKUTENSHI POWERS:**

Usable only by Akutenshi and exceedingly rare others. Treated as Major Shadowlands Powers for activation purposes.

**Awe of the Dark One**

Exert will against Shadowlands creatures, forcing them to do whatever is desired — no roll needed. Works against all undead creatures, Shadowlands beasts and oni, and any Lost with lower Willpower. Does not work against Lost with equal or higher Willpower, Tainted humans who are not Lost, uniquely powerful creatures (Oni Lords, other Akutenshi), or Fortunes.

**Body of Damned Time**

Simple Action. Target up to Taint Rank opponents who can see the akutenshi. Affected targets momentarily see their own future death in a terrible void instead of the akutenshi. Contested Taint vs target Void roll — all who lose immediately age 1–10 years.

**Breath of Taint**

Exhale an invisible cloud of pure Taint. Against a creature within 10 feet: target must roll Earth (TN 25) or immediately gain 1–10 Points of Taint. Against a physical object (up to door or wagon size): it becomes Tainted — anyone subsequently touching it must roll Earth (TN 15) or gain 1 Point of Taint. Jade targeted this way instantly corrupts to black sludge. Crystal is immune.

**Claim the Body**

Send out spirit to possess Shadowlands creatures (Simple Action, Contested Willpower Roll). The akutenshi’s own body becomes immobile and inactive but perceives everything the possessed creature perceives. If previously possessed a particular creature, can do so from any distance across the Empire. If the possessed creature is killed, or the akutenshi’s body is harmed or disturbed, the spirit immediately returns. Can return voluntarily as a Free Action.

**Mastery of Darkness**

Supreme master of maho. Knows all maho spells and can cast them without spilling blood. If blood is spilled anyway, gain 3 Free Raises on that spell. Can also cast non-maho elemental spells as maho (must spill blood normally to do so).

**Passing Through the Light**

Move with inhuman speed, seeming to warp or fall through space. Water ×25 as a Free Action; Water ×50 as a Simple Action.

**Shapeshifting**

Freely alter appearance as long as broadly human-like in size and shape. Can shift instantly, combining human and monstrous features at will. Can imitate anyone previously seen, though those familiar with the original may see through the deception (Contested Roll: typically Investigation vs akutenshi’s Acting). Free Action, usable any number of times per Round.

**Soul Drinking**

Consume a victim’s soul by sucking it out through the mouth. Normally done to a dying victim, but can be used on a helpless living target with a Contested Roll of Taint vs target’s Honor/Willpower. Consumed souls are stored within the akutenshi’s intestines in endless torment and agony. Imprisoned souls can only be freed by killing the akutenshi.

**Terror of Fu Leng**

Complex Action. Unleash a wave of monstrous supernatural terror affecting everyone within line of sight. All targets are afflicted with a Fear 10 effect.
