# 43. Maho

Maho (blood magic) can be learned by bushi and courtiers alike, though the act of doing so is largely forbidden. Maho may be used regardless of Insight Rank. Requirements: the caster or a willing or helpless being must spill blood equal to twice the spell’s Mastery Level. Additional blood may be spilled to gain Raises: one Raise per additional increment of double the spell’s Mastery Level in spilled blood. Cost: the caster gains Shadowlands Taint Points equal to one less than the spell’s Mastery Level (minimum 1).

**MASTERY LEVEL 1:**

**Bleeding [CR]**

- Fire 1 | Range: 50' | Area: One target person/creature | Duration: Indefinite | Raises: Damage (+1 Wound of bleeding per Raise), Targets (+1 per Raise)

Target must already be injured (at least 1 Wound). Inflicts a malignant kansen that causes the wound to bleed, inflicting 1 Wound per Round at the start of the victim’s Turn. Continues indefinitely until the injury is bandaged with a successful Medicine (Wound Treatment)/Intelligence roll (TN 20), or the target is magically healed to full Wounds.

**Blood Rite [CR]**

- Earth 1 | Range: Touch | Area: One target person/creature | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Duration (+2 minutes per Raise), Special (heals extra +1k0 per Raise)

Allows maho-tsukai to masquerade as healing priests. Kansen flow into the target, stimulating healing and physical prowess. Target immediately heals 1k1 Wounds, and one of their physical Traits (caster’s choice) is treated as one Rank higher for the duration. Cruel price: the target immediately gains 1k1 Points of Shadowlands Taint.

**Blood and Darkness [CR]**

- Air 1 | Range: Centered on caster | Area: 30' radius | Duration: 10 rounds | Raises: Area (+5' radius per Raise), Duration (+2 rounds per Raise)

Plunges the area around the caster into unnatural visual obscurement (pitch black or dark blood red, caster’s choice) as kansen literally block the vision of everyone in the area. All within except the caster are considered Blind until they move out of the area. The caster sees normally. The zone does not move once created. Magical effects or Shadowlands powers overcoming blindness can see through it; nothing else can until the spell expires.

**Disrupt the Limb [CR]**

- Water 1 | Range: 50' | Area: One target person’s limb | Duration: 10 rounds | Raises: Duration (+2 rounds per Raise), Range (+10' per Raise)

Afflicts one target limb (arm or leg) with pain, weakness, and tremors. All physical action rolls using that limb suffer +15 TN for the duration. If the targeted limb is a leg, the victim is considered to have the Lame Disadvantage for the duration.

**Heart of the Damned [EoE]**

- Earth 1 | Range: Touch | Area: Caster | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: None

Necromantic ritual first practiced by Iuchiban. Requires touching a recently-dead human corpse (dead within the last day). The corpse shrivels and rots away as reddish-black energy flows into the caster. The caster heals 2k2 Wounds and regains 1 Rank in any Ring or Trait currently reduced below its normal level.

**Inspire Fear [CR]**

- Air 1 | Range: 50' | Area: One target person | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+10 minutes per Raise), Range (+10' per Raise), Targets (+1 per Raise)

The target gains a 3-Point Phobia of the caster’s choice for the duration. Common uses include making victims afraid of their own weapons, allies, or spell-scrolls.

**Legacy of the Dark One [CR]**

- Air 1 | Range: 100' | Area: One target person/creature | Duration: 5 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round per Raise), Targets (+1 per Raise), Special (+1 Void Point drained per 2 Raises)

Distracting kansen cluster around the target, disrupting their connection to the Void. The target immediately loses one Void Point and cannot regain it for the duration of the spell.

**Purge the Weak [EoE]**

- Earth 1 | Range: 50' | Area: Food and water for up to 5 people | Duration: Permanent | Raises: Range (+20' per Raise), Area (+1 person’s supplies per Raise)

Ruins food and drink, rendering it foul, diseased, and inedible. Effects are obvious (food rots, water discolors). Anyone who eats or drinks must roll Stamina (TN 20) or become severely ill: -3k0 to all Skill, Trait, and Spell Casting rolls. The illness is incurable with medical treatment and lasts two weeks unless magically cured.

**Sinful Dreams [CR]**

- Air 1 | Range: 300' | Area: One target intelligent creature | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Range (+50' per Raise), Special (+1 Free Raise awarded per 2 Raises)

Can only be cast on a sleeping target or one in a situation where they could easily fall asleep. The caster need not know exactly where the target is. Causes disturbing dreams of dark, sinful, and dishonorable acts. For the next 24 hours after the spell concludes, the caster gains a Free Raise on all Temptation and Intimidation rolls against the victim.

**Suck the Marrow [EoE]**

- Earth 1 | Range: Touch | Area: One target creature | Duration: 1 day | Raises: Duration (+1 day per 2 Raises), Range (50' for 2 Raises)

The victim becomes pale, weak, and malodorous, unable to heal Wounds normally or via Medicine. Cannot recover from illnesses or poisons. -2k0 to any roll to resist poison or disease effects. Magical healing still works normally.

**Summon Undead Champion [CR]**

- Earth 1 | Range: 100' | Area: One humanoid corpse | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+1 hour per 2 Raises), Range (+20' per Raise), Targets (+1 corpse per Raise)

Animates a corpse within range into a zombie (kansen-animated) that serves the caster. Obeys simple commands only (‘kill them’, ‘wait here’). Cannot understand complex or conditional orders. Once created, it may move freely beyond the spell’s initial range. If beheaded or destroyed, it will not re-animate unless the spell is recast. When the duration expires, the kansen departs and the zombie collapses.

**Symbol of Blood [EoE]**

- Water 1 (Wards) | Range: Touch | Area: 30' radius | Duration: 12 hours | Raises: Area (+5' per Raise), Duration (+2 hours per Raise)

Inscribes a sinister ward in the blood spilled to cast it. Cannot be destroyed except by physically destroying the surface on which it is written. Anyone other than the caster who enters the area suffers -2k0 to all physical Actions while within. Does not stack with other Symbols of Blood.

**Ward of Divine Peace [EoE]**

- Air 1 (Wards) | Range: Touch | Area: 50' radius | Duration: 8 hours | Raises: Area (+10' radius per Raise)

Favored by Bloodspeaker cell leaders. Creates a false aura of calm and well-being that makes it difficult to focus attention or resist appeals. Anyone within the area (or who enters while the spell is active) suffers -1k0 to all Skill and Trait rolls using Awareness or Willpower until the duration ends. Does not stack with itself.

**Written in Blood [CR]**

- Fire 1 | Range: Touch | Area: One message (up to 10 words) | Duration: 1 week | Raises: Duration (+1 day per Raise), Special (+3 words per Raise)

A blood-written message sinks into the surface and disappears, reappearing when a condition set by the caster is fulfilled. Conditions may be as simple or complex as desired (kansen must be able to reasonably perceive them). Until the message reappears, it is physically undetectable, though Sense or By the Light of the Moon will detect something magically concealed. If the duration expires without the trigger, the blood reappears without forming the message.

**MASTERY LEVEL 2:**

**Caress of Fu Leng [CR]**

- Earth 2 | Range: 50' | Area: One target jade object | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+10' per Raise)

Summons dark kansen to corrupt and destroy jade. The caster may target any visible jade item within range. The jade is instantly corrupted — its blessed properties annihilated and only a foul black slime remains. Cannot affect awakened nemuranai containing jade.

**Curse of the Clan [EoE]**

- Air 2 | Range: 50' | Area: One target samurai | Duration: 1 month | Raises: Duration (+10 days per Raise)

Causes a samurai to manifest their clan’s most stereotypical and notorious behavior — a Crab becomes more crude and ruthlessly pragmatic; a Phoenix becomes more arrogant and obsessed with magical power. Changes are significant but not overwhelming; the victim rationalizes their behavior. For PCs, the GM should present this as a role-playing challenge rather than dictating behavior.

**Curse of the Kansen [EoE]**

- Air 2 | Range: 50' | Area: One target creature | Duration: 8 hours | Raises: Range (+10' per Raise), Duration (+1 hour per Raise)

Attracts hostile kansen to harass the target, filling their head with constant whispers urging sinful and corrupt behavior. -1k0 to all Courtier, Etiquette, and Sincerity rolls. -2k0 when rolling to resist the Temptation Skill. Effect stacks with Sinful Dreams. If optional Honor Roll rules are in use, -1k0 to Honor Rolls as well.

**Curse of the Unblinking Eye [CR]**

- Air 2 | Range: 100' | Area: One target creature | Duration: 2 days | Raises: Duration (+1 day per 2 Raises, max 7 days), Targets (+1 per 2 Raises)

Prevents the target from sleeping for the duration. Cannot recover Void Points or spell slots from rest. For each day without sleep, must make a Raw Stamina roll (TN = 5 + 5 × nights without sleep) or suffer the Fatigued condition.

**Curse of Weakness [CR]**

- Water 2 | Range: 50' | Area: One target person or creature | Duration: 10 rounds | Raises: Duration (+2 rounds per Raise), Range (+10' per Raise), Targets (+1 per 2 Raises)

A more powerful form of Disrupt Limb. Angry Water kansen flow through the target’s body, inflicting headache, weakness, fatigue, and muscle tremors. +10 TN to all physical and mental Skill and Trait rolls (including Spell Casting rolls). -10 to Armor TN.

**Dark Wings [EoE]**

- Water 2 (Maho) | Range: Self | Area: Caster | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Duration (+2 minutes per Raise)

The caster sprouts monstrous bat-like wings from their back. Can fly for the duration, considered to have Swift 3 while flying.

**Drain the Soul [CR]**

- Earth 2 | Range: 50' | Area: One target person/creature | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Duration (+2 minutes per Raise), Range (+10' per Raise), Targets (+1 per Raise), Special (+1 Stamina Rank per 2 Raises)

Malignant Earth kansen drain away the target’s natural Earth. The victim’s Stamina Rank is reduced by 1 (minimum 1). This can lower the Earth Ring, reducing Wound Ranks and making the target more vulnerable to injury.

**Eternal Unrest**

- Earth 2 (Maho) | Range: Touch | Area: Target corpses | Duration: 1 month | Raises: Area (+1 corpse per Raise), Special (−10 minutes casting time per Raise)

A 1-hour ritual (minimum 10 minutes with Raises). Prepares up to Taint Rank corpses for later recall. At any time within the duration, spend a Complex Action and an Earth spell slot to instantly summon any prepared corpses to the caster’s location. Favored by Chuda Necromancers.

**Gift of the Maker [EoE]**

- Fire 2 | Range: Self or Touch | Area: One target creature | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+30 minutes per Raise)

Temporarily grants the blessings of the Taint, summoning the power of Jigoku. The caster (or a touched person) gains a Greater Shadowlands Power of their choice for the duration. Bloodspeakers rationalize this as evoking power from within themselves.

**Pain [CR]**

- Earth 2 | Range: 30' | Area: One target creature | Duration: 1 round | Raises: Duration (+1 Round per Raise), Range (+5' per Raise), Targets (+1 per 2 Raises)

Racks the target with intense physical pain throughout the body. Target falls Prone and cannot act on their next Turn. Must make a Willpower Trait Roll (TN 20) to avoid crying aloud in pain, which could cause a loss of Honor and/or Glory.

**Puppet Master [CR]**

- Fire 2 | Range: 100' | Area: One target undead creature | Duration: 4 hours | Raises: Duration (+2 hours per Raise), Range (+20' per Raise), Targets (+1 undead per Raise)

Takes control of undead creatures. Mindless uncontrolled undead (zombies) are controlled automatically. Free-willed undead or undead already controlled by another require a Contested Willpower roll (against the creature or its controller). Controlled undead obey commands for the duration subject to their intelligence. At duration’s end, they revert to their previous controller or become free-willed again.

**Spreading the Darkness [CR]**

- Earth 2 | Range: 30' | Area: Two target persons | Duration: Permanent | Raises: Range (+10' per Raise)

Causes Taint to transfer from one person to another. At least one target must have the Shadowlands Taint. Maximum Taint Points transferable = caster’s Earth + Insight Rank. Cannot remove the last Point of Taint from the source (the Taint will not fully release its grip). Unwilling recipients require a Contested Willpower roll against the caster, or the spell fails.

**MASTERY LEVEL 3:**

**Armor of Obsidian [CR]**

- Fire 3 | Range: Touch | Area: One target person or creature | Duration: 10 minutes or until used | Raises: Duration (+5 minutes per Raise), Special (absorb +1 additional spell per 2 Raises)

Wreaths the target in an invisible shield of kansen. When the target is struck or affected by a spell with the Jade keyword, the kansen intercede and negate the effect — spectacular, as the target is briefly wreathed in silent black fire. Normally absorbs one spell; skilled casting can make it absorb multiple.

**Dancing with Demons [EoE]**

- Air 3 (Maho) | Range: 500' | Area: Self or one target creature | Duration: 24 hours | Raises: Duration (+6 hours per Raise)

A ritual requiring the caster to dance for at least one hour (Perform: Dance/Awareness roll, TN 25). If the dance is interrupted or the roll fails, the spell fails. On success: either gain a Physical or Mental Advantage for the duration, or inflict a Physical or Mental Disadvantage on a target within range for the duration (the caster need not see the target). Maximum Advantage/Disadvantage value = caster’s Air + Insight Rank + Taint Rank. Cannot make extreme physical changes (no removing limbs, etc.).

**Death Beyond Life [CR]**

- Earth 3 | Range: Touch | Area: One target person | Duration: 24 hours | Raises: Duration (+24 hours per Raise)

Must be cast while the target is still alive. If the target dies within the duration, powerful kansen carry their soul away, preventing it from passing to Meido. Exactly 8 hours after death, the kansen return the soul to the body: the target rises at the Down Wound Rank. Resurrection inflicts a full Rank of Taint from the soul’s time in kansen care. Works as long as the corpse is intact (severed limbs or decapitation will reassemble). Only prevented by scattering body parts or burning the corpse to ashes.

**Essence of Undeath [CR]**

- Earth 3 | Range: 50' | Area: One humanoid corpse | Duration: Indefinite | Raises: Range (+20' per Raise), Targets (+1 per 3 Raises)

A more powerful undead summoning than Summon Undead Champion. Binds a kansen into a dead body, creating a powerful Tainted revenant that serves the caster. Lasts indefinitely until destroyed. Cannot speak but understands and obeys any verbal command. Serves until physically destroyed.

**Hate's Heart [CR]**

- Air 3 | Range: 50' | Area: One target creature | Duration: 3 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round per 2 Raises), Range (+10' per Raise)

Infuses the target with Air kansen, causing sudden violent murderous rage against whoever the target is looking at or speaking with when the spell takes effect. For the duration, the victim is consumed with uncontrollable murderous rage, attacking the object of hatred to the best of their ability. At the start of each Turn, the victim may roll their Honor (TN 30) to control the rage for that Round (only Free and Simple Actions if successful, since so much energy is dedicated to controlling the anger). Savvy maho-tsukai cast this from concealment.

**Mists of Fear [EoE]**

- Air 3 (Illusion) | Range: 50' | Area: One target creature | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Special (increase Fear effect strength by 1 per Raise)

Summons a potent illusion of whatever the target fears most in the world. Visible only to the target for a few moments. The target must roll against a Fear 5 effect. If the target suffers from a Phobia, the spell also activates its effects.

**Summon Oni [CR]**

- Earth 3 | Range: 50' | Area: One summoned oni | Duration: Indefinite | Raises: Special (+1k0 per Raise to the Willpower roll to control), Special (more powerful oni, GM’s discretion)

Summons an oni from Jigoku. The oni must immediately be given a name (the caster’s own name, or a name of someone closely connected to the caster) or it departs back to Jigoku. Naming the oni anchors it in Ningen-do. The caster must then win a Contested Willpower roll against the oni (+3k2 bonus if the caster used their own name). If the caster wins, the oni obeys. If the oni wins, it runs amok.

**Summon Oni [CR] — Oni Mark ****&**** Name Rules**

- See Section 42 for full Taint rules. Additional Oni Mark mechanics:

Anyone sharing their name with an oni acquires an Oni Mark. Each day the bearer must roll Earth to resist gaining Taint (see periodic Taint rules). Each week, a Contested Willpower roll against the oni — the oni gains +1k1 per Taint Rank inflicted by the Mark on its bearer. The caster retains +3k2 if they summoned the oni themselves. If the oni ever wins, it steals the name and becomes an Oni Lord — the now-nameless victim becomes a slave of the oni. If the oni is destroyed or banished, the Oni Mark disappears.

**Symbol of the Bloodspeaker [EoE]**

- Air 3 (Wards) | Range: Touch | Area: 50' radius | Duration: 12 hours | Raises: None

Inscribed into a flat surface. The moment anyone not loyal to the Bloodspeaker Cult enters the radius, the symbol glows sickly green and the intruder is bathed in eerie green flames, inflicting 4k3 damage. Can discharge against any number of different targets, but each target can only be affected once per duration.

**MASTERY LEVEL 4:**

**Burning Blood [EoE]**

- Fire 4 | Range: 50' | Area: One target creature | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Damage (+1k0 per Raise), Range (+10' per Raise), Targets (+1 per 3 Raises)

Infuses the target’s bloodstream with angry Fire kansen, causing blood to superheat and boil through the skin as a cloud of reddish vapor. DR equal to the target’s Fire Ring. Target must roll Willpower (TN 20) or fall Prone. Whether Prone or not, the target is considered Fatigued on their next two Turns.

**Chains of Jigoku [CR]**

- Earth 4 | Range: 50' | Area: One creature or person | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Duration (+5 minutes per Raise), Range (+10' per Raise), Special (+1k0 per Raise to caster’s roll to contain)

Cannot affect the undead. Dark rusty iron manacles burst from the ground and ensnare the target, rendering them immobile and helpless (cannot take physical actions other than attempting to break free; can still speak). Break free with a Contested Strength vs caster’s Earth roll. When the spell expires, the manacles melt into black ooze.

**No Pure Breaths [CR]**

- Air 4 | Range: 100' | Area: One target creature | Duration: Instantaneous (see below) | Raises: Damage (+1k0 per Raise), Range (+20' per Raise), Targets (+1 per 2 Raises)

Instantly turns the air inside the target’s lungs to Tainted kansen that violently erupt out of the body, ravaging the lungs and airways. Target bleeds from the nose, mouth, and ears. DR equal to the victim’s Air Ring in kept dice. The lungs are left so ravaged that breathing is intensely painful: a continuous +10 TN to all actions. This effect cannot end naturally even if damage is healed; any magical healing effect, Kiho, or other supernatural healing restores the lungs and ends the TN penalty.

**Stealing the Soul [CR]**

- Earth 4 | Range: 1 mile | Area: One target person | Duration: 1 day | Raises: Special (+1 additional Rank of Trait drain per 2 Raises)

Requires a fetish or token of the target (lock of hair, drop of blood, personal item, etc.). May be cast as a ritual with multiple casters participating. A hostile kansen forcibly drains 1 Rank from a Trait of the caster’s choice (minimum Rank 1). Each additional maho-tsukai participating in the ritual can drain one additional Trait by the same amount. If this reduces the target’s Earth Ring, their Wounds are lowered, potentially resulting in death.

**Tomb of Earth [EoE]**

- Earth 4 | Range: 50' | Area: One target creature | Duration: Conditional | Raises: Damage (+1k0 per Raise)

Cannot affect anyone with at least 1 full Rank of Taint. Each Round on the caster’s Turn, make an Opposed Roll of Insight/Earth vs the target’s Insight/Air. If the caster wins: the target remains immobilized (cannot take any Actions) and suffers 2k2 damage. If the target wins: the spell ends. If the target is killed, they are turned completely to stone, which crumbles to dust within 24 hours.

**Truth is a Scourge [CR]**

- Air 4 | Range: 50' | Area: One target person | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Duration (+2 minutes per Raise), Range (+10' per Raise), Targets (+1 per Raise)

A kansen distorts the target’s thoughts, making it impossible to lie. Regardless of intent, the target will tell the truth and blurt out any dangerous or damaging secrets. To conceal the truth, the target must make a Willpower roll (TN 30).

**MASTERY LEVEL 5:**

**Blood Armor [EoE]**

- Earth 5 (Defense) | Range: 50' | Area: Caster and one target creature | Duration: 3 Rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 Round per 2 Raises)

When the caster suffers Wounds, they only take 25% of the rolled damage (rounded down). The designated target takes the other 75% (rounded up). Each time damage shifts, the caster glows with unholy reddish-black energy and appropriate injuries spontaneously appear on the target. Works on all Wound damage, even from spells like Jade Strike that normally cannot hurt the caster.

**Fierce Blood of the Earth**

- Earth 5 | Range: Touch | Area: Caster | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Duration (+6 hours per 2 Raises)

Consumes the life force of a touched living being who is willing or physically helpless. Unwilling victims require a Contested Earth Roll. If successful, the victim is killed instantly. The caster heals all injuries, regrows any missing limbs or organs, and extends their life by one year. Repeated use causes the maho-tsukai to gradually acquire a desiccated, mummified appearance.

**Possession [CR]**

- Air 5 | Range: 100' | Area: One target person | Duration: 1 day | Raises: Duration (+1 day per 2 Raises), Range (+25' per Raise), Special (+1k0 to Contested Willpower roll per Raise)

Requires knowing the target’s name and possessing a drop of their blood or a piece of their hair. Caster makes a Contested Willpower Roll against the target. If the target wins: possession fails and the caster suffers 2k1 Wounds. If the caster wins: complete possession of the victim’s body. The caster does not gain the body’s memories, knowledge, or instincts. The possessed body uses its own Physical Traits and Advantages but the caster’s mental Traits, Skills, and School Techniques. The victim experiences events in a dream-like state. If the possessed body is killed, the caster immediately returns to their own body. If the caster’s body is killed during possession, the caster dies when the spell expires (no body to return to).

**Strength of Darkness [CR]**

- Fire 5 | Range: Personal / Touch | Area: One target person | Duration: 10 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 Round per Raise), Special (+1 Rank to one physical Trait per 2 Raises)

Infuses the target with the power of the Taint, enhancing physical prowess. Gain +1 Rank to Earth Ring and the other three physical Traits. Can also see through all visual impairments (fog, smoke, darkness). Obvious and grotesque: flesh swells and bulges unnaturally, eyes turn blood-red. When the spell expires and the Earth Ring drops, Wounds return to normal — potentially lethal if the target sustained heavy damage.

**Touch of Death [CR]**

- Earth 5 | Range: 50' | Area: One target creature | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+10' per Raise), Targets (+1 per 2 Raises)

The most dreaded of all maho spells. Ages, blackens, and blights the target: skin and flesh shrivel and blacken, hair turns gray and falls out, shattering pains rack the body. The victim physically ages 10 years and suffers 7k7 Wounds. Wounds can be healed normally; the aging cannot be reversed. Multiple hits will leave the target elderly and decrepit.

**MASTERY LEVEL 6:**

**Take the Body [EoE]**

- Air 6 | Range: 500' | Area: Caster and one target person | Duration: Permanent | Raises: Range (+500' per Raise), Special (cast as a Simple Action for 4 Raises)

Known only to Iuchiban and Yajinden. Allows the caster to permanently leap their soul into another person’s body. Requires a Contested Willpower roll against the victim. If the victim wins, the caster is hurled back into their current body. If the caster wins: the abandoned body simply falls dead; the caster permanently inhabits the new body. The occupied body retains its Physical Traits and physical Advantages/Disadvantages but instantly acquires the caster’s mental Traits, Void, Skills, and Mental/Spiritual Advantages and Disadvantages. If the maho-tsukai inhabits the body for six months or more, it gradually changes to physically resemble the caster’s original form.
