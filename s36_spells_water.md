# 36. Spells — Water

**MASTERY LEVEL 1:**

**Bo of Water [CR]**

- Water 1 (Craft) | Range: Personal or 20' | Area: 1 created weapon | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Damage (+1k0), Duration (+5 minutes), Range (+5')

Summons a staff of pure water, rigid despite its fluid nature. DR 1k2. Default form is a bo; 1 Raise changes it to any other staff. Caster may use School Rank in place of Staves Skill. If the caster has Staves, grants one Free Raise usable only on the Knockdown Maneuver. Disappears if lost from hand. May appear in an ally's hands within 20' — ally treated as caster but without the Free Raise bonus.

**Clarity of Purpose [CR]**

- Water 1 (Battle) | Range: Personal | Area: 10' radius | Duration: 2 rounds | Raises: Area (+5'), Duration (+1 round per 2 Raises)

All allies within the area of effect gain +5 to their Initiative Scores for the duration.

**Ebbing Strength [CR]**

- Water 1 (Defense) | Range: 20' | Area: 1 target creature | Duration: 3 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round), Range (+5')

Reduces one of the caster's own Physical Traits by up to School Rank; the target gains the same Physical Trait increased by the same amount. If the caster's Trait is reduced to 0, they immediately fall unconscious and the duration is reduced to 1 round. No Trait may be enhanced above double its normal rank by this spell.

**Path to Inner Peace [CR]**

- Water 1 | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: None

Water kami dramatically hasten natural healing. Restores Wounds to the target equal to the amount by which the Spell Casting Roll exceeded the spell's TN.

**Purification of the Kami**

- Water 1 | Range: Touch | Area: Body of water up to 500' radius | Duration: Permanent | Raises: Area (+500' radius per Raise)

Invokes Water kami to purge filth and contamination from a body of water. Renders all water within the area wholly pure and safe to drink, removing dirt, disease, and toxins (cannot purge the Taint). A one-off event — cannot prevent contamination from returning. May also be cast on water in a single container (bucket, barrel, bottle). Purges alcoholic beverages of their flavor and alcohol content, leaving pure water.

**Reflections of Pan Ku [CR]**

- Water 1 (Divination) | Range: Touch | Area: 1 object | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: None

Automatically learn all powers and abilities an object possesses — supernatural qualities (nemuranai, cursed weapons), spells contained in scrolls, etc. Does not allow reading a scroll in an unknown cipher but can identify the spell in question. Also grants broad knowledge of the item's origin (where forged, which Clan carried it longest, etc.) at the GM's discretion.

**Reversal of Fortunes [CR]**

- Water 1 | Range: 10' | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: 3 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round), Range (+5')

For the duration, the target may immediately re-roll any one roll per round. Must be done immediately after the first roll is completed; the target may keep either result.

**Speed of the Waterfall [CR]**

- Water 1 (Travel) | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target individual or creature | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+10 minutes), Range (10' for 2 Raises)

Increases the target's maximum possible movement distance per Round to Water ×20' plus twice the caster's Water Ring. Does not grant additional movement actions — only increases the maximum distance possible.

**Spirit of the Water [CR]**

- Water 1 (Battle) | Range: 20' | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+5'), Special (upgrade additional action to Complex for 5 Raises)

The target gains one additional Simple Action during the Reactions Stage of the Round in which this spell is completed. This Action cannot be used to make an attack.

**Suitengu's Curse**

- Water 1 | Range: 20' | Area: One target creature | Duration: 10 Rounds | Raises: Range (+10' per Raise), Duration (+3 Rounds per Raise)

Invokes Suitengu (Fortune of the Sea) to drag down the target. The target's Reflexes Rank is lowered by 1 and they move as though their Water is 1 Rank lower for the duration.

**Sympathetic Energies [CR]**

- Water 1 | Range: 25' | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+5'), Special (see below)

Transfer any one existing spell effect from the caster to a willing target. With 3 Raises, transfer 1 spell effect from one willing living target to another willing target. Cannot transfer spells between unwilling targets.

**The Rushing Wave [CR]**

- Water 1 (Travel) | Range: 10' | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: 1 round | Raises: Duration (+1 round), Range (+5')

Allows the target to make a Free Move Action of up to Water Ring ×10' (instead of the normal ×5'). Without Raises for Duration, the benefit must be used on the target's next Turn or it is lost.

**The Swell of the Storm**

- Water 1 (Defense) | Range: 25' | Area: One target creature | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+10' per Raise), Area (+1 target per 2 Raises), Special (+1k0 to Contested Roll per Raise)

Summons a sudden blast of water that strikes with the heavy power of the tide. Target must make a Contested Strength vs caster's Water roll — failure means Knockdown (Prone). Also extinguishes any open flames the target carries (torches, lanterns, candles). Can affect Huge creatures (GM may award bonus on the Contested Roll).

**MASTERY LEVEL 2:**

**Cloak of the Miya [CR]**

- Water 2 (Defense) | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 5 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round)

Wraps the caster in the protective embrace of the wave. Armor TN increased by Water Ring + School Rank for the duration.

**Heaven's Tears**

- Water 2 (Crystal) | Range: Centered on caster | Area: 30' radius | Duration: 2 Rounds | Raises: Area (+5' radius per Raise), Duration (+1 Round for 2 Raises, once only)

Outdoors only. Summons a brief deluge of spiritually pure rain. Those of pure soul (no Taint or Shadow corruption, Honor 4.0+) are healed each Round by Wounds equal to the caster's Water Ring. Those with Taint or Shadow corruption suffer 1k1 Wounds per Round of exposure.

**Inari's Blessing [CR]**

- Water 2 (Craft) | Range: Personal | Area: Created sustenance | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Special (+1 person's food per Raise)

Invokes Inari (Fortune of Rice) to create nourishing food and drink for School Rank +1 individuals for one day. Without Raises: bland but sustaining (unflavored rice and water). With Raises: quality improves (seafood, tea, etc. at GM's discretion). TN to cast is doubled in the Shadowlands.

**Judgment of Yomi**

- Water 2 | Range: 50' | Area: One individual | Duration: Concentration | Raises: Range (+10'), Targets (+1)

Brings the attention of the Realm of Blessed Ancestors onto the target. If human: for every Social or Spiritual Disadvantage the target possesses, they suffer -1k0 to all physical Skill checks. Additionally, they must make an Honor check (TN 20) or be unable to move any closer to the caster than the spell's maximum range (but not pushed back if already closer). Penalties last one full Round or while the caster maintains concentration.

**Reflective Pool [CR]**

- Water 2 (Divination) | Range: 10 miles | Area: Body of water / target location | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Duration (+1 minute), Range (+10 miles)

Gaze into any body of water (even a puddle) and view a familiar location as if present — visual only, no audio. A location is familiar if the caster spent considerable time there or meditated there for at least 10 minutes. Images are clearer in larger bodies of water.

**Rejuvenating Vapors [CR]**

- Water 2 | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Targets (+1 per 2 Raises)

Instantly refreshes the target as if they had just arisen from a full night's sleep. Eliminates Fatigue and exhaustion but does not restore Void Points. When used on a shugenja, restores spell slots associated with the Void Ring only (not other Ring slots). Abilities limited to a number of uses per day are not refreshed. No individual can benefit more than once per 24 hours.

**Stand Against the Waves [CR]**

- Water 2 (Battle) | Range: 10' | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+10' per 3 Raises)

The target gains a Simple Action during the Reaction Stage of the current Combat Round usable only to make an attack. If incapable of attacking as a Simple Action, grants a Complex Action instead. Cannot grant a shugenja the ability to cast a second spell in the same Round.

**Strength of the Tsunami**

- Water 2 | Range: Touch | Area: Caster | Duration: 3 Rounds | Raises: Area (cast on someone else for 2 Raises), Duration (+1 Round per Raise)

A surge of Water kami briefly enhances the target's muscles. Strength Rank increased by half the caster's Water Ring (rounded down). Cannot raise Strength above 9.

**Surging Soul**

- Water 2 (Battle) | Range: 10' | Area: One target person | Duration: 3 Rounds | Raises: Range (+10' per Raise), Duration (+1 Round per 2 Raises)

Unleashes a surge of chi through the target's limbs. The target gains +1k1 to all Attack rolls but cannot enter or benefit from the Center Stance and must take at least one Move Action every Round for the duration.

**The Ties That Bind [CR]**

- Water 2 (Divination) | Range: 10 miles | Area: Self | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+2 miles)

Seek out the Water spirits within one specific object the caster is familiar with (has spent considerable time around or handled personally). If within range, instantly know the direction and relative distance of the object from the caster's current position.

**Wave-Borne Speed [CR]**

- Water 2 (Travel) | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 2 Rounds | Raises: None

Water Ring increased by 2 for the purposes of determining movement distance for any Move Actions made during the present Round and the following Round.

**Wisdom ****&**** Clarity [CR]**

- Water 2 | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+30 minutes), Targets (target another for 2 Raises)

By entreating the insight of Water kami, the caster's reading speed doubles and they have perfect recall of everything read while under the spell's influence. Does not enhance comprehension — unknown languages or ciphers remain indecipherable.

**Yuki's Touch**

- Water 2 (Travel) | Range: Touch | Area: Body of water 100' radius from caster | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Area (+50' radius per Raise)

Named after the Yuki no Onna. Persuades Water spirits to expel all Fire, causing a natural body of water to instantly freeze solid to 100' from the caster. Persons or creatures in the water are trapped; those beneath the surface suffocate (treat as Drowning). Breaking free requires a Contested Strength vs caster's Water roll. Ice melts normally.

**MASTERY LEVEL 3:**

**Endless Deluge**

- Water 3 | Range: Centered on caster | Area: 1 mile diameter | Duration: 12 hours | Raises: Area (+1 mile per 2 Raises), Duration (+6 hours per Raise)

A ritual (minimum 10 minutes to cast; dry regions or drought may require more time or Raises). Water spirits in the sky congregate and unleash a massive rainstorm. Chief use: extinguish fires (large conflagrations doused within ~30 minutes) and abate drought. Side effects: -1k0 to all physical actions, -2k0 to ranged attacks, -1k1 to Fire Spell Casting Rolls within the storm. May cause flash flooding (GM's option).

**Near to Ice [CR]**

- Water 3 | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: 5 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round), Range (10' for 2 Raises)

Water within the target becomes sterner and more durable. All current Wound Penalties are negated for the duration. Any additional penalties incurred still take effect, but only the difference between the negated penalty and the new penalty applies. Wounds are not healed — they simply cease to inhibit.

**Regrow the Wound [CR]**

- Water 3 | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: Concentration | Raises: Special (Water Ring +1 for healing purposes per Raise)

Water flows out, carrying pain and injury with it into the infinite ocean. Target recovers Wounds equal to Water Ring + School Rank each Round the spell is maintained. Must touch the target when the spell is first cast; after that it may be maintained without physical contact.

**Sanctuary of the Waves**

- Water 3 (Defense) | Range: 50' | Area: One target person | Duration: 10 Rounds | Raises: Duration (+3 Rounds per Raise), Area (+1 person per Raise)

Can only be cast within range of a large natural body of water (pond, stream, or larger). Water lunges out, scoops up willing targets, and conceals them beneath the surface. Targets have enough air to breathe for the duration only. Completely protected from outside attacks but also completely cut off — cannot see what is happening above water without a Perception roll (TN 20) to make out blurry images. When the spell expires, the water releases the targets where they are — they must swim to the surface on their own.

**Silent Waters [CR]**

- Water 3 (Defense) | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: Variable | Raises: None

Upon successful casting, immediately cast a second spell of any element (Mastery Level 3 or lower) that the caster can normally cast. If that Spell Casting Roll succeeds, the second spell is stored and will not activate until a specific physical trigger (set at the time of casting: a spoken word, drawing a blade, falling in battle, etc.). When the trigger occurs, the stored spell activates immediately as if just completed. Only one Silent Waters effect may be active at a time — a new casting dispels and replaces the current stored spell.

**Strike of the Tsunami [CR]**

- Water 3 (Battle) | Range: 25' | Area: Cone originating from caster, 10' wide at end | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Damage (+1k0), Range (+5'), Special (+5 to Earth Roll TN per Raise)

Summons a crushing wave of water. Inflicts 3k3 Wounds on everything within the area. All opponents within the area must make an Earth Roll (TN 15) or suffer Knockdown. Everything within the area is saturated with water.

**The Inner Ocean**

- Water 3 | Range: 50' | Area: One willing person | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Range (+20' per Raise), Duration (+1 minute per Raise)

The target (may be the caster) is physically transformed into pure clear water, along with anything worn or carried. While water: cannot speak, attack, cast spells, or directly manipulate objects. Can move more quickly (Move Actions as though Water Ring is +1), indirectly push objects, and flow through narrow openings (mass does not change). Effectively immune to most physical harm but Fire spells can damage them, as can intense heat (burning castle, lava flow). Certain physical events (being scattered, partially soaked up) may also harm them (GM's option). Returns to normal when the spell expires.

**Typhoon's Surge**

- Water 3 | Range: 50' | Area: Up to Water Rank target allies | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+10' per Raise), Area (+1 target beyond Water Rank per 2 Raises)

Targets up to Water Rank allies with a sudden surge of invigorating chi. All targets are freed from the Fatigued and Dazed conditions and each heals Wounds equal to the caster's Water Rank.

**Visions of the Future [CR]**

- Water 3 (Divination) | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 1 minute | Raises: None

Requires gazing into a large pool of still water. Upon completion, the caster enters a brief trance and sees symbolic images of things not yet come to pass. The events foreseen are completely accurate and will occur unless directly prevented by the caster's intervention. Only the casting shugenja may see the visions. First developed by the Tonbo family of the Dragonfly Clan; rare outside their ranks. Many shugenja regard use of this spell as thwarting the natural order.

**Walking Upon the Waves [CR]**

- Water 3 (Travel) | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Duration (+1 minute), Targets (+1 per Raise)

Water kami buoy the target, granting passage across the surface of water as if it were solid ground (Basic Terrain). If the water surface is disturbed (storm, rolling waves, etc.) it counts as Difficult Terrain.

**Water Kami's Blessing [CR]**

- Water 3 | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: 5 rounds | Raises: Special (Water Ring considered 1 Rank higher per 2 Raises)

Calls upon the clarity of Water to dramatically increase the target's perception. Target gains additional rolled dice equal to the caster's Water Ring on all Perception-based rolls (Trait rolls, Skill rolls, or anything else Perception-based).

**MASTERY LEVEL 4:**

**Dominion of Suitengu [CR]**

- Water 4 (Divination) | Range: 100 miles | Area: 2 bodies of water | Duration: Concentration | Raises: Range (+10 miles)

Created by the Mantis Clan. Gaze into any body of water at least 1 foot across and 1 inch deep to see out of any other known body of water within range. Can see everything around the target body of water as if submerged within it. Visual only — no audio.

**Ebb ****&**** Flow of Battle [CR]**

- Water 4 (Battle) | Range: Personal | Area: All selected allies within 50' radius | Duration: 5 Rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round per 2 Raises)

Increases the movement speed of all chosen allies within range (specific allies can be excluded). All affected may move Water Ring ×10' as a Free Action (this is normally a Simple Action).

**Heart of the Water Dragon [CR]**

- Water 4 | Range: 25' | Area: Targets equal to caster's School Rank (may include caster) | Duration: 1 round per School Rank | Raises: Duration (+1 round), Special (+1k0 healed per 2 Raises)

The Water Dragon's blessing. Any time a target of this spell suffers damage during the duration, they instantly regain 1k1 Wounds.

**Master of the Rolling River**

- Water 4 (Battle) | Range: 100' | Area: Up to 25 allies in one group | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Range (+50' per Raise), Duration (+1 minute per Raise), Special (affect non-samurai for 2 Raises, non-humans for 4 Raises)

Devised by Kitsu and Iuchi battle shugenja. Infuses a military unit with the flowing speed and strength of Water. All troops in the unit may move as though their Water is 1 Rank higher and are considered to have Strength 1 Rank higher. Also usable in Mass Battle: each casting awards a bonus equal to the caster's Water Ring to the commanding general's next Battle Skill roll to determine winning or losing.

**The Mirror's Smile**

- Water 4 (Kolat) | Range: Touch | Area: Caster | Duration: 1 Day | Raises: Special (cast after seeing target's face only once for 4 Raises)

Water kami physically re-mold the caster's face to exactly match another person's (actual change to flesh and bone, not an illusion). Requires familiarity with the target's face (at least 8 hours of study, or 4 Raises if seen only once). Even close friends and family will believe the caster is the target. Only affects the face — not the rest of the body, hair, or voice. Takes several minutes and is a painful experience.

**Seed of Qanan**

- Water 4 | Range: Touch | Area: Any two people | Duration: 5 Rounds | Raises: Duration (+2 Rounds per Raise)

A Unicorn secret spell based on gaijin magic. The caster must simultaneously touch both targets to establish a link of Water kami. The linked people may use each other's Skills, employing whichever Skill Rank is higher for a given task. Mastery Abilities are not gained. The link breaks if the targets exceed 50' from each other.

**Steed of the Ebbing Tides**

- Water 4 (Travel) | Range: 25' | Area: One summoned steed | Duration: 4 hours | Raises: Duration (+2 hours per Raise)

Devised by the Iuchi; the Isawa have since created a similar spell independently. Summons powerful Water kami in the form of a watery, translucent horse. Bears the caster or one designated rider, controlled with Horsemanship normally, and fights selflessly in its rider's defense. Mechanically equal to an Utaku Warhorse but with Reduction 15. Untiring for the duration. Collapses into normal water when the spell expires.

**Strike of the Flowing Waters [CR]**

- Water 4 | Range: 10' | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: 1 round | Raises: Duration (+1 Round), Range (+5')

The target may ignore Armor TN bonuses from worn armor, spell effects of Mastery Level 3 and below, and other non-Technique mechanical effects. Against non-human creatures that do not wear armor, treats their Armor TN as 5 lower. Does not negate Reduction from armor or Armor TN bonuses from Defense or Full Defense Stances.

**Symbol of Water [CR]**

- Water 4 (Wards) | Range: Touch | Area: Special | Duration: Permanent | Raises: None

Inscribes a ward on a passageway. Those passing through must make a Contested Fire vs caster's Water roll. Those who fail must immediately roll against a Fear 7 effect. Only one Symbol of Water may exist at a time. Cannot overlap with other element Symbols. Dispelled by another Symbol of Water casting or by destroying the inscribed surface.

**The Emperor's Road**

- Water 4 (Travel, Imperial) | Range: Touch | Area: One Imperial road up to 10 miles | Duration: 8 hours | Raises: Area (+1 mile per Raise)

Devised by the Seppun to ease the Emperor's journeys. Creates an aura of friendly Water spirits along a specific Imperial road. The road is cleared of minor obstacles. Honorable travelers loyal to the Emperor move as though their Water is 2 Ranks higher while on the road and do not become Fatigued from travel on it.

**The Path Not Taken [CR]**

- Water 4 | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 1 day | Raises: Special (+1 bonus spell slot per 5 Raises)

Select one Ring to be temporarily weakened and one to be temporarily strengthened. Transfer any number of unused daily spell slots from the weakened Ring to the strengthened Ring. Effects last exactly one day, then are lost.

**Within the Waves**

- Water 4 (Travel) | Range: Self | Area: 10' diameter sphere | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+1/2 hour per Raise), Special (add one additional person inside the bubble for 3 Raises)

Water kami hold themselves away from the caster in a spherical formation, creating an air bubble. The caster can move the bubble by concentrating, exploring freely beneath the surface of a river, lake, or ocean (movement at normal Water-based speed). Other persons can enter and leave the bubble by swimming. When the spell's duration expires, the Water kami push the bubble to the surface.

**MASTERY LEVEL 5:**

**Chi Reversal**

- Water 5 | Range: 20' | Area: One target person/creature | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Range (+20' per Raise), Duration (+1/2 hour per Raise), Special (+1 pair of Traits per 2 Raises)

Invokes Water's control of chi flow to flip one pair of the target's mental and physical Traits from the same Element (caster's choice) for the duration. Example: if Fire Traits are chosen, an Intelligence 2 / Agility 4 becomes Intelligence 4 / Agility 2. Additional pairs of Traits may be affected with Raises.

**Ever-Changing Waves [CR]**

- Water 5 (Illusion) | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+1 hour)

Allows the caster to physically alter their mortal body, changing shape to match any other natural creature. Keeps Mental Traits. For Physical Traits, keeps whichever is higher (own or the creature's). Gains other natural abilities including natural weapons and sensory abilities. Some traditionalist shugenja sects consider this unclean.

**The Final Bond [CR]**

- Water 5 (Divination) | Range: Special | Area: 1 target object or individual | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: None

Detects the location of one object or individual well known to the caster, regardless of where they are. Object must be one the caster has spent tremendous time around or handled frequently. Individual must be well known (immediate family, close friend). Instantly know their approximate location with enough detail to significantly narrow any search (e.g. 'the city of Ryoko Owari Toshi'). Automatically fails if the target is outside the Empire of Rokugan.

**Hands of the Tides [CR]**

- Water 5 (Battle, Travel) | Range: 100' radius centered on caster | Area: Targets up to caster's Water Ring | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Area (+10'), Targets (+1 per 2 Raises)

Within the area of effect, switch the positions of up to Water Ring willing targets in any combination. At the end of the spell, there must be a person in every position that was occupied when the spell began, but who occupies which position can vary depending on the number of targets affected.

**Open the Waves**

- Water 5 (Battle) | Range: 300' | Area: Trench up to 30' wide, 30' deep, 300' long | Duration: 15 minutes | Raises: Area (+5' to width or depth per Raise), Duration (+5 minutes per Raise)

Calls Water kami to hold themselves apart, creating a trench through a body of water to allow passage of men, animals, wagons, or war machines. Used primarily to help armies cross river obstacles but has also been used to help peasants escape floods. Cannot always clear water all the way to the bottom — there are limits to what can be asked of the kami.

**Power of the Ocean [CR]**

- Water 5 (Defense) | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: Days equal to School Rank | Raises: Duration (+1 day per 3 Raises)

A complex 1-hour ritual cast only on a willing target. For the duration: target requires no food, drink, or sleep. School Rank times during the duration, the target may replenish Void Points as a Simple Action (equivalent to a full night's rest). Target recovers Wounds per hour equal to twice the caster's Water Ring. If the target is a shugenja, they regain all expended spell slots at sunrise regardless of rest. After the spell expires: the target lapses into complete exhaustion for exactly half the duration of the spell effect, unable to take significant physical actions or travel.

**Suitengu's Embrace [CR]**

- Water 5 (Thunder) | Range: 25' | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+5')

Fills the target's lungs with seawater. On success, the target can take no actions other than attempting to resist. Treated as Down for all action purposes. Each Round the target must make a Stamina Roll (TN 15). Three total successes: one additional Round of vomiting water, then fully recovered. Two consecutive failures: the target falls unconscious and will die within 1 minute without magical or medical intervention. Can be countered with Medicine/Intelligence (TN 50).

**Whirlpool**

- Water 5 (Thunder) | Range: 1 mile | Area: Whirlpool 200' radius | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Area (+25' radius per Raise), Duration (+2 minutes per Raise)

Credited to the Yoritomo. Can only be cast near or on a large body of open water (lake or sea). Water kami are excited into a tremendous rage, creating a massive whirlpool. Swimmers in the area must roll Athletics (Swimming)/Strength (TN 30) each Round or be sucked under. Ships in the area must roll Navigation/Intelligence (TN 30) every minute to avoid being dragged down. Once underwater, victims are subject to Drowning and must roll Athletics (Swimming)/Strength (TN 40) to reach the surface.

**MASTERY LEVEL 6:**

**Breath of Mist**

- Water 6 (Defense) | Range: 1/2 mile | Area: 200' radius circle | Duration: 30 minutes | Raises: Area (+25' radius per Raise), Duration (+10 minutes per Raise)

Evokes hundreds of Water kami to reduce the ground to a half-liquid mire while filling the air with water vapor to obscure vision. The ground within the area becomes Difficult Terrain. Fog reduces normal visibility to 10'. Between 10' and 30', characters discern vague outlines (+15 TN penalty on ranged attacks). Beyond 30', all characters are effectively Blind. Entire armies have been rendered effectively helpless by this spell.

**Opening the Veil**

- Water 6 (Travel) | Range: 10' | Area: One spirit portal | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+10 minutes per Raise), Special (Sakkaku or Yume-do for 1 Raise, Gaki-do or Toshigoku for 2 Raises, Yomi for 4 Raises)

Known only to the Kitsu and Isawa. Invokes Water's Travel aspect to open a temporary portal into the Spirit Realms. Default access: Meido or Chikushudo. With sufficient Raises, can access Yomi, Sakkaku, Toshigoku, Yume-do, or Gaki-do. The portal lasts only a short time — passage through it is fraught with risk. Cannot function in areas warded against Spirit Realm influences (GM's discretion).

**Peace of the Kami [CR]**

- Water 6 | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: None

The ultimate benevolence of the Water Dragon. The target is instantly cured of all diseases, their system completely purged of all poisons, and all Wounds are completely healed.

**Rise, Water [CR]**

- Water 6 | Range: 30' | Area: One summoned spirit | Duration: Concentration | Raises: None

Summons a massive Water kami in a 10-foot vaguely humanoid shape with a constantly shifting fluid outline. Moves up to 15' × Water Ring per Round. Saturates the ground in a 20' radius, ensuring all terrain in that area is at least Moderate (unless already Difficult). All Physical Traits equal to caster's Water Ring. Attacks with Jiujutsu Skill Rank equal to half the caster's Water Ring; DR equal to Water Ring. Wounds as a human with Earth equal to the caster's Water Ring but no Wound Penalties. Invulnerable. Dispelled at zero Wounds.

**Water's Sweet Clarity [CR]**

- Water 6 (Divination) | Range: Personal | Area: Target body of water | Duration: 3 rounds | Raises: None

Focus on a still body of water and invoke powerful visions of the future in response to one question. The answer appears as three separate images, which may be connected in various ways (events over time, three facets of a single event, etc.). Unlike other augury, others may also witness these visions.
