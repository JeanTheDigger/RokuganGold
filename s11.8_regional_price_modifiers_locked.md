## 11.8 Regional Price Modifiers — LOCKED

Source: Chapter Six (Tables 6.2–6.9). Explicit price and availability modifiers for goods in each clan’s territory, applied at the character level when purchasing or selling items. Integration rule: see below.

- Crab: Weapons −20% (common), Food +20%, Spices +50% (rare), Iron/Steel −20%.

- Crane: Silk −10% (common), Fine Paper −10% (average), Small Paintings −30%, Tea Sets −10%.

- Dragon: Food +20%, Iron/Steel −20%, Tattoo Needles −30%, Divination Kit −30%.

- Lion: All armor/weapons −10% (common), Brazier −20%.

- Mantis: Spices −30% (common), Food +20%, Iron +20%, Jade Finger +50% (rare).

- Phoenix: Fine Paper −30% (common), Medicine Kit −30%, All weapons +20% (rare).

- Scorpion: Food −30% (common), Dice −40%, Jade Finger −10%.

- Unicorn: Books +20% (rare), Jade −10%, Yumi −20%.

**⚙️ Integration Rule — LOCKED. The clan territory modifiers above apply at the character level when a named character purchases or sells an item within that clan’s territory. The modifier adjusts the base market price of the listed item category before any other modifiers are applied. Resolution order: (1) apply clan territory modifier to base price; (2) apply Commerce skill purchase price reduction if the character makes a successful Commerce + Intelligence roll vs TN 15 (Section 51). The territory of the transaction is the sub-tile where the character is physically located when the purchase occurs — buying a yumi while passing through Unicorn territory costs −20%, regardless of the buyer’s own clan. These modifiers do not affect the world-map resource simulation (Rice, Iron, Koku, PU). They apply only to character-level item purchases through the Commerce system (Section 51) and are relevant to gift appraisal, equipment acquisition, and trade skill checks.**
