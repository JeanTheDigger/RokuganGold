## 29.1 Crab Clan Schools

**BASIC SCHOOLS:**

**Hida Bushi [CR]:**

Endure and crush. Shaped by centuries of fighting Shadowlands creatures — emphasizes damage mitigation and heavy weapon use. Not the most accurate warriors in the Empire, but devastating when they connect and nearly impossible to put down.

- Benefit: +1 Stamina

- Skills: Athletics, Defense, Heavy Weapons (Tetsubo), Intimidation, Kenjutsu, Lore: Shadowlands, any one Bugei Skill

- Honor: 3.5 | Outfit: Light or Heavy Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, Heavy Weapon or Polearm, Traveling Pack, 3 koku

- Rank 1 — The Way of the Crab: Ignore TN penalties for heavy armor (except Stealth). +1k0 to damage rolls with Heavy Weapons.

- Rank 2 — The Mountain Does Not Move: Gain Reduction equal to Earth Ring.

- Rank 3 — Two Pincers, One Mind: Attack as Simple Action with Heavy Weapons or Samurai keyword weapons.

- Rank 4 — Devastating Blow: Once per encounter with Heavy Weapon — lower enemy Reduction by 4, Daze target on hit.

- Rank 5 — The Mountain Does Not Fall: Spend Void Point — act as if Healthy for one Turn, ignore Dazed/Fatigued/Stunned.

**Hida Pragmatist [Bushi] [EE]:**

Unarmed combat specialists from the Kobo Ichi-Kai dojo at Clear Water Village. Developed to fight inhuman Shadowlands opponents without weapons. Effective yojimbo — can leave weapons at the door with minimal loss of effectiveness.

- Benefit: +1 Agility

- Skills: Athletics, Defense, Jiujutsu (Improvised Weapons) 2, Kenjutsu, Lore: Shadowlands, any one Bugei Skill

- Honor: 2.5 | Outfit: Light or Heavy Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, Heavy Weapon or Polearm, Traveling Pack, 3 koku

- Rank 1 — The Eternal Stone Unleashed: +1k0 to resist Intimidation/Fear. +1k0 attack and damage unarmed/improvised (must keep high dice).

- Rank 2 — Wearing Down the Mountain: Extra Attack Maneuver costs only 3 Raises unarmed/improvised.

- Rank 3 — Fury of the Avalanche: Attack as Simple Action unarmed/improvised or Samurai keyword weapons.

- Rank 4 — Stone Turns Steel Aside: Use Jiujutsu to turn enemy's weapon against them — Contested Roll, strike with their own weapon.

- Rank 5 — Fight to the End: Spend Void Point — make one attack ignoring all Wound penalties, Status effects, and physical Disadvantages. +3k1 damage.

**Hiruma Bushi [CR]:**

Speed, avoidance, and targeted strikes over endurance. Shaped for survival in the Shadowlands — move like smoke, strike like lightning. Excel at eliminating priority targets while allies handle rank-and-file opponents.

- Benefit: +1 Willpower

- Skills: Athletics, Hunting, Kenjutsu (Katana), Kyujutsu, Lore: Shadowlands, Stealth, any one Skill

- Honor: 4.5 | Outfit: Ashigaru or Light Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, Bow with 20 Arrows or Knife, Traveling Pack, 3 koku

- Rank 1 — Torch's Flame Flickers: +1k0 to attack rolls in Attack Stance. Food/water/jade rations last twice as long for Hunting Rank people.

- Rank 2 — Wolf's Little Lesson: In Attack Stance, +5 Armor TN per melee hit (stacks up to School Rank times).

- Rank 3 — Hummingbird Wings: Once per Round — gain double School Rank bonus to Armor TN against one attack.

- Rank 4 — Shark Smells Blood: Attack as Simple Action with Samurai keyword weapons.

- Rank 5 — Daylight Wastes No Movement: Excess Wounds from overkill carry over to next target hit.

**Hiruma Scout [Bushi] [IH]:**

Developed after the fall of Hiruma Castle when ancient techniques were lost. Based on Shinjo scout methods adapted for Shadowlands survival — stealth and speed over direct combat. Taught alongside the recovered Hiruma Bushi school in modern times.

- Benefit: +1 Reflexes

- Skills: Athletics, Hunting, Kenjutsu, Kyujutsu, Lore: Shadowlands, Stealth (Sneaking), any one Bugei Skill

- Honor: 4.5 | Outfit: Ashigaru or Light Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, Bow with 20 Arrows or Knife, Traveling Pack, 3 koku

- Rank 1 — Dance the Razor's Edge: Add Stealth rank to Initiative. Rations last twice as long. In Shadowlands: +1k0 Stealth, always know direction of Empire.

- Rank 2 — Run Like the Wind: Free and Simple Move actions in Full Defense. Run at high speed for Stamina + School Rank hours.

- Rank 3 — Veil of the Spirits: Spend Void Point while stationary with cover — add School Rank kept dice to Stealth. Lasts until movement or noise.

- Rank 4 — Harness the Wind: Attack as Simple Action with Samurai weapons, knives, or bows. Also Simple Action against any Shadowlands/Tainted foe.

- Rank 5 — Strike of the Stalker: Against surprised/unaware foes — Raises not limited by Void, ignore 10 points of Reduction.

**Kaiu Engineer [Artisan/Bushi] [GC]:**

Master craftsmen of the Empire — construction, siegecraft, arms and armor manufacture. Many also serve as battlefield commanders. The Kaiu Method is applied to every School Skill.

- Benefit: +1 Intelligence

- Skills: Battle, Craft: Armorsmithing, Craft: Weaponsmithing, Defense, Engineering (pick one Emphasis), Lore: Architecture, War Fans

- Honor: 4.5 | Outfit: Light or Heavy Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, Heavy Weapon or War Fan, Traveling Pack, 3 koku

- Rank 1 — The Kaiu Method: +1k0 to all School Skill rolls. Void Points on School Skills grant +2k2 instead of +1k1.

- Rank 2 — The Path of Stone: Engineering roll (TN 25) adds School Rank ×100 Wounds to large structures. Re-roll siege engine damage dice below School Rank.

- Rank 3 — The Path of the Shell: Double crafting time/cost to add School Rank to armor Reduction and half School Rank to Armor TN bonus.

- Rank 4 — The Path of War: Modify Mass Battle result by half School Rank additional. Attack as Simple Action with katana, dai tsuchi, or war fan.

- Rank 5 — The Path of Steel: Crafted weapons gain either +1k0 attack or +0k1 damage. Katana can be made unbreakable by expending all Void Points (no Void recovery for one week).

**Kuni Shugenja [CR]:**

Obsessive researchers who sacrifice inhibitions to serve the clan. Specialize in fighting Shadowlands creatures — bonus damage against Tainted targets, advantage with Jade spells. Earth affinity reflects their connection to endurance and purification.

- Benefit: +1 Willpower

- Skills: Calligraphy (Cipher), Defense, Lore: Shadowlands 2, Lore: Theology, Spellcraft, any one Weapon Skill

- Honor: 2.5 | Outfit: Robes, Wakizashi, Knife, Scroll Satchel, Traveling Pack, 3 koku

- Affinity/Deficiency: Earth / Air

- Starting Spells: Sense, Commune, Summon, 3 Earth, 2 Fire, 1 Water

- Technique — Gaze Into Shadow: +1k0 on Spell Casting Rolls targeting non-human creatures. Damage spells deal +1k1 against Shadowlands Tainted targets. Free Raise on Jade keyword spells.

**Kuni Witch-Hunter [Monk] [GC]:**

Elite hunters of maho-tsukai and Shadowlands infiltrators. Travel freely through Rokugan disregarding clan borders. Feared and respected in equal measure — their appearance often signals Taint nearby.

- Benefit: +1 Willpower

- Skills: Investigation, Jiujutsu, Kenjutsu, Lore: Shadowlands, Lore: Maho, Meditation, any one Skill

- Honor: 5.5 | Outfit: Rugged Clothing, Daisho, Knife, Jade Pendant, Traveling Pack, 3 koku

- Rank 1 — To See the Darkness: Detect Taint with jade pendant — Contested Investigation/Awareness vs Sincerity/Willpower, +1k0 per Taint Rank. +1k1 to resist Taint and attack Tainted/Shadowlands foes.

- Rank 2 — To Ride the Darkness: Free Action Lore: Shadowlands roll (TN 20) to recall one strength or weakness of any Shadowlands creature.

- Rank 3 — To Strike the Darkness: Attack as Simple Action against Shadowlands creatures or known Tainted foes.

- Rank 4 — To Repel the Darkness: +3k0 to Taint detection and creature knowledge rolls. Learn one Kiho.

- Rank 5 — To Shatter the Darkness: +4k1 to attack and damage against Shadowlands creatures and known Tainted foes (stacks with Rank 1).

**Toritaka Bushi [CR]:**

Specialized warriors against creatures from other Spirit Realms — oni, ghosts, hungry dead. Fluid style to adapt to unpredictable supernatural enemies. Less effective against human opponents but devastating against their intended targets.

- Benefit: +1 Strength

- Skills: Animal Handling (Falcons), Hunting 2, Kenjutsu, Etiquette, Lore: Spirit Realms, Spears, any one Skill

- Honor: 5.5 | Outfit: Light Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, any 1 weapon, Traveling Pack, 3 koku

- Rank 1 — The Falcon's Eyes: +1k0 to all Perception Trait rolls. +1k0 damage against Spirit Realm creatures (oni, hungry dead, etc.).

- Rank 2 — The Falcon Takes Flight: Detect nearby Spirit Realm creatures — Perception roll vs Air Ring ×5.

- Rank 3 — The Falcon's Wings: Attack as Simple Action with spear or Samurai keyword weapons.

- Rank 4 — Vigilant and Strong: Spend Void Point to negate darkness/blinding penalties for two Rounds.

- Rank 5 — Claws of the Falcon: Attacks ignore up to 5 Reduction. Against Spirit Realm creatures, spend Void Point to reduce their Reduction by 10 for one Round.

**Yasuki Courtier [CR]:**

Masters of commerce as a political weapon. Originally Crane, now the Crab's merchants and diplomats. Find what opponents need and provide it — the most effective supply chain in the Empire. Use pressure, false sincerity, and commercial leverage as court weapons.

- Benefit: +1 Perception

- Skills: Commerce (Appraisal), Courtier, Defense, Etiquette, Intimidation, Sincerity (Deceit), any one Merchant Skill

- Honor: 2.5 | Outfit: Traditional Clothing, Wakizashi, Knife, Calligraphy Set, Traveling Pack, 5 koku

- Rank 1 — The Way of the Carp: Free Raise on Commerce. No Honor/Glory loss for public commerce. Contested Commerce/Perception roll to discern what someone wants.

- Rank 2 — Do As We Say: Re-roll failed Sincerity or Intimidation rolls a number of times per session equal to School Rank.

- Rank 3 — Treasures of the Carp: Commerce/Awareness roll (TN 20) to locate rare or useful items for others. Raises for higher quality items.

- Rank 4 — Wiles of the Carp: Anyone lying to you has their Social Skill TN increased by 5 × School Rank.

- Rank 5 — What is Yours is Mine: If you arranged for someone to get something they need, +5k0 to all Contested Social Rolls against them for 24 hours.

**ADVANCED SCHOOLS:**

**Defender of the Wall [Bushi] [CR]:**

The perfect guardian — will let the world end before allowing an attacker to pass. Requires Earth 4, Strength 5, Defense 4, Heavy Weapons 4, Lore: Shadowlands 5.

- Rank 1 — The Flames of Purity: Shadowlands Tainted creatures cannot re-roll 10s on attack or damage rolls against you.

- Rank 2 — Hida's Strength: Gain Reduction 8 (stacks with all other Reduction sources).

- Rank 3 — The Crab are the Wall: Once per Round, immediately negate one Condition on yourself (except Mounted or Grappled).

**ALTERNATE PATHS:**

**Crab Berserker [Bushi] [CR]:**

Replaces: Any Crab Bushi 2. Requires: Earth 4. Manifests after time on the Kaiu Wall. Technique — Berserker's Rage: Once per skirmish, enter rage lasting Earth Ring ×2 Rounds. Cannot take Center/Defense/Full Defense stances or use non-Bugei skills. Ignore all Wound Rank penalties. +2k1 attack and damage. If killed while raging, remain alive until rage ends — cannot be healed.

**Crab Defender [Bushi] [BoA]:**

Replaces: Hida Bushi 2 or Hiruma Bushi 2. Requires: Awareness 3, Agility 3, Iaijutsu 4. Unbreakable Blade Dojo — endure the first blow to survive and deliver your own. Technique — Warrior of Earth: During Strike step of iaijutsu duel, gain Reduction equal to Earth ×3.

**Crab Knife-Fighter [Bushi] [BoF]:**

Replaces: Hida Bushi 4, Hida Pragmatist 4, Hiruma Bushi 3, or Hiruma Scout 3. Requires: Knives 3. Favored by Yasuki sailors. Technique — One Blade, Both Hands: Empty hand and tanto — +3k1 tanto damage, ignore armor effects. Against creatures: ignore 5 points inherent Reduction.

**Crab Sumai Wrestler [Bushi] [BoE]:**

Replaces: Hida Bushi 2 or Yasuki Courtier 2. Requires: Jiujutsu (Sumai) 4. Grip of Earth Dojo. Special: Removes Small disadvantage or grants Large at no cost. Technique — The Way of Sumai: +1k0 (or +2k0 if Large) to control Grapple. +1k1 to damage in Grapple.

**Falcon's Strike [Bushi] [BoA]:**

Replaces: Toritaka Bushi 2, Hiruma Scout 2, or Hiruma Bushi 2. Requires: Kyujutsu (Yumi) 3. Technique — Spotting the Prey: Use Perception instead of Reflexes for ranged attack rolls with bows. With yumi, also use Perception instead of bow Strength for damage rolls.

**Hiruma Slayer [Bushi] [BoE]:**

Replaces: Hiruma Bushi 4 or Hiruma Scout 4. Requires: Heavy Weapons (Masakari) 5. Hunt Shadowlands creatures deep in Fu Leng's realm. Technique — Deny the Horde: No off-hand penalty with masakari. Attack as Simple Action with masakari. +3k0 damage in Full Attack (any foe) or Attack Stance (Shadowlands/Tainted foe).

**Hiruma Sniper [Bushi] [BoA]:**

Replaces: Hiruma Scout 4 or Hiruma Bushi 4. Requires: Kyujutsu 5, Reflexes 4. Born from fall of Hiruma lands. Technique — The Crab's Eyes: Attack with yumi as Simple Action. Ranged attacks ignore all natural Reduction and half magical Reduction.

**Hiruma Yojimbo [Bushi] [StE]:**

Replaces: Hiruma Bushi 3 or Hiruma Scout 3. Requires: Defense 3. Best defense is destroying threats quickly. Technique — The Crab's Shell: After successful melee attack, perform Guard Action as Free Action. Add attack roll excess over Armor TN to charge's Armor TN until next Turn.

**Kaiu Shipmasters [Artisan/Bushi]:**

Rank 3 path. Replaces: Kaiu Engineer 3 or Kaiu Siegemaster 1. Requires: Battle 4, Sailing 5. Created by Kaiu Utsu to defend Earthquake Fish Bay. Technique — Umasu's Steel: At start of naval battle, Battle/Intelligence (TN 25) grants ship Reduction equal to Sailing Rank ×2 (×4 for Turtle Ships).

**Kaiu Siegemaster [Bushi] [BoE]:**

Replaces: Kaiu Engineer 5. Requires: Battle (Mass Combat) 5, Engineering (Siege) 5. Elite siege specialists — render castles nigh-impregnable. Technique — The Hammer of Kaiu: Add/subtract full School Rank to Mass Battle Table result (replaces Rank 4 half modifier). On defense: +2k0 Engineering/Siege for siege weaponry. Defending structures get +50% Reduction.

**Kuni Crystal Master [Shugenja] [BoE]:**

Replaces: Kuni Shugenja 4. Requires: Earth 5, Spellcraft 5, Lore: Elements (Crystal) 5. Must possess pure crystal. GM approval required. Technique — Strike of Purity: Awaken a piece of crystal. Fire or Earth damage spells channeled through it gain +1k1 damage and Crystal keyword — but adds one Complex Action to casting time.

**Oni Slayer [Shugenja] [CR]:**

Replaces: Kuni Shugenja 3. Requires: Lore: Shadowlands 3. Elite Kuni sect for binding demons too powerful to fight directly. Technique — Bound by the World: Spend Earth spell slot + 1 hour to prepare binding circle (School Rank ×5 sq ft). Oni in circle must beat Willpower TN equal to your Spell Casting roll to leave. Costs one Earth spell slot per day while active.

**Rajya ke Varisa [Bushi]:**

Rank 4 path. Replaces: Hida Bushi 4, Shinjo Bushi 4, Moto Bushi 4, Yoritomo Bushi 4. Crab/Unicorn/Mantis cross-clan study of Ivory Kingdoms kshatriya techniques. Technique — Legacy of the Kshatriya: Twice per skirmish, spend Void Point before attack — on hit, add unkept dice to damage equal to Lore: Theology rank.

**Severed Hand [Courtier] [BoE]:**

Replaces: Any Crab Bushi 4. Special: Must be reassigned to courts due to physical inability to continue military service (e.g. Missing Limb, Lame). Veterans using Earth to survive court. Technique — Strength of Bamboo: Use Willpower as Trait when rolling to resist Social Skills or Courtier Techniques.

**Toritaka Exorcist [Shugenja] [GC]:**

Replaces: Kuni Shugenja 2. Requires: Willpower 3, Perception 3, Calligraphy 3. Detect and purge dangerous spirits — ghosts, gaki, yokai. Technique — Purge the Darkness: Detect spirits with Investigation/Perception (TN 20). 2 Raises to identify spirit type. Complex Action exorcism — Contested Willpower vs spirit (+2k2 with ward placed first) to force spirit out. Cannot re-possess for 24 hours.

**Tsuru's Legion [Bushi] [S****&****F]:**

Replaces: Hida Bushi 2 or Hiruma Bushi 2. Requires: Horsemanship 3. Special: Grants Rokugani pony if not already owned. Created by Hida Tsuru — semi-independent strike cavalry force. Technique — Overrun: When mounted, Knockdown maneuver costs one less Raise. Spend Void Point to add mount's Strength (unkept dice) to attack roll.

**Yasuki Enforcer [Bushi] [StE]:**

Replaces: Yasuki Courtier 2 or Hida Pragmatist 2. Requires: Intimidation 3. The threat that can be followed through on. Technique — Gentle Encouragement: Contested Willpower/Intimidation vs Willpower/Etiquette — success grants +1k0 attack (+1k0 per Raise) against that individual in first Round of next skirmish. Complex Action during a skirmish.

**Yasuki Extortionist [Courtier] [BoE]:**

Replaces: Yasuki Courtier 4. Requires: Willpower 4, Commerce 4, Intimidation 5. Moneylending and financial coercion. Technique — Do Me a Favor: If you supplied something the target needs, Contested Intimidation (Control)/Willpower vs Etiquette (Courtesy)/Willpower — success inflicts Obligation (if they can pay) or Blackmail (if they cannot). Default value 3 points.

**Yasuki Taskmaster [Bushi] [S****&****F]:**

Replaces: Hida Bushi 4, Hida Pragmatist 4, Yasuki Courtier 3. Requires: Willpower 4, Intimidation 5, Battle 3. Sunda Mizu Mura dojo — organize and command ashigaru forces. Technique — Fear is a Gift: Intimidation treated as Bugei Skill. Use Willpower as Trait on any Battle roll. Use Intimidation Rank in place of Battle Rank on Mass Battle Step 2. Soldiers under your command add your Intimidation Rank to Fear resistance rolls.
