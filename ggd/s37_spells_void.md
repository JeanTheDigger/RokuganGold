# 37. Spells — Void

Void spells are only castable by ishiken — shugenja with the Ishiken-do Advantage. Void spells use the Void Ring for both the casting roll and spell slots. The Void has no opposing element and no standard Affinity or Deficiency applies.

**MASTERY LEVEL 1:**

**Boundless Sight [CR]**

- Void 1 | Range: 50 miles | Area: Self | Duration: 4 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round), Range (+5 miles)

The caster looks through the Void to perceive a familiar location (must have been there at least once). Can see and hear all that transpires there but cannot be detected and cannot interact with anything or anyone. During the spell the caster is in a trance-like state — Armor TN is reduced to 5.

**Drawing the Void [CR]**

- Void 1 | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Special (+1 Void Point per 3 Raises)

Reach into the Void to gain strength. Gain additional Void Points equal to School Rank +1. If this exceeds the normal maximum, one extra Void Point is lost each Round the caster does not spend one.

**Flow Through the Void [CR]**

- Void 1 | Range: Touch | Area: Up to 1 cubic foot of pure elemental matter | Duration: Permanent | Raises: Area (+1 cubic foot)

Tap into the Void's connection of all things to transform a small amount of pure elemental matter (water, fire, air, or earth in the form of dirt or soil) into any one other element.

**See Through Lies [CR]**

- Void 1 | Range: 25' | Area: Target individual | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+5'), Special (+1 additional Advantage/Disadvantage per Raise)

The patterns comprising all things are recognizable to a trained ishiken. Learn the highest-point Advantage or highest-point Disadvantage (caster's choice) possessed by the target.

**Sense Void [CR]**

- Void 1 | Range: Personal | Area: 25' radius | Duration: Concentration | Raises: Area (+5')

Enter a trance, becoming aware of the existence and location of all living creatures within the radius. Provides location only — no visual details or identification (would know three humans are outside the castle wall but not who they are). Also detects non-sentient natural living creatures. Spirits and Shadowlands creatures register as a gap in awareness, but Tainted humans are not distinguishable from normal humans.

**Touch the Emptiness [CR]**

- Void 1 | Range: 30' | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+5')

The disturbing sensation of experiencing the Void firsthand. The target suffers 1k1 damage and is Dazed.

**The Void's Caress [CR]**

- Void 1 | Range: Touch | Area: One target individual | Duration: 1 minute | Raises: Duration (+1 minute)

Make short-term alterations to the Void's pattern. Negate any one Mental or Spiritual Disadvantage the target possesses, up to a maximum of 5 points in value. The Shadowlands Taint itself cannot be removed, but mental disorders caused by it can be temporarily alleviated.

**Witness the Untold [CR]**

- Void 1 (Divination) | Range: 15' | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: 3 rounds | Raises: Range (+5')

Glimpse things that have not yet happened by peering through the Void. While delaying an action, the caster may interrupt the target after they have declared their action but before they have taken it, on each Round for the duration. (Normally someone delaying cannot interrupt another participant's declared action.)

**MASTERY LEVEL 2:**

**Altering the Course [CR]**

- Void 2 | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 1 minute | Raises: Duration (+1 minute)

Ishiken can draw upon the Void consciously rather than unconsciously. For the duration, the caster may spend multiple Void Points on any single roll where Void Point expenditure is normally allowable. Damage rolls may not be augmented.

**Balance in All**

- Void 2 | Range: Self | Area: Self | Duration: One Round | Raises: Duration (+1 Round)

Cast spells of any given element as if they were a different element — affecting the Ring used to roll, the number of spells per day, and any relevant Affinity, Deficiency, or other mechanics. GM may interpret how a different element affects a spell's description (e.g. a Katana of Fire cast as an Air spell might create a blade of burning wind).

**Commune with the Void [BoV]**

- Void 2 | Range: N/A | Area: Self | Duration: 1 Round per question (concentration) | Raises: Special (+1 question per 3 Raises)

Enter direct communion with the ineffable Void to gain information. Allows 2 questions (Raises for more). The Void is not an array of individual spirits but a single all-encompassing whole — the caster follows connections between things to seek insight into their nature and history. The GM may answer as completely or as confusingly as desired, may provide information from past or future, or may even volunteer unrequested information. Information will never be outright false, but may be confusing or misleading.

**Drink of Your Essence [CR]**

- Void 2 | Range: 30' | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+5')

Examines an individual's pattern within the Void. Learn the target's individual Ring Ranks (not Traits), their current Wound penalties, and a one-word summation of their present mood (e.g. 'hostile', 'sorrowful', 'confused').

**Strengthen the Void [BoV]**

- Void 2 | Range: N/A | Area: 20' radius from caster | Duration: 5 Rounds | Raises: Area (+5' radius per 2 Raises), Duration (+1 Round per Raise)

Thins the veil between the Void and reality within the local area. Effects: Free Raise to cast Void spells for anyone within the area; +5 TN penalty on all non-Void spells; anyone spending a Void Point within the area gains +2k1 instead of the normal +1k1. Creatures of the Lying Darkness (or any mortal with at least 1 Shadow Rank) who enter must roll Willpower (TN 20) or suffer +10 TN to all Skill and attack rolls while within. Failing by more than 10 forces them to leave and prevents voluntary re-entry.

**The Empty Voice [CR]**

- Void 2 | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: None

Drawing on the essence of everything allows the caster to invoke kami with far greater ease. For the duration, may cast spells of other elements without speaking prayers aloud. Applies only to spells of equal or lower Mastery Level (Mastery Level 1 or 2 only).

**False Whispers [CR]**

- Void 2 | Range: 30' | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: 1 minute | Raises: None

Briefly mingles the Void of two beings. The target will repeat the next sentence the caster says, verbatim, in their own normal voice, without foreknowledge of what they are doing. Cannot be used to cast spells even if a spell phrase is recited. Effect occurs only once — the first time the caster speaks a single sentence during the duration.

**Reach Through the Void [CR]**

- Void 2 | Range: 50' | Area: Target object up to 5 pounds | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Area (+1 pound per 2 Raises), Range (+5')

All things exist within the Void and can be touched through it. Manipulate small objects without touching them, moving them telekinetically. Maximum weight is 5 pounds without Raises.

**Severed from the Stream [CR]**

- Void 2 | Range: 25' | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: 5 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round), Range (+5')

Robs the target of their ability to tap into the Void temporarily. If this spell succeeds, the target must win a Contested Void Roll against the caster before spending any Void Point. This roll does not count as an action; only one Contested Roll is required per Turn even if the target is spending multiple Void Points.

**MASTERY LEVEL 3:**

**Banish the Void [BoV]**

- Void 3 | Range: N/A | Area: 20' radius from caster | Duration: 5 Rounds | Raises: Area (+5' radius per 2 Raises), Duration (+1 Round per Raise)

The exact counterpoint to Strengthen the Void. Thickens the veil between the Void and reality. Effects within the area: casting Void spells suffers +10 TN; spending Void Points costs two to gain the effect of one; a character with only one Void Point cannot spend it at all. Often used by ishiken to control dangerous apprentices. GM's note: whether this also affects Void-like effects (Naga Akasha, Ratling Name) is left to the GM's discretion.

**Echoes in the Void [CR]**

- Void 3 | Range: 25' | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: Concentration | Raises: Range (+5')

Thoughts are whispers in the Void. For as long as concentration is maintained on one target within range, the caster can hear what that person is thinking. How this manifests varies by individual — a stalwart warrior may think in short curt statements; an artisan may think in poetry.

**Kharmic Intent [CR]**

- Void 3 | Range: 20' | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Duration (+1 minute), Range (+5')

All remaining Void Points the caster and the willing target possess are pooled; either participant may draw from this shared pool as normal. This also allows sharing of additional Void Points gained from other Void spells. At the duration's end, remaining Void Points are divided between participants up to each one's normal maximum.

**Moment of Clarity [CR]**

- Void 3 | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 2 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round)

The entirety of human achievement is reflected in the Void. Select any one Skill. Gain temporary Ranks in that Skill equal to Void Ring. If the caster already possesses Ranks in the Skill, the temporary level replaces the existing one (not cumulative).

**Read the Essence [CR]**

- Void 3 (Divination) | Range: Personal | Area: 1 target object | Duration: Instantaneous (see below) | Raises: None

Objects have resonance and retain memory of those who have held them and events that have involved them. Meditate on an object to gain a vision of whoever held it last, or of a major event that involved it. A calligraphy brush might show the last person who wrote with it; a weapon might show the last death it inflicted. The GM has ultimate authority over the vision's content. Extremely important objects (ancestral weapons, clan standards) may have powerful visions that grip the caster for extended periods rather than instantaneous glimpses.

**Void Release [CR]**

- Void 3 | Range: 25' | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+5')

Make a Contested Void Roll against the target. On success, the target loses one Void Point and the caster gains one. One additional point is exchanged per 5-point margin by which the caster exceeds the target's roll. Temporary Void Points gained this way may exceed the normal maximum but expire within 1 hour if unused. Cannot target a creature without Void Points or with a mechanic substituting for Void (Naga Akasha, Ratling Name).

**MASTERY LEVEL 4:**

**Balance of Elements [CR]**

- Void 4 | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: 5 rounds (combat) / 5 minutes (non-combat) | Raises: Duration (+1 round or +1 minute), Special (+1k1 Wounds healed per 2 Raises)

Flaws in the great pattern of the Void can be overcome. Negate all Disadvantages of the target for the duration. Negated spell effects (TN penalties, Armor TN reduction, Trait/Ring reduction) with Mastery Level 3 or less are also negated. Also heals 3k3 Wounds immediately.

**Dart of Void [SE]**

- Void 4 | Range: 100' | Area: One target creature | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+100' per Raise), Targets (+1 per 2 Raises, max equal to Void Ring)

Created by the shugenja Ekuro; preserved as a secret of the Order of Five Weapons, possibly also known to the Phoenix. Requires spending a Void Point to cast. Summons a missile of pure Void that unerringly strikes any target within range. DR equal to the caster's Void Ring. This damage ignores Invulnerability and Reduction from all sources.

**Draw Closed the Veil [CR]**

- Void 4 | Range: 50' | Area: 1 target spirit | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+5'), Targets (+1 per Raise)

Strengthens the Void separating spirit realms from Ningen-do. Target any one spirit not native to the mortal realm — that spirit is banished to its home realm for a minimum of Void Ring months. Spirits with a physical body in Ningen-do (oni, shapeshifters) require a Contested Willpower Roll. Spirits significantly more powerful than the caster (GM's discretion) may be immune.

**Essence of Void [BoV]**

- Void 4 | Range: 50' | Area: One target creature | Duration: Concentration | Raises: Range (+10' per Raise), Special (+1k0 to Contested Roll per Raise)

Suffuses a living creature with pure Void, overwhelming its bodily functions and rendering it inert. Requires the caster to see the target and win a Contested Void Roll (creatures without Void may resist with Name, Akasha, or Willpower at GM's discretion). On success: the target is held immobile, unable to move, speak, or act while the caster maintains concentration. On the second and subsequent Rounds, the target may make another Contested Roll during the Reactions Stage to break free.

**Fill the Emptiness [CR]**

- Void 4 | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (10' for 3 Raises)

Strengthens an individual's connection to the Void, replenishing their inner energy. The target regains all lost Void Points up to their normal maximum.

**Void Strike [CR]**

- Void 4 | Range: 50' | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+5'), Special (+1k0 damage per 2 Raises)

The mortal shell can endure only so much direct contact with the Void. Inflicts damage with DR equal to the caster's Void Ring.

**MASTERY LEVEL 5:**

**Divide the Soul [CR]**

- Void 5 | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 1 minute | Raises: Duration (+1 minute)

Divide the caster's soul across the Void to exist in two places in the mortal realm simultaneously. The second manifestation appears anywhere within the caster's sight when the spell is cast. Both manifestations have full knowledge of everything that happens at each location and may take actions simultaneously as two separate individuals. Only one manifestation may make a Spell Casting Roll per Round. Wounds suffered by either manifestation are combined when the spell ends. If either manifestation dies, both die.

**Reforge [CR]**

- Void 5 | Range: Touch | Area: 1 object | Duration: Permanent | Raises: None

All is one and one is all. Transform any single uniform object (not assembled from multiple pieces; not living material) into any other object of comparable size. Effect is permanent but reversible by a second casting on the same object, even by a different ishiken.

**Unbound Essence [CR]**

- Void 5 | Range: 25' | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+1 hour), Range (+5')

Tampers with the target's pattern in the Void, randomly reordering their Rings along with their Traits. For example, the target might exchange Air Ring for Fire Ring, and Water Ring for Earth Ring. Mental and Physical Traits exchange their values if those values are greater than the new Ring. The resulting combination is determined randomly.

**MASTERY LEVEL 6:**

**Ring of the Void [CR]**

- Void 6 | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: Concentration (requires 1 hour to cast) | Raises: Duration (+5 minutes to casting time)

A rare and extraordinary prayer — not taught by others but learned through insight alone, with no scroll associated with it. Essentially a meditation ritual that allows communion with the Void Dragon itself. During brief contact, the caster may ask questions. The Void Dragon knows all that can be known but is under no compulsion to answer directly or at all. Respect must be maintained at all times — those who have attempted this without proper homage have ceased to exist altogether.

**Rise from the Ashes [CR]**

- Void 6 | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target individual | Duration: Instantaneous (requires 1 hour to cast) | Raises: Special (+1 hour of regression per Raise)

Recalls the memory of a living creature's pattern in the Void and restores their existence to how it was 8 hours prior to when the spell began. Any injuries, disease, poisons, and even supernatural conditions (Shadowlands Taint, Lying Darkness corruption) acquired during that window can be undone. Cost: upon completion, the caster loses all Void Points and cannot regain Void Points for 3 days, after which they recover at 1 Void Point per day until their maximum is restored. Cannot be used to restore someone who has already died.

**Unmake the World [CR]**

- Void 6 | Range: 50' | Area: 1 target creature, individual, or object | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+10')

The most powerful and terrifying ability of those who command the Void. Eradicates the bonds between a target's elements, causing it to cease to exist. Against a living creature: must win a Contested Void vs Earth roll — on success, the target is instantly killed and their remains disappear into the Void. Against a non-magical object: automatic success. Against an awakened object (nemuranai): may have dire ramifications at the GM's discretion, as the kami within tend to respond very poorly to this manner of attack.
