## 29.3 Dragon Clan Schools

**BASIC SCHOOLS:**

**Kitsuki Investigator [Courtier] [CR]:**

Alone in the Empire, they believe in evidence. Nearly infallible memory, perfect recall, inhuman attention to detail. Primary role is court representation but excel as magistrates. Gather information from both environment and people.

- Benefit: +1 Perception

- Skills: Courtier, Etiquette (Courtesy), Investigation (Interrogation), Kenjutsu, Meditation, Sincerity, any one Lore Skill

- Honor: 5.5 | Outfit: Traditional Clothing, Wakizashi, Knife, Calligraphy Set, Traveling Pack, 5 koku

- Rank 1 — Kitsuki's Method: Free Raise on all Investigation rolls. Add Perception Trait Rank to Armor TN in skirmishes.

- Rank 2 — Wisdom the Wind Brings: Anyone lying to you or using Feint/Disarm against you adds +5 TN per Kitsuki Investigator School Rank.

- Rank 3 — Know the Rhythm of the Heart: After speaking briefly — Investigation (Notice)/Perception vs Intelligence ×5 — success gives true picture of personality and motivations.

- Rank 4 — Finding the Path: Contested Investigation (Interrogation)/Intelligence vs Etiquette/Intelligence — identify one ally or enemy. Each Raise reveals another. Once per day per person.

- Rank 5 — The Eyes Betray the Heart: Automatically make Contested Investigation/Perception vs Sincerity/Willpower against anyone who knowingly lies to you. +5k0 bonus if you already know they're lying.

**Mirumoto Bushi [CR]:**

The unique Niten two-sword technique — katana in main hand, wakizashi in off hand simultaneously. Controversial but predates the one-sword schools. Heated rivalry with Kakita Bushi. Surprisingly calm and implacable despite Dragon's erratic reputation.

- Benefit: +1 Stamina

- Skills: Defense, Iaijutsu, Kenjutsu (Katana), Lore: Shugenja, Meditation, Theology, any one Bugei or High Skill

- Honor: 4.5 | Outfit: Light Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, any 1 weapon, Traveling Pack, 5 koku

- Rank 1 — Way of the Dragon: No dual-wielding penalties with katana/wakizashi. School Rank bonus to Armor TN (cumulative with normal two-weapon bonus). May raise or lower TN of spells targeting you by 5.

- Rank 2 — The Calm in Midst of Thunder: In Center Stance, add Kenjutsu Skill Rank to Iaijutsu rolls.

- Rank 3 — Strong and Swift: Attack as Simple Action with Samurai keyword weapons.

- Rank 4 — Furious Retaliation: During Reactions Stage, choose one opponent who attacked you this Round — +3k0 to all attacks against them next Turn.

- Rank 5 — Heart of the Dragon: When attacking twice with katana/wakizashi in same Turn, make one additional off-hand attack as Free Action.

**Mirumoto Taoist Swordsman [Bushi] [EE]:**

Ascetic warriors pursuing enlightenment and swordsmanship with equal dedication. Remote mountain dojo — train alongside Togashi monks. Rarely leave their mountains. All members are treated as Ascetic (no XP for it, doesn't count against Disadvantage limit).

- Benefit: +1 Void

- Skills: Athletics, Defense, Iaijutsu, Kenjutsu (Katana), Lore: Theology, Meditation, any 1 Bugei or High Skill

- Honor: 5.5 | Outfit: Sturdy Clothing, Katana, Wakizashi, Tanto, Traveling Pack, 1 koku

- Rank 1 — Aligned With the Elements: Sword with no armor — Reduction equal to School Rank +2. +1k0 sword damage.

- Rank 2 — Fist and Blade: Knockdown costs 1 less Raise with sword. Inflict sword damage while controlling Grapple with sword.

- Rank 3 — Strike of Harmony: Spend Void Point on damage rolls while wielding sword (stacks with natural katana ability).

- Rank 4 — Master of Steel: Attack as Simple Action while wielding a sword.

- Rank 5 — Balance of Nothingness: Once per skirmish, spend as many Void Points on damage as desired while wielding sword.

**Tamori Shugenja [CR]:**

Formed from Agasha remnants after the defection. Masters of alchemy — contain spell essence in ritually prepared liquids for later use. Particularly deadly in ambush. Martial training unlike most shugenja families.

- Benefit: +1 Stamina

- Skills: Athletics, Calligraphy (Cipher), Defense, Divination, Lore: Theology, Medicine, Spellcraft

- Honor: 4.5 | Outfit: Robes, Wakizashi, any 1 weapon, Scroll Satchel, Traveling Pack, 5 koku

- Affinity/Deficiency: Earth / Air | Starting Spells: Sense, Commune, Summon, 3 Earth, 2 Fire, 1 Water

- Technique — Flesh of the Elements: Expend one spell slot to store a single-target spell in a potion. Anyone activates it — Simple Action to drink, Complex Action to throw. Counts against daily spell slots while stored. Creation takes Mastery Level hours. Free Raise on Craft keyword spells.

**The Togashi Tattooed Order [Monk] [CR]:**

The ise zumi — the most recognizable Dragon Clan members. Tattoos inked with Kami Togashi's divine blood grant supernatural abilities. Specialize in unarmed combat and supernatural athleticism.

- Benefit: +1 Void

- Skills: Athletics, Artisan: Tattooing, Defense, Jiujutsu, any one Lore Skill, Meditation, any one non-Low Skill

- Honor: 4.5 | Outfit: Robes, Bo, Traveling Pack, 5 koku

- Rank 1: Gain two Tattoos. Rank 2: +1k1 unarmed attack and damage. Rank 3: Gain two Tattoos. Rank 4: Unarmed attacks as Simple Action. Rank 5: Gain two Tattoos.

**The Hoshi Tsurui Zumi Order [Monk] [IH]:**

Sub-group of the Togashi Order. Less direct combat, more mystical — ki techniques and spiritual fighting. Many Hoshi eschew combat altogether for meditation. Treated mechanically as Brotherhood of Shinsei school.

- Benefit: +1 Void | Skills: Athletics, Defense, Etiquette, Jiujutsu, Meditation (Void Recovery), any 2 Skills

- Honor: 4.5 | Outfit: Robes, Bo, Traveling Pack, 2 koku

- Technique — Swift Fist, Subtle Heart: Spend Void Point as Free Action — active for School Rank Rounds. Each Turn choose +1k1 unarmed attack OR +1k0 unarmed damage. Begin with one Tattoo and 2 Kiho. Gain one Tattoo at Rank 4.

**The Hitomi Kikage Zumi Order [Monk] [IH]:**

The violent path to enlightenment — embrace physical action over mysticism. Kaze-do martial art — move as swiftly and unpredictably as wind. Most prominent during Hitomi's reign but predates and outlasts the family.

- Benefit: +1 Reflexes | Skills: Athletics, Defense, Jiujutsu, Lore (choose one), Meditation, any 2 Non-Low Skills

- Honor: 4.5 | Outfit: Sturdy Clothing, Traveling Pack, 4 koku

- Rank 1: Gain 1 Tattoo. Add Reflexes to Armor TN. May use Reflexes instead of Strength in Grapple control.

- Rank 2: Spend Void Point on unarmed damage rolls including Grapple.

- Rank 3: Unarmed attacks as Simple Action. Gain one Tattoo.

- Rank 4: Knockdown costs 1 less Raise unarmed. +1k1 to all unarmed damage including Grapple.

- Rank 5: Knockdown with 2 extra Raises also Stuns. Throwing in Grapple — opponent rolls Earth (TN 20) or Stunned. Gain 1 Tattoo.

**ADVANCED SCHOOLS:**

**Mirumoto Master Sensei [Bushi]:**

Tiny elite — seldom exceeding two dozen total. True mastery of swordsmanship, body, and soul. Cannot be Brash or Proud. Requires Air 5, Earth 4, Void 5, Kenjutsu 5, Meditation 6. Must be chosen and taught by existing Master Sensei.

- Rank 1 — The Sword and the Soul: Reduce opponent to Down/Out/Dead with sword — regain 1 Void Point (can temporarily exceed Void Ring, excess vanishes at end of skirmish).

- Rank 2 — The Body is Illusion: Simple Action, spend Void Point — ignore Wound Penalties (including Down, not Out) for rest of skirmish or 10 minutes outside.

- Rank 3 — Sword of the Sensei: Once per day, Simple Action — Meditation/Awareness (TN 25 + 5 per ally) — success grants allies benefit of Rank 1 for the skirmish.

**Swordmasters [Bushi] [CR]:**

Evolution of Niten — master every aspect of the two-sword style for any battlefield situation. Pragmatic combat focus vs Kenshinzen's duel focus. Requires Fire 4, Void 4, Iaijutsu 5, Kenjutsu 5, Lore: Theology 4.

- Rank 1 — The Silence of Two Strikes: While wielding katana/wakizashi, may spend Void Points twice per Turn.

- Rank 2 — Mirumoto's Strength: In iaijutsu duel, always gain +1k1 to Focus roll as if beaten opponent in Assessment, regardless of actual result.

- Rank 3 — Harmony and Precision: While wielding katana/wakizashi, ignore opponent's Reduction entirely.

**Tamori Master of the Mountain [Shugenja]:**

Loose organization dating to Agasha mountain explorations. Prefer impetuous, self-reliant shugenja — teach them to channel anger into power. Requires one Ring at 4, two other Rings at 3, Spellcraft 5.

- Rank 1 — Integration of the Gods: Spend 2 spell slots of another Ring instead of 1 correct Ring (cannot use opposing elements).

- Rank 2 — Inner Fortitude: School Rank +1. Spend spell slot as Free Action — Reduction 2 and +10 Armor TN for Earth Ring Rounds. While active, must call one Raise to no effect each time you cast.

- Rank 3 — Power in Need: Simple Action, spend Void Point and one spell slot — choose keyword, gain +4k1 to all Spell Casting Rolls with that keyword for the encounter.

**ALTERNATE PATHS:**

**Dragon Clan Overseer:**

Rank 2. Replaces: Kitsuki 2, Mirumoto Bushi 2, Taoist Swordsman 2, or Togashi Monk 2. Requires: Lore: Shadowlands 2, Lore: Theology 3. Oversee Spider Clan in the Colonies. Technique — The Empress's Guiding Hand: If Lore: Theology exceeds Courtier or Etiquette when dealing with lower Honor Rank individuals, gain bonus rolled dice equal to the difference on Contested Social rolls. Tainted opponents −2k0. Theology lesson (1 min + TN 25) grants +2k0 to resist Taint for 8 hours.

**Dragon's Flame [Bushi]:**

Replaces: Any Dragon Bushi 4. Requires: Kyujutsu 5. Individualistic archery tradition — defend the Dragon mountains. Technique — Rain of Death: +1k0 to attack rolls or +2k2 while using a bow.

**The Dragon's Wind [Bushi]:**

Replaces: Mirumoto Taoist Swordsman 3 or Mirumoto Bushi 4. Requires: Horsemanship 5, Kyujutsu (Dai-Kyu) 5. Grants steed and Dai-Kyu if not owned. Cavalry archers — arrive, volley, withdraw. Technique — Way of the Horse and Bow: Steed treats Moderate terrain as Basic, Difficult as Moderate. Attack with bow as Simple Action while mounted.

**Hojatsu's Legacy [Bushi] [BoF]:**

Replaces: Mirumoto Bushi 4 or Mirumoto Taoist Swordsman 5. Requires: Iaijutsu 5. Disrupt opponent's rhythm rather than match speed — developed for the final Hojatsu-Kakita duel (both died). Technique — Strike When You Cannot: During Assessment, spend Void Point as Free Action — Contested Kenjutsu/Fire roll — win and opponent cannot spend Void Points on their Focus roll.

**Kaze-Do Fighter:**

Replaces: Any Rank 2. Requires: Jiujutsu (Kaze-do) 3. Ancient Togashi Kaze techniques — basis for almost all modern Rokugani martial arts. Technique — The Way of Air: Fighting unarmed, opponents attacking you with melee weapons suffer −Xk0 where X = their Air Ring. Cannot apply to more opponents per Round than your Insight Rank.

**Kitsuki Debater [Courtier]:**

Replaces: Kitsuki Investigator 3. Requires: Courtier 3, Etiquette (Conversation) 5. Destroy opponent's court logic point by point — strip away their mask of courtesy. Technique — The Ebb and Flow of Deception: Etiquette/Awareness (TN 20 + optional Raises) — switch topic to something uncomfortable. Opponent rolls Sincerity/Awareness (TN 20 + 5 per Raise called) or suffers −1k1 to their next Social roll against you.

**Kitsuki's Eye [Courtier]:**

Rank 6. Must attain Kitsuki Rank 5 and be recruited by sensei. The true elite — what average Kitsuki are to the Empire, the Eye is to average Kitsuki. Technique — The Eye Sees All: Spend Void Point, observe area 1 minute, Perception (TN 25) — spot tracks, handprints, out-of-place items. Raises for additional senses or hidden objects. +2k2 to avoid being surprised.

**Kitsuki Justicar [Bushi] [IH]:**

Replaces: Kitsuki Investigator 3. Requires: Iaijutsu 4, Investigation 4. Hunt fugitives and challenge them to duels to the death — death proves guilt. Technique — The Purity of Justice: Add Investigation Rank to Assessment rolls in duels. +1k0 Focus and Strike vs those decreed guilty. +1k0 to Hunting and Investigation to track such targets.

**Mirumoto Mountaineer [Bushi] [CR]:**

Replaces: Mirumoto Bushi 2. Requires: Athletics (Climbing) 3. Hardened by treacherous Dragon terrain. Technique — Heart of the Mountains: When surprised (failed Investigation/Perception), immediately roll Athletics/Agility — success = not surprised. Add half Athletics Ranks (rounded up) to all ranged attack rolls.

**Mirumoto Sentinel [Bushi]:**

Replaces: Mirumoto Bushi 4 or Taoist Swordsman 3. Requires: Way of the Land Advantage. Secure large sections of Dragon mountain territory — intimate knowledge of every province. Technique — Master the Land: In provinces with Way of the Land, choose 3 Bugei Skills gaining +1k0. At start of skirmish, Contested Void Roll vs one opponent — their Water Ring counts as one lower for Move Action distances.

**Tamori Warrior Priest [Shugenja] [CR]:**

Replaces: Tamori Shugenja 4. Requires: 1 weapon Skill at Rank 3. Martial training equally important as kami connection. Technique — Strength of the Soul: Expend spell slot as Free Action for +1k0 on any Bugei Skill roll (max = School Rank per Round). Simple Action attacks while wielding elemental weapon spells (Bo of Water, Tetsubo of Earth, Katana of Fire, Yari of Air).

**Tamori Weaponsmiths [Shugenja/Artisan]:**

Replaces: Tamori Shugenja 2. Requires: Craft: Weaponsmithing 3, one weapon-creating spell. Marry blade design with Earth power. Technique — Soul of the Stone: On successful weapon Craft roll, expend all Void Points to instill Free Raises equal to Earth Ring — usable on any roll with the blade, refresh after 30 days. Max weapons equal to Earth Rank may exist at one time.

**Togashi Defender [Monk]:**

Replaces: Togashi Tattooed Order 3. Physical focus — hone body through martial arts. Protect villages, fight alongside Mirumoto. Technique — Power Within and Without: While unarmored with no Kiho or Tattoos active, gain Reduction equal to 3 + Void Ring. Gain one additional Tattoo.

**The Transcendent Brotherhood [Monk]:**

Rank 2. Replaces: Any Tattooed Order 2. Requires: Fire 3, Earth 3, Meditation 3. Excommunicated from Brotherhood of Shinsei — believe pain tempers toward enlightenment. Housed by Dragon. Technique — Apotheosis of Fire: Once per day, 1 hour meditation, Meditation/Fire (TN 20) — immune to normal fire for Fire Ring hours (still feel pain). 1 Raise: magical fire immunity. 2 Raises: all fire immunity. While burning and for 10 minutes after, +Fire Ring to all Awareness/Perception/Intelligence rolls.

**Water Hammer Smith [Artisan]:**

Replaces: Any Dragon School at Rank 2. Requires: Any one Craft Skill at 3+. Masters of Water Hammer City's sacred forge. Anyone can gain apprenticeship. Technique — Child of the Water: Spend Void Point (or Water spell slot if shugenja) to add double Water Ring to all Craft Skill rolls for rest of day. Only one use per day.
