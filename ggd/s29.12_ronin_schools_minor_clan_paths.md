## 29.12 Ronin Schools & Minor Clan Paths

Wave-men and independent minor groups. Most ronin bands can only develop one or two Techniques — the rare exceptions that endure long enough to build true schools are noted. Paths are organized by minimum School Rank required.

**BASIC SCHOOLS:**

**Generic Ronin [Bushi] [SE]:**

A generalist option for ronin, minor vassal family samurai, or Minor Clan bushi without their own named school. Represents martial training without access to a full clan dojo. Resilience and adaptability over specialization.

- Benefit: +1 Agility

- Skills: Athletics, Defense, Hunting, Intimidation, Kenjutsu, Knives, any one Weapon Skill

- Honor: 4.5 | Outfit: Light Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, any 1 weapon, Traveling Pack, 1 koku

- Rank 1 — Strength of My Father: When suffering TN penalties from Wounds, make an Earth Roll (ignoring Wound penalties) during the Reactions Stage, TN = 5 + 5× Wound Ranks currently suffered. On success, ignore Wound penalties until the next Reactions Stage. Does not work at Down or Out.

- Rank 2 — Never Yield: When outnumbered, gain bonus to Armor TN equal to the amount enemies exceed allies. Cannot exceed School Rank ×2.

- Rank 3 — Bite of the Wolf: Attack as a Simple Action when wielding a katana or knife.

- Rank 4 — Twice-Cutting Spirit: Choose one additional melee weapon — attack with it as a Simple Action. Once per skirmish, spend a Void Point on a damage roll regardless of weapon used.

- Rank 5 — Valor of the Wolf: Choose another melee weapon — attack with it as a Simple Action. Spend a Void Point to activate the Rank 1 Technique without a Willpower Roll; lasts Rounds equal to your Fire Ring.

**Dutiful Disciple Shugenja [EnE]:**

For shugenja trained directly by a personal mentor rather than a formal school. Their training is shaped entirely by their sensei’s tradition.

- Benefit: +1 Intelligence

- Skills: Calligraphy, Lore: Theology, Lore (pick one), Meditation, Spellcraft, any two Skills

- Honor: 4.0 | Outfit: Sturdy Robes, Wakizashi, any 1 weapon, Traveling Pack, Scroll Satchel, 5 bu

- Affinity/Deficiency: Same as mentor. Choose one non-Void element for Affinity; automatically Deficient in the opposing element.

- Starting Spells: Sense, Commune, Summon, 3 spells of Affinity element, 2 of a non-Deficient element, 1 of a second non-Deficient element.

- Technique — Soul of Study: When casting one of your starting spells (those given by your mentor), roll an additional +1k0 on the Spell Casting Roll.

**Ronin Order Shugenja [EnE]:**

For shugenja trained by a ronin order such as the Brotherhood or similar groups. Emphasizes practical utility over raw power, favoring spells for daily life.

- Benefit: +1 Awareness

- Skills: Calligraphy (Cipher), Divination, Lore: Theology, Lore (pick any), Meditation, Spellcraft, any one Skill

- Honor: 5.0 | Outfit: Sturdy Robes, Daisho, Traveling Pack, Scroll Satchel, 5 bu

- Affinity/Deficiency: Air / Earth | Starting Spells: Sense, Commune, Summon, 3 Air, 2 Water, 1 Fire

- Technique — The Path of Discipline: Free Raise on all Sense, Commune, and Summon spells for all elements except Void and Earth.

**Self-Taught Shugenja [EnE]:**

Shugenja who learned the ways of the kami through trial and error rather than formal instruction. Great holes in training but a raw affinity for the kami that many clan-trained shugenja would envy.

- Benefit: +1 Willpower

- Skills: Athletics, Hunting, Meditation, Spellcraft, any three non-High Skills

- Honor: 4.0 | Outfit: Sturdy Robes, Wakizashi, any 1 weapon, Traveling Pack, Scroll Satchel, 5 bu

- Affinity/Deficiency: Choose any one non-Void element for Affinity. Automatically Deficient in the opposing element and in any one other element of your choice.

- Starting Spells: Sense, Commune, Summon, and any five castable spells (no Deficiency elements).

- Technique — The Kami’s Gift: When spending a Void Point on a Spell Casting Roll, gain +2k2 instead of +1k1. Does not automatically gain additional spells when advancing in Rank — must seek them out independently.

**Void Mystic [Shugenja]:**

Born with a close attunement to the Void but lacking conventional training. Cannot cast the four elemental spells or Importune for spells, but possesses an innate and silent mastery of Void magic.

- Benefit: +1 Willpower

- Skills: Defense, Divination, Hunting, Lore: Elements (Void), Meditation, Stealth, any one non-High Skill

- Honor: 3.5 | Outfit: Sturdy Clothing, Wakizashi, any 1 weapon, Traveling Pack, 5 bu

- Affinity/Deficiency: None. Begins play with both the Ishiken-do Advantage and the Touch of the Void Disadvantage. Cannot cast non-Void elemental spells except Sense, Commune, and Summon. Cannot Importune.

- Technique — The Void’s Caress: May cast any Void spell up to current Mastery Level, expending a spell slot as normal. Does not need to speak aloud to cast spells. May only cast each spell once per day. Cannot cast Void spells above Rank 5. Free Raise on Divination Skill.

**ADVANCED SCHOOLS:**

**Disciples of Sun Tao [Advanced] [SE]:**

An ancient ronin band based around Sun Tao’s legendary book. Old enough and dedicated enough to have developed multiple Techniques — an extremely rare accomplishment for wave-men.

- Requirements: Fire 4, Water 3 | Skills: Battle (Mass Battle) 4, any two Weapon Skills at 4 | Other: Must be born into or recruited into the Disciples of Sun Tao.

- Rank 1 — Terumoto’s Lesson: Choose any three Weapon Skills; gain +1k0 to rolls with those Skills.

- Rank 2 — Sun Tao’s Legacy: +1k1 to Battle Skill rolls when determining who is winning a battle; +1k0 when determining status on the Mass Battle Table.

- Rank 3 — Aikumo’s Perfection: Make attacks with melee weapons as a Simple Action.

**Kolat Assassin [Ninja]:**

Among the deadliest killers in the Empire. Trained in infiltration, disguise, and all possible methods of murder. Those marked for death by the Kolat rarely escape the reach of Lotus assassins.

- Requirements: Agility 4, Reflexes 4 | Skills: Acting 5, Knives 5, Stealth 5 | Other: Must be a Kolat member recruited into the Lotus Sect. Cannot be Tainted or corrupted by the Nothing.

- Rank 1 — Kiss of the Lotus: When spending a Void Point on a Stealth or Acting roll, gain +2k2 instead of +1k1.

- Rank 2 — Tiger’s Claw: When striking on the first Round against an unaware opponent or one who does not realize you are a threat, +1k0 to attack rolls and may make melee attacks as Simple Actions.

- Rank 3 — Steal the Light: Once per opponent per skirmish, call 2 Raises to make a melee attack that keeps only 1 damage die but leaves the opponent Blind, Deaf, or Mute (your choice) for minutes equal to your Water Ring.

**Legion of Two Thousand [SE]:**

Sponsored by the Imperial house. Recruited from some of the finest soldiers in Toturi’s ronin army. Enjoys resources most ronin can only dream of, enabling it to develop extensive martial techniques.

- Requirements: Fire 3, Water 3 | Skills: Battle 3, Defense 4, Kenjutsu 3 | Other: Honor 5.0+, must be recruited into the Legion.

- Rank 1 — Stand as Two Thousand: +1k0 to Defense Skill rolls. When fighting in defense of the innocent or common people, +1k1 instead.

- Rank 2 — Kuronada’s Honor: While Honor is 5.0+, gain +2k1 when spending a Void Point on a School Skill roll (instead of +1k1). Cannot be used in Center Stance.

- Rank 3 — Tamago’s Expertise: Make melee attacks with Samurai weapons as Simple Actions.

**RANK 1 PATHS:**

**Disciples of Sun Tao [Bushi] [CR]:**

Ronin who apply Sun Tao’s tactical wisdom to the art of the duel.

- Benefit: +1 Reflexes | Skills: Athletics, Defense, Hunting, Iaijutsu, Kenjutsu, any 2 Skills | Honor: 4.5 | Outfit: Light Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, any 1 weapon, Traveling Pack, 1 koku

- Technique — The Gaze of Sun Tao: Add your Honor Rank to the total of all Iaijutsu Skill Rolls.

**Forest Killers [Bushi] [CR]:**

Perhaps the most ruthless and fearsome bandit organization in the Empire’s history. They exploit the superstitions surrounding the Shinomen Mori by hiding within its depths.

- Benefit: +1 Agility | Skills: Athletics, Defense, Hunting, Kenjutsu, Kyujutsu, Stealth, any 1 Weapon Skill | Honor: 1.5 | Outfit: Light Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, any 1 weapon, Traveling Pack, 2 koku

- Technique — Strength of the Forest: Gain additional Wounds per Wound Rank equal to your Stamina. Add Stamina to the total of all melee damage rolls.

**Hawk Purist [Courtier]:**

Ronin who impersonate members of the (non-canonical) Hawk Clan during the Heresy era to gain status. Can also represent any ronin band that aspires to become a Minor Clan.

- Benefit: +1 Reflexes | Skills: Athletics, Defense, Hunting, Jiujutsu, Kenjutsu, Kyujutsu, any one Skill | Honor: 3.5

- Technique — Grace from the Shadows: Assert membership in a named clan and make a Contested Awareness roll (Complex Action). On success, the opponent treats your Status as one Rank higher.

**Koga Ninja [Ninja]:**

The Koga specialize in protecting and avenging the common people by killing samurai who abuse their station. Only available to those born into the Koga “family” or recruited by existing members.

- Skills: Acting, Athletics, Chain Weapons, Craft: Poison, Knives (Tanto), Stealth, any one Merchant or Low Skill | Outfit: Peasant Clothing, Dark Clothing, Knife, any three ninja weapons, Traveling Pack, 5 zeni

- Technique — The People’s Vengeance: +1k1 to any Skill roll made against a member of the samurai caste.

**People’s Legionnaire [Bushi] [IH]:**

A spear-fighting technique devised by the monk Rojin for Asahime’s peasant army, designed for simple ashigaru to stand their ground against samurai using numbers and discipline. Can represent any peasant or disciplined ronin army.

- Benefit: +1 Stamina | Skills: Athletics, Defense, Hunting, Knives, Spears, Stealth, any 1 Merchant or Low Skill | Honor: 0.0 | Outfit: Ashigaru Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Yari, any 1 Peasant weapon, Backpack, 5 zeni

- Technique — Unity of Purpose: After Initiative is rolled, take a Free Action to lower your Initiative to match an ally also trained in this Technique. If you and such an ally attack the same opponent, gain +1k0 per such ally striking the same foe. Max bonus dice equal to Insight Rank +1.

**Tawagoto’s Army [Bushi] [CR]:**

Inspired by the young ronin Tawagoto, who rallied ronin and peasants to protect the innocent from violent peasant revolutions inspired by the mad monk Kokujin.

- Benefit: +1 Agility | Skills: Battle, Defense, Etiquette, Investigation, Kenjutsu, Spears, any 1 Skill | Honor: 4.5 | Outfit: Light Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, any 1 weapon, Traveling Pack, 1 koku

- Technique — The People’s Will: When spending a Void Point to add +1k1 to any Skill or Trait Roll, also add your Honor Rank to the total of that roll.

**Tengoku’s Justice [Bushi] [CR]:**

Mountain bandits with a heretical name. Extremely ruthless; they make their home in the Dragon mountains and are hunted by Mantis magistrates.

- Benefit: +1 Strength | Skills: Athletics, Defense, Horsemanship, Hunting, Kenjutsu, Kyujutsu, any 1 Skill | Honor: 4.5 | Outfit: Ashigaru or Light Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, Bow or Knife, Traveling Pack, 1 koku

- Technique — Heaven’s Curse: When you surprise an opponent in a skirmish, double your Strength for determining how many damage dice you roll on that attack.

**The Tessen [Bushi] [CR]:**

Former Crane and Lion who banded together to protect the people of Toshi Ranbo. Because the Crane forbade blades within the city, they took up the war fan. They continue to patrol the streets of the Imperial City today.

- Benefit: +1 Stamina | Skills: Battle, Defense, Investigation, Jiujutsu, War Fans, any 2 Skills | Honor: 3.5 | Outfit: Light Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, War Fan, any 1 weapon, Traveling Pack, 2 koku

- Technique — Folds of the Iron Fan: No off-hand attack penalties when wielding a war fan in the off hand. Add War Fans Skill Rank to Armor TN at all times while wielding a war fan.

**RANK 2 PATHS:**

**Claws of the Wolf [Bushi] [EnE]:**

Brutal vigilantes who subscribe to the ideals of Toturi Sezaru. Members wear ceramic masks similar to those made famous by Sezaru. Ruthless in judging and punishing violations of Imperial law.

- Requirements: Wary, Driven.

- Technique — Hunting the Darkness: +2k2 to the total of your first attack roll in a skirmish if the opponent is someone you know without question is guilty of a violation of Imperial law.

**East Wind [Bushi] [EnE]:**

A benevolent brotherhood devoted to protecting the common people of East Hub Village. Originally employed as merchant guards, now patrolling the city to curb crime — eventually sponsored by the Unicorn Clan.

- Requirements: Commerce 1+, Horsemanship 2+, Honor 4+.

- Technique — Shielded by the East: When in Defense or Full Defense Stance performing the Guard Action, increase both your Armor TN and your charge’s Armor TN by 5.

**Eyes of Nanashi [Bushi] [EnE]:**

The law enforcement of Nanashi Mura (“Anonymous Village”) — an unusual settlement where the Dragon have always permitted resident ronin to govern their own affairs.

- Requirements: Lore: Law 3, Honor Rank 4+.

- Technique — Strike the Center: +1k1 on any attack roll intended to disable rather than kill (GM’s discretion; Disarm, Knockdown, and certain Called Shots are typical indicators).

**Fireman Gang Lord [Bushi]:**

Ronin who have risen to control criminal enterprises in cities like Ryoko Owari. Can represent any local crime lord who reigns over his own territory.

- Technique — Master of the Dice: Add your Intimidation Skill Rank to the total of any Contested Social Roll you make.

**The Guards’ Wrath [Bushi]:**

A reasonably honorable ronin otokodate who hire themselves out as guards and escorts. Can represent any group of honorable ronin-for-hire.

- Special: Cannot be Forest Killers or Tengoku’s Justice.

- Technique — The Guards’ Wrath: When you take the Guard Action, your Armor TN is not reduced.

**Hateru Ninja:**

Specialists in impersonating Dragon samurai who have actually infiltrated the Kitsuki family to monitor the clan.

- Requirements: Acting 3, Stealth 3.

- Technique — The False Dragon: +2k1 to Acting rolls when playing the role of a Dragon samurai. Free Raise on any Skill roll for the purpose of avoiding leaving clues to your identity or creating false clues to lead enemies away.

**Kaze-do Fighter [Bushi/Monk]:**

Ancient techniques of Togashi Kaze, the basis for almost all modern Rokugani martial arts. Preserved in abbreviated form among peasants and Brotherhood monks. Normally only available to peasants, monks, or in extremely rare cases a ronin with a suitable teacher.

- Requirements: Jiujutsu (Kaze-do) 3.

- Technique — The Way of Air: When fighting unarmed, any opponent attacking you with a melee weapon suffers −Xk0 to their attacking Skill Roll, where X equals their Air Ring. Cannot apply to more opponents per Round than your Insight Rank.

**Master of Games [Courtier]:**

Diplomats of Nanashi Mura or similar long-lived and resourceful ronin groups. Masters at turning their low status to tactical advantage in social situations.

- Requires: Courtier 3, Etiquette 3. Must be a resident of Nanashi Mura or belong to a ronin group with similar resources.

- Technique — Forge Your Own Fate: When making a Social Skill Roll against someone of higher Status, gain +2k1 as long as you maintain a modest and subservient manner.

**Moonless Riders [Bushi] [EnE]:**

Former Unicorn, Lion, and Dragon samurai who train exclusively at night, becoming the premier nocturnal scouts and couriers in the Empire. Often employed along contested clan borders.

- Requirements: Horsemanship 3+, Way of the Land.

- Technique — Moving the Shadow: No penalties from natural darkness. When active in darkness, gain +2k1 instead of +1k1 when spending a Void Point on any non-Weapon Bugei Skill.

**Silent Blades [Bushi] [EnE]:**

Would-be assassins who overestimate their own skill. Unknowing pawns of the more capable Shadowed Steel, brought out when a scapegoat is needed or a low-risk target eliminated.

- Requirements: Ninjutsu 3+, Stealth 3+.

- Technique — Black Hearts, Red Blades: When attacking an opponent unaware of your presence, add your Stealth Skill Rank to the total of all attack and damage rolls against that opponent for the duration of the skirmish.

**The Thousand [Bushi] [SE]:**

Formed during the First Yasuki War under a Crane ronin named Tamedaore. The band peaked at over a thousand men and fought for the Crane; its last remnants were wiped out by the Unicorn in 816.

- Requirements: Iaijutsu 4, Void 3.

- Technique — Tamedaore’s Secret: While in Center Stance and on the Round immediately after leaving it, gain a bonus to Armor TN equal to 2× your Void Ring.

**RANK 3 PATHS:**

**Broken Guard [Bushi] [EnE]:**

Former Akodo family members who became ronin after the family’s dissolution. Led by Saigorei, a former officer with a deep grudge against the Unicorn. Renowned as dangerously effective anti-cavalry fighters.

- Requirements: Polearms 3+, Heart of Vengeance (Unicorn).

- Technique — The Tiger’s Teeth: Make a melee attack as a Simple Action when using a polearm. +1k1 on attack rolls against targets on horseback or similarly-sized mounts.

**The Ghosts of the Forest:**

A small ronin band with a legacy many generations old, defending Nazo Mori. Tends to recruit from its own family lines — some members are sixth or seventh generation.

- Requirements: Way of the Land (Nazo Mori), Lore: Spirit Realms 2.

- Technique — Walk Among the Trees: Free Raise on all Athletics, Hunting, and Stealth rolls within any woodland area. Within sight of Nazo Mori, also gain +1k0 on any Perception Trait roll (effects stack).

**Hidden Sword [Bushi] [EnE]:**

A large group patrolling the outskirts of the Shinomen Mori. Sworn enemies of the Forest Killers. Honorable but extremely practical — they deal regularly with Scorpion, Crab, and Unicorn and resist blind idealism.

- Requirements: Hero of the People.

- Technique — Keeping the Peace: When fighting against an opponent with lower Honor Rank than yours, make melee attacks as a Simple Action with Samurai keyword weapons.

**Machi-Kanshisha [Bushi] [EnE]:**

Guardians of the City of the Rich Frog, named for their preferred weapon — the iron smoking pipe. They prefer to subdue rather than kill, since the Lion and Unicorn closely watch any significant violence near their borders.

- Requirements: Athletics 2+, Staves 3+.

- Technique — Smoke and Mirrors: When using an iron pipe, Free Raise on any Disarm or Knockdown attack. Spend a Void Point to make a melee attack with a machi-kanshisha as a Simple Action (up to two Void Points per Round this way).

**Order of Isashi [Shugenja] [SE]:**

Founded after the second war with Iuchiban. Members travel to the most war-torn and disaster-plagued regions healing the injured and treating the sick with no expectation of payment. Welcomed in Crab lands; viewed with suspicion elsewhere.

- Replaces: Ronin Shugenja 3. Requires: Medicine 3, Honor 5.0+.

- Technique — Isashi’s Mercy: +1k0 to Medicine Skill rolls. Free Raise when casting any spell that heals Wounds or cures illness.

**Scales of the Carp [Courtier]:**

A ronin smuggling organization run by the Yasuki family to conduct trade with the Crane, who otherwise largely cut off mercantile contact with the Crab. The Yasuki found them too useful to disband even after trade barriers diminished.

- Requirements: Commerce 4, Lore: Underworld 3. Must be recruited by existing members.

- Technique — Swimming Beneath the Waves: +1k0 to all Temptation (Bribery) rolls. Against a Crab or Crane samurai, the bonus is +1k1 instead — and if they agree to the bribe, spend a Void Point (with GM permission) to gain them as an Ally with 1 Devotion (permanent unless betrayed).

**Serpents of Sanada [Bushi] [EnE]:**

One of the most successful and hated pirate groups in nearly a thousand years. Have attacked the Mantis Clan on its home islands — something no pirate ever before dared. The Mantis hunt them relentlessly.

- Requirements: Knives 3+, Sailing 3+, Honor Rank 3 or lower.

- Technique — The Serpents’ Coils: When wielding a wooden weapon or Peasant keyword weapon, make melee attacks as Simple Actions.

**The Silken Promises Geisha [Courtier]:**

A secretive organization of geisha who use performance skills to develop covert political influence. Primary sponsors are the Scorpion Clan; have also worked with the Kolat and Spider Clan.

- Requires: Must be a geisha admitted to the Silken Promises with Acting 3 and at least three Perform Skills at Rank 3.

- Technique — Dance of Silk: Make a Contested Perform (or Acting)/Awareness vs Etiquette (Courtesy)/Willpower roll during an artistic performance. On success, lure the target into a preferred attitude or choice. May also take them as a 1-point Devotion Ally (GM’s option). Cannot force decisions directly against the target’s character or true loyalties. Cannot target the same person more than once per month.

**Snow Riders [Bushi] [EnE]:**

Survivors from Moto Chagatai’s notorious winter campaign who went on to sell their services as couriers, messengers, and cavalry specialists for winter operations. Always employed during winter months.

- Requirements: Athletics 3+, Horsemanship 4+.

- Technique — The Journey’s Beginning: Ignore movement penalties caused by snow or ice. Spend a Void Point while mounted to make a single attack as a Simple Action.

**Tsume Pikemen [Bushi] [IH]:**

Former retainers of the Tsume vassal family (exterminated in the War of Bleeding Flowers) who became ronin but preserved their traditional fighting methods. Reliable and trustworthy — they never betray their employers.

- Requirements: Spears 3+, Polearms 3+, Honor Rank 3+.

- Technique — Wall of Pikes: While in Defense Stance with a spear or polearm, when an opponent makes a melee attack against you or an ally within 10’, spend a Void Point as a Free Action to immediately attack that opponent. In Full Defense, costs 2 Void Points instead.

**The Unbroken [Bushi]:**

One of the most legendary and transient ronin bands — formed and collapsed many times when a Tainted wave-man unearths their technique. Their training purges the Taint through defeating powerful Shadowlands creatures, but is far more likely to result in death than success. All members wear masks.

- Requirements: Lore: Shadowlands 3. Must have at least one Rank of Taint and find a way to learn the Technique.

- Technique — The Unbroken: When fighting a Shadowlands creature or one of the Lost, add Taint Rank in unkept dice to attack rolls (gain 3 Taint Points each time this is used). Any time you singlehandedly kill a Shadowlands creature or Lost, spend a Void Point to permanently lose one Taint Point. If you singlehandedly kill an extremely powerful Shadowlands foe (major oni, Onisu, or Lost of at least two Insight Ranks higher), spend a Void Point and make a Contested Earth roll against the foe’s Earth — on success, purge all Taint from your body (and permanently lose the ability to use this Technique).

**RANK 4 PATHS:**

**Guardian of the Hidden Temple [Bushi]:**

Elite defenders of the Kolat’s Hidden Temple. Recruited from skilled, loyal ronin already within the conspiracy. Taught defensive tactics to defeat the specialized warriors of the Great Clans, with no compunction about ambushes and surprise attacks.

- Requirements: Must be recruited by the Kolat for their Hidden Guard. Cannot be Tainted or corrupted by the Nothing.

- Technique — Tigers Do Not Fall: When an opponent declares any Maneuver against you (including Increased Damage), spend a Void Point as a Free Action to negate the Maneuver’s beneficial effects — the opponent still must make Raises to hit, but gains no benefit from them. Also ignore the Armor TN penalty for being Grappled at all times.

**Kenburo’s Way [Bushi] [IH]:**

Devised by Akodo Kenburo while a young ronin and later taught to his most trusted magistrates. Core tenet: “Break their spirit and their skill doesn’t matter.” Known within the Ruby Dojo; GMs may also make it available to ronin.

- Requirements: Intimidation 5, Iaijutsu 5. Must be a student of the Ruby Dojo.

- Technique — The Butcher’s Gaze: Before Assessment rolls in an iaijutsu duel, spend a Void Point as a Free Action and make a Contested Intimidation/Willpower roll against your opponent (counts as a Fear effect). On success, your opponent’s dice do not explode on his Assessment and Focus rolls for this duel.

**Minor Clan Alliance Diplomat [Courtier]:**

Minor Clan samurai who have survived the minefield of Great Clan courts are sent to Kudo Mura to learn from the Alliance’s finest diplomats — studying how to soothe Great Clan samurai and avoid provoking them.

- Replaces: Any Minor Clan School 4. Requires: Must belong to a Minor Clan.

- Technique — The Courts of Kudo Mura: When conversing with a Great Clan or Imperial Family samurai, gain +3k0 on all Contested Rolls using Etiquette or Sincerity.

**Order of Five Weapons [Shugenja] [SE]:**

Founded by a Void shugenja named Ekuro. Teaches mastery of the four Elemental weapon spells and maintains a secret spell for its most trusted members.

- Replaces: Ronin Shugenja 4. Requires: Kenjutsu, Heavy Weapon, Spears, and Staves all at Rank 3+. Must be able to cast at least two of the four Elemental Weapon spells.

- Technique — Ekuro’s Weapons: Cast any of the four Elemental Weapon spells as a Simple Action. Spend a Void Point as a Free Action to attack with summoned elemental weapons as Simple Actions for Rounds equal to your Insight Rank. If you have Ishiken-do, may learn the secret spell “Dart of Void.”

**Seven Waves Mercenaries [Bushi] [EnE]:**

An older ronin organization founded by seven outcasts, one from each original Great Clan. When taking a job, members associated with the relevant clans step aside to avoid bias. Organized into seven-man teams.

- Requirements: Clan Ronin only.

- Technique — Roaring to Shake Heaven: When fighting alongside allies, gain +2 to the total of all attack and damage rolls for each different Basic School represented in your group. Cannot exceed 2× your Void Ring total bonus.

**Sword of Yotsu [Bushi] [EnE]:**

Perhaps the most successful ronin brotherhood in the Empire — one of only a handful of actual ronin families. The Yotsu earned their family name by saving the Emperor’s son. They allow other ronin to swear fealty and take their name in exchange for a vow to protect the defenseless.

- Requirements: Kenjutsu 4+, Hero of the People, Honor Rank 5+.

- Technique — Shelter the Blameless: During any Round when you are actively defending or protecting another, gain one bonus Void Point for use during that Round. Unspent Void Points from this Technique are lost at the end of the Round.

**Weavers [Bushi] [EnE]:**

Elite agents of the Kolat conspiracy — unknown even to most Kolat subordinates. Communicate through encoded messages, discrete tattoos, and secret hand signals. Rarely meet in person. Masters of deceit and violence.

- Requirements: Allies (Kolat) OR Dark Secret (Kolat) OR Obligation (Kolat).

- Technique — Twist the Weave: If you have ever won a Contested Skill Roll against someone using Sincerity (Deceit), Intimidation, or Temptation, when you subsequently face that person in a skirmish gain a Free Raise usable on an attack roll during the first Round.

**RANK 5 PATHS:**

**Iron Gauntlet Brotherhood [Bushi] [EnE]:**

Battered survivors of ronin groups that have been completely wiped out. Among the most seasoned and fearsome ronin in all of Rokugan. Utterly ruthless in battle. Rarely speak save to negotiate contract terms.

- Requirements: Earth 4, any one Weapon Skill at 5+.

- Technique — For My Brothers: When fighting alongside other Iron Gauntlet Brotherhood members, spend a Void Point to negate damage inflicted on an ally by an amount equal to your Void Ring × 5.

**Shadowed Steel [Bushi] [EnE]:**

Former Scorpion vassals carefully cultivated into a completely deniable and disposable asset. Answer only to the Shosuro family through covert means. Called upon when an objective absolutely must be achieved at all costs and the risk is too high to use the Shosuro’s own assets.

- Requirements: Athletics 4+, Ninjutsu 4+, Stealth 4+.

- Technique — Death’s Dark Shadow: When you succeed at a Contested Stealth Skill Roll against an opponent trying to detect you, spend a Void Point to add the margin of your success to your next attack roll against that opponent. Must be used within one hour of the Contested Roll.

**The Taoist Archers [Bushi/Monk]:**

Devotees of perfect harmony through kyujutsu. Have existed within the Brotherhood of Shinsei for centuries. In the Pre-Coup era a ronin named Gahei learned and began teaching the technique, making it available to ronin characters who can find a willing teacher.

- Requirements: Void 4, Kyujutsu (Yumi) 5, Lore: Theology 3, Meditation 4.

- Technique — Flight of Innocence: When making archery attacks, ignore all TN and dice penalties for being Blind, Missing Eye, or target being concealed by darkness, magical illusion, camouflage, or similar visual obstructions. Penalties for physical obstacles and obstructive conditions still apply. Spend a Void Point while wielding a bow for +1k1 to damage.

**Wolf Legion [Bushi] [EnE]:**

Formed from the survivors of Toturi’s Army who did not rejoin their former clans after the Clan War. For decades, the Wolf Legion continued protecting the people of Rokugan, alternately ignoring or confronting the selfish politics of the Great Clans.

- Requirements: Honor Rank 5+.

- Technique — Black Lion Talon: Whenever you spend a Void Point to add +1k1 to an attack roll, also add your Honor Rank to the total of that attack roll.
