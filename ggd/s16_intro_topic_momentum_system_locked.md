# 16. Topic & Momentum System — LOCKED

Topics are the political agenda of every court session. They emerge from real world events — crises, wars, disasters, supernatural threats — and grow in urgency over time. Every character has their own personal relevance score for each active crisis, shaped by proximity, clan affiliation, and the nature of the threat itself. A distant major war is a topic of conversation, but it should not dominate the agenda if something is burning in your own territory.
