## 57.20 Missing NPC Decision Paths --- Construction, Marriage, Appointment --- LOCKED

This amendment adds three new NeedTypes and eight new ActionIDs to close the remaining gaps where designed game systems have no NPC decision path. NeedType count: 76 → 79. ActionID count: 108 → 117 (86 standard + 31 Kolat). Annex A alignment entries and cross-NeedType ActionID additions transcribed in v454.

**57.20.1 BUILD_INFRASTRUCTURE NeedType**

Fires when a lord evaluates their province and identifies a structural gap that construction can fill. The NeedType decomposes into the appropriate construction ActionID based on what the province needs. Decomposition logic: if province has no worship infrastructure and Fortune worship is failing → BUILD_SHRINE scores highest. If province is a border province with no fortification → BUILD_FORTIFICATION scores highest. If province population exceeds comfortable capacity and suitable terrain exists → FOUND_VILLAGE scores highest. If lord is a Family Daimyo+ and province needs a major religious site → FOUND_TEMPLE or FOUND_MONASTERY. If lord is coastal, has no ships, and faces naval threats → COMMISSION_SHIP scores highest. Priority: low (1–2) under normal conditions. Rises to priority 3 when the gap is actively causing problems (worship failure, no fortification on a contested border, pirate fleet with no ships to respond).

ActionIDs under BUILD_INFRASTRUCTURE: FOUND_VILLAGE (1 AP). Authority: Provincial Daimyo+. Requirements: 3 Koku units, PU to relocate, suitable terrain. Skill map: Engineering + Intelligence, Category 6. Mechanics: Section 4.3.22. Personality leans: Jin +10 (expanding settlement for the people), Kyoryoku +5 (expanding territory), Seigyo +8 (controlling development). BUILD_FORTIFICATION (1 AP). Authority: Provincial Daimyo+. Requirements: 5 Koku units. Skill map: Engineering + Intelligence, Category 6. Mechanics: Section 4.3.22. Personality leans: Yu +10 (defensive preparation), Chugi +8 (protecting lord’s territory), Seigyo +5 (strategic positioning). BUILD_SHRINE (1 AP). Authority: Provincial Daimyo+. Requirements: Koku cost per worship infrastructure rules. Skill map: Theology + Awareness, Category 6. Mechanics: Section 4.3.21. Personality leans: Jin +8 (spiritual care for the people), Rei +10 (proper respect for the Celestial Order), Makoto +5 (sincere devotion). FOUND_TEMPLE (1 AP). Authority: Family Daimyo+ or Clan Champion. Requirements: founding population, Koku investment. Skill map: Theology + Awareness, Category 6. Personality leans: same as BUILD_SHRINE with higher baseline scores. FOUND_MONASTERY (1 AP). Authority: Family Daimyo+ with Brotherhood approval. Same skill map and leans as FOUND_TEMPLE. COMMISSION_SHIP (1 AP). Authority: lord of a coastal province or settlement with shipbuilding capability. Requirements: Koku cost per ship type (Kobune: 3 Koku units, Sengokobune: 8 Koku units — PROVISIONAL). Construction time: 1 season per ship. Skill map: Commerce + Intelligence, Category 3 (Economic). Personality leans: Seigyo +8 (strategic naval investment), Dosatsu +5 (foresight), Kyoryoku +5 (building strength).

**57.20.2 ARRANGE_MARRIAGE NeedType**

Fires when a lord has unmarried characters of marriageable age under their authority and a political opportunity exists. Marriage in L5R is a political tool — alliances, succession security, clan relations, and dispute resolution all flow through marriage arrangement. The NeedType evaluates: does the lord have an unmarried child, sibling, or retainer? Is there a clan relationship that would benefit from a marriage alliance? Is succession insecure (no heir)? Is there an active dispute that a marriage could resolve? Decomposition: the NeedType produces a single ActionID. ARRANGE_MARRIAGE (1 AP). Authority: lord of the character being married (daimyo, family head, or Clan Champion). The lord initiates a marriage proposal to the target character’s lord. This generates a diplomatic exchange — the target lord evaluates the proposal through their own decision engine (disposition toward the proposing clan, political benefit, character compatibility). Acceptance creates a marriage event, generates a court topic, and modifies clan dispositions. Rejection generates a minor diplomatic slight (−3 disposition). Skill map: Courtier (Manipulation) + Awareness, Category 2 (Social). Personality leans: Seigyo +12 (strategic alliance building), Rei +8 (proper social arrangement), Dosatsu +10 (identifying the right match), Jin +5 (matching compatible people), Kyoryoku −8 (Strength sees marriage as weakness — prefers military solutions). Objective alignment: RAISE_DISPOSITION: 70 (marriage as relationship tool). SEEK_PEACE: 80 (marriage to end a conflict). GATHER_INTELLIGENCE: 40 (marriage places an ally in another clan’s household). met_characters constraint applies — a lord can only propose marriage to families they have met or know of through topics, letters, or intelligence.

**57.20.3 FILL_VACANCY NeedType**

Fires when a position under the lord’s appointment authority becomes vacant — the previous holder died, was convicted, was promoted, or was removed. The lord must fill the position or leave it empty (which has consequences: an unfilled magistrate position means no law enforcement, an unfilled military command means no operational direction for the unit). Decomposition: the NeedType evaluates candidates from the lord’s vassals and retainers, scoring them by competence (relevant skills), loyalty (disposition toward the lord), personality fit (certain positions favor certain virtues — a magistrate benefits from Gi, a military commander from Yu), and political considerations (appointing a rival’s ally creates leverage, appointing a loyalist ensures reliability). The NeedType produces a single ActionID. APPOINT_TO_POSITION (1 AP). Authority: the lord with appointment authority for that position type (Section 22.8). The lord selects a character and assigns them to the vacant position. The appointed character gains the position’s Status, responsibilities, and operational_superior_id assignment per Section 11.3.18. Skill map: Courtier (Manipulation) + Awareness, Category 2 (Social). Personality leans: Chugi +10 (duty to fill the role), Seigyo +12 (controlling who holds power), Dosatsu +8 (evaluating candidates), Gi +5 (selecting the most qualified). Objective alignment: DEFEND_PROVINCE: 80 (filling a military vacancy). UPHOLD_LAW: 85 (filling a magistrate vacancy). EVALUATE_PROVINCES: 60 (routine governance — filling positions as part of provincial review). Priority: 3 for critical positions (magistrate, military commander). 2 for important positions (school master, temple head). 1 for routine positions. An unfilled critical position generates an escalating urgency modifier: +5 priority per season the position remains vacant.
