## 27.7 The Scorpion Clan

The Emperor's Underhand. The Scorpion Clan was tasked at the Empire's founding with doing what others cannot — protecting the Emperor through deception, assassination, and manipulation so that the honorable clans need never dirty themselves with such work. They wear masks, practice deception openly, and are reviled by most of the Empire. And they bear this willingly, because it is their duty. 'Bayushi, I will be your villain' — the clan's founding philosophy.

- Clan identity: Deceptive, manipulative, utterly loyal to the Emperor in their own way. They sacrifice their honor so others need not. The most sophisticated political operators in the Empire alongside the Crane.

- Key historical events: The Scorpion Clan Coup (1123); the disbanding and restoration of the Scorpion; the Lying Darkness corruption of the Shosuro/Soshi; Bayushi Kachiko and the Clan Wars.

- Inter-clan relations: Rival/partner relationship with Crane — they play the same political games and respect each other while competing fiercely. Enemies of Lion (the most honorable clan despises the least). Cooperative with Crab on maho matters (Kuroiban).

**The Bayushi Family:**

Motto: 'I can swim.' The preeminent Scorpion family — most Champions come from the Bayushi. Sinister grace, dark charm. Men are roguishly handsome, women seductively coy. Masters of manipulation: subtle psychological tactics (sleep deprivation of guests, mid-bath check-ins), psychological pressure, and genuine military force. The public face of the clan. Consider the number three unlucky; two is lucky (twinned relationship of Bayushi and Shiba).

**The Shosuro Family:**

Second in prominence to the Bayushi — but this ranking is carefully cultivated. In reality the Shosuro provide the bulk of the clan's spies, saboteurs, and assassins. Bland, forgettable, unassuming by design. Also operate the Silken Smile theatre in Shiro no Shosuro — renowned acting troupes that provide cover for spy networks. The Empire's foremost experts in poison — maintain extensive poison gardens. Deeply corrupted by the Lying Darkness for centuries, now purged.

**The Soshi Family:**

The spiritual center of the Scorpion — and the focus of its most insidious secrets. Founded by the returned Shosuro after her contamination by the Lying Darkness. Studied 'tejina' shadow magic alongside conventional elemental magic. Particular affinity for Air — illusion, observation, communication. Stay in the background gathering intelligence and spreading disinformation. Study the Tao but interpret it to find advantages.

**The Yogo Family:**

The most notorious of the Scorpion families — their founder was cursed by Fu Leng to betray the one he loved most. This curse passed through his bloodline and haunts the family to this day. Every Yogo samurai lives with the knowledge they may one day betray someone they love. Viewed with caution even within the Scorpion. Known for expertise in wards and protective magic — fighting the curse through containment. They manage much of the Scorpion's dark magical knowledge.
