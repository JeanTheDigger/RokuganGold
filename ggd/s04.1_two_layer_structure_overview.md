## 4.1 Two-Layer Structure Overview

The two layers are:

- The World Map — a macro-level strategic view of all of Rokugan, tracking provinces, armies, political events, clan relationships, and the passage of in-game time.

- The Local Interface — a micro-level view where individual player characters exist, move, fight, and talk. Composed of two sub-modes: the MUD Interface and the ASCII Map.
