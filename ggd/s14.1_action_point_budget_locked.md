## 14.1 Action Point Budget — LOCKED

Action Point budgets, costs, and per-tier allowances. See Section 14.2 for the Player Reaction Menu which consumes these AP. All characters receive 2 AP per IC day (Morning and Afternoon slots), 8 AP per OOC day. This is flat across all position tiers — a Provincial Daimyo and a wandering ronin have the same personal AP. Lord-tier characters have a separate Civilian Order budget (Section 57.34) for administrative actions, and military commanders have a separate Military Order budget (Section 14) for command actions. Neither draws from personal AP.
