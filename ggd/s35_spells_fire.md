# 35. Spells — Fire

**MASTERY LEVEL 1:**

**Biting Steel [CR]**

- Fire 1 (Craft) | Range: Touch | Area: 1 bladed weapon | Duration: 1 minute | Raises: Duration (+1 minute)

Fire spirits infuse a metal bladed weapon (sword, knife, naginata, etc.) with their fury. DR increased by 1k1 for the duration. Cannot affect non-metal blades, nemuranai, or weapons already enhanced by magical effects.

**Burning Kiss of Steel [CR]**

- Fire 1 (Battle) | Range: Touch | Area: One melee weapon in caster's hand | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Duration (+2 minutes)

A tendril of fire engulfs the caster's weapon. Weapon gains +1k1 to melee attack rolls (+2k2 against mounted opponents or opponents larger than human size). Spell ends if the weapon is dropped or lost.

**Elemental Crucible**

- Fire 1 | Range: Touch | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Area (+1 cubic foot)

Strips a touched object of all elements except one (declared by the caster, cannot be Void). Other elements are destroyed in appropriate fashion (water boils out, earth crumbles, fire dissipates, air creates a vacuum), leaving only the chosen element. Effects on the object depend on what is removed (loss of water = loss of pliability; loss of earth = brittleness; etc.). Affects one object of 1 cubic foot + 1 per Raise. GM may rule certain complex objects (such as a katana) have too many intertwined elements to be affected.

**Envious Flames [CR]**

- Fire 1 | Range: 30' | Area: 1 target | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: None

Invokes a single Fire kami to lance unerringly at the target (cannot miss within range). Deals 2k2 Wounds. Burns are very painful — if this spell targets a shugenja in the process of casting, their Willpower roll TN is 20 + damage dealt (instead of the normal 10 + damage).

**Extinguish [CR]**

- Fire 1 | Range: Personal | Area: 100' radius | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Area (+20')

Dismisses active Fire kami in the area. All non-magical fire within the area is immediately snuffed out. Any fire damage (magical or not) has its DR reduced by 1k1 until the start of the next Round.

**Fire Kami's Blessing**

- Fire 1 | Range: Touch | Area: One target creature | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Duration (+1/2 hour per Raise)

Fire kami stimulate the target's mind and memory, allowing recall of even briefly encountered obscure information. Target gains +Xk0 (X = caster's Fire Ring) to all High or Merchant Skill rolls using the Intelligence Trait.

**Fires of Purity [CR]**

- Fire 1 (Defense) | Range: 25' | Area: 1 target | Duration: 1 minute | Raises: Damage (+1k0 per 2 Raises)

Envelops the target in a shroud of bright flames. The target and their carried items take no damage. Anyone who comes into contact with the target or strikes them in melee takes 2k2 Wounds. Anyone the target strikes unarmed or with a carried weapon also takes an extra 2k2 Wounds. Items set down cannot be picked back up without taking the damage. Ranged attacks bypass the barrier and deal damage normally.

**The Fires That Cleanse [CR]**

- Fire 1 | Range: Self | Area: 30' radius | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Area (+5' per 2 Raises)

Whips Fire kami into a frenetic chaos. Everyone in the area of effect, including the caster, suffers damage with DR equal to the caster's Fire Ring (one roll applied to all targets). The caster takes only half damage (rounded up) as the kami make some effort to avoid them.

**Fury of Osano-Wo [CR]**

- Fire 1 (Thunder) | Range: 300' | Area: 1 target | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Damage (+1k0 per 2 Raises)

Can only be cast outdoors. Summons a bolt of lightning from the sky that strikes the target for 5k2 Wounds. Everyone within 10' of the target must roll Stamina (TN 15) or be Deafened for 2 Rounds. During a moderate thunderstorm: 6k2 damage. During a disastrous storm or hurricane: 6k3 damage.

**Gift of Amaterasu**

- Fire 1 (Defense) | Range: 50' | Area: 50' radius sphere | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Range (+25' per Raise), Duration (+5 minutes per Raise)

A prayer to Amaterasu (or the Jade Sun in the twelfth century) that causes warm sunlight to blaze down on the chosen area, bathing it in full daylight even at night, indoors, or underground. Effective deterrent to thieves, assassins, and spies.

**Katana of Fire [CR]**

- Fire 1 (Battle, Craft) | Range: Personal or 20' | Area: 1 created weapon | Duration: 5 minutes | Raises: Damage (+1k0), Duration (+5 minutes), Range (+5')

Summons a blade of pure fire. DR 2k2. Default form is a katana; 1 Raise changes it to any other sword. Caster may use School Rank in place of Kenjutsu Skill. If the caster has Kenjutsu, adds Honor Rank to all damage rolls made with this weapon. Disappears if lost from the hand. May appear in an ally's hands within 20' — ally treated as caster but without the Honor Rank damage bonus.

**Never Alone [CR]**

- Fire 1 | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target | Duration: 5 Rounds (see below) | Raises: Targets (+1)

Opens the target's eyes to the courage of their ancestors. The target receives a bonus to all attack rolls, Skill rolls, and Trait rolls equal to the caster's Fire Ring. Effect lasts until the spell expires, the target fails an attack or Skill roll, or the target suffers Wounds from any source — whichever comes first.

**Osano-Wo's Blessing**

- Fire 1 | Range: 100' | Area: 40' radius | Duration: 30 minutes | Raises: Range (+25' per Raise), Area (+10' radius per Raise), Duration (+10 minutes per Raise)

The opposite of Extinguish — urges Fire kami to greater energy and vigor, making all fires within the area of effect impossible to extinguish by normal means for the duration. Any magical attempt to extinguish them (such as Extinguish) must win a Contested Fire roll against the caster. Controversial, as it can cause controllable fires to rage out of control.

**The Raging Forge [CR]**

- Fire 1 (Craft) | Range: Touch | Area: 1 weapon or armor | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: None

Invokes the power of the forge to remake a material item into its perfect form. The target loses all blemishes including cracks and nicks. Cannot repair items that are actually broken or destroyed. Can only affect items of ordinary quality.

**Warning Flame**

- Fire 1 (Battle) | Range: Touch | Area: One target person | Duration: 10 Rounds | Raises: Duration (+2 Rounds per Raise), Area (+1 person per 2 Raises)

Excites the Fire kami within the target's mind, allowing them to notice threats and react far more quickly. Target is immune to being surprised, gains +1k0 to Initiative rolls, and may add +3 to their Initiative total during the Reactions Stage of each Round.

**MASTERY LEVEL 2:**

**Disrupt the Aura [CR]**

- Fire 2 | Range: 50' | Area: Target person or creature | Duration: 24 hours | Raises: Duration (+12 hours per Raise)

Aggravates the Fire present in the target's body, disrupting their elemental balance. While the spell is active, the target cannot be healed by magical means — spells, items, or Techniques that attempt to restore Wounds automatically fail. Mundane Medicine Skill treatment still works. Sense (Fire) will reveal many excited Fire spirits within the target.

**Enticing the Dance of Flame [CR]**

- Fire 2 | Range: 50' | Area: 20' radius | Duration: 2 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round)

Fire kami erupt into a violent dance at a location of the caster's choice, igniting the area. Every target in the area takes 3k2 Wounds on the Round the spell takes effect. Each subsequent Round while the spell remains active, every target still in the area takes an additional 2k1 Wounds.

**The Fires From Within [CR]**

- Fire 2 | Range: 100' | Area: 1 target | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Targets (+1 per 2 Raises)

The most widely known offensive Fire spell in Rokugan, used since the Empire's earliest days. Summons Fire kami as an orb of flame that streaks toward the target with spectacular visual effect. DR equal to the caster's Fire Ring.

**Hurried Steps [CR]**

- Fire 2 | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 2 rounds | Raises: None

Draws on the speed of Fire kami to accelerate the next spell the caster casts. The casting time of the next Fire spell cast is reduced by 4 Rounds. If the next spell is Mastery Level 3 or lower, it casts instantaneously as a Simple Action. If the caster does not begin casting within 2 Rounds, the benefit elapses.

**Mental Quickness [CR]**

- Fire 2 | Range: Touch | Area: 1 item | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: None

Imbues an item with the cleverness and wit of Fire. Anyone who carries that item has their Intelligence Trait increased by 3 for the duration.

**Purity of Shinsei**

- Fire 2 (Defense) | Range: 50' | Area: One target person or effect | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+10' per Raise), Area (+1 target per 2 Raises)

Calls on Shinsei’s principles of Elemental unity and harmony to oppose unnatural foreign magic. Ends the effects of a gaijin spell or spell-like effect on an individual, or on a non-personal effect (a curse on a castle, village, or item). GM has final discretion on applicability. If optional Counterspelling rules are in use, GM may allow this spell to Counterspell meishodo and gaijin magic.

**Relentless Heat [CR]**

- Fire 2 (Defense) | Range: Touch | Area: 1 armor | Duration: 10 rounds | Raises: Duration (+2 rounds)

Infuses a Fire kami into a suit of armor, causing it to glow with the strength of the desert sun. Any opponent who attempts to strike the wearer (whether or not the attack hits) is immediately considered Fatigued until the beginning of their next Turn. Penalty applies to the triggering attack roll. Attackers in Full Attack Stance immediately assume Attack Stance. No effect on ranged attacks or spells.

**Tail of the Fire Dragon [CR]**

- Fire 2 | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 4 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round)

Invokes Fire kami to form a whip-like tendril extending from the caster's hand. Strikes enemies up to 30' away. Attack roll: Agility + (twice School Rank), keeping Agility. DR equal to the caster's Fire Ring. The tendril does not harm the caster.

**Ward of Purity [CR]**

- Fire 2 (Ward) | Range: Touch | Area: 15' radius from the inscribed object | Duration: 1 day | Raises: None

Requires elaborate kanji inscribed on a flat surface (1 minute of concentrated effort). Protects the area against Shadowlands influence and the Lying Darkness. Tainted creatures (1+ Rank) or those under the Lying Darkness must make a Contested Willpower roll against the caster (+5 bonus to caster) to enter. Those who do enter suffer Wounds equal to Fire Ring + Insight Rank each Round they remain. Spells and missiles are unaffected. The inscription must remain visible and immobile or the ward is dispelled. Overlapping Wards do not stack.

**Wings of Fire**

- Fire 2 (Travel) | Range: Touch | Area: Self | Duration: 10 minutes | Raises: Duration (+5 minutes per Raise), Special (move at +1 Water Rank for 3 Raises)

Fire kami form large feathery wings on the caster's arms and back. Caster may fly slowly (Water 1 speed regardless of actual Water Ring; or Water 4 if already at significant height and gliding). Caster cannot use arms for anything but controlling the wings and will ignite easily flammable objects touched. When the spell ends Fire kami immediately disperse regardless of whether the caster is airborne.

**MASTERY LEVEL 3:**

**Agasha's Shield (Tamori's Curse)**

- Fire 3 (Defense, Wards) | Range: Touch/Self | Area: 30' radius | Duration: 6 Rounds | Raises: Area (+5' radius per Raise), Duration (+1 Round per Raise)

Creates a zone where Fire kami friendly to the caster reject intrusion by other Fire kami. Any hostile Fire spell cast within the area suffers -4k0 to the Spell Casting Roll. Any hostile Fire spell cast from outside attempting to project effects into the area suffers -3k0 to DR as the Shield disperses and weakens the attacking kami.

**Breath of the Fire Dragon [CR]**

- Fire 3 | Range: Personal | Area: 15' long, 5' wide blast | Duration: 4 rounds | Raises: None

Entreats the Dragon of Fire for a portion of his power. Once per Round as a Simple Action, the caster may breathe a bolt of flames. DR equal to the caster's Fire Ring, striking every target in front of the caster within the area. The caster cannot speak or cast other spells while the effect is active. May choose to end the spell during the Reactions Stage of any earlier Round.

**Fiery Wrath [CR]**

- Fire 3 | Range: 100' | Area: One free-standing structure or 50' × 50' area | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: None

Appeals to excitable Fire kami within a target structure's materials. All flammable materials within the area immediately catch fire and burn to ash. The fire cannot be extinguished by mundane means — only magic. The fire will not spread to adjoining structures (kami are kept under tight control). Living beings and non-flammable materials are not affected; even clothing on people within the area will burn, but their flesh will not singe.

**The Fist of Osano-Wo [CR]**

- Fire 3 (Thunder) | Range: 50' | Area: 20' radius | Duration: 1 round | Raises: Area (+10' radius)

Invokes the Fortune of Thunder's anger. Massive lightning strikes and fist-shaped flame bolts streak from the sky onto the targeted area. Weak or easily flammable structures are destroyed or set ablaze. DR equal to the caster's Fire Ring, inflicted on anyone caught within the area. Using this spell in populated areas is generally considered a criminal act unless extreme circumstances can be cited.

**Haze of Battle [CR]**

- Fire 3 (Battle) | Range: 10' | Area: 1 target | Duration: 5 rounds in combat, 1 hour out of combat | Raises: Duration (+1 Round)

Fills the target with the unfocused fury of Fire. The target immediately assumes the Full Attack Stance and cannot switch from it for the duration. Out of combat: target gains the Brash and Contrary Disadvantages for the duration. The target may resist with a Contested Willpower roll (caster adds Fire Ring to their roll). In combat: resist during the Reactions Stage each Round. Out of combat: resist every 10 minutes.

**Hungry Blade [CR]**

- Fire 3 (Craft) | Range: 50' | Area: 1 weapon | Duration: 5 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 Round)

A more potent version of Biting Steel that enhances any weapon. Strengthens Fire spirits in the weapon, forming a faint sheath of fire around it. Wielder adds +1k0 to all attack rolls. All damage dice explode on a result of 8 or better (each die can only explode once per roll on an 8 or 9, even with other effects that allow 9s to explode).

**Oath of the Heavens**

- Fire 3 | Range: Touch | Area: Two target persons | Duration: 5 Rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 Round per Raise), Special (+1k0 to the bonus per 2 Raises)

Originally an Agasha family secret; both the Phoenix Agasha and the Dragon Tamori know it today. Forms a Fire kami link between the ferocity of a bushi and the intellect of a shugenja (or two shugenja). Both recipients gain +2k0 to all rolls involving the Fire Ring and Fire Traits (Agility and Intelligence). However, the link makes each vulnerable to the other's conditions — if either becomes Fatigued, Dazed, or Stunned, the other immediately shares the Condition. If either is reduced to Down, Out, or Dead, the spell ends immediately.

**Ravenous Swarms [CR]**

- Fire 3 | Range: 30' | Area: 1 target person | Duration: 5 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 round)

A more powerful and sophisticated version of Fires From Within that also disrupts the target's Fire magic. A bolt of flames deals 5k3 Wounds on impact and then encircles the opponent for the duration. If the target casts any Fire spell during that time, the Fire kami instantly strike, dealing 3k3 extra Wounds and causing the Spell Casting Roll to automatically fail (target loses the appropriate spell slot).

**Shining Light [CR]**

- Fire 3 (Defense) | Range: 30' | Area: 1 target armor | Duration: 10 rounds | Raises: Damage (+1k0 per 2 Raises), Duration (+2 Rounds)

Temporarily binds a Fire kami into a piece of armor. The armor emits bright light that becomes blinding when the wearer is attacked. Any time the wearer is attacked in melee, the opponent takes 2k2 Wounds and is Blinded until the Reactions Stage of the same Round. No effect on ranged attacks.

**The Breath of Battle**

- Fire 3 (Battle, Imperial) | Range: 30' | Area: One target person | Duration: 5 Rounds | Raises: Area (+1 target per 2 Raises), Duration (+1 Round per Raise), Range (+10' per Raise)

The positive counterpoint to Haze of Battle. Invokes a positive and honorable ferocity by using Fire to call on the favor of the Sun. Target gains +1k1 to all Agility-based Skill Rolls and +1k0 to all damage rolls while under direct sunlight. Cannot function underground, at night, or when the Sun is obscured by clouds (though Gift of Amaterasu can circumvent this). Ignores minor obstructions such as foliage or shoji screens.

**Whispering Flames**

- Fire 3 (Illusion) | Range: 50' | Area: 10' radius | Duration: 3 Rounds | Raises: Range (+10' per Raise), Area (+5' radius per 2 Raises), Duration (+1 Round per Raise)

Fire kami gather and dance in a pleasing, entrancing visual pattern with soothing rhythmic light. All persons within the area who gaze upon them are considered Dazed for the duration. Targets may attempt a Willpower roll (TN = caster's Fire ×10) during the Reactions Stage of each Round to shake off the effect. Less potent against gaijin and nonhumans (GM may grant bonus to resist). Animals, bestial creatures, and mindless undead are completely immune.

**MASTERY LEVEL 4:**

**Blessing of the Sun**

- Fire 4 | Range: Touch | Area: One willing target person | Duration: 3 Rounds | Raises: Range (10' for 2 Raises), Duration (+1 Round per Raise)

Infuses the target with aggressive Fire kami. The target instantly recovers from Dazed. For the duration, Fire-related rolls (Agility, Intelligence, and Fire Ring Spell Casting Rolls) ignore the effects of Fatigue and Wound Penalty TNs. Cost: when the spell expires, the target becomes Fatigued and cannot recover from this Fatigue by any means (rest, magic, or Techniques) for 24 hours.

**Death of Flame [CR]**

- Fire 4 | Range: 100' | Area: 1 target | Duration: 5 Rounds | Raises: None

Brings the anger of Fire onto the target, suppressing their elemental Fire. Lowers both Agility and Intelligence Ranks by the caster's Fire Ring (minimum 1). If the caster maintains Concentration for the full duration, the target cannot escape. If not maintaining Concentration, the target may make a Contested Fire Roll each Round (at the start of their Turn, using their original unmodified Fire Ring) to end the spell.

**Defense of the Firestorm [CR]**

- Fire 4 (Defense) | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target armor | Duration: 5 Rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 Round)

A more potent form of Shining Light. Summons an aura of magical flames from the armor, surrounding and protecting the wearer. The flames do not harm the wearer or living things. All wooden weapons (including arrows and many polearms) burn instantly before reaching the target, dealing no damage. The flames impede sight, increasing the wearer's Armor TN by 20.

**Essence of Fire**

- Fire 4 (Wards, Imperial) | Range: 10' | Area: Two target duelists | Duration: One iaijutsu duel | Raises: Special (see below)

Developed by the Asahina to prevent magical tampering with iaijutsu duels. Fire kami race through the chosen targets, ending all ongoing spell effects on them. The kami remain until the end of the duel — any spell targeting either duelist before the duel concludes suffers -3k0 to its Spell Casting Roll. With 4 Raises: a controversial variant also uses Fire kami to suppress the targets' memories of bushi Techniques, making the duel purely a test of raw skill.

**Eyes of the Phoenix**

- Fire 4 (Illusion) | Range: 25' | Area: One target creature | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Range (+5' per Raise), Area (+1 creature per 2 Raises)

Persuades Fire kami to float directly in front of the target's eyes, making it appear their eyes have caught on fire. When the spell is cast, all allies of the target suffer a Fear 3 effect (if vulnerable; undead are immune). For the duration, the target is considered Blinded.

**The Mending Forge [CR]**

- Fire 4 (Craft) | Range: Touch | Area: One target item | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: None

Calls on the creative power of Fire to restore a damaged or destroyed material item, provided all pieces are gathered before the caster. Takes 1 minute of full concentration. On success, the item is restored to a whole and undamaged state. To repair a nemuranai or Fine quality item or better, the Fire kami must be offered an appropriate gift (another item to burn and destroy).

**Symbol of Fire [CR]**

- Fire 4 (Ward) | Range: 100' | Area: 10' radius | Duration: Permanent | Raises: None

Inscribes a ward on a passageway. Those passing through must make a Contested Water vs caster's Fire roll. Those who fail are Dazed, Blinded for 1 Round, and take 3k3 Wounds. Only one Symbol of Fire may exist at a time. Cannot overlap with other element Symbols. Dispelled by another Symbol of Fire casting or by destroying the inscribed surface.

**Wall of Fire [CR]**

- Fire 4 | Range: 100' | Area: 10' tall, 1' wide, 25' long | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: Area (+1 increment: height +10', width +1', or length +25')

Invokes Fire kami to form a wall of flames. Inflicts 6k6 Wounds to anyone who touches it. Can be made shorter or thinner as desired within total specifications. If created where people or creatures are standing, they must roll Reflexes (TN 20) to avoid taking Wounds.

**Ward of Thunder [CR]**

- Fire 4 (Defense, Ward, Thunder) | Range: Touch | Area: 15' radius from the armor | Duration: 1 hour | Raises: None

Inscribes the kanji for thunder on a suit of armor, invoking Osano-Wo’s protection. Those within 15' of the armor are completely protected from non-magical fire and thunder for the duration. Any Fire spell cast by a shugenja other than the caster that targets something within the area has its Spell Casting TN increased by 20.

**MASTERY LEVEL 5:**

**Castle of Fire**

- Fire 5 (Battle, Defense) | Range: Centered on caster | Area: 30' radius circle | Duration: 10 Rounds | Raises: Area (+5' to radius per 2 Raises), Duration (+2 Rounds per Raise)

A more sophisticated version of Wall of Fire, erecting a circular fiery barrier surrounding the caster. 1' thick and 10' high. Anyone standing where it appears must roll Reflexes (TN 20) to leap clear or be burned. Instantly incinerates arrows and other missiles passing through it. Creatures passing through the barrier (or caught by it when it appears) suffer 6k6 damage and are set afire.

**Consumed by Five Fires**

- Fire 5 | Range: 100' | Area: One target creature (and caster) | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+50' per Raise)

One of the most potent and deadly Fire spells, used only in the most desperate need. Invokes powerful Fire kami to instantly kill the target, burning them to ashes. The target is immediately reduced to Dead. However, the caster immediately suffers the same number of Wounds as were inflicted on the target — often lethal. This damage cannot be reduced or mitigated. Cannot target creatures resistant to Fire and cannot kill Fortunes.

**Destructive Wave [CR]**

- Fire 5 | Range: Personal | Area: 25' radius | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Damage (+1k1 per 2 Raises)

Summons a great mass of Fire kami that roll outward from the caster in a wave of searing flames. Every target within the area of effect, friendly or hostile, takes 7k7 Wounds. The caster alone does not take damage.

**The Dragon's Talon (Kuro's Fire)**

- Fire 5 (Battle) | Range: 100' | Area: Up to 10 target creatures | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Range (+25' per Raise), Area (+2 targets per Raise)

Calls on the Dragon of Fire to strike down large numbers of lesser foes. Bolts of fire resembling snarling dragon heads streak from the caster's hands. Each target suffers 8k6 damage. The Fire kami will only attack targets they consider weak and unworthy — in game terms, can only target foes of Insight Rank 1 or 2, or comparable nonhumans (GM's judgment).

**Everburning Rage [CR]**

- Fire 5 | Range: 25' | Area: 1 target | Duration: 1 Round | Raises: Duration (+1 Round), Targets (+1)

Infuses the target's body with angry Fire spirits, wracking them with searing pain. For the duration, the victim is considered to be at the Down Wound Level and suffers all associated penalties and conditions, but takes no actual Wounds. When the spell expires, the pain instantly ceases and the target may stand as if nothing happened.

**Follow the Flame [CR]**

- Fire 5 | Range: Personal | Area: See below | Duration: 5 Rounds | Raises: Damage (+1k0 per Raise)

Grants exceptional control of nearby Fire kami. On the Round cast and every Round thereafter, designate a line-of-sight target within 300' (as a Simple Action on later Rounds). A stream of fire snakes along the ground at 75' per Round, moving around impassable barriers to reach the target. On arrival: the target bursts into flames, suffering 6k5 Wounds. The subject catches fire, taking half that many Wounds (rounded down) every subsequent Round until doused normally or the spell's duration expires.

**Light of the Sun [CR]**

- Fire 5 (Jade) | Range: 100' | Area: 30' radius | Duration: 10 rounds | Raises: None

Invokes the power of the Sun to punish the unworthy. Everyone in the area takes 2k2 Wounds per Round from intense heat. Human targets take additional 2k1 Wounds for every Honor Rank they are below 4. Additional 2k2 Wounds if they have at least 1 Rank of Shadowlands Taint or are controlled by the Lying Darkness. Human targets with Honor Rank 0 are Blinded for Rounds equal to the caster's Fire Ring.

**Wings of the Phoenix [CR]**

- Fire 5 (Travel) | Range: Personal | Area: Self | Duration: 10 Rounds | Raises: None

Summons Fire kami as giant wings sprouting from the caster's back, granting the power of flight for the duration. Movement: Water ×10' as a Free Action or Water ×20' as a Simple Action. If airborne when the spell expires, the Fire kami will bear the caster safely to earth before departing.

**MASTERY LEVEL 6:**

**Beam of the Inferno [CR]**

- Fire 6 | Range: 200' | Area: 1 target | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: None

Perhaps the deadliest single-target spell available to shugenja. Invokes a tremendous blast of fire against the chosen target: 10k10 Wounds. All Fire kami in the area are agitated by the spell — normal fires within range will burn hotter and more violently for many minutes afterward.

**Curse of the Burning Hand**

- Fire 6 | Range: 10' | Area: One target creature | Duration: Infinite | Raises: Range (+5' per Raise)

Requires 1 full minute to cast. Caster must win a Contested Fire Roll against the target to inflict the Curse. Binds a hostile Fire kami to the target's soul. The target is constantly wreathed in flames that do not harm them, but harm any friends, allies, or innocents who touch them (3k3 damage). Any physical objects touched that are flammable catch alight. The fire recedes if the target tries to use it as a weapon. Duration is indefinite. May potentially be ended by spells that remove magical effects or drive out kami, at the GM's judgment.

**Globe of the Everlasting Sun [CR]**

- Fire 6 (Defense) | Range: 500' | Area: 1 mile | Duration: 1 day | Raises: None

Calls on all Fire kami in the area to remain quiet and calm. Often invoked during major festivals. For the duration: all buildings within the area are immune to magical fire; all Fire spells cast within the area have their Spell Casting Roll TN increased by 15. Normal fires still ignite and burn but do so sluggishly and do not spread easily.

**Rise, Fire**

- Fire 6 | Range: 30' | Area: One summoned spirit | Duration: Concentration | Raises: None

Summons a massive Fire kami in a 10-foot fiery humanoid shape with constantly shifting outlines. Moves up to 10 × Fire Ring per Round. Ignites all easily flammable objects (paper, leaves, grass, silk) within 10' of itself. All Physical Traits equal to caster's Fire Ring. Attacks with Jiujutsu Skill Rank equal to half the caster's Fire Ring; DR equal to the caster's Fire Ring. Targets struck catch on fire, suffering +1k1 damage per Round until extinguished (does not stack). Wounds as a human with Earth equal to caster's Fire Ring but no Wound Penalties. Invulnerable. Dispelled at zero Wounds.

**The Elements' Fury**

- Fire 6 | Range: Self | Area: Caster | Duration: Instantaneous | Raises: Special (+1 additional spell per 3 Raises)

A supremely advanced form of Hurried Steps, credited to Naka Kaeteru, the first Grand Master of the Elements. The caster's mind and body are infused with energetic Fire kami. May immediately cast a number of spells of Mastery Level 4 or less equal to their Fire Ring, each as a Free Action regardless of normal casting time. Spell Casting Rolls must be made and spell slots used as normal; no need to Raise for shorter casting time.

**The Soul's Blade [CR]**

- Fire 6 (Craft) | Range: Touch | Area: 1 target weapon | Duration: 5 rounds | Raises: Duration (+1 Round per 3 Raises)

Imbues incredibly powerful Fire kami into a weapon, granting it the full fury of a raging firestorm. The weapon overcomes Invulnerability. Every target hit with it is automatically Stunned.
