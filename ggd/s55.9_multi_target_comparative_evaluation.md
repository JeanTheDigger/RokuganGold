## 55.9 Multi-Target Comparative Evaluation

**STATUS:** LOCKED

Solves: “Family Daimyo oversees 5 provinces. Which one needs attention most?”

### 55.9.1 Province Triage Function

Runs as the FIRST step in any multi-province decomposition. Scores each province:

Active crisis: +100. Active insurgency: +80. Stability ≤25 (Broken): +60. ≤50 (Volatile): +30. ≤75 (Restless): +10. Garrison deficit: +20. Information stale (3+ seasons): +25.

**Key insight:** Stale information scores HIGHER because uncertainty is itself a risk. This drives the NPC to refresh neglected provinces before assuming they’re fine.

### 55.9.2 How Triage Feeds Decomposition

Worst province’s status determines the NeedType: stale data → INVESTIGATE_THREAT. Active crisis → DEFEND_PROVINCE. Low stability, no crisis → PATROL_PROVINCE. All stable → REST.

### 55.9.3 Afternoon AP Pattern

Morning AP addresses the top-priority province. Afternoon AP cycles to the next unaddressed province. Over multiple days, the lord systematically works through their territory in order of urgency.
