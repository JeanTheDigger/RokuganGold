## 57.2 Alignment Score Compression Principle --- LOCKED

AFFECTS: All social/political NeedTypes in Annex A (objective_alignment.json).

**Problem: **Alignment scores range 0--100 while personality lean ranges +/-25. With original score spreads (e.g., CHARM 90, DELIVER_GIFT 85, LISTEN_REFLECT 85, NEGOTIATE 70), personality lean cannot differentiate behavior within the top cluster. Every virtue produces the same winning action for the same NeedType.

**Solution: **Compress the top cluster of alignment scores for social/political NeedTypes to a 10-point band (e.g., 65--75). Maintain a large gap down to intelligence/preparation actions (35--40) and to DO_NOTHING (0). Personality lean (+/-25), competence (+/-20), and disposition (+/-25) can now tip the balance within the compressed cluster while unable to promote irrelevant actions.

**Which NeedTypes to compress: **Social: RAISE_DISPOSITION, MOVE_TOPIC_POSITION, TRIGGER_COMMITMENT, DAMAGE_RELATIONSHIP, SEEK_PEACE, REQUEST_AID, RESTORE_HONOR, IDENTIFY_CONTACT, SEEK_GLORY. Keep sharp winners for operational NeedTypes: LEVY_TROOPS, DEPLOY_ARMY, TRAVEL_TO, SEND_LETTER, REST, ADJUST_TAX, TRAIN_TROOPS, MAINTAIN_FORTIFICATION, DEPLOY_SCOUTS, ORDER_SHADOWLANDS_SORTIE, MANAGE_TAINT.

**Example --- RAISE_DISPOSITION compressed: **CHARM 75, DELIVER_GIFT 75, LISTEN_REFLECT 75, IMPRESS 70, PERFORM_FOR 70, NEGOTIATE 70, PERSUADE 70, OFFER_FAVOR 65, READ_CHARACTER 35, PROBE 30, WRITE_LETTER 25, DO_NOTHING 0. Result: Jin NPC leads with DELIVER_GIFT (75+15=90). Rei NPC gravitates toward PERFORM_FOR (70+15=85) or LISTEN_REFLECT (75+10=85). Kyoryoku NPC selects IMPRESS (70+0=70, highest after CHARM at 75-15=60). Each virtue produces distinct behavior.

**Tuning pass required: **The specific compressed values for each social/political NeedType in Annex A are pending a dedicated tuning session. The principle and the RAISE_DISPOSITION example are locked. The nine NeedTypes transcribed from gap sections (Section 57.7) retain their original uncompressed scores and should be compressed during the tuning pass where applicable.
