## 27.2 The Crane Clan

Creed: 'Beauty and precision, form and function in all things.' Founded by Lady Doji, the Emperor Hantei's sister. All four Crane families can trace their lineage directly to Lady Doji. The Crane are the Empire's taste-makers, arbiters of art and fashion, political operators of the highest order. For most of the Empire's history they have held the Emperor's favor through the tradition of the Emperor marrying a Crane woman. Their political power, economic resources, and mastery of court make them formidable despite a relatively modest military.

- Clan identity: Sophisticated, graceful, politically dominant. Masters of court manipulation. The Doji face of the clan is charming and cultured. The Daidoji shadow is quietly ruthless when needed.

- Key historical events: The First Yasuki War; Doji Hoturi and the Clan Wars; the Crane Civil War caused by the Lying Darkness; the Four Winds era; the Second and Third Yasuki Wars; the Daidoji Harriers scandal; the War of Silk and Steel with Dragon.

- Inter-clan relations: Traditional enemies of the Lion (centuries of border wars, Kakita-Matsu rivalry). Chronic conflict with Crab over Yasuki. Complex relationship with Scorpion — the Doji and Bayushi are rivals but also frequent political partners. Generally good relations with Phoenix.

**The Asahina Family:**

Motto: 'Knowledge must be earned, not simply learned.' Committed pacifists and ascetics. Wholly uninterested in politics or wealth. Produce extraordinary magical items (tsangusuri fetishes) highly sought throughout the Empire. Will not kill — even in defense of the clan they use magic to reduce harm to their own people rather than harm the enemy.

**The Daidoji Family:**

Motto: 'The spear waits not for its master, but rushes forth to guard the way.' The warrior family of the Crane — though they prefer not to be called that. Silent, steely protectors. Map territory obsessively for military intelligence. Willing to sacrifice their own honor for the clan's benefit. Maintain 'swamp dwellers' — merchants who operate just outside the law. Notable for the Daidoji Harriers scandal (covert operatives using poison and gaijin pepper) which ended by Champion's order.

**The Doji Family:**

Motto: 'Be more concerned with good actions than great ones.' The elegant face of the Crane. Arbiters of art, fashion, and Imperial politics. Created most of the Empire's political and cultural institutions. Almost always hold the position of Imperial Advisor. Their philosophy: life is too short for the shoddy or make-do — every moment should be lived fully and beautifully.

**The Kakita Family:**

Motto: 'For the coward there is no life. For the hero there is no death.' Masters of both the sword and the arts. Created the dueling form of iaijutsu. The Kakita Dueling Academy and the Kakita Artisan Academy are the Empire's unquestioned standard of excellence — no artisan from any other clan is considered great until a Kakita Master praises their work. Devoted to perfection to a near-religious degree.
