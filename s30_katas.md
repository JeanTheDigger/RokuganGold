# 30. Katas

Kata are fighting postures and movements evolved over centuries and reinforced through constant repetition. They differentiate bushi characters without the significance of Techniques but with more martial specificity than Skills or Advantages. A bushi may purchase a Kata if their School is listed and their relevant Ring equals or exceeds the Mastery Level. Cost equals the Mastery Level in Experience Points. Executing a Kata is a Simple Action; the benefit lasts until the effect expires or the character drops it (Free Action). Only one Kata may be active at a time.

**AIR KATA:**

**Striking as Air**

- Ring/Mastery: Air 3 | Schools: Any

- Effect: When in Defense Stance, Armor TN is increased by your Air Ring.

**Breath of Wind Style**

- Ring/Mastery: Air 3 | Schools: Kakita Bushi, Bayushi Bushi

- Effect: Initiative Score increases by 2 during the Reactions Stage of each Combat Round. Stacks, but disappears immediately if the kata ends.

**Dance of the Winds**

- Ring/Mastery: Air 3 | Schools: Daidoji Bushi, Shiba Bushi

- Effect: When wielding a polearm or spear, Initiative Score is increased by 3.

**Strength of the Mantis**

- Ring/Mastery: Air 3 | Schools: Any Mantis Bushi

- Effect: The attack penalty for ranged attacks fired against opponents currently in melee range is reduced by 3.

**Strength of the Crane**

- Ring/Mastery: Air 3 | Schools: Any Crane Bushi

- Effect: When fighting with a sword or spear, add Honor Rank −3 (minimum 1) to Armor TN.

**Iron Forest Style**

- Ring/Mastery: Air 4 | Schools: Daidoji Iron Warrior, Heichi Bushi, Shiba Bushi

- Effect: While using a spear or polearm, use Air Ring instead of Agility for attack rolls.

**Veiled Menace Style**

- Ring/Mastery: Air 4 | Schools: Bayushi Bushi, Hiruma Bushi, Tsuruchi Archer, Yoritomo Bushi

- Effect: Once per Turn, add Stealth Skill Rank to Armor TN against a single attack.

**North Wind Style**

- Ring/Mastery: Air 4 | Schools: Any bushi School

- Effect: Add Air Ring to the total of any attack roll made while attempting the Increased Damage Maneuver.

**South Wind Style**

- Ring/Mastery: Air 4 | Schools: Any bushi School

- Effect: Add Air Ring to the total of any attack roll made while attempting the Called Shot or Knockdown Maneuvers.

**Hidden Blade Style**

- Ring/Mastery: Air 4 | Schools: Bayushi Bushi, Yoritomo Bushi

- Effect: Disarm attacks deal normal damage rather than 2k1. Damage on these attacks may not be increased via Raises (including from the Feint Maneuver).

**EARTH KATA:**

**Striking as Earth**

- Ring/Mastery: Earth 3 | Schools: Any

- Effect: When in Full Defense Stance, gain Reduction equal to Earth Ring. Cumulative with other Reduction.

**The Power of the Mountain**

- Ring/Mastery: Earth 3 | Schools: Hida Bushi, Hiruma Bushi, Matsu Berserker, Ichiro Bushi

- Effect: Reduce Armor TN by up to Earth Ring; all damage rolls while active have their totals increased by the same amount.

**The Strength of the Mountain**

- Ring/Mastery: Earth 3 | Schools: Hida Bushi, Hiruma Scout, Shiba Bushi, Daidoji Iron Warrior

- Effect: Reduce Initiative Score by up to Earth Ring (minimum 0). While active, Armor TN is increased by the same amount.

**Strike as the Avalanche**

- Ring/Mastery: Earth 3 | Schools: Hida Bushi, Hiruma Bushi, Ichiro Bushi, Moto Bushi, Moto Vindicator

- Effect: When using the Heavy Weapons Skill, Strength is considered one Rank higher for determining damage.

**Strength of the Spider**

- Ring/Mastery: Earth 3 | Schools: Any Spider Bushi

- Effect: Once per Round, if you successfully cause at least 15 Wounds to your opponent, they suffer −3 to the total of all rolls on their next Turn.

**Strength of the Crab**

- Ring/Mastery: Earth 3 | Schools: Any Crab Bushi

- Effect: While wearing Armor and in Attack Stance, armor provides an additional +2 Reduction.

**Iron in the Mountains Style**

- Ring/Mastery: Earth 3 | Schools: Daidoji Iron Warrior, Hida Bushi

- Effect: Use Earth Ring in place of Air Ring for the Defense Stance.

**Indomitable Warrior Style**

- Ring/Mastery: Earth 4 | Schools: Daigotsu Bushi, Hida Bushi, Ichiro Bushi, Moto Bushi

- Effect: Reduce TN penalties from Wound Ranks by your Earth Ring.

**Lee of the Stone**

- Ring/Mastery: Earth 4 | Schools: Hida Bushi, Hida Pragmatist, Shiba Bushi, Daidoji Iron Warrior

- Effect: While in Defense or Full Defense Stance, Armor TN is increased by an additional amount equal to Earth Ring.

**Weathered and Unbroken**

- Ring/Mastery: Earth 5 | Schools: Hida Bushi, Hiruma Bushi, Hiruma Scout, Ichiro Bushi

- Effect: Water Ring is considered two Ranks lower (minimum 1) for movement purposes. All Heavy Weapons attack rolls gain one Free Raise usable only for the Knockdown Maneuver.

**FIRE KATA:**

**Striking as Fire**

- Ring/Mastery: Fire 3 | Schools: Any

- Effect: When in Full Attack Stance, add Fire Ring to the total of one attack roll per Round.

**Strength of the Scorpion**

- Ring/Mastery: Fire 3 | Schools: Any Scorpion Bushi

- Effect: Once per Turn, after successfully making the Feint Maneuver, damage total is increased by +3 Wounds.

**Strength of the Dragon**

- Ring/Mastery: Fire 3 | Schools: Any Dragon Bushi

- Effect: While wielding a katana in the main hand and wakizashi in the off hand, gain +3 to Armor TN.

**Reckless Abandon Style**

- Ring/Mastery: Fire 4 | Schools: Daigotsu Bushi, Matsu Berserker, Usagi Bushi

- Effect: Add Fire Ring to Armor TN while in Full Attack Stance.

**Disappearing World Style**

- Ring/Mastery: Fire 4 | Schools: Akodo Bushi, Kakita Bushi

- Effect: Choose one opponent. Once per Turn against that opponent, use Agility Trait instead of Strength Trait for damage rolls.

**Spinning Blades Style**

- Ring/Mastery: Fire 5 | Schools: Mirumoto Bushi, Yoritomo Bushi

- Effect: Once per Turn while wielding a weapon in each hand, make the Extra Attack Maneuver for 3 Raises instead of 5. The second attack comes from the off-hand weapon at normal weapon damage. Damage on either attack may not be increased via Raises.

**WATER KATA:**

**Striking as Water**

- Ring/Mastery: Water 4 | Schools: Any

- Effect: When in Attack Stance, move 5 additional feet as a Free Action.

**Strength of the Lion**

- Ring/Mastery: Water 3 | Schools: Any Lion Bushi

- Effect: Once per Round during the Reactions Stage, add +3 to the Initiative Score of one ally in a skirmish. Effect stacks but disappears immediately if the kata ends.

**Son of Storms**

- Ring/Mastery: Water 3 | Schools: Akodo Bushi, Shosuro Infiltrator, Yoritomo Bushi

- Effect: When attacking with a Small melee weapon, any Reduction an opponent possesses is decreased by 1.

**Strength of the Unicorn**

- Ring/Mastery: Water 3 | Schools: Any Unicorn Bushi

- Effect: While mounted, your steed gains +3 to Armor TN and an additional 3 Reduction.

**Waves upon the Breakers**

- Ring/Mastery: Water 3 | Schools: Akodo Bushi, Kakita Bushi, Shinjo Bushi

- Effect: While wielding a weapon in which you have at least three Skill Ranks, damage is increased by +1k0.

**Leaves in the Stream**

- Ring/Mastery: Water 3 | Schools: Bayushi Bushi, Hiruma Bushi, Mirumoto Bushi, Shiba Bushi

- Effect: Reduce Armor TN by up to 5 × Water Ring (minimum 5). In return, maximum movement distance for the Round is increased by the same amount.

**Power of the Tsunami**

- Ring/Mastery: Water 4 | Schools: Daigotsu Bushi, Hida Bushi, Moto Bushi

- Effect: Once per Round when attacking, ignore Reduction equal to Water Ring.

**Strength in Arms Style**

- Ring/Mastery: Water 4 | Schools: Hida Bushi, Ichiro Bushi, Moto Bushi

- Effect: Once per Turn while wielding a Heavy Weapon, use Strength Trait instead of Agility for an attack roll.

**Art of Ninjutsu**

- Ring/Mastery: Water 5 | Schools: Daigotsu Bushi, Bayushi Bushi, Daidoji Scout, Shosuro Actor, Shosuro Infiltrator, Goju Ninja

- Effect: Once per Round, calculate the distance of a Move Action as if Water Ring equals Stealth Skill Rank. Does not change the maximum distance moveable in a Round.

**VOID KATA ****&**** MULTI-RING KATA:**

**Striking as Void**

- Ring/Mastery: Void 3 | Schools: Any

- Effect: When in Center Stance, Armor TN is increased by Void Ring.

**Balance the Elements Style**

- Ring/Mastery: Void 3 | Schools: Mirumoto Bushi, Shiba Bushi

- Effect: Use Void Ring in place of Reflexes for Initiative Rolls.

**Strength of Purity Style**

- Ring/Mastery: Void 3 | Schools: Akodo Bushi, Kakita Bushi, Matsu Berserker, Utaku Battle Maiden

- Effect: Once per Turn when rolling damage, roll Honor Rank instead of normal Strength + DR of the weapon. Keep dice equal to the DR of the weapon as usual.

**Strength of the Phoenix**

- Ring/Mastery: Void 3 | Schools: Any Phoenix Bushi

- Effect: Once per Turn when declaring a Guard Action, increase the Armor TN of the person being guarded by an additional +3.

**The Empire Rests on its Edge**

- Ring/Mastery: Air 3 or Fire 3 (Mirumoto and Kakita Bushi reduce Ring requirement by 1) | Schools: Any | Special: Katana or daisho only

- Effect: Upon learning this Kata, choose one non-combat High Skill (costs +2 XP per Rank increase in that Skill). While this Kata is active, gain a bonus to Kenjutsu or Iaijutsu Skill Rolls equal to your Rank in that chosen Skill.

**The World Is Empty**

- Ring/Mastery: Air 4 or Fire 4 (Mirumoto and Kakita Bushi reduce Ring requirement by 1) | Schools: Any | Special: Katana or daisho only

- Effect: Upon activation, gain +Xk0 to Kenjutsu or Iaijutsu attack rolls where X equals current Void Points. Lasts Rounds equal to Void Points at activation, ending during the Reactions Stage of the last Round. When it ends, lose 1 Void Point.

**Victory of the River**

- Ring/Mastery: Air 5 or Fire 5 (Mirumoto and Kakita Bushi reduce Ring requirement by 1) | Schools: Any | Special: Katana or daisho only

- Effect: When you successfully strike an opponent, their Armor TN is reduced by 10 against your subsequent attacks for three Rounds. Your own Armor TN is also reduced by 10 while this benefit is active. Only one opponent may be affected at a time; effects are not cumulative, though subsequent strikes reset the three-Round limit.

**Standing on the Heavens**

- Ring/Mastery: Air 6 or Fire 6 (Mirumoto and Kakita Bushi reduce Ring requirement by 1) | Schools: Any | Special: Katana or daisho only

- Effect: Once per Round, when struck by a successful attack, spend a Void Point as a Free Action to force the opponent to immediately reroll the attack.
