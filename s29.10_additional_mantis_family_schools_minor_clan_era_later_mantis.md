## 29.10 Additional Mantis Family Schools (Minor Clan Era — Later Mantis Clan)

Note: The schools below belong to families that are independent Minor Clans in the game’s default era. In later eras (post-Yoritomo’s Alliance), these families join the Mantis Great Clan. All schools are presented here for completeness. GMs should determine which schools are available based on the era of play.

**BASIC SCHOOLS:**

**Kitsune Shugenja [CR] — Fox Clan / later Mantis Clan:**

The Fox Clan dwells beside one of the largest forests in Rokugan, a fact that has shaped every aspect of their lessons. Each Kitsune shugenja gains a profound love for the spirits and tranquility of the deep forest through countless hours of meditation within its borders. Their technique is versatile and deeply connected to wilderness and animals — few from other schools can duplicate the Fox’s abilities.

- Benefit: +1 Stamina

- Skills: Calligraphy, Defense, Hunting, Medicine (Herbalism), Meditation, Spellcraft, any one High or Bugei Skill

- Honor: 4.5 | Outfit: Robes, Wakizashi, Knife, Scroll Satchel, Traveling Pack, 3 koku

- Affinity/Deficiency: Earth / Air | Starting Spells: Sense, Commune, Summon, 3 Earth, 2 Water, 1 Fire

- Technique — Essence of Chikushudo: May use the Sense, Commune, and Summon basic spells to affect animal spirits of Chikushudo as well as kami (Summon produces a single animal spirit rather than elemental material). Free Raise on any non-damaging spell cast on an animal.

**Moshi Shugenja [CR] — Centipede Clan / later Mantis Clan:**

Developed through unwavering devotion to the Sun Goddess. Since joining the Mantis, their focus has shifted from pure theology to practical weather control. More numerous than the Yoritomo shugenja bloodline, they are frequently stationed aboard Yoritomo vessels to placate the seas during mercantile voyages. Their ability to gain an additional Affinity during the day without a corresponding Deficiency gives them versatility rivaling the Isawa.

- Benefit: +1 Awareness

- Skills: Calligraphy (Cipher), Divination, Lore: Theology, Meditation, Spellcraft, any two High or Bugei Skills

- Honor: 4.5 | Outfit: Robes, Wakizashi, Knife, Scroll Satchel, Traveling Pack, 10 koku

- Affinity/Deficiency: Air / Earth | Starting Spells: Sense, Commune, Summon, 3 Air, 3 Fire

- Technique — Favor of the Sun: During the day, gain an additional Affinity for Fire spells (lost at night). Free Raise on any spell with the Thunder keyword.

**Tsuruchi Archer [Bushi] [CR] — Wasp Clan / later Mantis Clan:**

The greatest archers in the Empire — unquestioned even before joining the Mantis. The Tsuruchi have virtually set aside the blade, believing in the purity of the bow. The arrow knows the way and should be fired without hesitation or aiming. While unquestionably the finest ranged combatants in all of Rokugan, a Tsuruchi pressed into melee is badly outclassed by virtually any other bushi school.

- Benefit: +1 Reflexes

- Skills: Athletics, Defense, Hunting, Investigation, Kyujutsu (Yumi) 2, any one Bugei or High Skill

- Honor: 3.5 | Outfit: Ashigaru or Light Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Bow and 20 Arrows, Wakizashi, Traveling Pack, 10 koku

- Rank 1 — Always Be Ready: +1k0 to all attack rolls using a bow. +3 to Initiative Score.

- Rank 2 — The Arrow Knows the Way: One Free Raise usable only for the Called Shot Maneuver. +2k0 to all damage rolls while using a bow.

- Rank 3 — The Wasp’s Sting: Attack as a Simple Action when using a bow.

- Rank 4 — Flight of No-Mind: Once per skirmish, spend a Void Point as a Complex Action to shoot an arrow that ignores all armor effects and penalties from Wound Ranks or visibility. May make Raises but not the Extra Attack Maneuver. Cannot target beyond normal range.

- Rank 5 — Tsuruchi’s Eye: Make a ranged attack as a Complex Action to gain +4k1 to both attack and damage rolls. Cannot be used in conjunction with Flight of No-Mind.

**Tsuruchi Bounty Hunter [Bushi] — Wasp Clan / later Mantis Clan:**

The Wasp Clan was authorized by the Emerald Champion to hunt fugitives for a fee — for many years their primary source of income. The contract still exists; the family can impart authority roughly equivalent to an Emerald Magistrate for the apprehension of known criminals, but is extremely cautious about abuse. Bounties are generally koku equal to the target’s Insight Rank, doubled for violent criminals or shugenja.

- Benefit: +1 Agility

- Skills: Athletics, Hunting (Tracking), Intimidation, Investigation, Kenjutsu, Kyujutsu, Lore: Underworld

- Honor: 4.5 | Outfit: Ashigaru or Light Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, Bow or Knife, Traveling Pack, 10 koku

- Rank 1 — A Hunter’s Sense: +1k1 to Intimidation against lower-caste individuals you believe can help find your prey; +1k0 but applying to all Social Skill Rolls against samurai. When hunting a target, roll additional unkept dice equal to School Rank on any Hunting or Investigation Skill Rolls to track the quarry.

- Rank 2 — No Prey Escapes: Spend a Void Point to automatically succeed at a Lore: Underworld roll to identify criminals in an area who can be pressed for information. Honor loss for associating with such individuals applies as normal.

- Rank 3 — Justice of the Wasp: Against an opponent declared guilty by Imperial or higher Mantis authority, the Disarm or Knockdown Maneuvers cost one Raise less than normal. Successfully executing either Maneuver automatically Dazes the opponent.

- Rank 4 — Twin Sting Strike: Make ranged attacks as a Simple Action with a yumi. Against any target decreed guilty by Imperial or higher Mantis authority, also make melee attacks as a Simple Action with a katana or knife.

- Rank 5 — Eyes of the Wasp: When identifying a samurai with a Lore: Heraldry roll, also learn their Honor Rank and all Social and Mental Disadvantages. Any opponent targetable by your Rank 3 Technique is now automatically Dazed when you successfully hit them, regardless of whether a Disarm or Knockdown Maneuver was used.

**ALTERNATE PATHS:**

**Acolyte of Thunder [Shugenja]:**

The guidance of the Oracle of Thunder allows the shugenja of the Mantis Clan to discover new ways to call on the power of Thunder.

- Replaces: Yoritomo Shugenja 4. Requires: Fire 3, Void 4.

- Technique — Thunder’s Call: Cast Fury of Osano-Wo as a Simple Action by sacrificing a spell slot of any Element. Raises for damage on that spell award +1k1 per Raise instead of +1k0.

**Children of Chikushodo [Shugenja] — Fox/Kitsune:**

Certain Kitsune individuals can slip free of the normal confines of Earth magic and accomplish amazing feats through their pacts with the animal spirits of Chikushudo.

- Replaces: Kitsune Shugenja 3. Requires: Hunting 3, Lore: Spirit Realms (Chikushudo) 2.

- Technique — Born of the Earth: While sitting on bare earth, sacrifice two Earth spell slots and make a Meditation roll (TN 15). After ten minutes of meditation, fall into a trance and an animal erupts from the ground before your body. Your mind inhabits and controls this animal until you choose to end it, your real body is disturbed or harmed, or the animal form is killed. The animal form crumbles to dirt when the effect ends. Use the animal’s physical/mental Traits if they are higher than yours, otherwise use your own. Gain all creature abilities but cannot speak or cast spells while in animal form.

**The College of Clarity [Courtier] — Moshi/Centipede:**

A philosophical movement of the Moshi family focused on allowing ascendant Fire-aligned chi to grant a pure view of the world. Some duelists have traveled great distances to avail themselves of its teachings once they discovered its application to iaijutsu.

- Replaces: Any School 2. Requires: Fire 3, Meditation 2.

- Technique — The Clarity of Fire: Meditate for at least half an hour and roll Meditation/Fire (TN 15). On success, gain the Clear Thinker and Precise Memory Advantages for hours equal to your Fire Ring. If you already have either Advantage, their bonuses increase by +1k0. Special: May instead gain +1k0 to one Iaijutsu (Focus)/Void Roll made during the same period.

**Kitsune Artisan — Fox/Kitsune:**

A unique naturalist art tradition that in its purest form is performed without damaging its materials — living sculptures from tree trunks, art from boulders left in place. An instant sensation in many courts; the displays found in Broken Wave City are among the most popular in recent court seasons.

- Replaces: Kitsune Shugenja 2. Requires: Any one Artisan or Craft Skill at 3.

- Technique — The Beauty of the World: When working with natural materials, expend an Earth spell slot to gain +1k0 on any Artisan or Craft Skill Roll. May expend up to School Rank spell slots this way; may be used in conjunction with Void Points.

**Kitsune Ranger [Bushi] — Fox/Kitsune:**

Not all Kitsune can speak to the kami, but those who cannot still possess the same deep link with the wilderness as their shugenja cousins. Among the finest woodsmen in the Empire.

- Replaces: Any Mantis Bushi 2. Requires: Hunting 3. Special: Prior to joining the Mantis, may be taken as a Rank 2 Path by any bushi-trained Kitsune or as a Rank 2 Ronin Technique for any ronin with a Kitsune Ally Advantage.

- Technique — One With the Wild: Free Raise on all Hunting Skill Rolls and on Stealth Skill Rolls in rural environments. +2k0 on any Contested Roll involving detection (whether avoiding detection or detecting a hidden opponent).

**Moshi Guardian of the Sun [Bushi] — Centipede/Moshi:**

The Moshi family’s own bushi tradition — born of necessity when the family had few warriors. Not as famous as the Shiba or Daidoji yojimbo traditions, but well regarded for dedication and prowess in defense of Moshi holdings and shugenja.

- Replaces: Any Mantis Bushi 1. Benefit: +1 Stamina | Skills: Athletics, Defense, Jiujutsu, Kenjutsu, Lore: Theology, Spears, any 1 High or Bugei Skill | Honor: 6.5 | Outfit: Ashigaru or Light Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, Bow or Knife, Traveling Pack, 5 koku. Special: Prior to the Moshi joining the Mantis, may also replace Insight Rank 1 for a Moshi bushi without a school.

- Technique — Defended as the Sun: When defending a Moshi personage or holding, choose at the start of each Turn: gain +1k0 to your attack roll, or add +1k1 to the Armor TN of a person or object you are defending with the Guard Action.

**Sisters of the Sacred Light [Shugenja] — Centipede/Moshi:**

See also Section 29.2 (Crane) and 29.5 (Phoenix) — this organization recruits women from several clans. Founded by the Centipede Clan to deal with infestations of mischievous spirits. Members forsake their former clan loyalties and dress in red hakama and white gi with only a single clan mon.

- Replaces: Asahina Shugenja 2, Isawa Shugenja (Air) 2, Moshi Shugenja

- Technique — Light Banishes Lies: When attempting to Banish the kami of Air, require only 2 Raises on the Sense spell (instead of 3) and only 3 Raises on the Commune spell (instead of 5).

**Tsuruchi Master Bowman [Bushi] — Wasp/Tsuruchi:**

Perfected by Tsuruchi himself shortly before he was critically wounded at the Battle of Oblivion’s Gate. Taught to only a bare handful of his most trusted followers. Only the most worthy and elite of the Tsuruchi family have been trusted with its secrets since.

- Replaces: N/A (Rank 6). Requires: Kyujutsu 7. Must have School Rank 5 in either Tsuruchi Archer or Tsuruchi Bounty Hunter, and be selected by Tsuruchi sensei.

- Technique — The Way of the Archer: When using a bow, range is increased by 150’ (applied before the 50% bonus from Kyujutsu Rank 5). Raises with a bow are no longer limited by Void Ring (or Skill Rank if you have Great Potential). Called Shots cost half the normal number of Raises (rounded up).

**Tsuruchi Swordsman [Bushi] — Wasp/Tsuruchi:**

The founder of the Wasp Clan despised the blade and his followers did not even carry katana. Generations of war and Tsuruchi’s retirement to the Brotherhood eventually gave rise to a new generation who saw no reason to reject it. The Tsuruchi swordsmen are rapidly developing a reputation as the finest in the entire Mantis Clan.

- Replaces: Tsuruchi Archer 4, Tsuruchi Bounty Hunter 4. Requires: Kenjutsu 3.

- Technique — Ascendancy of Steel: Make a melee attack as a Simple Action rather than a Complex Action when wielding a sword.

**Rajya ke Varisa [Bushi] (cross-clan):**

See also Section 29.7 (Unicorn). A cross-clan group of Crab, Unicorn, and Mantis samurai who study the fighting techniques of the Ivory Kingdoms kshatriya.

- Replaces: Hida Bushi 4, Shinjo Bushi 4, Moto Bushi 4, Yoritomo Bushi

- Technique — Legacy of the Kshatriya: Twice per skirmish, take a Free Action to spend a Void Point before an attack. If the attack succeeds, add unkept dice to the damage roll equal to your Lore: Theology Skill Rank.
