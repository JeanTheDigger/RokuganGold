## 29.2 Crane Clan Schools

**BASIC SCHOOLS:**

**Asahina Shugenja [CR]:**

As much monks as priests. Scholarly, meditative, pacifistic. Focus on defense and protection above all else. Regard everything as a teaching opportunity. Emphasize creation of artwork as essential to improving the world.

- Benefit: +1 Awareness

- Skills: Any one Artisan Skill, Calligraphy (Cipher), Etiquette, Lore: Theology, Meditation, Spellcraft, any one High Skill

- Honor: 6.5 | Outfit: Robes, Bo, Wakizashi, Scroll Satchel, Traveling Pack, 10 koku

- Affinity/Deficiency: Air / Fire

- Starting Spells: Sense, Commune, Summon, 3 Air, 2 Water, 1 Earth

- Technique — The Soul's Grace: Spend Void Point to reduce all opponents' damage rolls within 20' by 0k1 for School Rank Rounds. Free Raise on spells with the Defense keyword.

**Daidoji Iron Warrior [Bushi] [CR]:**

The silent steel of the Crane. Rank and file and heavy infantry. Also serve as yojimbo. Versatile — techniques emphasize enduring damage, protecting charges, and fighting defensively to great effect.

- Benefit: +1 Agility

- Skills: Battle, Defense 2, Iaijutsu, Kenjutsu (Katana), Kyujutsu, any one Skill

- Honor: 6.5 | Outfit: Light or Heavy Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, Heavy Weapon or Polearm, Traveling Pack, 10 koku

- Rank 1 — The Force of Honor: Bonus Wounds per Wound Rank equal to (Honor Rank rounded down − 4, minimum 1). +1k0 to all attack rolls in Attack Stance.

- Rank 2 — The Shield of Faith: Guard Maneuver lasts one additional Round. Armor TN bonus of Guard increased by 5 (both you and your charge gain +15 instead of +10, and you suffer no TN penalty).

- Rank 3 — Strike Beneath the Veil: Make melee attacks as Simple Action in Attack Stance.

- Rank 4 — Vigilance of Mind: Spend Void Point during Reactions to target an opponent who attacked you or your charge — +2k1 attack and damage against them next Round.

- Rank 5 — To Tread on the Sword: Spend two Void Points as Free Action when someone declares action against your Guard target — you become the target instead. Also gain one Free Action to move toward your charge.

**Daidoji Scout [Bushi]:**

Scouting, ambush, and precision strikes against commanders and supply caravans. Readily employ traps and surprise attacks — sacrifice personal honor for the clan's greater good. Rely on Doji courtiers for political cover.

- Benefit: +1 Reflexes

- Skills: Athletics, Hunting (Traps), Kenjutsu, Knives, Kyujutsu (Yumi), Stealth (Sneaking), any 1 Bugei or Low Skill

- Honor: 3.5 | Outfit: Light or Ashigaru Armor, Rugged Clothing, Daisho, Bow, Knife, any 1 weapon, Trap-making Gear, Camouflage Face-paint, Traveling Pack, 5 koku

- Rank 1 — Surveying the Land: +1k0 to Stealth and Hunting rolls. Rural/improvised traps deal +1k1 extra damage.

- Rank 2 — Scouring the Shadows: +2k0 to attack rolls against unaware opponents.

- Rank 3 — Weaken the Resistance: Ignore Reduction from armor or school techniques. If opponent has no such Reduction, +1k0 damage.

- Rank 4 — Strike and Move: Attack as Simple Action with katana, wakizashi, knife, or bow. Traps now deal +2k1 extra damage.

- Rank 5 — Cunning of Daidoji: Spend Void Point — next attack gets all Maneuvers cost 1 less Raise, +1k1 damage on hit. Against unaware opponents, Raises not limited by Void.

**Doji Courtier [CR]:**

The source of the Crane's unquestioned mastery of court. Built on trading favors and vast networks of allies. Perceive what individuals need and offer it in a manner that makes refusal nearly impossible.

- Benefit: +1 Awareness

- Skills: Calligraphy, Courtier (Manipulation), Etiquette (Courtesy), Perform: Storytelling, Sincerity, Tea Ceremony, any one Artisan or Perform Skill

- Honor: 6.5 | Outfit: Extravagant Clothing, Wakizashi, any 1 weapon, Calligraphy Set, Traveling Pack, 10 koku

- Rank 1 — The Soul of Honor: While Honor 6.0+, Free Raise on all Courtier, Sincerity, and Etiquette rolls. Contested Courtier/Awareness vs Etiquette/Awareness to discern what someone needs.

- Rank 2 — Speaking in Silence: Learn 'Cadence' — communicate simple instructions through body language with other trained Crane. TN 15, Raises for complexity.

- Rank 3 — The Perfect Gift: Courtier/Awareness (TN 20) to find suitable gift or political favor. If accepted, gain target as 1-point Devotion Ally at no XP cost.

- Rank 4 — Voice of Honor: Contested Courtier/Awareness vs Etiquette/Awareness — win and opponent must concede their position conflicts with Honor/Bushido or commit a breach of etiquette.

- Rank 5 — The Gift of the Lady: Contested Courtier/Awareness vs Etiquette/Willpower after a few minutes of conversation — success shifts target's emotions favorably. Target goes along with anything not conflicting with basic loyalties.

**Doji Magistrate [Bushi] [EE]:**

The Crane magistrate tradition predates all other magistrate schools in the Empire. Style called Mizu-do (Way of Water) — fight with compassion, avoid injury to self and others. Specialize in disarming and capturing rather than killing.

- Benefit: +1 Reflexes

- Skills: Defense, Horsemanship, Jiujutsu, Kenjutsu, Knives (Jitte), Polearms, any High or Bugei Skill

- Honor: 5.5 | Outfit: Light Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, any 1 weapon or 2 Knives, Traveling Pack, 10 koku

- Rank 1 — Temper Steel With Honor: Add Air Ring to Armor TN (unless helpless/unaware). +1k0 attack with jitte or sasumata.

- Rank 2 — Flowing Like Water: When controlling a Grapple or resolving Disarm, use opponent's Strength Rank in place of your own.

- Rank 3 — Breath of the Law: Successfully Grappling or Disarming an opponent also Dazes them.

- Rank 4 — Flowing Like Air: Attack as Simple Action unarmed or with jitte, sasumata, or Samurai keyword weapons.

- Rank 5 — The Willow in the Storm: Spend Void Point during Reactions — anyone attacking you next Round subtracts their Air Ring from each die.

**Kakita Bushi [CR]:**

The Kakita Dueling Academy — unabashedly focused on iaijutsu above all else. Fastest duelists in the Empire. Battles have been won before they began by slaying an enemy general in a duel. The Center Stance is their signature tool.

- Benefit: +1 Reflexes

- Skills: Etiquette, Iaijutsu (Focus), Kenjutsu, Kyujutsu, Sincerity, Tea Ceremony, any one Bugei or High Skill

- Honor: 6.5 | Outfit: Light Armor, Sturdy Clothing, Daisho, any 1 weapon, Traveling Pack, 10 koku

- Rank 1 — The Way of the Crane: Add twice Iaijutsu Skill to Initiative. +1k1 plus School Rank to attack and Focus rolls in Center Stance (and the following Round).

- Rank 2 — Speed of Lightning: +2k0 to attack rolls against opponents with lower Initiative.

- Rank 3 — First and Last Strike: May Strike first in duel if Iaijutsu/Void roll exceeds opponent's by 3+. Free Raise on Strike per additional margin of 3 (instead of 5).

- Rank 4 — One Strike, Two Cuts: Attack as Simple Action with Samurai keyword weapons.

- Rank 5 — Strike With No Thought: Take one Simple Action per Turn while in Center Stance. Gain Center Stance benefits immediately (not next Turn). May remain in Center Stance as many consecutive Turns as desired.

**Kakita Artisan:**

Masters of all forms of art — performance and creation both. Study art and how to turn it to the service of the Crane Clan. Utterly devoted to one chosen art above all else, while also learning to be a ruthless critic of rivals.

- Benefit: +1 Awareness

- Skills: Courtier, Games: Sadane, Etiquette, Sincerity, any 3 Skills from: Acting, Artisan (Gardening/Ikebana/Origami/Painting/Poetry/Sculpture), Perform (Dance/Oratory/Puppeteer/Song/Storytelling/instrument)

- Honor: 5.5 | Outfit: Court Robes, Beautiful Clothing, Wakizashi, Art supplies, Fan, Steed (Rokugani pony), Traveling Pack, 10 koku

- Rank 1 — Soul of the Artisan: Choose one School Skill as 'chosen art' — +2k0 bonus, Raises not limited by Void. Free Raise on Games: Sadane when criticizing others' art.

- Rank 2 — The Soul's Dream: Public performance of chosen art — Contested (chosen art)/Awareness vs Etiquette/Willpower — failures shift audience emotions as desired for Insight Rank hours.

- Rank 3 — Free the Spirit: Select a second chosen art. Bonus increases to +2k1 for both.

- Rank 4 — Undying Name: (chosen art)/Awareness roll (TN 20 + 5×Glory Rank) to increase or decrease target's Glory by up to 5 points (+5 per Raise). 2 additional Raises for Infamy instead. Once per six months per target.

- Rank 5 — A Gift Beyond Price: Once per month, gift chosen art to someone from another clan. Contested (chosen art)/Awareness vs Etiquette/Willpower — success permanently shifts their attitude toward your clan favorably. Failure permanently shifts it hostile.

**ADVANCED SCHOOLS:**

**Children of Doji [Courtier]:**

Elite order claiming absolute dominance over the Crane system of allies and favors. Know what everyone desires and use it to control entire courts. Requires Awareness 5, Void 4, Courtier 6, Etiquette 6, Sincerity 5, any one Perform/Artisan 4, four Allies (one at Devotion 4), Honor 5.0+.

- Rank 1 — Social Butterfly: +Xk0 to Contested Social rolls where X = number of Allies with Devotion 2+ in this court (max Insight Rank). Spend three such Allies to gain Darling of the Court Advantage for the season.

- Rank 2 — All is Fair: Spend one week in a neutral/friendly court with 5+ Allies (Devotion 2+), Courtier/Awareness (TN 40) — grant Free Raises on Battle rolls to favored commander (+1 per Raise). Contested if opposed.

- Rank 3 — Gild the Lily: Spend Void Point + Courtier roll to grant any Ally a Social/Material Advantage (up to Honor Rank in XP value) for one month. Also deny enemies a Social Advantage for one month via Contested roll.

**Daidoji Harriers [Bushi] [IH]:**

Covert operatives using dishonorable methods — sabotage, assassination, gaijin pepper. Founded in the ruins of Shiro Giji in 330. Exposed during the War of Silk and Steel, disbanded by Champion Doji Domotai. Requires Agility 4, Air 4, Craft: Explosive 3, Hunting (Traps) 3, Stealth 5, Knives 4. Must be Daidoji and recruited.

- Rank 1 — Move Like Shadows: Lose only half Honor (rounded down) for dishonorable actions on behalf of the Crane. +1k1 bonus to rural trap damage (stacks with Scout techniques).

- Rank 2 — Iron Feather: In light/ashigaru/no armor, add Stealth Skill Rank to Armor TN.

- Rank 3 — Unforgiving Steel: Attacking surprised/unaware foes also Dazes them. Cannot recover from Daze until next Reactions Stage. Daze recovery TN +5.

**Kakita Master Artisan:**

Secret techniques of artistic perfection passed down from the Empire's finest artists. Art so perfect it temporarily changes reality — mystifies shugenja. Requires Awareness 5, Void 5, chosen art at Rank 8+. If Kakita Artisan: must have Great Potential in chosen art. If not: must have Prodigy and Great Potential in one art skill.

- Rank 1 — The Master's Touch: Chosen art roll (TN 40) — success gives 5+ Glory and audience gains one bonus Void Point for 24 hours (two Void Points with 4+ Raises).

- Rank 2 — Mastery Unbound: +2k0 to chosen art skill rolls. Select a third chosen art if trained as Kakita Artisan.

- Rank 3 — The Perfect Art: Once per month, spend 2 Void Points and roll (TN 50) — success brings the art to life. Manifestation lasts a few minutes (one hour with 4 Raises). GM determines the helpful and extraordinary manifestation.

**Kenshinzen [Bushi] [CR]:**

The pinnacle of iaijutsu mastery. Monsters of single combat — impeccable technique and lethal aura. Entire consciousness devoted to the fast draw and perfection of the single strike. Requires Fire 4, Void 4, Iaijutsu 5, Lore: Bushido 4, Meditation 5. Must defeat a current Kenshinzen in a legal duel.

- Rank 1 — Drawing the Void: In Center Stance, add +10 to Armor TN.

- Rank 2 — Kakita's Strength: Assessment and Focus rolls in iaijutsu duels — dice explode on 9 as well as 10.

- Rank 3 — A Single Moment: If you make only one attack this Turn, the opponent you hit is Stunned until beginning of your next Turn.

**ALTERNATE PATHS:**

**Aerie Falconer:**

Rank 2 (or 4). Replaces: Doji Courtier 2, Kakita Bushi 2, or Daidoji Iron Warrior 4. Requires: Animal Handling (Falcon) 3. Finest falconers in the Colonies — use birds as political tools and weapons. Technique — Spirit of the Falcon: When hunting with trained falcon, spending Void Point on Hunting adds half Animal Handling (Falcon) as bonus unkept dice. Verbal command as Free Action to send trained falcon to attack an enemy.

**Asahina Archers [Bushi]:**

Replaces: Daidoji Iron Warrior 4 or Doji Magistrate 4. Requires: Air 3, Kyujutsu 5, must be Asahina or Daidoji. Technique — No Regrets: Called Shots cost 1 less Raise (minimum 1). Add half Air (rounded down) as unkept dice to archery attacks.

**Asahina Artisans [Shugenja]:**

Replaces: Asahina Shugenja 3. Founded by Asahina Hake — use magic to create transitory but supremely memorable art through illusion. Technique — Hake's Lesson: When making Artisan Skill Roll, expend spell slots to add +2 per slot to total (Air slots add +3). Maximum slots equal to twice Insight Rank.

**Asahina Fetishist [Shugenja] [CR]:**

Replaces: Asahina Shugenja 2. Requires: one Craft or Artisan Skill at Rank 3. Master creators of tsangusuri fetishes — highly prized magical tokens. Technique — The World in the Palm of the Hand: Spend Void Point while creating small token — imbue one-time blessing granting +2k0 to one High Skill Roll when activated. Max one token per person per day.

**Asahina Fire Sculptors [Shugenja, Artisan]:**

Rank 2. Replaces: Any Shugenja 2. Requires: Fire 3, Artisan (Sculpture) 2. Create fleeting sculptures of fire, heat, light, or smoke. Technique — The Inner Shape of Fire: Expend Fire spell slot, Artisan (Sculpture)/Fire roll (base TN 20) — create fire sculpture lasting Fire Ring minutes requiring full concentration. GM awards Glory for successful performance.

**Crane Elite Spearman [Bushi]:**

Replaces: Daidoji Iron Warrior 2 or Daidoji Scout 2. Requires: Spears 3. Daidoji spear tradition — exploit numerical disadvantage with superior reach. Technique — Talons of the Daidoji: When attacking with spear, if you strike an opponent who has not yet acted this Round, they must make two Raises to attack you for the remainder of the Round.

**Daidoji Heavy Regulars [Bushi]:**

Replaces: Daidoji Iron Warrior 4. Requires: Heavy Weapons 5. Train alongside Crab on the Kaiu Wall. Blunt and break enemy charges. Prefer tetsubo or dai tsuchi. Technique — Way of the Iron Crane: In Attack Stance with heavy weapon, +1k1 to attacks against opponents in Attack or Full Attack Stances. In Defense/Full Defense with heavy weapon, add Heavy Weapons Rank to Armor TN.

**Daidoji Spymaster [Courtier/Ninja]:**

Rank 3. Replaces: Daidoji Iron Warrior 3, Daidoji Scout 3, or Doji Courtier 3. Requires: Crafty Advantage or Honor 4.0 or less. Must be Daidoji. Run the clan's information networks. Technique — Truth in Shadows: Gain Spy Network Advantage free (or use existing one twice as often). +2k0 to Stealth, Investigation, and Temptation. Lose only half Honor (rounded up) for moderately dishonorable actions on behalf of the Crane.

**Daidoji Trading Council [Courtier]:**

Replaces: Doji Courtier 3, Doji Magistrate 3, Daidoji Iron Warrior 3, or Daidoji Scout 3. Requires: Commerce (Merchant) 3 and either Gentry or Wealthy. Technique — The Golden Path: Once per month, wage commercial warfare — Contested Commerce/Awareness. Win: opponent loses koku equal to margin. Lose: you lose half that. May purchase Gentry/Wealthy/Servants for 1 less XP.

**Doji Apologist [Courtier]:**

Rank 4. Replaces: Doji Courtier 4. Requires: Etiquette (Courtesy) 5. Cover for the errors of comrades and allies. Technique — All Is Forgiven: Etiquette (Courtesy)/Awareness (TN 25) to erase a Minor Breach. 2 Raises to reduce Major to Minor. 5 Raises to reduce Blasphemous to Major. Minor: act within 2×Insight Rank hours. Major: within that many minutes. Blasphemous: immediately. Cannot cover for own errors.

**Doji Innocents [Courtier]:**

Replaces: Doji Courtier 3. Requires: Honor 5.0+, Sincerity (Honesty) 4. Win allies through radical honesty rather than favors. Technique — The Power of Innocence: When telling the truth instead of lying for advantage, Sincerity (Honesty)/Awareness vs Investigation/Perception — success impresses target, gaining them as 1-point Devotion Ally. Each success increases Devotion by 1 (max 4). Knowingly lying costs 2 Honor and disables technique for one month.

**Doji Marines [Bushi]:**

Rank 3. Replaces: Doji Magistrate 3 or Daidoji Iron Warrior 4. Requires: Sailing 3. Naval combat and boarding actions — created by Doji Chomei to counter Mantis fleet dominance. Technique — Chomei's Courage: No TN penalties for fighting on slick, irregular, or moving surfaces. In Attack or Full Attack Stance where such a penalty would apply, +Xk0 to melee attacks where X = Reflexes.

**Doji Warrior-Poet [Bushi]:**

Replaces: Kakita Bushi 2. Requires: Iaijutsu 2, Artisan: Poetry 2. The Doji tradition of sword and art as equals. Technique — Fan & Sword: Spending Void on Iaijutsu or Artisan: Poetry rolls grants +2k1 instead of +1k1. Winning a duel or poetry contest grants +1 Glory (and champion gains +1 Glory too if championing someone).

**Empress Guard [Bushi] [CR]:**

Replaces: Kakita Bushi 3 or Daidoji Iron Warrior 4. Requires: Perception 3. Elite protection for the Empress — the Crane's finest warriors. Technique — To Defend Unto Death: Add half Honor Rank to all attack and Perception rolls. While defending Imperial family, +10 Armor TN.

**Kakita Jesters [Artisan]:**

Replaces: Kakita Artisan 5. Specialized kabuki variant — satirize and mock the arrogant and powerful. Speak in rhymes and riddles, foretell doom for attackers. Given sanction to say things otherwise never tolerated. Technique — The Art of Mockery: Contested (chosen art)/Awareness vs Etiquette/Willpower — lose: lose Glory equal to target's Status. Win: target loses Glory equal to your Glory Rank and Honor equal to half that. Once per month per target.

**Sisters of the Sacred Light [Shugenja]:**

Replaces: Asahina Shugenja 2, Isawa Shugenja (Air) 2, or Moshi Shugenja 2. Women only — recruit across clans, forsake former loyalties. Founded after creation of the Centipede Clan. Specialize in exposing illusions. Technique — Light Banishes Lies: Banishing Air kami (illusions) requires only 2 Raises on Sense (instead of 3) and 3 Raises on Commune (instead of 5).

**Student of Mizu-Do [Monk/Artisan]:**

Rank 3. Replaces: Any non-martial Brotherhood Monk 3, Doji Courtier 3, Kakita Artisan 3, or Asahina Shugenja 3. Requires: Jiujutsu (Mizu-do) 4, Water 3, access to sensei. Crane martial art from stage performance — soft, defensive, use enemy's strength against them. Technique — The Way of Water: In Grapple, use opponent's Strength instead of your own. In Defense/Full Defense when melee attack misses — spend Void Point, Jiujutsu/Strength (TN = opponent's attack roll, using their Strength) — success allows immediate Throw or Grapple control.

**Tsume Pikemen [Bushi] [IH]:**

Replaces: Any Crane Bushi 3. Requires: Spears 3, Polearms 3, Honor 3.0+. Tsume vassal family tradition continued by Daidoji after Tsume ruling bloodline exterminated in War of Bleeding Flowers. Defensive spear wall. Technique — Wall of Pikes: In Defense posture with spear/polearm, spend Void Point as Free Action to attack immediately after any opponent attacks you or an ally within 10'. In Full Defense, costs 2 Void Points.
