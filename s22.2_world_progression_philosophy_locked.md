## 22.2 World Progression Philosophy — LOCKED

This is the foundational design principle of the Named Character System. Powerful characters are a finite and irreplaceable resource. The world does not generate replacements at the same level — it generates beginners who must grow.

- New characters enter the world only at Insight Rank 1.

- They advance organically through play and time — gaining experience, completing missions, participating in battles and courts.

- When a high-Insight-Rank character dies, their position must be filled by someone less experienced.

- The world becomes naturally less capable in an area where powerful characters die — a clan that loses its best general must promote a lesser one.

- Recovery is slow and organic — it takes years of in-game time to develop a replacement to the same level.

- This creates real strategic weight to character death. Assassination, battle casualties, and siege losses have lasting consequences that cannot be quickly repaired.
