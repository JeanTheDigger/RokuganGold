## 55.6 Information Transfer on Objective Assignment

**STATUS:** LOCKED

When a lord assigns a primary objective to a vassal (Section 18.2), the assignment carries information. The assigner’s relevant Known Information is copied to the recipient’s knowledge pool:

All topics tagged with the objective’s target fields (province, clan, NPC).

Crisis data: type, estimated strength, affected area.

Contact knowledge: known_contacts for the target clan.

Province status: stability, garrison, stockpile, known threats.

Relevant disposition intel from Probe results.

**Limitations:** Snapshot at moment of assignment (not synchronized). Secrets NOT transferred (must use Reveal action). Letter delivery has time delay. Entries tagged confidence=FRESH.

This means a samurai who receives “protect Province Kuni from brigands” immediately knows: brigands exist, they’re in Kuni, stability is X, garrison is Y PU, the lord considers it priority Z. The decomposition function can assume this baseline knowledge exists.
