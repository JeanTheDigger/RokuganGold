## 55.7 Contact Discovery System

**STATUS:** LOCKED

Solves: “I want to improve Crab relations but I don’t know any Crab people.”

### 55.7.1 known_contacts Dictionary

Every ContextSnapshot includes known_contacts: {clan_id: [npc_ids]}. “Met” means: shared court, exchanged letters, daily conversation, or third-party introduction.

### 55.7.2 IDENTIFY_CONTACT NeedType

Every clan-targeted decomposition starts with: do I know anyone from this clan? If contacts.is_empty() → IDENTIFY_CONTACT. If I know people but none are present → SEND_LETTER or TRAVEL_TO.

### 55.7.3 New Actions

**OBSERVE_COURT_ATTENDEES (1 AP):** Perception + Investigation vs TN 15. Learn clan/family/status of 1–3 unknown attendees. Available at AT_COURT.

**ASK_FOR_INTRODUCTION (1 AP):** Awareness + Courtier vs TN 15. Ask a Friend+ contact to introduce you. Success: new contact with +3 starting disposition. Available at AT_COURT, VISITING, AT_OWN_HOLDINGS (via letter). Kuge-tier gate: when the target of the introduction holds Status 7.0 or higher (Imperial family members, Clan Champions, Emperor’s Chosen, Jeweled Champions — the kuge tier per Section 2.1 Celestial Order), the action uses Etiquette / Awareness instead of Courtier / Awareness, reflecting that accessing kuge-tier figures requires navigating the Imperial or clan bureaucracy rather than ordinary court networking. The Bureaucracy emphasis applies its standard +1k0 emphasis bonus when the roll fires — characters trained in bureaucratic navigation handle these introductions better, as expected. Characters lacking the emphasis roll base Etiquette / Awareness at TN 15 without the emphasis bonus. The intermediary (Y, the Friend+ contact) must themselves hold Status 4.0 or higher to have plausible kuge access — a ji-samurai contact cannot introduce anyone to the Emperor no matter how beloved. On success against a kuge target, the introduction grants +2 starting disposition rather than +3 (kuge figures maintain greater social reserve toward new contacts). Bureaucracy emphasis note — this gate applies only to the direct-introduction use case. Other Bureaucracy-specific mechanics (Otomo Bureaucrat “Imperial Scrutiny”, Section 29) retain their own rules.

**WRITE_LETTER (non-lords: 0 AP free + 1 AP for batch; lords: 1 Civilian Order — Section 57.34):** Existing action. For IDENTIFY_CONTACT: write to a Friend asking for introductions.
